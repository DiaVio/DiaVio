
Justification:
- Vehicle one (V1) failed to stop and impacted the back of vehicle two (V2).
- Vehicle two (V2) stopped behind vehicle three (V3).
- The front of vehicle two (V2) contacted the back of vehicle three (V3).
- All three vehicles came to a final rest near the points of impact, facing north in lane one.

This sequence of events indicates that the rear of one vehicle collided with the front of another vehicle, which is characteristic of a Rear-End Collision.
