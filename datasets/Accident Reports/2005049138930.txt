The crash occurred mid-afternoon involving two vehicles a passenger car and a SUV on a two-way, two lane, undivided roadway. The road was dry, new, level asphalt. The posted speed limit is 56 kph (35 mph). There was rush hour traffic at the time of the crash. There were no adverse weather conditions.

V 1 a 2000 Nissan Altima driven by a 16 year-old female was northbound in the first travel lane. V 2 a 1999 Ford Explorer driven by a 28 year-old male was southbound in the first travel lane. V 1 attempted to pass the traffic ahead by departing the travel lanes to the right, struck a retaining wall and lost control. V 1 rotated counterclockwise, reentered the roadway and was struck on the right side by the front of V 2. V 1 continued to rotate counterclockwise and departed the travel lanes to the west and came to rest facing north. V 2 continued southbound and came to rest on the east side of the travel lanes facing southwest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
