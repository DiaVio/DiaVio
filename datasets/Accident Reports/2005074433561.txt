This single vehicle crash occurred on a two-lane, two-way, non-divided, bituminous roadway with a -13% slope, and no shoulders on both sides of roadway. No adverse weather conditions present at the time of collision. Collision occurred during early evening rush hour traffic. Posted speed limit 72 kmph (45 mph).

Vehicle one (V1), a 1961 Oldsmobile Dynamic 88 driven by a 42 year-old female was traveling east in lane one. V1 driver attempted an avoidance action when eastbound traffic apparently came to a stop. V1 driver applied brakes and steered left in an attempt to avoid a collision. Vehicle went into a counterclockwise yaw, crossed into westbound traffic, departed roadway and collided with a tree. Vehicle struck tree with front/right fender and wheel. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

V1 was towed from scene.

The pre-event movement for V1 coded going straight, category other motor vehicle in lane, other vehicle stopped. Critical reason for the critical event coded vehicle related factor "brakes failed".

Associative factors, decision error since driver had knowledge of deficient braking system driver should have adjusted speed and gap due to heavy traffic conditions and negative slope on roadway.
