This crash occurred on a rural, two lane, traveled asphalt roadway. The road was wet from rain. The speed limit was 56 KMPH (35 MPH). The grade was level. The driver interview and vehicle inspection were denied by the owner.

Vehicle 1 was a 1990 Oldsmobile 88 driven by a 44 year old female, there were no other occupants. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1.

Vehicle 1 had successfully negotiated a curve when the vehicle departed the roadway on the right side ran over the curve sign and traveled approximately 40 meters and struck a utility pole.

The Critical PreCrash Event was this vehicle traveling off the road edge on the right side. The Critical Reason for the Critical PreCrash Event was too fast for conditions. The conditions being rain, wet road surface and 10+ miles per hour over the speed limit.
