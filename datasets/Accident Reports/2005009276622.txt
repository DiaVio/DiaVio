This incident occured on a two lane highway. The weather conditions were wet and rainy. There was an overcast throughout most of the morning and it had been raining most of the morning.

Vehicle 1 was a 2000 Kia Sephia driven by a 24 year old male. The driver of vehicle 1 stated that he was traversing a curve at a constant rate of speed when he lost control of the car because the road was wet. He said he started to slide left and right and tried to get control of the car, but couldnt. Vehicle 1 then struck a telephone pole and came to rest there. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.

The critical pre-crashh event for this vehicle is This vehicle lost control due to traveling to fast for conditions. Critical reason for critical pre-crash event was Decision Error: too fast for conditions (i.e., rain).
