This crash occurred in the afternoon of a weekday on the western roadside of a two-way, five-lane undivided roadway. The roadway consisted of two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes, separated by a two-way left turn lane and a posted speed limit of 56kmph (35 mph). The bituminous roadway was dry, straight, and level. It was daylight with normal traffic flow and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.

V1, a 2004 Jeep Liberty Sport, was southbound traveling in lane one. For unknown reasons, the driver of V1 drove off the right side of the roadway. The front of V1 struck a large plastic garbage container. V1 than continued forward and struck a tree (diameter greater than 10cm). The driver and any other occupants fled the scene on foot prior to the arrival of rescue and police personnel. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.

The Critical Precrash Event for V1 was this vehicle traveling, off the edge of the road on the right side. The Critical Reason for the Critical Event was coded as a driver related factor, an unknown driver error. No associated factors could be determined for the driver of V1 due to lack of information regarding this crash.
