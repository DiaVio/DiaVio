The crash occurred at the intersection of a two-way, four lane (seven overall), divided, level roadway without a positive barrier (V1) and a two way, three lane, undivided roadway (V2). The four lane road was dry, traveled concrete. The three lane road was dry, traveled asphalt. Vehicle #2 had a -6.6% grade. The posted speed limit was 40 mph (64 kph) for V1. The posted speed limit was 35 mph (56 kph) for V2. Traffic was light at the time of the crash. There were no adverse weather conditions. The crash time was 0714 hours on a sunny, clear day.

Vehicle #1 was a 2001 Mazda 626 eastbound in the third travel lane. Vehicle #2 was a 2002 Ford Taurus stopped northbound in the second travel lane. Vehicle #1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of vehicle #2. The right side of V1 then side slapped the left side of V2. Vehicle #1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing northeast. Vehicle #2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing southeast. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The critical pre-crash event for vehicle #1 was "this vehicle traveling-crossing over (passing through) intersection.” The critical pre-crash event for vehicle #2 was "other vehicle encroachment-from crossing street across path.”
