Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 2005 Toyota Matrix, four-door (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a female, age 38 as its driver and lone occupant. V1's occupant was wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the driver frontal airbag deployed. The crash occurred during the hours of darkness. The weather at the time of the crash was raining. The roadway surface was wet. The posted speed limit is 25 mph (40 kmph). The crash occurred on a two lane roadway. The roadway travels primarily east and west. The eastbound direction has a right hand curve and a uphill grade. The westbound direction has a left hand curve and a downhill grade. The roadway is bordered by a dirt shoulders and ditches on both sides. The roadway is divided by a double yellow line. V1 was traveling westbound. V1 ran off the right side of the road onto the dirt shoulder. V1's driver steered to the left in an attempt to re-enter the roadway. V1 returned to the roadway and crossed into the eastbound lane. V1's driver then steered to the right, causing lateral loading of the left tires, resulting in a two quarter turn, left side leading roll ont its left side and coming to final rest on its top. The case study participant were transported by air to a trauma center.
