This crash occurred on the Southbound part of a 3 lane highway with a grassy median. There were two turn lanes. One for a left turn and one for a right turn that began about 50 meters from the traffic light. The highway had a posted speed limit of 88 kmph (55 mph). Conditions were nighttime and dry. The crash occured in the late part of the evening.

Vehicle 1 was a 2005 Kia Sedona driven by a 69 year old male who was in the left turn only lane at the intersection. Vehicle 2 was a 2005 Acura RL driven by a 22 year old female who was traveling southbound on the highway at a constant rate of speed. Vehicle 1 made an illegal manuever by turning right from the left turn only lane, crossed over 3 lanes resulting in Vehicle 2 striking Vehicle 1 on its right side making it overturn onto its left side. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was 'This vehicle traveling over the lane line on right side of travel lane. The critical pre-crash event was 'Decision error: Illegal Manuever.

Several attempts have been made to get in contact with both drivers with negative results.
