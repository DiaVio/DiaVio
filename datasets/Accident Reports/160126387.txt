Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Lexus SC430 convertible coupe, was traveling south in lane one of a two lane, two-way roadway under dry, daylight conditions. Vehicle 2 (V2), a 2005 Ford Explorer, was traveling north on the same roadway. V1 attempted to pass an uninvolved vehicle and the front of V1 contacted the front of V2. Both vehicles came to final rest in the roadway. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. The case study occupant was the driver and sole occupant of V1 and is a 56-year-old male. The driver was wearing a three-point lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbags deployed in V1. The driver of V1 was transported to a level-one trauma center for treatment.
