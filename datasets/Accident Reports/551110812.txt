This crash occurred during daylight hours with wet roadway surface. Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Mitsubishi Montero Sport utility vehicle was travelling southwest in lane one of a four lane, two way street that curved to the left south. An unknown vehicle in the lane two changed lanes into lane one forcing V1 to swerve right and lock up the brakes. V1 then departed the roadway to the right and impacted a rockery wall adjacent to the sidewalk where it came to rest. The unknown vehicle was not contacted and departed the scene. V1 was towed and disabled. The case occupant is the driver of V1, a 28 year old female, who was wearing the manual lap and shoulder belt with the frontal airbag deploying. No other occupants were in V1 and the driver was transported to the trauma center.
