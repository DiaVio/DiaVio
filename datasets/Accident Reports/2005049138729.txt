The crash occurred on the roadside of a two-way, two lane, undivided, straight, level, roadway. The road surface was dry, traveled concrete. The posted speed limit was 48 kmph (30 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions. The crash time was early morning on a sunny, clear day.

Vehicle one (V1) was a 1994 Dodge Ram 1500 northbound in the first travel lane. V1 departed the roadway to the right and the front struck a utility pole where it came to rest. V1 came to rest partially off the roadway facing northeast. The driver of V1 fled the scene on foot and was unidentified. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded: this vehicle traveling, off the edge of the road on the right side.
