This crash occurred early evening hours involving one passenger car and teo pick-up trucks on a two lane (two-way traffic) bridge. The speed limit for the roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). The road surface concrete and dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash. The bridge is illuminated at night.

V 1 a 2001 Chevrolet Silverado Pick up driven by a 30 year-old male and V 3 a 1992 Chevrolet Pick up driven by a 38 year-old male were traveling west in the first lane of the eastbound lanes. V 2 a 2002 Honda Civic driven by a 68 year-old male with two passengers was traveling east in the first lane. At the curvature of the roadway, V 1 attempted to pass V 3 which was traveling at a slow pace in front of V 1. V 1 changed into the oncoming traffic lane. The left front corner of V 1 impacted the left side of V 2. Both vehicles sideswiped each other before they disengaged. The rear axle of V 1 became dislocated. V 1 reentered lane one and continued to drive for about 25 meter before it slightly impacted the rear of V 3. V 1 and V 3 both came to rest in lane 1, facing west. V 2 came to rest in the eastbound lane, facing east. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

V 1 and V 2 were towed from the scene. V 3 was able to drive off the scene with minor damage. The left rear occupant in V 3 was treated at the scene by EMS.
