The crash occurred in the center of a "T" intersection composed of a three-lane north/southbound roadway, and a two-lane one-way eastbound roadway. All roadways were bituminous and level, with posted speed limits of 40 kmph (25 mph). Yield Signs controlled the eastbound roadway. It was afternoon on a weekday. The roads were wet and it was raining. Traffic flow was moderate.

V1, a 1999 Toyota Corolla with one occupant, was traveling east in lane two when it attempted to turn left at the intersection without observing the Yield Sign and was struck by V2 on the left side. V2, a 1995 Ford F350 Pickup with one occupant, was traveling south in lane one. As V2 passed through the intersection it struck V1 on the left side. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

V1, driven by a 39-year-old female who reported a complaint of pain to the neck and was transported, stated that she was uncomfortable with traffic density and road conditions, and stated that she should have used her turn signal. The driver stated that she did see V2, but believed that she had time to make the turn, so she continued through the intersection without stopping. V1 made no avoidance maneuver.

The Critical Precrash Event for V1 was "this vehicle traveling-turning left at intersection". The Critical reason for the Critical Event was "misjudgment of gap or other's speed", the driver misjudged V2's speed. The following associated factors were assigned: She was taking several medications Buspar, Lasix, Seroquel, Zoloft, Depakote and Topomax, spina bifida, inattention due to preceding argument, failed to stop for TCD, proceeded with insufficient clearance, she suffers from various nervous disorders, uncomfortable with surrounding traffic densities, uncomfortable with general traffic speeds and uncomfortable with general traffic flow (driver generally uncomfortable with all aspects of local traffic).

The Driver of V2, a 49-year-old male, reported no injuries. Driver stated that he was traveling at approximately 40 kmph (25 mph) as he entered the intersection, when V2 turned across his path and he struck V1 on the left side. V2 applied his brakes and turned to the left in an attempt to avoid V1.

The Critical Precrash Event for V2 was "other vehicle encroachment-from crossing street, turning into opposite direction". No Critical Reason was coded to this vehicle. No associated factors were coded to this vehicle.
