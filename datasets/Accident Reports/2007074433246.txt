This two-vehicle crash occurred on an undivided, level, two-lane roadway in a three-legged intersection. In the eastbound direction, the travel lane curves to the right and in the westbound direction, the travel lane curves to the left. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). The intersecting roadway is on the north side of the east/west roadway. A sign on the south side of this roadway reads "left turning traffic must yield." This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.

Vehicle one (V1), a 2007 Kia Sedona, was eastbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Chrysler Pacifica, was westbound in lane one. A non-contact vehicle (NCV) was eastbound in front of V1. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The driver of V1, a thirty-four-year-old female, was on her way home after dropping some children at a foster home (work-related). She was well rested, in good health and familiar with her vehicle. She rarely travels this route. She has epilepsy and depression and takes the medications Topomax and Effexor XR. This driver stated that she was focused on the NCV in front of her that also turned left in the intersection.

The driver of V2, a thirty-seven-year-old female, was on her way home from work. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. She travels this route daily. This driver stated that she saw V1 before it started the turn and applied the brakes when she realized that V1 was turning.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded as "this vehicle traveling; turning left at intersection." The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded as "driver related factor; inadequate surveillance (e.g. failed to look, looked but did not see)." This driver's focus on the vehicle in front of her was an associated factor in this crash.

The critical pre-crash event for V2 was coded as "other vehicle encroachment; from opposite direction - over left lane line." The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for V2 was "not coded to this vehicle."
