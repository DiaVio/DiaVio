This case involves a front impact in an off-set mode to the right, between a passenger car and the back-end of a tractor-trailer. The case vehicle rode under the back end of the trailer. The restrained 83-year-old male driver is the case occupant and the steering-wheel air bag deployed. The case vehicle, a four-door 2011Toyota Avalon, was westbound in the curb lane of a dry, level, divided, four-lane, asphalt traffic-way. Vehicle two (V2), a tractor-trailer, was parked along the curb in the same lane. The case vehicle struck the back-left corner of the trailer and rode under it approximately three meters, rotated clockwise and came to rest facing northwest. The restrained 83-year-old male driver was the only occupant of the case vehicle. The driver sustained serious injuries and was transported to a level-one trauma center and enrolled as a case occupant.
