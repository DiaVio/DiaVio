This crash occurred at night with street lights and clear, dry conditions. Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Toyota Camry four door sedan, was southbound in the left turn lane of a six lane, two way street and at a controlled intersection. The speed limit was posted at 35 mph/56 kmph. Vehicle two (V2), a1987 Toyota Cressida sedan, was northbound in lane two on the same roadway passing through the intersection. As V1 began to make a left turn, V2 locked up the brakes and swerved right before the front of V2 impacted the right side of V1. The impact forced V1 laterally with no rotation into the northeast corner of the intersection. V2 came to final rest near V1. The front right passenger of V1 is the case occupant who is a 21 year old female wearing the manual lap and shoulder belt. Front right door panel intrusion was noted into the seating area of the case occupant. The case occupant was transported to the trauma center and both vehicles were towed and disabled.
