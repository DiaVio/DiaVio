This frontal crash involves an 81 year-old restrained male without any air bag deployment who sustained serious injury. The crash occurred during daylight hours dry, clear conditions. The collision occurred in a parking lot.. The case vehicle, a 2003 Ford Focus 3-door was traveling east in the parking lot. The case vehicle attempted to pull into a parking spot. The driver attempted to press the brake but pressed the gas instead and the case vehicle went through the parking spot, off the roadway and struck a building with its front. The driver of V1 was able to get the vehicle into reverse and pull back into the parking spot. V1 was driven by an 81-year-old male case occupant who was restrained by the available 3-point manual belt. The available frontal air bags did not deploy as a result of the impact. The case occupant went home and was taken by ambulance to a hospital later in the day.
