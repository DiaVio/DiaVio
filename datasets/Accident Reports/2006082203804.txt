This single-vehicle crash occurred on the roadside of a level roadway in a road construction area. This roadway consists of one lane in each direction with a center left-turn lane. This roadway curves left in the southbound pre-crash area and is straight in the area of the crash. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours. It was foggy at the time of the crash and the roadway was wet.

Vehicle one (V1), a 1994 Chevrolet S10 pickup, was southbound in lane one. V1 departed the right side of the roadway and the front struck a utility pole and a metal box. V1 came to rest facing southwest and was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

The driver of V1, a forty-one-year-old female, was not interviewed due to a language barrier. No translator was available.
