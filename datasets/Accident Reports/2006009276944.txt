This crash occured on a two lane highway with a slight curve to the left in the west bound lane. Conditions were dark with dry roads.

Vehicle 1 was a 1996 Dodge Caravan driven by a 27 year old female. The driver stated that she was driving when a deer walked out and stopped in the middle of the roadway. Occupant of Vehicle 1 stated that they were driving along when a deer walked out and stopped in the road also The driver swerved to miss the deer and departed the roadway, driving the vehicle into a ditch. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
