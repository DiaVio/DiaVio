This crash occurred just past noon, involved are two vehicle's a passenger car and a pick-up truck on a three lane freeway with a exit ramp to the right also a positive barrier. The roadway surface is concrete, dry with a posted speed limit of 97 kmph (60 mph). The weather was clear and daylight hours..

Vehicle one (V1), an unidentified Dodge pick-up traveling eastbound in the second lane, crossed over the lane to the right to exit. V1 contacted the right front side of vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Oldsmobile Alero driven by a 25 year-old male who was traveling eastbound in the first lane going straight. V2 steered right to avoid the collision. After the impact with V1, V2 left the roadway to the right and rolled over continuing down an embankment coming to final rest upright. V1 fled the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded: this vehicle traveling, over the lane line on right side of travel lane. The critical reason for the critical event was coded to V1 as a driver related factor: type of driver error unknown.

The critical pre-crash event for V2 was coded: other vehicle encroachment: from adjacent lane (same direction) - over left lane line. The critical reason was not coded to this vehicle.
