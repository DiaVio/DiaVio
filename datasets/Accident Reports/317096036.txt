This case involved a 21 year old restrained female driver of a 2007 Nissan Versa four door sedan (V1). The driver was utilizing the available three point manual safety belts and had a deployed frontal steering wheel mounted airbag and driver side inflatable curtain airbag. The crash occurred at night on a lighted two lane asphalt roadway. The roadway was straight and level and it was raining at the time of the crash. V1 was reportedly traveling at the posted speed limit of 72 kph (45 mph). V1 was traveling north and drifted across the center line and off the left side of the roadway. V1 struck a ditch with its frontal plane. V1 slid in a clockwise yaw and impacted a utility pole with its left front door. V1 came to rest at impact and was towed from the scene due to disabling damage. The driver of V1 was transported by ground ambulance to a level one trauma center and hospitalized due to the injuries sustained in the crash.
