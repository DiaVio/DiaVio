This crash occurred in the northbound lane of a 4-lane undivided roadway. There were two lanes going north and two lanes going south. This was a straight, level, and bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). It was early afternoon on a weekday and the roads were wet and skies were cloudy with light rainfall. Traffic flow was congested with a lot of stop-and-go traffic activity.

V1, a 2003 Jeep Liberty sport utility vehicle was traveling north in lane two intending on continuing straight. V2, a 2007 Honda Accord 4-door was traveling north in lane one intending on continuing straight. As V1 was traveling in lane two another non-contact vehicle cut in front of V1. V1 avoided the collision by switching lanes into lane one into V2. The right side of V1 struck the left side of V2. Both vehicles came to final rest on the shoulder. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

V1, the 2003 Jeep Liberty, was driven by an 18-year-old male who reported no injuries. The other passengers were a 25-year-old male and a 23-year-old female who also reported no injuries. The driver said that he was continuing north in lane two intending on continuing straight when another non-contact vehicle cut in front of him forcing him to switch lanes to avoid a collision with that vehicle. The driver stated that he thought lane one was clear, but as he was negotiating the changing lanes he realized that it was not and attempted to avoid the collision by braking but was unsuccessfully and struck the left side of V2.

The Critical Pre-crash Event for V1 was, "This vehicle traveling- over the lane line on the right side of travel lane". The Critical Reason for the Critical Pre-crash Event was coded as, "Inadequate surveillance- looked but did not see". The other associated factors coded to this driver was inexperienced driver since the driver has only has his license for a little over a year. The other associated factors coded to this driver were wet roads, cloudy and rainy conditions, and congested roadways.

V2, the 2007 Honda Accord, was driven by a 57-year-old female who reported an unknown injury but was not transported to medical facility. At the time of case release, no driver interview was provided. According to the police report, the driver stated that she was traveling in lane one intending on heading straight when the left side of her vehicle was struck by V1.

The Critical Pre-crash Event for V2 was, "Other motor vehicle encroachment-from adjacent lane (same direction) over left lane line". No Critical Reason for Critical Precrash Event was coded to this vehicle. Without a driver interview, the only associated factors that could be coded to this vehicle were, wet roads, cloudy and rainy conditions, and congested roadways.
