This single vehicle crash occurred at approximately 0650 hours on a clear day. The crash occurred on a four lane divided interstate with a positive barrier. The bituminous roadway is straight, level and was dry. Posted speed limit 112 kph (70 mph).

V 1, a 1998 Dodge Neon sedan , driven by a 44 year-old female was eastbound in lane one. A non-contacted semi truck with trailer was eastbound in lane two. The semi truck changed lanes to the right into V 1's travel lane. In an attempt to avoid a collision with the semi truck vehicle one locked its brakes and steered right. V 1 went into a clockwise yaw and departed lane one to the right into the shoulder then departed the right road edge. V 1 started down the roadside slope and the left side struck a light pole. V 1 bounced off the light pole and subsequently rolled onto the right passenger side of the vehicle one quarter turn and slid to final rest. V 1 came to final rest off the right roadside facing southeast. V 1 was towed from the scene the driver was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1.

The critical pre-crash event for vehicle one was coded as "other vehicle encroachment: from adjacent lane [same direction] - over left lane line". The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event was coded as "driver related factor: poor directional control".
