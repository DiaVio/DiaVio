This two-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours in the middle of a four-way intersection. The east/west roadway is comprised of three lanes, one through lane, and a left-turn lane in each direction. The north/south roadway is comprised of two through lanes and a left-turn lane in each direction. Road surface dry,level asphalt. The intersection is controlled by traffic lights. The speed limit is 40 kph (25 mph).

Vehicle one,(V 1) a 2000 Buick Century sedan driven by a 79 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling eastbound in the left turn lane. Vehicle two,(V 2) a 1995 Buick LaSabre sedan driven by a female with one passenger, was traveling westbound in the first lane. V 1 was attempting to make a left turn when V 1's right side was struck by the front of V 2. V 1,V 2 came to final rest within the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene and the passenger in V 2 was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
