This crash occurred in the southbound divided freeway lanes and on a bridge. The weather was overcast and the roadway surface was wet. It was dark and no street lights located in the area of this crash. Case vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Mazda 626 four door sedan was traveling in the southbound lane one of three lanes when it rear ended the back of a transit bus that was stopped in the lane. The front of V1 rode under the back bumper of the bus with damage occurring mainly at the grill and hood of V1. The driver of V1 was braking prior to impact. V1 had to be pulled out from under the bus and towed from the scene. The bus was driven from the scene. The CIREN subject is the driver of V1 and the only occupant in the case vehicle. The case driver is a 49 year old female who was restrained with a manual lap/shoulder belt and the frontal steering column air bag deployed. She sustained serious injuries and was transported to the trauma center.
