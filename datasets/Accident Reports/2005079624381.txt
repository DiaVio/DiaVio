A two vehicle collision occurred at 1820 hours on a Friday. The collision occurred on an undivided, dry, level, asphalt, two-lane roadway with parallel parking allowed on both sides of the road. For eastbound traffic, the roadway curves to the left. The speed limit is designated a 25 mph (40 kph) zone.

Vehicle one (V1) is a 1997 Honda Civic that was parallel parked facing east along the south edge of the roadway. Vehicle two (V2) is a 1985 Honda Civic that was traveling eastbound in the number one lane of the roadway. As V2 was traveling eastbound, V1 left its parked position in an attempt to drive into a driveway that was directly across the street. The front of V2 struck the left side of V1. Both vehicles came to rest facing east adjacent to the impact location. V2 was towed; V1 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The driver of vehicle one is a 19-year-old unlicensed female. The driver was waiting for her husband to get out of work. The driver became impatient and decided to drive across the street into the parking lot. This driver attempted a left turn from a parking position in front of V2. The driver was not available for an interview. The pre-event movement for V1 was coded "leaving a parking position." The critical pre-crash event was coded "this vehicle traveling-turning left at intersection." The critical reason was coded "decision error-illegal maneuver." Driver attempted a left turn from a parked position.

The driver of vehicle two is a 24-year-old unlicensed male who drives the route daily and is familiar with the vehicle. He attempted to brake when V1 pulled out from the parking position. The pre-event movement for V2 was coded "going straight." The critical pre-crash event was coded "other vehicle encroachment-from parking position." The critical reason was not coded to this vehicle.
