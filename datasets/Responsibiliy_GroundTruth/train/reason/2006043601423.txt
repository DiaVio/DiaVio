The driver of V1 admitted to taking the right turn too fast for the wet road conditions, which caused him to lose control of the vehicle and hydroplane into opposing traffic. The Critical Precrash Event for V1 was coded as vehicle control loss due to traveling too fast for conditions, and the Critical Reason for the Critical Event was also coded as traveling too fast for conditions (turn and wet roads). Therefore, V1's excessive speed for the conditions and failure to maintain control of the vehicle on the wet road are the main reasons for the crash.
