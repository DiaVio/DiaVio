The crash occurred on a north/south 5-lane undivided roadway at an entrance to a stadium. The roadway was dry and bituminous with a 5.7 percent downhill grade in the northbound direction and a 5.7 percent uphill grade in the southbound direction. The posted speed limit was 72 kmph (45 mph). It was night on a weekend but the roadway was illuminated by streetlamps. Traffic flow was moderate.
V1, a 2005 Toyota Camry 4-door sedan with one occupant, was traveling north in lane two when the driver attempted to make a left turn in front of V2. V2, a 1995 Chevrolet Lumina 4-door sedan with one occupant, was traveling south in lane three when V1 turned left in front of it. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a 79-year-old male who reported injuries and was transported to the hospital. The driver stated that he was going to a baseball game when he attempted to turn left and does not know what happened next. No avoidance maneuver was attempted.
V2 was driven by a 31-year-old male who reported minor injuries but was not transported to the hospital. The driver stated that he traveling south when V1 suddenly turned in front of him. The driver attempted to avoid the crash by applying his brakes.
