This three-vehicle collision occurred in the evening hours on a dark street that was lit with street lights, The collision occurred on a three-lane undivided roadway. The roadway is straight with a 5% uphill grade, with bituminous paving, just approaching an intersection that is controlled by traffic lights with pedestrian crossing. The roadway was wet and it was raining. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). Access to the roadway is not controlled.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Chevy Prizm sedan driven by a nineteen year (19) old female with one passenger, was headed eastbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2) ,a 1987 Acura Legend sedan driven by a forty four (44) year old male, was also traveling eastbound in lane one. Vehicle three (V3) a 1989 Mitsubishi Precis 5-door hatchback was legally parked facing southbound on the right shoulder of the north/south roadway. V1 initiated a U-turn in the intersection and was struck in the left side by the front of V2. V1 then began to rotate counterclockwise slightly as it was pushed to the northeast. It is unknown if the driver had any acceleration input between impact one and impact two. As her foot may have been pushing the pedal. V1 impacted and came to rest against V3, a legally parked vehicle on the west side of the road. V2 pulled off the roadway. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 was driven from the scene. V3 remained at the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
