This two vehicle crash occurred during early morning daylight hours on a urban, two way, bituminous ,roadway at the entrance to a private driveway. There were no roadway deficiencies, no sight line restrictions and no traffic controls present. The weather was clear and the posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1999 Chevrolet S-10 4x2 pickup, driven by a 17 year-old female who was traveling westbound in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 2001 Ford Escape compact SUV driven by a 23 year-old female with two passengers. V2 was also westbound in the number one lane and was stopped waiting to make a left turn into a private driveway south of the roadway. The driver of V1 attempted to avoid a collision by braking with lockup and was not successful. The front of V1 struck the back of the V2. V1 came to final rest near the point of impact facing generally west. V2 was propelled forward and exited the roadway in a counterclockwise rotation coming to final rest in soft sand and gravel shoulder south of the roadway facing generally southwest. V1 was towed due to damage, V2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
