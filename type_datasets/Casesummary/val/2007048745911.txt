It was dark with no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of this single-vehicle crash. The dirt roadway is undivided, level, curved to the right and consists of one lane in each direction. The speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Chevrolet Trailblazer, was northbound in lane one. V1 traveled over the center line and the driver abruptly steered right. V1 rotated clockwise and traveled off the right side of the roadway. The front of V1 struck an embankment and V1 rolled over to the left four quarter-turns. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a thirty-four-year-old male, was conversing with his two small children as they traveled from their home to a store. This driver had been driving this type of vehicle only one month but stated that he felt comfortable with his vehicle and passengers. This driver provided no further information.
