A three vehicle daytime collision occurred during the morning rush hour. The collision occurred in the southbound lanes of an undivided rural highway, in lane one. The pre-crash roadway for both vehicles is a straight, dry and level bituminous roadway. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 64 kmph (40 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Toyota Celica, was driven by a 22 year-old male who was traveling southbound on the roadway in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Dodge Neon driven by a 19 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling southbound in lane one. Vehicle three (V3), a 2003 Saturn Vue, was parked in a parking lot on the east side of the roadway. V1 was attempting to make a U-turn. V1 first moved to the right to swing wide, and then turned left in front of V2. The front of V2 struck the left side of V1. V2 continued in a southeast direction and impacted a chain-link fence, and then impacted parked V3. V2 came to rest against the front of V3. All three vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
