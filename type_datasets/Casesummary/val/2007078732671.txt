This two-vehicle crash occurred at a four-way intersection that is controlled by traffic lights on colors. There is one westbound and two eastbound lanes at the east side of the intersection. There is one westbound right turn lane and two westbound left turn lanes. There are two westbound lanes and one eastbound lane and one left turn lane at the west side of the intersection. There are two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes separated by a left turn lane. There is one northbound right turn lane at the intersection. The speed limit on the north/south roadway is posted 72 kmph (45 mph). The speed limit on the east/west roadway is posted 64 kmph (40 mph). Both roadways were straight, level and constructed of bituminous asphalt. The roadways were dry and the sky was clear at the time of this nighttime crash. The area was dark but lit.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Mitsubishi 4 door Galant with one occupant, was traveling southbound and made a left turn on green light. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Ford Expedition XLT with 7 occupants, was traveling northbound in the number two lane at about 64 kmph (40 mph). V2 entered the intersection on a green light when V1 made a left turn and traveled across the path of V2. The front of V1 struck the left side of V2. The impact caused V1 to rotate counterclockwise and travel across the intersection in a northwesterly direction coming to rest near the north side of the intersection facing in a northwesterly direction. V2 came to a controlled stop in the intersection and drove east of the intersection and parked. There was a non-contact vehicle to the left of V2 that avoided V1 prior to the crash in the intersection. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 was parked at the scene and later removed. The driver of V1 was treated at the scene by EMS. There were no injuries reported by the driver and occupants of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
A sixty one (61) year old female drove V1. There was a language barrier between the researcher and the driver and an interview could not be conducted on-scene. The driver could not be located after several attempts and no interview was obtained.
A thirty two (32) year old male drove V2. He said that he was traveling northbound in the number 2 lane (next to the right turn lane) approaching the intersection at about 64 kmph (40 mph). He said that he saw the other car coming the opposite direction in the left turn lane. The car was slowing down and then turned left across the path of his vehicle. He said that he steered to the right and applied his brakes. He said the he felt the brake pedal pulsating just prior to impact. He said that there was a jeep next to his vehicle that had to steer to the left to avoid hitting the car. He said that after he hit the other vehicle, it spun around and came to rest near the northwest corner of the intersection. He said that he had the green light prior to entering the intersection. He said that he had just left a grocery store and was on his way home. He said that prior to that, he and his family were just taking a drive. He said that he thought the other car was going to stop and let him pass through the intersection. The driver worked approximately 48 hours during the last 7 days and had the last 2 days off. The driver is in good health and takes no medication. The driver said he is comfortable driving his vehicle and with the passenger load. There were 6 other occupants in the vehicle at the time of the crash.
