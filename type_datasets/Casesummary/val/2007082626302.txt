This two-vehicle daytime collision occurred in the intersection of a two-lane two-way arterial road, and a two-lane two-way city street. Both roadways are paved with asphalt. The north/south roadway is controlled by a stop sign, with an uphill grade of 3%, the east/west roadway has a downhill grade of 7%. The speed limit for both vehicles is 48 kmph (30 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was clear with no adverse conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Infinity J-30 sedan driven by a seventy one (71) year old male, was traveling north in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Chevrolet Aveo sedan driven by a fifty six (56) year old female, was traveling west in lane one. V1 ran through the stop sign entered the intersection and was impacted on the right rear by the front of V2 causing V1 to rotate clockwise. Driver of V1 over corrected and lost control-causing it travel west. V1 then drove through a chain link fence, and come to final rest facing west on the side of a house. V2 rotated clock-wise and came to rest in a ditch on the north/west corner of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 verbally insulted the researcher; therefore, no interview was obtained with this driver.
