The crash occurred on a secondary arterial road that runs northeast to southwest. The road is two travel lanes undivided with a double yellow stripe. It is straight with a 3% positive grade if traveling northeast. It is intersected from the east by a residential side street that is two lanes undivided. The posted speed limit for both roadways is 48kph/30mph. Conditions were daylight and dry at mid morning on a weekday.
Vehicle 1, a1997 Honda Civic was stopped waiting to turn left onto the residential side street from the southwest direction travel lane on the arterial road. As Vehicle 2, a 1986 Ford Aerostar approached, it was headed northeast in an opposing direction of travel from V1. When V2 was close, V1 attempted the left turn and was struck on the front end by the left front corner of V2. Both vehicles came to rest within a few meters of POI. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Two pedestrians standing on the southeast corner of the intersection were struck by flying debris receiving possible injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle 1 was driven by a twenty-year-old belted male who was uninjured in the crash. He was sited by police for no license, failed to yield and no insurance. His eighteen-year-old passenger was also uninjured in the crash. No airbags were deployed in this vehicle.
Vehicle 2 was driven by a belted 16 year old female who was uninjured in the crash. The belted right front passenger was also uninjured in the crash. The ten-month passenger in the first position second row was listed as having non-incapacitating injuries, although she was not transported for medical treatment. The two-year-old male passenger in the second row third position was uninjured in the crash. The driver of vehicle two is the mother of both children that were in the vehicle and a stay at home mom with no driver's license.
