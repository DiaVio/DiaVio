This two-vehicle crash occurred in a three-legged intersection. The east/west trafficway is straight, level and divided by a grass median. The eastbound roadway consists of two through lanes, a left turn lane and a right turn/through lane. This roadway is controlled by a yield sign. The westbound lanes consist of two through lanes and a right turn/through lane. The speed limit for this trafficway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1987 Mercury Grand Marquis, was eastbound in lane four. Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 Saturn Ion, was westbound in lane one. V1 turned left and the right side was struck by the front of V2. V1 continued to the northeast and the front struck a utility pole. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a seventy-year-old female, was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
The driver of V2, a thirty-one-year-old female, was talking with her thirty-six-year-old and ten-year-old female passengers. This driver was healthy and familiar with her vehicle. She travels this route weekly. She stated that she did not see V1 until it turned in front of her, at which time she applied the brakes (skidding longitudinally) in an attempt to avoid the crash.
