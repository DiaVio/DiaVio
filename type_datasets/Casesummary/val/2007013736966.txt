This crash occurred off the right side of an undivided straight two-lane asphalt roadway with a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55 mph). The crash occurred during the mid-afternoon hours while daylight. There were no adverse conditions at the time of the crash.
V1, a 2001 Ford Ranger pickup truck, was traveling northbound when the driver fell asleep. V1 drifted over the left lane line, returned to its original travel lane, and then departed the roadway to the right. The tires of V1 dug into the soft ground and began a trip-over type rollover. V1 rolled 2-quarter turns to the right and the vehicle came to rest on its top facing west. The driver of V1 was transported to the hospital due to minor injuries. V1 was towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
The driver of V1, a 21-year old male, stated he was on his way home from work. He reported he had only gotten 4 hours of sleep the night before the crash due to work and school schedules. He awoke to find he had drifted into the left lane and was drifting back into his original travel lane. He stated he was headed for the ditch on the right side of the roadway and attempted to brake slightly but could not avoid driving into the ditch and rolling over.
