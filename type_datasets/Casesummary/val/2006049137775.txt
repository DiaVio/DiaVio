The crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a three-lane one-way roadway. This was a straight, level, concrete roadway with a posted speed of 64 kmph (40 mph). It was late afternoon on a weekend and the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic flow was moderate.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Dodge Durango four door with one occupant, was stopped at the intersection southbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Ford F-250 four door with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane three approaching the same intersection. When the light turned green, V1 attempted to turn left across the path of V2. The front left of V1 contacted the right side of V2. In an attempt to avoid the collision, the driver of V2 applied the brakes and steered to the left. The evasive maneuver and the impact with V1 directed V2 onto a median of the cross street where it's front struck a bridge support pole. V2 came to rest at the point of impact with the support pole. V1 steered back to the right and came to rest in the third southbound lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
