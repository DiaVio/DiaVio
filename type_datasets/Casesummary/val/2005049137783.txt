This two vehicle crash occurred on a three-lane highway with a positive barrier during early morning daylight hours. The roadway was dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 80 kmph (50 mph). There was rush-hour traffic and the roadway was under construction. There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Mazda 6 sedan driven by a 42 year-old male, was traveling eastbound in the third lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Honda Civic sedan driven by a 33 year-old male, was traveling eastbound in the third lane. V1 struck a tire and wheel in the roadway, causing the driver to lose directional control of the vehicle. V1 departed the left side of the roadway and struck the traffic barrier. V1 rotated counterclockwise and reentered the roadway. It's left side rear struck the left side of V2. V1 continued to rotate and came to rest in the roadway facing west. V2 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing west. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
