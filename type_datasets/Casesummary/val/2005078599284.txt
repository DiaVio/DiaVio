This collision involved two passenger cars that crashed at an intersection just before the early dawn hour. This crash occurred during darkness with no street lighting in a rural area. The thru street is a bituminous roadway with two traffic lanes, one east and one west separated by a double yellow line with no traffic controls. The cross street is a hard-pack gravel roadway with an emulsion sprayed surface. It is not paved. Traffic crossing the thru street is controlled by stop signs located at the southeast and northwest corners of the intersection. The center of the intersection is slightly elevated above the four legs leading into it to accommodate an irrigation canal that passes under the intersection. The weather was clear with no adverse conditions. The headlights were in use by both vehicles at the time of the crash. The posted speed limit is 80 kmph (50 mph) for both roadways.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993- Ford Taurus 4 door sedan, was traveling east on the thru street in the eastbound lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1991 Honda Accord LX 4 door sedan, that was traveling north on the cross street approaching the stop sign at the intersection. V2 made a rolling stop, accelerated and proceeded into the intersection to attempt a left turn to travel westbound. The driver of V1, a 21-year old male with 14-year old male right front passenger, applied the brakes with lock-up, leaving 41.8 meters (137 feet) of skid marks before the front of V1 struck the left side of V2. The impact caused V2 to rotate counterclockwise, exit the thru street in the northeast quadrant of the intersection, and come to final rest facing south. V1 after impact rotated counterclockwise and came to final rest with the left rear wheel off the south side of the thru street and the remaining three tires on the pavement in eastbound lane one. V1 was facing northwest at final rest. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. The driver of vehicle two was a 42-year old female who sustained fatal injuries. The right front passenger of V2 was a 29-year old male. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1 and 113 kmph for V2.
