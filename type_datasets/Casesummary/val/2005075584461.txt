This single vehicle crash occurred a couple of hours before mid-night on a dark but lighted, straight,asphalt roadway with a 6% downhill grade, two-lane residential road approaching a four-leg intersection. The east/west legs controlled by stop signs. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Isuzu Rodeo SUV driven by a 18-year-old female with four passengers was traveling westbound in lane one. The driver traveled slightly over into the eastbound lane to make a left turn at the intersection and ran off the southwest corner. The front of V1 struck a light pole and then a newspaper box that was attached by a chain to the light pole. V1 rotated clockwise, continuing southwest and the back struck a wooden fence. V1 rotated slightly counter clockwise and came to rest on its wheel facing northeast against the wooden fence. V1 one was towed from the scene due to damage. According to the police report, alcohol was involved. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1.
Note:The driver was eluding the police, alcohol involvement and conversation with passengers.
