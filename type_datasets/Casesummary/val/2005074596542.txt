This intersection type collision occurred at an intersection controlled by overhead traffic control signals that were functioning properly. The east/west street is a 4 lane, one-way, bituminous, level, straight roadway. The speed limit is 25 mph (40 kmph). The north/south street is also a 4 lane, one-way, dry, bituminous, level, straight roadway with a speed limit of 25 mph (40 kmph). There were no environmental or traffic conditions present that contributed to the collision.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1986 Cadillac Eldorado, was traveling east in the second lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle two V2, a 1997 green Oldsmobile Achieve, was traveling north in the second lane approaching the intersection. As both vehicles entered the intersection, the front of V2 struck the right side of V1. Neither driver attempted any evasive maneuvers. At impact, V1 spun clockwise to end up facing southwest upon the north edge of the roadway and sidewalk. At impact, V2 spun clockwise to end up facing southeast in the middle of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
