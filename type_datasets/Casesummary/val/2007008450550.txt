The crash occurred on the shoulder of the northbound lane of a two lane undivided roadway. There was one northbound lane and one southbound lane. This was a straight, bituminous roadway with an uphill grade of 4%. The posted speed limit was 56 KMPH (35 MPH). It was afternoon on a weekday and road was dry and the skies were clear. Traffic flow was light and flowing freely.
V1, a 1997 Ford Aerostar minivan with three occupants, was traveling north when the van departed the roadway and struck a utility pole with its front. The van spun clockwise and came to rest on the roadway blocking both lanes of travel. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
V1, the Ford Aerostar, was driven by a 35 year old female who was transported due to unknown injuries. No interview could be obtained for the driver of Vehicle 1.
