This crash occured on a northbound two lane highway. The intersection is controlled by traffic signals. The speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph). Conditions were daylight with clear skies but very cold. The crash occured in the late morning hours.
Vehicle two was a 1988 Ford Thunderbird driven by a 53 year old male. Vehicle two was stopped at the stop light. The light had turned green and Vehicle two was attempting to proceed with the traffic. Vehicle 1 was a 1993 Cadillac Fleetwood driven by a 21 year old male. Vehicle 1 was traveling at a constant velocity. The driver of V1 failed to give full time and attention to V2 and struck it in the rear end. The driver of V2 was transported due to injury and the vehicle was also towed due to damage. Vehilce 1 was not towed and removed by a friend of the driver. The passenger in V1 stated that V2 had a slow start and assumed that he would proceed once the light was green. V2 didn't so V1 struck it. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
