This crash occurred off the right side of a two lane undivided roadway with a left curvature of 563 meters and at an intersection. There were two flashing yellow lights and a yellow suggested speed limit of 16kmph (10mph) sign prior to the intersection. The super elevation was 1%. This crash occurred on a weekend night on a dark lighted roadway. The weather was clear and the roadway was dry. The traffic was normal for that time of day.
Vehicle #1, a 1992 Chevrolet Van, was negotiating a left turn in the roadway when it went off the road to the right and impacted and severed a wooden post. It continued on across a large parking lot and impacted with a corner of a brick garage with its front. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
This vehicle came to final rest facing in northwesterly direction.
The driver of the Chevrolet Van, (vehicle #1)f was transported to an area hospital, treated and released. This driver told the police she had felt tired prior to the crash. A relative of the driver gave an interview and stated this driver has had seizures in the past and most probably had one prior or during the crash, besides being tired. This surrogate did not know it the driver had taken any anti-seizure medication.
The Police did not consider alcohol or illegal drugs to be a factor in this crash and ordered no tests for this driver
