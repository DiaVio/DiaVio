This single-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions. The roadway is straight, level and consists of two lanes in each direction with a center left turn lane. The speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Ford Taurus, was southbound in the center turn lane. V1 traveled into southbound lane two, back into the turn lane, back into the southbound lanes and then off the right side of the road. The front of V1 struck a utility pole. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a forty-one-year-old female, was not interviewed. This driver's medical record indicates positive results for benzodiazepines, cocaine and opiates. No further information is available for this driver.
