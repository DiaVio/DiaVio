The one-vehicle crash occurred at the intersection of two roadways that are two-lane, two-way, undivided roadways. For eastbound traffic there is one travel lane. Both roads were dry, traveled, level asphalt. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). Traffic was light at the time of the crash. This crash occurred during daylight hours with clear skies.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1988 Chrysler New Yorker was eastbound in the first travel lane. V1 departed the travel lane on the left side, and then departed the roadway on the left side. The front of V1 one impacted a utility pole on the northeast corner. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing north in the intersection. The driver fled the scene prior to emergency personnel arrival. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
No information is available on the identity of the driver of V1.
