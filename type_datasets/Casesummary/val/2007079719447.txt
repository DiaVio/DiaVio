This two-vehicle collision occurred during early evening daylight hours at a four-leg intersection. The westbound and eastbound roadway is five lanes, dry, level and has a left turn pocket. The southbound and northbound roadway is six lanes, dry, level and has both left and right turn pockets. The intersection is controlled by a on-colors signals that were working properly at the time. All four legs are comprised of asphalt. Both roadways are designated 56 kmph (35 mph). The weather was clear and dry.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2006 BMW 325i 4-door sedan, driven by a nineteen (19) year old female. V1 was traveling north in the number four left turn lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2002 Mitsubishi Eclipse GS 2-door hatchback, driven by a twenty four (24) year old male. V2 was traveling south in the number three lane. As V1 made its left turn its front plane was impacted V2's front plane. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing southeast south of the intersection in the northbound left turn lane. V2 continued southwest up onto the sidewalk where its front impacted a cement wall. V2 came to rest facing southwest on the southwest corner. Both vehicles were towed with major damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
