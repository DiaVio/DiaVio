The crash occurred in the late afternoon, during daylight hours, on a two-lane, two-way, non-divided city roadway with a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph).
V1 a 1978, Chevy Impala was in the right lane traveling northbound when a legally parked car to his right opened the drivers door. The driver of V1 steered hard to the left to avoid contact with the parked cars door, went off the road to the left, striking a tree, then a chain link fence, then striking a parked full size van pushing the van 15m into a front porch. The vehicle was towed away due to damage. The driver was taken to the hospital with minor to moderate injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 a 79 year-old male stated that he was very familiar with this vehicle, and has owned it for a long time. He also stated that it was in excellent working condition. The driver stated that after he swerved that he thinks he hit the gas instead of the brakes, causing him to lose control, he does not remember anything after departing the road.
