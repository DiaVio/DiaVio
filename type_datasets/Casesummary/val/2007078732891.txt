This single vehicle ran off the right side of the roadway crash occurred on a two-way, five- lane roadway. There are two northbound and two southbound roadways that are separated by a continuous left turn lane. The roadway is level, dry and constructed of bituminous asphalt. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 89 kmph (55 mph). The crash occurred during the early morning daylight hours. There were no sight line restrictions and no obstructions noted at the scene.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Ford Focus 3dr coupe with one occupant, was traveling northbound in the right lane between 89-97 kmph (55-60 mph) when the driver fell asleep. The vehicle drifted across the lane to the right. The driver apparently woke up just prior to striking the curb. The driver then steered the vehicle to the right causing the vehicle to rotate clockwise and strike the curb. The vehicle traveled across the shoulder on a northeasterly direction and traveled through a cotton field. The vehicle then began to travel north in the cotton field and came to rest facing north. The driver of V1 was transported from the scene by EMS. The vehicle was towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver a twenty two (22) year old male drove V1. He said that the last thing he remembers was traveling north in the right lane. He said that he fell asleep and woke up when his car hit the curb. He said that he hit his brakes and steered to the left but he went into the field. He said that he was driving back from Mexico after partying with friends all night that live in Mexico. He said that he did not drink any alcohol. He said that he had been awake for the last 25 hours. He also said that he was off work for the past two days and works Monday thru Thursday 10 hours a day. He said that he does not take any medication and was not sick at the time of the crash. He said that he almost fell asleep a couple of times while he was driving just before this crash.
