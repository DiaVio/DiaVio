This crash took place on a two lane, two way roadway at a driveway enterance. Conditions were dry, just after noon on a weekday and the bituminous roadway had a posted speed limit of 35mph (56kmph).
V1, a 1990 Volvo 240, was traveling east and climbing a slope approaching the driveway. V2, a 2000 Volkswagen Jetta, was heading west stopped waiting to turn left. V2 initiated a left turn attempting to enter the driveway and V1's front plane came into contact with V2's right plane. V1 continued in it's original travel lane before coming to rest. V2 was rotated clockwise and came to rest on the south side of the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
