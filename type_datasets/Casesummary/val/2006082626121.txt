This two-vehicle collision occurred at the intersection of a two-way city street and an arterial road. The arterial road has bike lanes and both roadways have parking along both sides of the street. The intersection with the city streets has stop signs for the north/south traffic ways. The roadway for southbound traffic has a downhill grade of 7% and for eastbound traffic an uphill grade of 9% both roadways are straight and paved with blacktop. The speed limit for all roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Toyota Camry was southbound in the first lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Jaguar X Type was eastbound in the first lane. V1 and V2 both entered the intersection. V1 was turning left at the intersection. The front of V2 impacted the right of V1. V2 came to rest in the southeast corner. V1 continued east 85 meters (279 feet) and then turned left at the next road. V1 then became involved in another crash involving two parked vehicles, which is not coded as part of this case. The front of V1 impacted the left of both parked vehicles and came to rest in that roadway. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a male of an unknown age, ran from the scene and was not identified. There was no interview for this driver.
The driver of V2, a twenty-five year old male refused to be interviewed, as did his passenger.
