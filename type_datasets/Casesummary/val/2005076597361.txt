This single vehicle crash occurred before mid-night on a rural asphalt constructed roadway. This roadway runs north to south with the northbound lane having a +6 percent uphill grade and a posted speed of 89 kmph (55 mph). This roadway is straight with no safety pull outs and no emergency lane. There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2003 Subaru Impreza 4 door sedan driven by a 17 year-old female who was on her commute home from work. She was traveling northbound when the driver looked down at her cell phone to get the time. When she looked back up to the roadway a large elk had entered the roadway from the left and was impacted by V1. V1 had its front window shattered and the driver brought the vehicle to a controlled stop off the left side of the roadway coming to final rest facing west. V1 was towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
