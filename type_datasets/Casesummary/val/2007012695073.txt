The crash occurred in the intersection of two roadways with on-color traffic control devices. The north/south roadway had five undivided lanes of travel and was straight and level. The east/west roadway also had five undivided lanes but had a downhill grade of 2 percent and curved to the right. Both roadways were bituminous with a posted speed 56 kmph (35 mph). It was early afternoon on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic was flowing.
V1, a 1999 Chevrolet Venture minivan with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane two. V1 was intending to go straight through the intersection. V2, a 1998 Pontiac Grand Prix four-door sedan with two occupants, was traveling eastbound in lane one. V2 was also intending to go straight through the intersection. V3, a 2004 Chevrolet Impala four-door sedan with three occupants, was stopped in lane three northbound. V3 was waiting for the light to turn green so she could turn left at the intersection. The driver of V1 stated that she did not see the light turn red and proceeded through the intersection. She stated that she saw V2 approaching from her right at the last moment and applied the brakes but could not avoid V2. The driver of V2 stated that he had a green light and did not see V1 prior to impact so he could not react. The driver of V3 stated that she did not see V1 until right before she was struck. The front of V2 contacted the right of V1. V2 came to final rest facing south in the southbound lanes. V1's front went on to contact the front of V3. V3 was pushed back and came to final rest in the northbound lanes facing north. V1 came to final rest in the northbound lanes facing south. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was an 18-year old female. She reported no injuries and was treated on scene. She advised that he had just she had just left rehab and was on her way home. She stated that he was traveling between 50-64 kmph (31-40 mph) when she was struck by V2. She stated that he did not see the light turn red. She attempted to apply the brakes but could not avoid V2.
The driver of V2 was a 43-year old male. He reported no injuries and refused treatment. He stated that he had just left home and was taking his daughter to college. He stated that he had been traveling between 34-48 kmph (21-30 mph) when he struck V1.
The driver of V3 was a 64-year old female. She reported no injuries and refused treatment. She advised that she was coming from picking up the occupants and was on her way to drop them off. She stated that she was stopped in lane three when she was struck by V1.
