The crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a four-lane undivided roadway. There were two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes at this T-intersection. The east/westbound roadway has 4-lanes. Two eastbound lanes and two westbound lanes. These roadways were straight, level, bituminous roads with a speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). It was the early morning on a weekday shortly after rush hour. Traffic flow was moderate.
V1, a 2008 Infiniti G35 two door coupe was traveling south in lane one. V2, a 1998 Plymouth Neon 4-door sedan was traveling east. V2 was stopped waiting to turn right. As V2 was making its right turn V1's front contacted V2's left side. V2 started rotating clockwise and as it did the left back of V2 contacted the right door on V1. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1 and No PAR obtained (created) for V2.
V1, a 2008 Infiniti G35 two door coupe was driven by a 30 year old male who was traveling alone on his way to work for a meeting. V1 was traveling in lane 1 at a rate of speed of 56-64 kmph (35-40 mph). V2 was in lane one of the eastbound lanes attempting to turn right into the northbound lanes of the roadway. The driver of V1 stated that a limousine/bus had entered the roadway from the left side of the roadway and turned into the northbound lanes. At the same time he stated that V2 had entered the intersection at a slow pace into his pathway. V1 driver slammed on his brakes and struck V2 on its front left side. V1 came to a rest 22.9 meters away from impact location. V2 after impact turned clockwise 135 degrees clockwise and came to rest on partially on the curb. The limousine/bus that was not involved in the crash left the scene without stopping. Driver of V1 was not injured.
V2, the 1998 Plymouth Neon 4-door sedan was driven by a 68-year old male who was traveling alone. The driver had just dropped off a son for work and was headed back to his residence. He stated that he was turning right to head into the northbound lanes of the roadway. He stated he did see V1 coming down the roadway so V1 must have been traveling at a higher rate of speed than he knew. He stated that he felt the impact and was a little delirious afterwards. He also stated that the driver of V1 asked him if he was ok. The driver was transported due to possible injuries.
