Case #2007-144
The crash occurred in the intersection of two roadways. The northbound roadway consisted of three one-way travel lanes, with a posted speed of 56 kmph (35 mph). The westbound roadway was a two-way, three-lane undivided street, with a posted speed of 48 kmph (30 mph). Conditions were dark with streetlights on a weekday evening. The roads were dry and the skies were clear. Traffic flow was normal. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
V1, a 2002 Nissan Altima with two occupants, was traveling northbound in lane one. V2, a 1996 Toyota Camry with one occupant, was traveling westbound in lane one. Both vehicles were approaching the same intersection. As the two vehicles entered the intersection, the front of V1 made contact with the left side of V2. V1 continued northbound. V1 traveled approximately 25 meters before the back of the vehicle made contact with a concrete wall. V1 was towed due to damage.
