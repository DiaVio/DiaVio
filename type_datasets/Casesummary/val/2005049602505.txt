The crash occurred mid-afternoon involving two vehicle's at a signalized intersection. The speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). The road is 4-lane divided concrete roadway in both directions of travel. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash it was daylight .
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Pontiac Sun fire convertible, was traveling east in the fourth left turn lane, making a left turn at the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Ford Explorer Sport Trac was traveling west in the second lane approaching the intersection. The front of V2 impacted the right side of V1. At impact V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest at the close to the southbound traffic lanes, facing northeast. V1 rotated counter clockwise and came to rest within the intersection, facing west. Both vehicles side slapped each other during rotation. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. The drivers of both vehicles refused to be treated by the ambulance. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
