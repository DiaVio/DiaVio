This four-vehicle crash occurred a little past noon on a physically divided, straight, dry, bituminous, seven-lane state highway at an interchange with another highway. The highway runs north/south at the location. Northbound traffic consists of two through lanes and a left hand turn lane. Just south of the crash there is a on-colors with pedestrian traffic signal. Also, just north of the crash is a on-colors traffic signal with pedestrian signals. The posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph) and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions present at the time.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1994 Cadillac Deville Concours sedan driven by a 76 year-old male, was traveling northbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1987 Jaguar XJ6 sedan driven by a 48 year-old female with one passenger, was stopped in lane one behind traffic that was stopped for the northernmost light. Vehicle three (V3), a 2001 Nissan Maxima sedan driven by a 63 year-old male, was also stopped in lane one, facing north, in front of V2. Vehicle four (V4), a 1982 Buick Regal coupe driven by a 18 year-old male with two passengers, was also stopped facing north, in front of V3. V1 was unable to stop and the front of V1 contacted the rear of V2. V2 was pushed forward and its front contacted the rear of V3. V3 was pushed forward and its front contacted the rear of V4. All four vehicles came to rest near the point of impact, facing north in lane one. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
