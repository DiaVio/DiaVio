This two vehicle daytime collision occurred just past noon. The collision occurred at the on-colors intersection on south and northbound travel lanes. The roadway consists of one lane each way with a straight and level concrete roadway. Travel lanes were dry and free of congestion. Both roadways have a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1999 Buick Park Avenue sedan driven by an 83 year-old male. Vehicle two (V2), a 1980 Datsun King cab pick-up driven by a 53 year-old male that was stopped in the northbound lane for a red light. As V1 traveled northbound he was swerving. V1 departed the lane to the left, returned to the lane and struck V2's right rear bumper with V1's front. V1 then departed the lane and road to the right. As V1 departed the roadway in a northwesterly direction it drove up an embankment with concrete staircase and came to final rest facing east. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 after impact was pushed forward coming to final rest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
