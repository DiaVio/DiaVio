This single vehicle crash occurred during early morning hours on a two-lane asphalt roadway. This roadway is straight with small grades and no defects. This crash occurred during daylight with no adverse weather. The posted speed is 89 kmph (55 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) a 2000 Jeep 4 door Cherokee driven by a 63 year-old male. V1 was traveling southbound in lane one when the driver saw a group of rocks on the roadway. V1 steered right off the right road edge to avoid these rocks and when the driver steered left back onto the roadway the vehicle began to rotate counterclockwise. The driver then steered hard right which caused V1 to begin to rotate. V1 then tripped with the left side tires and rolled 4 quarters coming to rest on its wheels heading in a easterly direction. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
