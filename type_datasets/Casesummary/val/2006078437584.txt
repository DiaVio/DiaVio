This two vehicle crash occurred on a four lane undivided asphalt roadway. This road is straight and level with two lanes in either direction. This crash occurred in a residential neighborhood with many driveways. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1989 Ford Tractor with an empty lowboy trailer was eastbound in the second lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Mazda Protégé was stopped eastbound in the second lane. The front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. V1 was not towed due to damage. V2 was towed due to damage. Both vehicles came to rest in the area of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1 and 64 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-seven year old male had left his shop and was enroute to a pickup. This driver had been driving for about one-half hour at the time of the crash. This driver indicated that as he was driving he looked into the rearview mirror at himself and was focused on his face. When he looked back, V2 was stopped and V1 did not have time to stop. This driver has been driving this class of vehicles for about fifteen years. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
The driver of V2, a seventeen-year-old female had just left her friends house heading home. This driver had been driving on this portion for about five minutes at the time of the crash. This driver had been driving for about three months. She drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
