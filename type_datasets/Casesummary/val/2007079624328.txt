This two vehicle collision occurred on a four-leg intersection comprised by a north/south roadway and an east/west roadway. The north/south roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of five lanes (including a left-turn lane). The east/west roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of five lanes (including a left turn lane). Both roadways are designated 56 kmph (35 mph) zones. The intersection is controlled by a on-color traffic signal that was working at the time of the collision.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2002 Chrysler Sebring four-door sedan, driven by a forty five (45) year old female with two passengers. V1 was traveling eastbound in the number two lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2006 Toyota Matrix compact utility vehicle, driven by a fifty nine (59) year old female. V2 was traveling northbound in the number two lane. As V2 was traveling through the intersection, the front plane of V1 contacted its left plane. V1 rotated counter-clockwise and V2 rotated clockwise. The right plane of V1 contacted the left plane of V2. V1 came to rest facing the northeast corner blocking the westbound number one lane. V2 came to rest facing southeast blocking the eastbound number two lane. V1 received moderate damage and was towed from the scene. V2 received moderate damage and was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
