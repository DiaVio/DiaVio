The four westbound lanes of the six-lane roadway is intersected by a two-lane roadway that is controlled with a stop sign. Roadway surface dry, asphalt with a posted speed limit 72 kmph (45 mph).
Vehicle one (V 1), a 1998 Buick Regal sedan, driven by a 32 year-old female with two passengers was southbound stopped at a stop sign waiting for traffic to clear to attempt to cross over the westbound lanes and turn left and head east. The westbound traffic was stopped and waved V1 across. V1 moved across and into lane three and was struck in the left side by vehicle two (V2), a 1999 Pontiac Grand Prix sedan. The driver of V1 stated she never saw V 2. V2 was driven by a 32 year-old male who was westbound in lane three. V1 was towed from the scene. The driver and front seat passenger of V1 were transported to a local trauma center. V 2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
