This five vehicle mid-afternoon crash occurred on a residential two-way roadway just south of an intersection. The intersection is controlled by four-way stop signs. The roadways are straight, level, consist of one lane in each direction and have parking lanes on both sides. At the time of the crash it was clear and there were no adverse weather conditions or road defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Kia Sportage compact utility, occupied by the driver, a 34-year-old female, and one passenger, was parked facing southbound on the west side of the north/south roadway at the curb, just south of the intersection. V1 entered the traffic way and the left side of V1 was struck by the front of V2. V1 then veered to the right and the front struck the left side of V3 and then the back of V4 V1 came to final rest against V4. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Chevrolet Malibu 4 door, occupied by the driver only, a 22-year-old male, was westbound approaching the intersection. After stopping at the stop sign, V2 turned left at the intersection and noticed children had crossed the roadway in front of V1. The V2 driver stated that his attention was then focused upon V1, and when V1 pulled away from the curb into the traffic lane, the left side of V1 and right side of V2 collided. After impact with V1, V2 rotated clockwise and rolled left one quarter-turn onto its driver side then came to rest in the middle of the roadway.
V3, a 1995 Chevrolet Lumina, was legally parked and unoccupied at the curb facing south in front of V1. V4, a 1983 Ford F-150 pickup truck, was legally parked and unoccupied at the curb facing south in front of V3. A 1998 Ford Contour was legally parked and unoccupied at the curb facing south in front of V4. A pedestrian, between V4 and the Contour, was in the process of closing the trunk of the Contour. When V2 struck V4, V4 moved forward and struck the pedestrian and the Contour. The passenger in V1 and the pedestrian were transported to a local trauma center for treatment.
