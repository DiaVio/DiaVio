A crash happened just past noon involving a passenger car on a divided roadway with a raised median. Traffic lights controlled traffic flow at the intersection. The speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph) The road surface was dry concrete and there was no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
The only vehicle in this crash, a 1993 Chevrolet Caprice, was traveling south in the third lane of a four lane divided (no positive barrier) passing through an intersection. The driver intended to go straight. After clearing the intersection, the vehicle was driven off the road to its left. Its front impacted a non-breakaway pole , located on the raised median of the roadway. It then slightly rotated counter clockwise and came to rest on the third lane of traffic.
