This two-vehicle collision occurred during late evening hours on a weeknight. The crash occurred at the four-leg intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-wet roadway. The intersection is controlled by tri-light traffic signals, and the speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph) in all directions. The north and south legs of the intersections are straight, level, bituminous three-lane (five lanes overall, divided by a raised median), two-way roadways. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet Aveo 4-door hatchback, was traveling northbound in the third lane, and approaching the intersection with the intention of turning left. Vehicle two (V2), a 1989 Ford Mustang 2-door coupe, was traveling southbound and approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. As V1 turned, the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. Both vehicles deflected southwest to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
