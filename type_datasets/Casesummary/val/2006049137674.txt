The crash occurred at 1035 at the intersection of a two-way, two lane (four overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier. Both roads were dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit for the east and west roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph) for the north/south roadway 56 kmph (35 mph). Traffic was light at the time of the accident. There were no adverse weather conditions. The intersection is controlled by a traffic signal.
V 1 a 1995 Toyota Tercel driven by a 30 year-old female with one passenger was westbound in the second travel lane. V 2 a 2006 Toyota Camry driven by a 60 year-old female was northbound in the second travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and it's front struck the right side of V 2. V 1 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing east off the roadway on the northwest corner. V 2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing south in the southbound lanes north of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, there were no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
