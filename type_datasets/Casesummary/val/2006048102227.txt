This two-vehicle crash occurred around mid-morning on a clear day. The crash occurred at a traffic signal controlled intersection on a five-lane median divided roadway without a positive barrier. The posted speed limit for this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Chevrolet Aveo, was northbound in lane one approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Nissan Maxima, was stopped in lane one at the traffic signal facing north. The front of V1 struck V2 in the rear. V2 was propelled several meters forward and came to final rest on the shoulder facing northeast. V1 rolled forward and came to final rest partially off the east road edge facing northeast. Both vehicles were towed due to damage and the driver of V2 was transported to a local trauma center with incapacitating injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
