This is a two-vehicle crash that occurred a few hours before midnight during dark hours at a four-way intersection that is controlled by a traffic light on colors. There are three westbound and three eastbound lanes that are separated by a raised concrete median. There are also two left turn lanes and a westbound and eastbound right turn lane. There are two northbound and two southbound lanes that are separated by a raised concrete median. There are also two left turn lanes and one northbound and one southbound right turn lane. The roadways were straight, level and dry asphalt at the time of the crash. The posted speed limit 72 kmph (45 mph). The sky was clear. The intersection was dark but lighted.
Vehicle one (V-1), a 1997 Saturn coupe driven by a 47 year-old male was traveling eastbound in the #3 lane. Vehicle two (V-2), a 2003 Hyundai Tiburon driven by a 25 year-old male was traveling northbound in the #2 lane. V-1 entered the intersection on a red light and was struck on the right side by V 2. The driver of V-1 steered to the left to avoid the impact. The impact caused V-1 to rotate counterclockwise and travel in a northeasterly direction. V-2 rotated clockwise. The left front side of V-2 struck the right rear area of V-1. The vehicles came to rest in the intersection and were able to be removed from the intersection. The vehicles were removed to the north side of the intersection in the #1 lanes. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V 1 stated that he was traveling eastbound in the #3 lane approaching the intersection. He said that he was on his way to see his wife for a medical emergency. He said that as he approached the intersection he looked both ways and turned on his flashing lights. He said that he was traveling between 64-72 kph (40-45 MPH) when he entered the intersection. He said that he saw V-2 approaching at the last second. He steered the vehicle to the left to avoid but was unsuccessful. The driver of V 2 stated that he was traveling northbound in the #2 lane intending on going straight through the intersection. He said that he had the green light as he entered the intersection. He said that he did not see V-1 until the last second. He said that the driver of V-1 steered to the left just prior to the crash. V 1 was towed from the scene, V 2 was driven from the scene.
