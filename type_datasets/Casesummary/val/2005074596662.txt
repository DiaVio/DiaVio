This three-vehicle collision occurred just past noon in a four-way intersection. The north/south roadway is undivided, two-lane, straight and uphill, controlled by stop signs. The east/west roadway is undivided, four-lane and straight, with no traffic controls. Both directions are downhill. A light snowfall had just begun; most of the roadway was slightly wet from melting snow. The speed limit 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Honda Accord sedan driven by a 22 year-old female, was eastbound in lane one intending to continue straight. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Oldsmobile Alero sedan driven by a 81 year-old male, was stopped westbound in lane two intending to turn left at the intersection. Vehicle three (V3), a 1991 Oldsmobile Cutlass sedan driven by a 45 year-old male with one passenger, was stopped facing north at the intersection waiting to turn right. As V2 turned left, the back right side was struck by the front of V1. V2 rotated clockwise and the back left side struck the front of V3. After impact, V1 traveled over the southeast corner curb and came to rest facing southeast. V3 was pushed back slightly in travel lane and came to rest. V2 came to rest facing northwest in southbound lane of traffic. V1, V2 and V3 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
