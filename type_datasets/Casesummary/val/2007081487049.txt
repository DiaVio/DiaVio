This single-vehicle collision occurred in the late afternoon during daylight hours The collision occurred on a three lane interstate roadway, in lane three. The pre-crash roadway is long sweeping slight curve to the right and level with bituminous paving. The weather was overcast and the roadway was dry. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1990 Honda Civic 4-door, driven by a seventy five (75) year old male with one passenger. V1 was traveling southbound in lane three. An unidentified vehicle changed lanes to the left causing V1 to swerve to the left then over corrected and lost control rotating clockwise. V1 crossed travel lanes to the right then impacted the concrete traffic barrier on the right shoulder. The vehicle came to rest facing northeast on the right shoulder up against the concrete barrier. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1's pre-crash factors are mostly unknown, as he was not interviewed due to a language barrier.
