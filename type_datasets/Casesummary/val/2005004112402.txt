The crash occurred in the eastbound lane of a two-lane roadway with a T-intersection. Conditions were dusk and dry in the evening on a weekend. The northbound lane had a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph) and the eastbound lane had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
V1 was a 2003 Hyundai Elantra sedan traveling eastbound in the right lane approaching a T-intersection. V2, a 1998 Ford Taurus sedan, was traveling northbound in the right lane approaching a stop sign intending on turning left. V2 stopped at the stop sign, looked in both directions but there was landscaping and a fence obstructing V2's view. V2 then proceeded to turn left and the front of V1 struck the left side of V2 in the intersection., as V1 passed by V2, V2's right front contacted the back of V1. V1 continued on and struck a curb and a tree. V1 came to final rest facing eastbound. V2 continued on and struck a curb and came to final rest facing northwest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a 61-year-old female who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital. She stated that she was traveling eastbound and saw V2 turn in front of her. The driver of V1 braked and swerved left but struck the left side of V2. V1 was towed due to damage.
V2 was driven by a 26-year-old female who was not injured. She stated that she stopped at the stop sign, looked in both directions but was unable to see V1 because of a fence and landscaping obstruction. V2 intending on turning left but was struck by V1. V2 was towed due to damage.
