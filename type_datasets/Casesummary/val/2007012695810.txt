The crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a four-lane roadway. There were two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes. This was a straight, level, bituminous, roadway with a posted speed of 72 kmph (45 mph). It was evening on a weekend and the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic was flowing.
V1, a 2002 Subaru Impreza four door with three occupants was traveling south in lane two. V2, a 2001 Buick Century four door with one occupant was also traveling south in lane two. V2 slowed down and came to a stop waiting for traffic to clear so that she could make a left turn on to an adjacent street. The driver of V1 stated that he looked down to shift and when he looked back up V2 was stopped. The driver of V1 attempted to apply the brakes but could not avoid V2. The front of V1 contacted the back of V2 before they both came to final rest in the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Subaru Impreza was driven by a 27-year old male who reported no injuries and refused treatment. He advised that he was coming from the passenger's house and were on their way to the drive-in. He advised that he had been traveling between 34-48 kmph (21-30 mph) prior to making the avoidance action to avoid contacting V2 who had stopped in front of him.
V2, the Buick Century was driven by a 53-year old female who reported no injuries and refused treatment. She advised that she had just left her mother's house and was on her way home. She stated that she had come to a complete stop prior to the impact and did not see V1 in her rearview mirror and did not take any avoidance actions.
