This two-vehicle crash occurred on a median-divided, straight roadway, one block from a traffic signal-controlled intersection. This roadway consists of two lanes in each direction. The westbound lanes have a downhill grade. The speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). Traffic was congested at the time of the crash. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Toyota Rav4, was westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2006 Ford F250 pickup, was westbound in lane one in front of V1. When V2 stopped, the front of V1 struck the back of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
The driver of V1 was a fifty-three-year-old male and the driver of V2 was a thirty-eight-year-old male. Neither driver was interviewed; no further information is available for these drivers.
