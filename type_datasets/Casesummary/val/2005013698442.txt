The crash occurred in the morning hours of daylight. The asphalt roadways were dry with a posted speed limit for V1 of 72Kmph (45Mph). The skies were clear with no clouds.
V1 a 1993 Ford F250 cargo van was stopped at a stop sign attempting to turn left. The driver of V1 stated that he looked both ways then pulled out into the road. The driver then stated that he was struck by V2; he said he never saw the other vehicle. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V2 a 1991 Mazda 626 was traveling northbound on a two lane, two-way (with a center turning lane) roadway. The driver of V2 stated that V1 turned in front of him; he hit the brakes, but could not avoid the crash.
