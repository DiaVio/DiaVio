This three-vehicle crash occurred just past noon at an intersection that was controlled by tri-color traffic signal. The road surface is concrete, dry and free of defects. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time. Posted speed limit for east/west traffic is 48 kmph (30 mph) for north/south 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1989 Chevrolet Van driven by a 65 year-old male was traveling southbound in the fourth lane (turn lane) of a four-lane, two-way without positive barrier roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Dodge Durango SUV driven by a 31 year-old female was traveling north bound in the second lane of a four-lane, two-way without positive barrier roadway. Vehicle three (V3), a 2003 Mercury Grand Marquis sedan driven by a 74 year-old female was traveling westbound and stopped in the second lane of a two-way, undivided, roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
As V1 attempted to cross the path of V2 it was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 then rotated counterclockwise and struck the left side of V3 and came to rest in the roadway at the point of impact. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest east of the point of impact. V3 rotated clockwise and came to rest north of its point of impact. V1 and V2 were driven from the scene without disabling damage. V3 was towed from the scene due to damage sustained in the collision. The driver of V3 was transported to the hospital for injuries sustained in the collision.
