This two vehicle intersection crash occurred shortly before noon. Traffic flow controlled by traffic signal, road surface asphalt, dry and level. Posted speed limit 56 kmph (35 mph).There are two westbound and two eastbound lanes and a separated left turn lane.
V-1 a 2004 Chrysler Sebring sedan driven by a 82 year-old female was traveling westbound in the left turn lane. V-2 a 1999 Mazda B2500 pickup driven by a 71 year-old male was traveling eastbound in the #2 lane. As V-1 attempted the left turn the front of V-2 struck the right side. Both vehicles came to rest in the intersection and were moved to a parking lot located at the southwest corner of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
