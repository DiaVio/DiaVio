This three-vehicle crash occurred just after noon in the number two lane of six southbound travel lanes of an urban expressway. The right lane on the expressway is the start of an exit ramp, however it does not apply to the crash. The speed limit on the expressway was 88 kmph (55 mph), and there were no adverse weather conditions or roadway restrictions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Mercedes Benz with one occupant, was traveling southbound in the second lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1993 Honda Accord with one occupant, was in lane two and was braking & decelerating due to forward stopped traffic. Vehicle three (V3), a 2001 Lexus RX300 with one occupant was stopped in traffic, ahead of vehicle two. Event one occurred when the front of V1 impacted with V2's back. Event two occurred when V2 was pushed forward after event one, and its front plane impacted with the back of V3. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
