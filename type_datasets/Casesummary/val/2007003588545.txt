The crash occurred in the western lanes of a two-way roadway with two travel lanes in each direction. This was a straight, level, bituminous, and snow/slush/ice covered road. The speed limit was 48kmph (30mph), and it was dark and lighted, and the sky was clear with no adverse conditions. It was the evening hours on a weekend, and traffic flow was normal.
V1, a 1999 Mitsubishi Galant four door, with three occupants, was traveling westbound in lane one. V2, an unoccupied Ford M/H Truck was parked in the parking lane. V1 was attempting to pass a Non-contact M/H Truck in lane two when the driver lost control on the snow-covered roadway and the front of V1 contacted the back of V2. Both vehicles came to a final rest within a meter of the point of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Mitsubishi Galant was driven by a 41 year-old male who reported no injuries. A 38 year-old female passenger in the right/front seat position reported minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. A two-year old female occupant in a Child Safety Seat reported no injuries. He advised that he was returning home from a restaurant when "slipped" on the snow/slush and crashed into the back of V2.
