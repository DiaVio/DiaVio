The crash occurred in the intersection of two roadways. The east/west street was a two-way, two-lane undivided road with a posted speed of 48 kmph (30 mph). The north/south street was a two-way, two-lane undivided road with a posted speed of 40 kmph (25 mph). The north/south street was controlled by a stop sign. Conditions were daylight on a weekday with dry roads and clear skies. Traffic flow was normal.
V1, a 1997 Ford Explorer, was traveling northbound in lane one. V2, a county transit bus carrying three passengers, was traveling eastbound in lane one. Both vehicles were approaching the same intersection. As V1 and V2 entered the intersection, the front of V1 made contact with the right side of V2. There were no injuries. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.
