This crash occurred in the intersection of a five lane highway that is divided by a grass median. There are three northbound lanes, two straight and one left turn and there are three southbound lanes, two straight and one right turn. This was a straight level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55mph). It was mid-evening on a weekday and the roads were dry. Traffic flow was at a minimum.
V1, a 2001 Kia Optima with 4 doors and one occupant, was in the northbound left turn lane at the intersection in lane three. V2, a 1985 Jeep Laredo with only one occupant was traveling in lane two in the southbound lane, crossing through the intersection. V3, a 1991 Mercury Cougar with 2 occupants was sitting at the intersection in lane two waiting for the green light. V1 turned left and V2's front hit V1's right side. V1 rotated about 30 degrees and V1's left side contacted V3's front. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a 25 year-old female who was not injured. Numerous attempts to get in contact with drivers have turned up unsuccessful.
V2 was driven by a 60 year-old male who was also not injured. Numerous attempts to get in contact with drivers have turned up unsuccessful.
V3 was not involved in the first harmful event. Therefore, no Critical Precrash Event or Critical Reason was coded to the driver of the Cougar.
