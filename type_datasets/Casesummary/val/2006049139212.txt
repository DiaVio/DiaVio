The crash occurred mid-afternoon at the intersection of a two-way, two-lane, undivided roadway with a left curve in the westbound direction of travel and a two-way, divided north/south roadway without a positive barrier . Both roads were dry, traveled asphalt. The east/west has a +3.28% grade, and the north/south roadway was level. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph) for east/west roadway and 64 kmph (40 mph) for north/south roadway. Traffic was light at the time of the collision and there were no adverse weather conditions. There were no sight line restrictions at this intersection.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1997 Volvo 850 driven by a 16 year-old male. It was stopped westbound in the second travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1999 Volkswagen Jetta driven by a 31 year-old female was southbound in the third travel lane. V1 entered the intersection across the path of V2. The front of V2 impacted with the right side of V1. V2 rotated clockwise coming to final rest in the intersection. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southwest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
