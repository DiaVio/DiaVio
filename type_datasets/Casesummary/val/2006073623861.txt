This single vehicle crash occurred on a two-lane two-way north/south residential roadway. This was a straight, level, bituminous asphalt roadway, with a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). It was late evening on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear. There was no congestion at the time of this crash.
Vehicle one (V 1) a 1996 Ford Contour four door sedan driven by an elderly male (age unknown) was traveling south in the first lane. V 1 left the roadway to the left and struck a tree. After impact V 1 came to a final rest in the northbound lane facing southwest. V 1 was towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
The driver advised that he was traveling home from visiting his sister and he was traveling about 40 kmph (25 mph). He began to feel ill and blacked out momentarily. He further stated he doesn't remember anything after that. This driver further stated that he has a problem with his sugar and is a diabetic.
