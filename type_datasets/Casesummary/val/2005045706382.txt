The crash occurred in the eastbound lanes of an interstate highway that was divided by a concrete barrier wall. The eastbound lanes consisted of a right side on-ramp lane and 5 additional through lanes totaling 6 travel lanes. The roadway was straight and level. Conditions were daylight and dry in the early evening on a weekday. The posted speed limit was 89kph/55mph. There was a construction zone on the eastbound side of the interstate that was another one-quarter mile from the crash scene.
Vehicle #1, a 1991 Chevrolet S-10 pickup, was traveling east on the interstate in lane 3. It then changed lanes to the right (to lane 2) and struck Vehicle 2, a 2002 Honda Accord that was stopped for traffic in the rear with its (V1's) front. Both vehicles came to rest in lane 2 facing east within a few meters of POI. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
