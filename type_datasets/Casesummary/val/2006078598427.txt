This is a two-vehicle crash that occurred at a four-way intersection that is controlled by traffic light on colors. There are two westbound and two eastbound lanes separated by a continuous left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is posted 86 kmph (55 mph). There is one southbound and one northbound lane with a left turn lane at the intersection. The speed limit on this roadway is posted 80 kmph (50 mph). The roadways are straight, level and constructed of bituminous asphalt. It was an early morning daylight crash on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Nissan 4dr Sentra with two occupants, was traveling westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Saturn MPV with two occupants, was traveling southbound in lane one. V1 entered the intersection on a red light and the front end struck the left side of V2. The impact caused V1 to rotate counterclockwise and came to rest just beyond the intersection facing in a westerly direction. V2 rotated counterclockwise in a southerly direction through the intersection rolled to its right 4-quarter turns until it came to rest off the south side of the roadway facing in a northerly direction. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. All four occupants were transported. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
A 48-year old female drove V1. She said that she was traveling westbound in the number one lane approaching the intersection and the light was green for her. She said that the van pulled out in front of her just as she entered the intersection. She said that she did not have time to avoid the crash. She said that she was coming from home on her way to visit a friend. She said that she is familiar with this road and is comfortable with her vehicle. The right front passenger, a 74-year old female, occupied the front right position and complained of pain to her chest, back and neck.
A 58-year old female drove V2. She said that she was traveling southbound in the number one lane approaching the intersection. She said that she was talking to the right front passenger, a 24-year old male, about business. She said that they were trying to locate an address for political reasons. She said that the light was green when she entered the intersection. She said that she also looked left and right and never saw V1 approaching. The driver was extricated from the vehicle by EMS and then transported from the scene.
