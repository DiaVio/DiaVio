This crash took place on a weekday evening on a two-lane, two-way roadway with a posted speed limit of 72kmph (45mph). The bituminous roadway was dry, straight, and level. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions along the cold, dark roadway. However there was snow on the roadside.
V1, a 2000 Honda Civic LX, was traveling south in lane one. V1 departed the roadway on the right side of the roadway at approximately 15 degrees to the edge of the road. V1 traveled 12 meters before its front plane came into contact with a tree and came to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
V1 was driven by a 57 year-old male with a history of hypoglycemia and diabetes. The driver stated that he was en route to the store after a long workout at the gym. He was feeling fatigued and sleepy from the prior exercise. The driver said that he had lost consciousness and did not recall the crash sequence. This driver further stated that he had taken his insulin much earlier in the day prior to the workout. This driver was transported and this vehicle was towed due to damage.
