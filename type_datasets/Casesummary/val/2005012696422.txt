V1, a 2005 Trailblazer was traveling westbound on a two lane, two way, icy gravel roadway in daylight conditions. The driver of V1 stated that she had just left her house and when she got to the top of the hill she started to loose control. She applied the brakes but could not avoid swerving. She departed the roadway on the right side and the front of V1 contacted a tree. V1 then spun clockwise before coming to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 62-year old female who had reported that she had been traveling between 34-48kmph (21-30mph) prior to the crash. She had a history of high cholesterol and had taken Nexium, Lipitor and Selectsa prior to the crash. She does not wear corrective lenses. She was rested and traveling to go X-mass shopping. She sustained incapacitating injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
