The crash occurred on a five-lane undivided roadway at a 4-way intersection. There were two eastbound lanes and two westbound lanes divided by a left-turn lane. This was a straight, level, bituminous, roadway with a posted speed of 72 kmph (45 mph). It was dusk on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear. The intersection was controlled by a traffic signal. Traffic was flowing.
V1, 2001 Chevrolet Monte Carlo two-door coupe with one occupant, was traveling westbound in lane three. V1 was approaching the intersection intending to turn left. V2, a 2003 GMC Sonoma pickup with three occupants was traveling eastbound in lane two. V2 was approaching the intersection intending to go straight. The driver of V1 stated that she had stopped for 1-2 seconds before proceeding to turn. She stated that she saw V2 and thought that she had enough time to turn. The driver of V2 stated that he saw V1 approaching the intersection and thought she was going to yield the right of way. V1 turned and the front of V2 contacted the right of V1 at the front of the vehicle. V1 then rotated counterclockwise and the front of V2 contacted the right of V1 at the middle of the vehicle. Both drivers stated that they did not have time to take any avoidance actions. V1 continued to rotate counterclockwise coming to final rest in the left-turn lane of the south leg of the intersection facing southeast. V2 rotated clockwise and came to final rest at the southeast corner of the intersection facing southwest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Chevrolet Monte Carlo, was driven by a 32-year old female. She reported injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. She stated that she had left a holiday party and was traveling home. She stated that she had been stopped for 1-2 seconds prior to proceeding at a speed of 2-16 kmph (1-10 mph) when she was contacted by V2. She stated that she did not have time to attempt any avoidance actions. The EDR revealed that V1 slowed down before the crash, but did not come to a complete stop.
V2, the GMC Sonoma, was driven by a 19-year old male. He reported no injuries and refused treatment. He stated that he and his friends had left one friend's house and was traveling to another friend's house. He stated that he had been traveling 67-80 kmph (41-50 mph) prior to contacting V1. He stated that he did not have time to attempt any avoidance actions.
