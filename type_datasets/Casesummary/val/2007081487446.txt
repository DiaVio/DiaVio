This single-vehicle crash occurred a few hours after noon The location was in the southbound lanes of a busy interstate highway. This roadway was a bituminous four-lane divided roadway. The weather was clear and dry, and visibility was clear. The posted speed limit was 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2001 Mitsubishi Galant 4 door sedan driven by a twenty eight (28) year old male with two passengers. V1 was traveling northbound in lane two, when a non-contact vehicle began to change lanes into the lane that V1 was traveling in. The driver hit the brakes, and steered to the right to avoid the non-contact vehicle. V1 began to rotate counter-clockwise and travel to the west side of the roadway driver continued to overcorrect. V1 then impacted the left-side concrete barrier with the front left corner then rotated off in a counter-clockwise direction and came to rest facing west partially on the left shoulder and partially in lane four. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
