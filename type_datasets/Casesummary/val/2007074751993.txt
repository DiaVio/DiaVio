This three vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon on a cloudy day, at an 4-leg intersection of 2 five-lane divided concrete, level roadways with traffic signals with pedestrian controls. The posted speed limit for east/west travel was 64 kmph (40 mph) and for north/south 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Kia Rio sedan driven by a seventy (70) year old female, was traveling south in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 2007 Ford Crown Victoria Police interceptor driven by a forty three (43) year old male, was traveling west in lane two. Vehicle three (V3), a 2004 Chevrolet Cavalier sedan driven by a forty three (43) year old female, was stopped facing north in lane two. As V1 entered the intersection, V1 was impacted on the left side by V2. V2 was a police car traveling west to an emergency call with lights and siren engaged. V2 impacted the left plane of V1 with its front bumper, then the left side of V1 and the right side of V2 side slapped before V2 veered to the left. V2 then struck a traffic signal on the median separating the north and south traffic with its front bumper. V2 continued heading east to stop in the middle of the second and third lanes of the north bound roadway. V3 was traveling north in the second lane and was stopped at the intersection when the first harmful event occurred in front of her. The front of V3 was then struck by the traffic signal knocked over by V2. After the side slap with V2 , V1 was redirected in a westerly direction and came to rest on the right shoulder of the westbound roadway. V1 and V2 were towed from the scene due to damage. V3 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
