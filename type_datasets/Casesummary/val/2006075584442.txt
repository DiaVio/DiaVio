This two-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours in a three-leg intersection. Eastbound and westbound travel lanes are straight, level and consist of one lane in each direction. The intersecting roadway is to the north and consists of one lane in each direction. This roadway is curved to the right southbound and there is a stop sign at the intersection. The speed limit for east and westbound is 64 kmph (40 mph) and for north and southbound is 48 kmph (30 mph). Traffic was moderate and the sky was cloudy at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one, a 1975 Dodge Ram pickup, was stopped in lane one at the stop sign facing south. Vehicle two, a 2000 Dodge Caravan, was westbound in lane one, intending to continue traveling straight. Vehicle one started to turn left. The driver of vehicle applied the brakes and steered left, but the front of vehicle two struck the left side of vehicle one. Vehicle one rotated clockwise and vehicle two rotated counterclockwise and the left side of vehicle one struck the right side of vehicle two. Vehicle one traveled off the south side of the road and the front struck a ditch. Vehicle one came to rest facing southwest at the ditch. Vehicle two traveled off the south side of the road, across the ditch and came to rest facing southwest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a twenty-five-year-old male, was traveling from a friend's home to another friend's home. He was well rested but was currently living in his vehicle. He stated that he has a "weak heart" but he takes no medication. He uses a chain saw to cut down trees and does gardening for two hours every other day. "He stated that he saw vehicle one but he thought he had enough space to make his turn. He is very familiar with his vehicle and his route.
The driver of vehicle two, a fifty-two-year-old female, was traveling from picking up her eleven-year-old son from a daycare facility to their home. She was listening to the radio and talking with her son, but noticed vehicle one entering her path. She was well rested and very comfortable with her vehicle and the roadway. She has high blood pressure and takes Lisinopril and Caltrate. She was wearing her prescription contact lenses and sunglasses at the time of the crash.
