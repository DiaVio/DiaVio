This two-vehicle crash occurred at the intersection of two seven-lane roadways. All roads are divided with a non-positive median. The crash occurred during daylight morning hours. The weather was clear and the roads dry. For eastbound traffic there was a slope of -5% and for west bound traffic there was a slope of 5%.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Chevrolet Blazer was turning left from the fourth eastbound left turn only lane at the intersection. Vehicle two (V2) was traveling west in the third lane going straight through the intersection. The front of V2 impacted the front of V1 within the intersection. V2 rotated clockwise and V1 counter clockwise before coming to final rest within the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene and both drivers were taken to the Emergency Room of a local hospital. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
Both drivers were well rested and familiar with the roadway. Both drivers had been on this part of their trips for less than 10 minutes. The rear tires on V1 had a low tread depth of 2 mm ( 0.07 ").
