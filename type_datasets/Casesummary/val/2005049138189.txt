The crash occurred during early morning hours hours on a two-way, three lane (six overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier near the intersection with a two way, three lane (seven overall), divided roadway. The road was wet, level, traveled concrete. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph) Traffic was light at the time of the collision. There were no adverse weather conditions. The ambient conditions were dark, but lighted.
Vehicle one (V 1) is a 2005 Chevrolet Impala driven by a 19 year-old female in good health and well rested headed westbound in the first travel lane who stated she was being chased and was on her cell phone for help. Vehicle two (V 2) is a 1997 Dodge Ram driven by an 18 year-old male in good health and rested was stopped westbound in the first travel lane. V 3 was an unknown make and model vehicle stopped ahead of V 2 in the first travel lane. V 1 was traveling above the speed limit approaching the other two vehicles. The driver claimed to have been chased by another unknown vehicle. The driver applied her brakes to stop behind the other vehicles, slid on the wet surface and struck the rear of V 2. V 2 then moved forward and it's front struck the rear of V 3. V 3 fled the scene without exchanging information with the other drivers. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
