This is a four-vehicle rear end crash that occurred in the number two northbound lane of a five-lane undivided roadway that has a center two-way left turn lane. There are two northbound and two southbound lanes. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 72 kmph (45 mph). There was a construction to the north of the roadway. The number one northbound lane merges into the number two-lane by cones. It was a late afternoon crash during daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred on a U.S Highway inside city limits. The roadway was dry, constructed of asphalt and the sky was clear.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Ford Ranger 2wd pickup with two occupants, was traveling northbound in the number two lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1991 Ford Ranger Xtra-cab pickup with one occupant, was decelerating in the number two lane traveling northbound. Vehicle three (V3), a 2005 Toyota 4 door Avalon with two occupants, was stopped in the number two lane behind vehicle four (V4). V, a 2006 Ford Mustang with one occupant, was stopped in the number two northbound lane, allowing traffic to merge in the number two northbound lane. The front of V1 struck the rear of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise and traveled across the southbound lanes and came to final rest in the number one southbound lane facing in a northwesterly direction. V2 was pushed forward into the rear of V3. V2 came to rest in the number two northbound lane. V3 was pushed into the rear of V4. V3 and V4 came to final rest in the number one northbound lane facing in a northerly direction. The occupants of V1, V2 and V3 were transported from the scene by EMS. Those vehicles were also towed from the scene due to damage. V4 was driven from the scene and the driver was not injured. Police Reported Travel Speed is 40 kmph for V1 and 40 kmph for V2.
An eighteen (18)year old female drove V1. She said that she does not remember what happened before the crash. She did remember that she was on her way to a friend's house coming from home. She said her friend lives about 2 miles from where the crash occurred. She lives approximately 3.2 kilometers (1 mile) from the crash location. She said that she is familiar with this roadway and knew that there was construction on the roadway at the time of the crash. A fourteen (14) year old female, sister to the driver, also occupied the vehicle. She was not ill and takes no medications.
A twenty seven (27) year old female drove V3. She said that she was stopped for about 2-3 seconds when she heard the crash. She looked in her rear view mirror and was then hit by the vehicle behind her. She said that she was stopped about 3 meters (10 feet) to the rear of the Mustang (V-4) in front of her, before she was hit. The driver said that she thinks the truck behind her was slowing down prior to the crash. She said that the right lane was blocked off by cones due to construction. She said that traffic was merging into her lane at the time of the crash. She was on her way home after shopping. The driver had about 8 ½ hours of sleep the night before and had been awake for about 10 hours before the crash. The driver is in good health and takes multi vitamins. The driver was 5 ½ months pregnant at the time of the crash. A 20-month old child occupied the second row center seat.
The twenty six (26) year old female driver of V4 said that she was stopped for about 3-4 seconds before the crash happened. She said that she heard tires screeching and then looked in her side mirror and saw the cars behind her coming at her. She said that she knew she was going to get hit and just braced for impact. She said that she was stopped for traffic that was merging in her lane. She said that there was construction in the right lane ahead of her. She said that the right lane was barricaded off with cones and there was a flashing arrow ahead.
