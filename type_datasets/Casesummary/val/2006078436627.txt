This single vehicle roll over occurred on a two lane, two-way roadway. There is one westbound and one eastbound lane that are separated by a painted centerline stripe. This was a straight, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (50 mph). The roadway is a 4% downgrade. It was an early morning and the road was dry and the sky was clear. The vehicle was being pursued by law enforcement agencies. A spike strip was thrown in the eastbound lane in front of the vehicle.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Chevrolet Suburban 4X4, was traveling eastbound at a [police reported speed of 137 kmph (85 mph) with twenty-three occupants. The vehicle was attempting to elude police. Police deployed a spike strip in the eastbound lane. The driver of V1 attempted to avoid the spike strip by steering to the left. The spike strip damaged the right rear tire. The vehicle rotated counterclockwise and traveled across the westbound lane in a northeasterly direction for about 57 meters (187 feet) before it departed the left side of the roadway onto the gravel shoulder. The driver then steered to the right and the vehicle began to rotate clockwise and traveled for about 41 meters (135 feet) until it entered the roadway. The left front tire was on the roadway and the left rear struck the edge of the roadway. The left rear tire came off the vehicle. The vehicle rolled 4-quarter turns for approximately 60 meters (197 feet) across both lanes of travel in a southeasterly direction until it came to rest on its wheels near the south edge of the roadway facing west. All twenty-three occupants were ejected. Five occupants were deceased at the scene and the remaining eighteen occupants were transported to the hospital. Five of the transported occupants expired at the hospital. One of the occupants pronounced deceased at the scene was pregnant. The driver, a 20-year old male, was transported from the scene by EMS. The driver was placed into federal custody after his release from the hospital. The vehicle was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 137 kmph for V1.
