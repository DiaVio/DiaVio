This crash occurred off the roadway of a 2-lane undivided roadway. There was one northbound and one southbound lane. This straight, level, and bituminous roadway had a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). It was early afternoon on a weekend and the roads were dry and skies were clear. Traffic flow was light to moderate.
V1, a 2001 Chevrolet Malibu 4-door with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane one. While V1 was traveling south in lane one, V1 drifted to the right off the right side of the roadway. Once off the roadway, V1 continued straight and the front of V1 struck a house. V1 came to final rest a few feet back from the point of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1, the Chevy Malibu, was driven by an 80-year-old male who reported chest pain and was transported to a medical facility. The driver passed away 3 days after the accident, unrelated to any injuries sustained from the accident. He stated to police at the scene of the accident that while he was driving he felt tired and must have fallen asleep. The officer stated that the scene evidence backed his story that he fell asleep while he was driving. The driver also informed the officers that he had an implanted defibrillator and a heart condition. The heart condition may have contributed to him falling asleep while driving.
