This is a two-vehicle rear end type crash that occurred in the number one lane of a dry, five-lane bituminous asphalt roadway. There are two westbound and two eastbound lanes that are separated by a continuous left turn lane. The intersection where the crash occurred is controlled by a traffic light that was functioning properly at the time of the crash. The crash occurred in the early evening during daylight hours. There were no roadway deficiencies and no sight line restrictions noted. The speed limit on the roadway where the crash occurred is posted at 56 kmph (35 mph).
V2, a 2003 Honda Accord, was traveling westbound in the number one lane of the westbound roadway when it stopped for the red light at the intersection. V1, a 1981 Dodge pickup, was also traveling westbound in the number one lane behind V2, at a minumum calculated speed of 72 to 80 kmph (45 to 50 mph). Skid marks measuring 35 meters (115 feet) in length were measured before the front of V1 contacted the rear of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in lane one facing west After impact, the driver of V1 was able to drive the vehicle into a parking lot located to the north of the crash location. The driver, a 45 year-old male was not injured. The vehicle was able to be driven from the scene on its own power. The three occupants in V2 complained of minor pain and were treated at the scene. V2 was towed due to damage Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
