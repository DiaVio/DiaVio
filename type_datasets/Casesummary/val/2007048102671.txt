This three-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours on a cloudy day. The crash occurred in an intersection on an eight-lane divided roadway that supports east to west traffic. The north to south roadway has two-lanes. Both roadways are straight, level, bituminous and dry. The intersection is controlled with a traffic control signal (on colors). The speed limit on the east to west roadway is 80kmph (50mph). The speed limit on the north to south roadway is 40kmph (25mph). There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Toyota Camry with four occupants, was eastbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2006 Cadillac STS with one occupant, was westbound in lane four attempting to make a left turn and head south. Vehicle three (V3), a 2006 Toyota Tundra with one occupant, was stopped in the northbound traffic lane waiting for the signal to change to turn left and head west. V2 turned left and was struck in the right side by the front of V1. V1 rotated clockwise and came to final rest in the intersection facing southwest. V2 rotated clockwise and the back of V2 struck the right side of V3. V2 came to final rest in lane one of the northbound lane against V3 facing northeast. All occupants of V1 were transported to a local trauma center. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. V3 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-five year-old healthy female, was traveling from her credit union to a movie theater. She is familiar with her vehicle and the roadway. She stated that she had a circular green signal and just before entering the intersection V2 turned in front of her and they crashed. Skid marks were noted pre-crash from V1.
The driver of V2, an eighty year-old male, was traveling from a nearby grocery store to the country club. He is in fair health and takes Trileptal for a nerve condition. He was in town for a relatives wedding and is not familiar with the roadway. He stated that he turned left with a circular green signal. This driver assumed oncoming vehicles would stop while he completed the left turn;
The driver of V3, a thirty-four year-old healthy male, was stopped in the northbound traffic lane. He saw V1 and V2 collide and then V2 spun around and struck his vehicle.
