This crash occurred early afternoon involving two passenger cars at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 54 kmph (35 mph). The road is a 4-lane divided (no positive barrier) concrete right curved roadway for both vehicles. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Chevrolet Monte Carlo driven by a 81 year-old female not fatigued taking several drugs for arthritis was traveling east in the fourth lane, attempting a left turn at an intersection of the roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1997 Nissan Altima, driven by a 22 year-old male not fatigued in good health was traveling west in the third lane. The front of V2 impacted the right side of V1. V1 rotated clockwise and moved into the eastbound lanes. V2 rotated clockwise after the impact, and came to rest within the intersection. The driver of V1 was treated at the scene. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
