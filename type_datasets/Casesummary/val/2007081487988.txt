This crash occurred shortly after noon during daylight hours. The location was a busy interstate highway. The roadway was a bituminous eight-lane divided roadway. The collision occurred in the eastbound lanes, on a downhill grade. The weather was clear and dry, and visibility was clear. The posted speed limit was 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2007 International Truck tractor pulling one trailer driven by a forty nine (49) year old male. V1 was traveling westbound in lane two, when it lost its left rear tire off the trailer. Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 Nissan Sentra sedan driven by a nineteen (19) year old male, was traveling eastbound in lane one. The driver of V2 noticed an uninvolved truck swerve ahead of him, then noticed a tire coming straight for his vehicle. He moved to the left in his lane, and the front right corner of his car hit the tire. The tire continued on and was not located. V1 immediately pulled onto the right shoulder. V2 remained stalled where it was at impact until the tow truck arrived and removed it. V1 was not towed disabled. The driver of V1 was not even aware it had lost its tire, and had driven for several kilometers/miles past where the tire came off of the vehicle. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
