This two vehicle crash occurred a few hours before mid-night during dark hours at an intersection without traffic lights. The road is a 5-lane divided asphalt roadway from west to east. It is a three lane (two-way traffic) asphalt parking lot exit from north to south. The speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V 1), a 1993 Mercury Capri convertible with one occupant was traveling west in the fourth lane of traffic approaching the intersection of both roadways. Vehicle two (V 2), a 1996 Toyota Tercel coupe with one occupant, was traveling south in the second lane, attempting to turn left into the eastbound lanes of traffic. At the intersection of both roadways, the front of V 1 impacted the left side of V 2. V 1 rotated counter clockwise and continued to travel west until came to rest within the intersection, facing southwest. V 2 rotated clockwise after the impact, and moved northwest, until it came to rest at the curb of the northwest corner of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene.
