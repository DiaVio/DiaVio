This three-vehicle crash occurred in the evening hours at dusk on a weekday at an urban intersection. The intersection consists of a four lane, two-way, north/south roadway and a two lane, two-way, east/west roadway. The roadways are straight, level, bituminous, and dry at the time of the crash. The intersection is controlled by control signals (on colors) with pedestrian signals. The posted speed limit on the north/south roadway is 56 KMPH (35 MPH), and the limit on the east/west roadway is 48 KMPH (30 MPH). The weather condition at the time was 'cloudy', and the streetlights were on.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet Malibu sedan occupied by the driver and one passenger, was southbound in the second lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1993 Chevrolet Lumina minivan occupied by the driver only, was northbound in the first lane. Vehicle three (V3), a 2005 Chevrolet Venture minivan occupied by the driver only, was stopped at the intersection westbound, waiting on the traffic control signal to turn green. V1 entered the intersection and proceeded to initiate a left turn (eastbound) where the front of V2 struck the right side of V1. After the initial impact V1 continued east where the left side struck the left side of V3. V1 came to final rest facing east against V3, which was facing west. V2 came to final rest facing northeast in the intersection, in the first lane. V1 and V2 were towed from the scene due to damage. V3 was driven from the scene. The passenger in V1 was transported to a local trauma center. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
