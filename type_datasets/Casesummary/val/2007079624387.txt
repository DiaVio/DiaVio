This three vehicle collision occurred just past noon on a five-lane roadway, on approach to an intersection. The north/south roadway is dry, level, undivided, and comprised of asphalt. The roadway is designated a 56 kmph (35 mph) zone.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1998 Volkswagen Beetle, two-door hatchback, driven by a twenty three (23) year old female who was traveling southbound in the number three lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 1998 Nissan Quest, minivan, driven by a forty nine (49) year old male who was also traveling southbound in the number three lane. Vehicle three (V3) is a 2005 Nissan Altima, four-door sedan, driven by a thirty eight (38) year old female, also traveling southbound in the number three lane. As V1 traveled, it encountered a stopped V2. V2 was stopped due to traffic waiting for the on-colors traffic signal at the approaching intersection. The front plane of V1 contacted the rear plane of V2. The front plane of V2 then contacted the rear plane of V3. All three vehicles came to rest in the number three lane facing southbound. V1 received major damage and was towed from the scene. V2 received moderate damage and not towed from the scene. V3 was reported to have no damage and was not towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
