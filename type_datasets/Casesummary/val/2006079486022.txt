A single vehicle crash occurred on a Wednesday afternoon. The crash occurred on a five lane, dry, level, asphalt roadway with a two way left turn lane. The roadway is designated 56 kmph (35 mph.). There were no detrimental environmental or road conditions at the time.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1993 Saturn SL that was traveling west in the number one lane. A non-contact vehicle in the eastbound number three lane attempted a left turn into a private driveway. In an attempt to avoid the non-contact vehicle, V1 departed the roadway to the right side then impacted a tree with its front right plane. V1 came to rest on the sidewalk facing north against the tree. V1 was towed with major damage. The driver was transported for treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
