This two-vehicle rear end crash occurred mid-morning in the eastbound # 6 lane of a six-lane, two-way roadway separated by a raised concrete median. This crash is intersection related. There are three westbound and three eastbound lanes. There are also two eastbound left turn lane and one eastbound right turn lane. The intersection is controlled by a traffic light on colors that was functioning properly at the time of the crash. The posted speed limit for the roadway is posted 56 kph (35 mph). the roads were dry asphalt and the sky was clear. The traffic at the time of the crash was congested.
Vehicle one(V-1), a 2006 Chrysler 300 sedan driven by a 48 year-old female was traveling eastbound in the number 6 eastbound lane. Vehicle two(V-2), a 2004 GMC Yukon suv driven by a 72 year-old male with one passenger was stopped in the number 6 lane. The front of V-1 struck the rear of V-2. The vehicles came to rest in the number six lane facing east. V-2 was removed from the roadway on it's own power. V-1 was towed due to damage. The driver of V-1 apparently fell asleep and then looked up and saw V-2 stopped. The driver of V-1 apparently applied the brakes but did not stop in time. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
