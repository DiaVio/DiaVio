This crash occurred mid-afternoon, involving two passenger cars at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 54 kmph (35 mph). The road is a 4-lane divided (no positive barrier) concrete roadway from south to north. It is a 2-lane undivided (two-way traffic) roadway from east to west. The east-west road intersects with the north-south road in a 60-degree angle. The polished asphalt road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Dodge Ram pick up truck driven by a 47 year-old male with one passenger was traveling west in the first lane, crossing through the intersection of both roadways. Vehicle two (V2), a 1989 Cadillac Sedan Deville, driven by a 70 year-old male was traveling north in the second lane. The front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V rotated counter clockwise and moved into the southbound lanes. It came to rest north of the intersection in southbound lanes, facing west. V1 rotated clockwise after the impact, and moved north. It came to rest within the intersection, facing north. The drivers of V1 and V2 were transported to local medical facility, both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
