This two-vehicle crash occurred on a two-lane, undivided, bituminous, uphill roadway that runs east to west. There was a curve 80 meters preceding the point of impact and then the roadway is straight until the impact location.. This roadway intersects with a two-lane, downhill, bituminous roadway that runs north to south and has no center stripe. The intersection was controlled by a stop sign. The posted speed limit was 72 kmph (45 mph). Traffic flow was light and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions present at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one, a 2000 Toyota Camry was traveling eastbound in the first lane. Vehicle two, a 1999 Ford F-150 was stopped in the first lane ahead of vehicle one. As vehicle one approached the intersection, the front of vehicle one contacted the rear of vehicle two. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Vehicle two was not towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a sixty-three year old female was not interviewed. There is no known pre-crash information for this driver.
The driver of vehicle two, a thirty-two year old male was not interviewed. There is no known pre-crash information for this driver.
