This crash occurred on the roadside of a two-lane undivided roadway. There was one westbound lane and one eastbound lane with a posted speed limit of 56KMPH (35MPH). The crash occurred on a bituminous downhill (-11.4 percent grade) left hand curve. It was early evening and the road edges were covered in ice and snow and snow was blowing across the road surface. It was dark out and the road was unlit. Traffic flow was light.
V1, a 1998 Neon 4-door sedan with one occupant, was traveling west. As the operator tried to negotiate the left curve, he lost control and went sliding off the road edge and into a utility pole. The pole snapped and fell onto the road blocking both lanes. The vehicle came to rest on the roadside at the point of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1.
V1 was driven by a 19 year-old male who reported no injuries. The driver stated that he was on his way to a friend's house and lost control on the icy road.
