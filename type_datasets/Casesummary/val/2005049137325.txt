This two-vehicle crash occurred during early evening hours. The roadway surface is asphalt ,dry and free of defects. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at that time. The accident occurred in a non-signalized intersection with six lanes two-way north/south divided by a median without a positive barrier. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Isuzu Rodeo SUV driven by a 30 year-old female with one passenger was stopped northbound waiting to make a left turn into a private driveway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1999 BMW M3 coupe, was traveling southbound in the second lane. The driver of V1 stated she believed V2 was in the turn lane coming the opposite direction. When V1 entered the intersection, it was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 rotated clockwise, rolled over onto its left side and came to rest on its left side west of the point of impact. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southward south of the point of impact. V1 and V2 were towed from the scene due to damage sustained in the collision. Neither driver was transported to the hospital for treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was well rested and familiar with area. She believed V2 was preparing to turn left and entered the intersection. When she realized that V2 was going straight she accelerated in an attempt to avoid the collision. The driver of V2 was well rested and familiar with area. As V1 crossed his path, he steered to the right and braked but was unable to avoid the collision.
