This crash occurred in the morning hours at the end of the week. The weather was overcast. The crash occurred at an intersection with five lanes of travel in all directions. The roadways were dry asphalt. The north/south lanes had an uphill grade of 3 percent in the northbound direction. The east and west lanes were level. The intersection was controlled by traffic signals. Both roadways had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle #1, a 2006 Chevrolet Malibu 4-door sedan, was traveling eastbound in the second lane of travel approaching the intersection. Vehicle #2, a 2003 Chevrolet Venture minivan, was traveling north in the second lane of travel also approaching the intersection. Both vehicles entered the intersection at about the same time and the front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise to face north on the north side of the intersection. V2 slid to the side to final rest blocking lane 1 and 2 of the westbound trafficway. The drivers were not injured in the crash. V1 had to be towed from the scene. V2 was driven away from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
A 65-year-old female who stated she was in good health drove V1. She reported taking medication for high blood pressure and wearing corrective lenses for farsightedness. She stated that she and her grandson where on their way home from having breakfast. She stated that as she approached the intersection in lane two the light was green. She then stated the next thing she knew was there was a van in the intersection traveling north. She stated she did not have time to react and contacted the left side of the van and rotated to face to the north for final rest. She stated she then checked on her grandson to see that he was not injured. He was seated in the back left seating area.
A 68-year-old female who stated she was in good health drove V2. She reported being under doctor's care and taking medications for "bad bones." She also stated she was wearing corrective lenses for an astigmatism. She stated she had been at the food bank to pick up food for her grandchildren to make sure they had food. She stated that as she left the parking lot she was following a vehicle that had been at the same location. She stated the vehicle ahead of her moved to lane #1 and she was beside them in lane #2. She stated as she traveled north in lane #2 she could see the traffic light turn green. She stated she was traveling about 40 to 45mph. She stated as she entered the intersection she spotted a silver vehicle coming from her left and she braced for the impact. She stated that at final rest she got out of her van and walked to the passenger side to find her cell phone. After finding it she called 911 and sat in the passenger side of the van until police arrived.
