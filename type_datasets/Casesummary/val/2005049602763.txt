This two vehicle crash occurred during early morning hours at a controlled four-leg intersection by on-color traffic signal. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph) prior to the intersection for both vehicles. The road surface is concrete, dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions. There are four lanes north/south and two lanes east/west.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Honda Accord coupe driven by a 41 year-old male with one passenger was traveling north in the first lane.. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Dodge Ram Pick up driven by a 24 year-old female with one passenger was traveling west in the second lane. As V2 entered the intersection the front of V1 impacted the left side. After impact V2 rotated counter clockwise and V1 rotated counter clockwise as well. Both vehicles came to rest within the intersection. V2 was towed from the scene. V1 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 claimed to be well rested and in good health. The driver of V2 claimed to be well rested and in good health. An additional factor for V1 according to a witness was excessive speed.
