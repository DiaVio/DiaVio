This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred at the intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-west roadway. Both roadways are straight, level, two-lane, two-way, bituminous roads, and the speed limit is 40 kmph (25 mph) in all directions. Northbound and southbound traffic are controlled by stop signs at the intersection, Eastbound and Westbound traffic have no controls. At the time of the collision, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1) was a hit-and-run vehicle of unknown make, model, or type. V1 was traveling southbound in the first lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. Vehicle two (V2), a 1992 Chevrolet S10 Blazer 4-door SUV, was traveling westbound in the first lane, and approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. In the intersection, the front of V1 impacted the left side of V2. Both vehicles deflected southwest to final rest. V1 departed the scene, unidentified. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
