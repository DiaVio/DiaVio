This two-vehicle crash occurred on a three-lane, level, bituminous, straight, dry roadway that supports two-way traffic. The speed limit for this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This roadway intersects with a one-lane interstate exit ramp. The intersection is controlled by a stop sign. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions present at the time of this daylight crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Honda Accord driven by a 18 year-old male was traveling eastbound in lane one of the interstate exit ramp. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Chevrolet Cavalier driven by a 24 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling southbound in lane one. V1 was attempting to make a left turn to travel north. As V1 entered the intersection, the front of V1 contacted the right side of V2. Both vehicles deflected to the left and came to rest facing east. Both vehicles were towed from the scene and the passenger in V2 was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
