Case Summary 2005-02-056
The crash occurred on a level two lane undivided roadway with a posted speed limit of 55 MPH (89 KPH). The weather was clear, the roadway dry and it was daylight at the time of this weekend afternoon crash. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
Vehicle 1, a 1998 Toyota Camry, was traveling north on the two lane undivided roadway. Vehicle 1 crossed into the southbound lane and off the left side of the roadway. Vehicle 1 continued striking a large boulder with its undercarriage and swiping a 10 cm tree with its left side. Vehicle 1 continued a short distance, struck a 25 cm tree with its front and came to rest facing in a northerly direction.
The Toyota Camry (Vehicle 1) was driven by a belted 21-year-old male who was transported, treated and released for minor injuries. No additional information was obtained as the driver could not be located or contacted. The driver did state to the police that he had fallen asleep causing Vehicle 1 to drift off the roadway. There were no indications of any type of avoidance maneuvers.
