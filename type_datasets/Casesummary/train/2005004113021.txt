The crash occurred in the northbound lane of a two-lane roadway. Conditions were daylight and dry in the morning on a weekday. The posted speed limit was 64 kmph (40 mph).
V1 was a 2000 Plymouth Voyager minivan traveling northbound in the 1st travel lane intending on going straight. V2 was a 2003 Kia Sedona minivan stopped in traffic directly in front of V1. V3 was a 2004 Toyota Avalon also stopped in traffic directly in front of V2. The front of V1 struck the back of V2 in the 1st travel lane. The front of V2 then struck the rear of V3. All vehicles came to final rest facing northbound. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a 32-year-old male who was not injured. He stated that he was traveling northbound in the 1st travel lane, looked back to check on his ill child and that is when the crash occurred. The front of V1 struck the back of V2. The front of V2 then rolled forward and struck the back of V3. V1 was towed due to damage.
V2 was driven by a 37-year-old female who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital. She stated that she was stopped in traffic when the vehicle behind her struck her vehicle. The driver of V2 did not take any avoidance actions and was stopped at the time of impact. V2 was towed due to damage.
V3 was driven by a 52-year-old female who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital. She stated that she was stopped in traffic when she was struck in the back by V2. The driver of V3 did not take any avoidance actions. V3 was towed due to damage.
