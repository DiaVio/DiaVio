This is a two-vehicle crash that occurred in the intersection of two, two way, 5-lane roadways. The east/west roadway has three eastbound lanes and two westbound lanes separated by double yellow lines. The north/south roadway has three southbound lanes and two northbound lanes separated by double yellow lines. Operating overhead traffic signals with pedestrian lights controls traffic at the intersection. Both roadways were straight and level with a bituminous surface. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph) for southbound traffic and 40 kmph (25 mph) for southbound traffic. The time of the crash was mid morning on a weekday. The road was dry and the sky was clear. There were no sight restrictions.
Vehicle one (V1) a 1995 Dodge Intrepid 4 door with one occupant, was traveling east in the number two lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Ford Taurus 4 door with one occupant, was traveling south in the number two lane. As both vehicles entered the intersection, the front of V2 struck the left side of V1. Both vehicles rotated and the vehicles side slapped as the right side of V2 struck the left side of V1. Both vehicles came to rest facing southeast in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Both drivers were examined on scene by EMS personnel but were not transported. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
