This crash occurred in the eastbound lanes of a four-lane roadway divided by a concrete barrier. The roadway was flat, level, concrete, and had a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55 mph). It was late in the evening on a weekend and conditions were clear.
V1 was traveling eastbound in lane two when it crossed the lane line to the left and impacted the concrete barrier. V1 came back across the roadway, onto the shoulder, and struck the curb. It followed the curb, contacted a concrete barrier protecting an overpass pier, and began rotating clockwise. The vehicle came to final rest facing south, partially in lane one, and partially on the shoulder. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
