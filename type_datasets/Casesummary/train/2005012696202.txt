V1, a 1999 Ford Contour was traveling northbound on a 4 lane, 2 way dry asphalt roadway with a raised median. V1 was traveling in lane two, intending to go straight. V2, an unknown Chevrolet Cavalier, was also traveling northbound on the same roadway. V2 was traveling in the first lane. V2 made a lane change to the left, in front of V1 and then hit his brakes. The front of V1 contacted the back of V2 before they both came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 27-year old female who had reported that she had been traveling between 50-64kmph (31-40mph) prior to the crash. She was rested and traveling home. She was in good health and wearing her prescribed lenses that corrected a myopic (near-sighted) condition. She sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 left the scene of the crash and was not available for an interview.
