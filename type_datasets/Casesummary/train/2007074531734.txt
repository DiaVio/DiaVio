This two-vehicle crash occurred during early morning hours at dawn at a busy mid-town intersection controlled by on-color traffic lights with pedestrian signals. The roadway surface dry level concrete with six lanes divided by raised median. The posted speed limit 56 kph (35 MPH) There were no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) a 1997 BMW 528i 4-door sedan, driven by a forty six (46) year-old male was eastbound in lane 3, going straight. Vehicle two (V2) a 1999 Ford Escort 4-door sedan, driven by a twenty five (25) year old female. was northbound in southbound lane 2. V2 violated the red traffic signal for north & southbound traffic and struck the right side of V1 with the front end. V1 rotated clockwise and ended up facing west on the east side of the intersection in eastbound lane 3. V2 ended up in the center of the intersection, facing southeast. Final rest of vehicles ascertained from D1 description. Vehicles were moved before researcher's arrival. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Medication taken by driver Synthroid.
Driver was hysterical at scene, no interview obtained.
