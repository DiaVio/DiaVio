This two-vehicle crash occurred at a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west trafficway was divided by a curbed median. The westbound lanes were level, curved to the left and consist of two through lanes, two left turn lanes and a right turn lane. The speed limit for this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours and it was raining.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Chevrolet Cavalier, was westbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Chevrolet K1500 pickup, was westbound in lane three in front of V1. V2 stopped at the intersection and the front of V1 struck the back of V2. V1 was towed due to damage.
