This is a single vehicle crash run off the left side of the roadway crash that occurred on a two way, two lane, divided asphalt roadway. The crash occurred at approximately 2041 hours; there were no roadway deficiencies and sight line restrictions noted. The roadway was dark with no overhead lighting and there is a depressed dirt median separating the westbound and eastbound lanes. There were no adverse weather conditions and the weather was clear. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 121 kmph (75 MPH).
V-1, a 2002 Chevrolet 4dr Cavalier, driven by a 18 year-old male was traveling westbound in the number two lane at approximately 136-144 kmph (85-90 mph) when the vehicle began to rotate counterclockwise. The vehicle continued to travel in a southwesterly direction while rotating counterclockwise until it traveled off the left side of the roadway into the dirt median. The vehicle then tripped on it's right wheels and began to roll laterally for approximately 2 quarter turns while rotating clockwise. The vehicle was facing in a southeasterly direction when the top of the vehicle struck the ground. The vehicle continued to rotate clockwise when the undercarriage of the vehicle struck the ground. The vehicle continued to rotate clockwise when the left side struck the ground. The vehicle was facing in a northwesterly direction at this time. The vehicle rolled until it landed on it's wheels facing in a northeasterly direction. The vehicle rolled on it's wheels in a southwesterly direction until it came to a stop. The vehicle was towed due to damage. The driver was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
