This crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a four-lane roadway divided by concrete median. There were two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes. The roadway curved very slightly to the left, was level, bituminous, and had a posted speed limit of 64 kmph (40 mph). It was dawn on a weekday and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 1990 Cadillac Deville 4-door sedan driven by a 25 year-old female. V2 was a 2001 Jeep Grand Cherokee utility vehicle driven by a 31 year-old female, and had one passenger. V3 was a 2005 Toyota Camry 4-door sedan driven by a 75 year-old male. V2 and V3 were stopped in traffic, waiting for the traffic light to turn green. V1 was traveling southbound when it rode over the curb on the right side of the roadway, returned to its original travel lane, and then struck V2 in the rear. V2 was then pushed into V3. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V3 was not involved in the first harmful event. Therefore, no Critical Pre-crash Event or Critical Reason was coded to this vehicle. No associated factors were coded to the driver.
