This crash occurred on a Thursday evening in the five westbound lanes of a 10-lane divided concrete interstate highway. This crash occurred in lane 2, on a downhill slope, approaching an interchange to another highway. Traffic was heavy with congestion due to rush hour traffic. The posted sped limit was 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2000 Volkswagen Jetta 4-door sedan, which was trailing all of the other vehicles involved in this collision in the number-two travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) a 1999 K1500 Suburban was stopped in traffic facing westbound in lane 2. Vehicle three (V3) a 1990 Subaru Legacy was stopped facing westbound in lane two, ahead of V2. Vehicle four (V4) a 2004 GMC Yukon was stopped facing westbound in lane two ahead of V3. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The front of V1 impacted with the rear of V2 pushing it forward. V2 then impacted the rear of V3 pushing V3 into the rear of V4. V2, V3 and V4 were driven from the scene, and V1 was pushed to the shoulder and then towed due to damage.
