This single vehicle crash occurred on a two-lane two-way roadway. This road is straight and level with no defects. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 1995 GMC Jimmy sport utility vehicle was eastbound in the first lane. Vehicle one departed the roadway and the front plane impacted a mailbox. Vehicle one then steered left to miss a tree. The front of vehicle one then impacted a speed limit sign. Vehicle one reentered the roadway and overcorrected and vehicle one the rolled over one quarter turn on to its left side. Vehicle one came to rest facing south in the first lane. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of vehicle one, a twenty-two year old male was heading home from a friends house. This driver had been driving for fifteen minutes at the time of the crash. This driver stated that as he was driving some papers that were in the back seat came off the seat and he reached back to fix them. That is when the vehicle left the roadway. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this route on a daily basis.
