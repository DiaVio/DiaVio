This two vehicle collision occurred early evening. The collision occurred on southbound travel lanes of a divided roadway with two lanes of travel in each direction. There were no lane delineation lines ; however, the roadway is customarily used as having two lanes in each direction. The pre-crash roadway for both vehicles is a 3% negative grade of traveled bituminous paving, with no traffic controls for southbound travel. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Dodge Caravan SE, driven by a 22 year-old male was traveling southbound on the roadway close to the right curb in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Subaru Outback wagon, driven by a 20 year-old male turned left onto the southbound roadway from a side street. V2 was in the number two lane and turned on his right turn signal to turn onto a westbound side street and began to move to the right. V1 was in the right lane attempting to pass V2 on the right. The left side of V1 collided with the right front fender of V2. V1 then deflected to the right and drove into a non-breakaway pole, coming to rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
