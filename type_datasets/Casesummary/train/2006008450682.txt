This crash occurred in the late evening of a weekday in the southbound lanes of a two-lane divided highway bridge. The concrete, straight, and level bridge featured a speed limit of 72kmph (45 mph). Atmospheric conditions at the time were below freezing with moderate snowfall covering the road. There was a crash being cleared from the roadway roughly 30 meters ahead of the point of impact.
V1, a 2005 Suzuki Reno was southbound in lane two preparing to slow down for a crash that had occurred up ahead. As V1 began to slow the vehicle slipped and spun approximately 120 degrees to the right and came to rest blocking both lanes of travel. V2, a 2003 Acura RSX, was approximately 15 meters behind V1. The driver of V2 also began to slow down for the upcoming accident but could not stop before hitting V1 in the middle of the road. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V1 was driven by a 34 year-old male in good health and wearing his prescription lenses that corrected a myopic (near-sighted) condition. The driver stated he slowing down for the crash when he lost control of his vehicle and came to rest straddling both lanes. The driver was not injured and V1 was towed due to damage.
V2 was a 2003 Acura RSX driven by a 25 year-old female in good health on her way home. She stated she tried to avoid V1 by braking and steering right but could not and collided with V1. The driver was not injured but V2 was towed due to damage.
