It was dark with streetlights on with no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of this single-vehicle crash. The roadway is undivided, level and consists of one lane in each direction. This roadway curves to the right in the precrash area. The speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Jeep Wrangler, was northbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the right side of the roadway. The driver steered left and applied the brakes. V1 rotated counterclockwise and traveled back across the roadway and the left front tire/wheel struck the curb. The driver then steered to the right and V1 rolled over to the left four quarter-turns. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a sixteen-year-old male, was on his way home from a restaurant. He was well rested, travels this route weekly and is familiar with his vehicle. He stated that he was getting over a severe cold and had taken Motrin. This driver was not wearing his required vision-correcting glasses and had one year of driving experience at the time of the crash.
