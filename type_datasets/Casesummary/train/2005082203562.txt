This three vehicle daytime collision occurred shortly before noon. The collision occurred in the northbound lane of a non-divided roadway with a two-way turn lane. The pre-crash roadway for all vehicles is level, dry and straight bituminous paving. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions at this time; however, traffic was congested during the noon rush hour.
Vehicle one (V1), an unknown van, was traveling north in the number one lane. V1, for an unknown reason, impacted with the back of V3. V3 was a 1992 Honda Civic sedan driven by a 67 year-old female who was stopped in traffic lane due to traffic congestion. The impact pushed V3 forward into the back of V2, a 1998 Chevrolet tracker driven by a 41 year-old female with one passenger. V2 was also stopped in the same traffic lane. After impact, V1's driver fled the scene in the vehicle and no additional information is available for this driver or vehicle. V3 and V2 came to final rest in travel lane. V3 was towed from the scene; there were no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
