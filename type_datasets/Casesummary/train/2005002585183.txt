This crash occurred on a two lane undivided roadway, slightly curving to the right prior to the crash site, with no grade and a posted speed limit of 72 KMPH (45 MPH). The weather was clear and the roadway was dry. This crash occurred on a weekday afternoon.
Vehicle #1, a 1998 Honda Accord, was traveling north on the two lane undivided roadway when it stopped to allow several southbound vehicles to pass before proceeding to make an intended left turn onto a side road. Vehicle #2, a 1994 Ford Escort LX, was traveling in the same direction behind Vehicle #1. Vehicle #2 impacted the rear of Vehicle #1 with it's front. Both vehicles came to rest in the roadway facing north. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The Honda Accord (Vehicle #1) was driven by a belted 37 year old female who was transported to an area hospital, treated and released, with a complaint of pain to her neck. She stated that she was stopped, waiting for the on coming traffic to clear before she made her left turn. Just as she glanced at her rear view mirror she saw Vehicle #2 coming at her fast. The driver of Vehicle #2 was not slowing down and then she heard brakes and she gripped the steering wheel, and then she was hit from behind. The driver of this vehicle was wearing prescription contacts at the time of the crash. Vehicle #1 was driven from the scene.
The Ford Escort LX, (Vehicle #2) was driven by a belted 44 year old male who was uninjured. . The driver of Vehicle #2 stated he was showing his girlfriend the trees in the area, as she was from out of town, and he was looking to the right at times. He stated he did see Vehicle #1 stopped in the roadway too late, tried to stop but didn't have enough time to stop. A passenger in the vehicle, an unbelted female, was transported to an area hospital, treated and released, for a minor head injury from impacting with the dashboard as stated by the driver. Vehicle #2 was towed from the scene.
