The crash occurred in the traffic signal controlled intersection. The northbound lane is a four lane non-divided, one-way city street, with a posted speed limit of 56 Kmph (35 Mph). The east-west street is a two-way non-divided city street with a posted speed limit of 40 Kmph (25 Mph).
V1 a 1993 Chevrolet Corsica was traveling west approaching the intersection at approximately 40 Kmph (25 Mph). The driver of V1 stated that he thought the light was green. The driver then stated that he proceeded into the intersection where he contacted V2 in the right side. The vehicle then bounced off V2 and ran into a utility pole. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V2 a 2004 Chevy Trailblazer was traveling north bound in the left curb lane at approximately 48 Kmph (30 Mph) also approached the intersection, tried to make a left turn when V1 hit him in the right side. V2 was driven away from the scene with minor damage. The driver stated that he was making the left turn when V1 hit him in the right side. The driver then stated that he did not have time to react to the crash.
The police officer at the scene talked with witness before my arrival and the witness stated that V1 ran the red light. The witnesses were not at the scene when I arrived. I tried to call the witness for an interview, but could not get a hold of her.
