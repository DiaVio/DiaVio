This two-vehicle crash occurred on a five-lane roadway. This road is straight with an upgrade. This crash occurred just on the other side of an intersection. Just ahead of the crash is a driveway to a restaurant. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). The intersection is four legged with a standard three-color stoplight. This crash occurred during darkness with street lighting and good weather.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Dodge Neon was eastbound passing through the intersection behind vehicle two (V2) in the first lane. V2, a 1985 Ford pickup was eastbound in the first lane and had just crossed thru the intersection when a non-contact vehicle slowed and turned right off this roadway into the restaurant parking lot. The front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 was not towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a sixteen-year-old female, was heading to a gas station to get gas at the time of the crash. This driver had been driving for about fifteen minutes. She stated that she had just looked into the left rear view side mirror to check traffic from behind and when she looked forward V2 had slowed to almost a complete stop. This driver was also involved in a social conversation with the passenger.
The driver of V2, a thirty-four year old female was not interviewed.
