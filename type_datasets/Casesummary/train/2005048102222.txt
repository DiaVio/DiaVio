This two-vehicle crash occurred on a clear day at mid-afternoon during daylight hours on a straight, four-lane, median divided, dry, level, and bituminous roadway. The crash occurred during heavy traffic. The vehicles were approaching an intersection that was controlled by a traffic signal.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 GMC Yukon, was westbound in lane-two behind vehicle two (V2). V2 was traveling at approximately 64 kmph (40 mph). V2, a 2003 Volvo Tractor with a single trailer, was westbound in heavy traffic traveling at approximately 48 kmph (30 mph). V2 was decelerating, the driver stated that he had locked his brakes to try to stop. V1 could not stop and struck the rear of V2's trailer. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1 and 64 kmph for V2.
