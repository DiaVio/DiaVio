This four-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours on a dry, asphalt, two-lane, straight, roadway. The road runs north and south and one through lane in each direction. The speed limit for the road is 40 kmph (25 mph) and there were no adverse weather conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Buick, LeSabre sedan driven by a 54 year-old male was southbound and the driver was getting ready to turn right into his private driveway. In front of his residences was Vehicle two (V2), a 1988 GMC Sierra pickup, which was legally parked and unattended facing south. Vehicle three (V3), a 2002 Chevrolet Cavalier sedan was legally parked and unattended in the next private driveway facing west. Vehicle four (V4), a 1982 Chevrolet Camaro was also legally parked and unattended on the south side of the same property as V3 facing east. Driver of V1 missed the right turn into his driveway and sideswiped V2, The driver continued southwest off the right side of the road and entered the yard and impacted V3 with his front to right side continued southwest and impacted V4 with his front to left side. V4 rotated slightly clockwise and impacted a wooden fence with its right side. V4 then impacted a trailer, which was parked behind it and the trailer impacted a lawn tractor, which was parked behind it. V1 came to rest on its wheels facing southwest against V4. V2 was pushed slightly clockwise and came to rest on its wheels facing southwest. V3 rotated ninety-degrees and came to rest on its wheels facing north. V4 rotated slightly clockwise and came to rest on its wheels facing southeast against the fence. V1 was towed from the scene due to moderate frontal damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 40 kmph for V1 and 40 kmph for V2.
