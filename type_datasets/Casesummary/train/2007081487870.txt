This two-vehicle crash occurred shortly after noon during daylight hours. It occurred in the south bound lanes of a divided interstate, the location was just north of an off-ramp in an interchange area. The roadway was concrete, level, and had four lanes. The weather was clear and dry with good visibility. The posted speed limit was 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Honda Accord sedan driven by a nineteen (19) year old male, was traveling southbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1979 Volkswagen Rabbit 4-door hatchback driven by a forty nine (49) year old male, was traveling southbound ahead of V1 in lane two. V1 planned to change lanes to the right, when V1 was checking his blind spot, traffic ahead came to a stop. At the last second the driver steered to the right. The front left corner of V1 impacted the right rear corner of V2. Both V1 & V2 came to final rest facing south in the median. According to the collision report, both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
