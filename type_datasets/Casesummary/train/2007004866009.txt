The crash occurred in the center of a "T" intersection composed of a two-lane east/west driveway, and a five-lane north/southbound roadway. Both roadways were bituminous. The north/south roadway was level and a posted speed limit of 80 kmph (50mph). The driveway was level with no posted speed. The roads were dry and the traffic flow was moderate.
V1, a 200 Mitsubishi Mirage with two occupants, was traveling south when it attempted to turn left into the driveway and was struck by V2 on the right side. V2, a 2001 Ford Ranger with one occupant, was traveling north in lane one. As V2 passed through the intersection it struck V1 on the right side. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, driven by a 47-year-old female and occupied by a 21-year-old female passenger, both of which reported Complaints of Pain and were transported. D1 stated that she slowed down to turn into the driveway even though she was aware it was illegal to do so. Norht bound traffic stopped and "waved" her through, and thinking she had enough room attempted to turn left. However V1 could not see the approach of V2 and was struck in the right side. V1 made no avoidance maneuver.
The Driver of V2, a 21-year-old male, reported a no injuries and was not transported. Driver stated that he was traveling at approximately 80 kmph (50 mph) as she entered the intersection, when V2 turned across her path and she struck V1 on the right side. V2 attempted to avoid the collision by applying his brakes.
