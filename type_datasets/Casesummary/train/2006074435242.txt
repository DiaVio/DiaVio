This single-vehicle collision occurred on a two-way, four-lane, undivided, and level bituminous roadway during morning daylight hours. Roadway runs east to west and curves sharply to the right. Posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). and is controlled by an advisory speed limit of 16 kmph (25 mph), a flashing yellow beacon, and directional arrow signs. No adverse weather present at the time of collision, Road surface asphalt with melting snow present on roadside edges.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Ford Tempo sedan driven by a 19 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling east in curb lane with the intention of negotiating sharp right-hand turn. According to driver, another vehicle was traveling in lane two at a higher rate of speed passing V1 as it negotiated turn. V1 driver stated that she was distracted by this passing vehicle and departed roadway on right side and collided with wooden utility pole. Also, driver was engaged in conversion with front passenger regarding her concern for other vehicle traveling alongside her vehicle as she was being passed on curb. V1 came to final rest against pole and in lane one facing southwest. V1 was towed due to damage and passenger transported. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
