This crash occurred on the roadway of a two-lane, two-way, non-divided rural, asphalt roadway with a posted speed limit of 72Kmh (45Mph). There were no adverse conditions present at the time of the crash.
V1 a 1989 Chevy truck was heading west bound. V2 a 2001 Kia Sephia was stopped in the west bound lane waiting to turn left. V1 contacted V2 in the rear bumper with its front bumper. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 a 24 year-old male driver stated that he was looking at a car wash sign that some kids were holding on the side of the road. He then stated that when he looked back forward the other vehicle was stopped in front of him; he hit the brakes but could not stop in time.
The diver of V2 a 49 year-old female stated that she was stopped in the roadway waiting for traffic to clear so she could turn left, when she was hit in the back of her vehicle by V2. She then stated that she did not see him coming from behind.
