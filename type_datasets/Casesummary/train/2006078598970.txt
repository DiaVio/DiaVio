This single vehicle ran off the left side of the roadway. The crash occurred mid-morning on divided Interstate roadway with two westbound and two eastbound lanes. The roadways are separated by a depressed earthen median with Oleander bushes in the center of the median. This was a straight section leading into a slight curve left. The roadway was level and bituminous with a posted speed limit of 105 KPH (65 MPH). it was raining heavily and the road was very wet.
Vehicle one(V-1), a 2001 Nissan Frontier Crew Cab 4x4 pickup, driven by a 31 year-old female with two passengers was traveling westbound at approximately 104 kph (65 MPH). The vehicle began to hydroplane on the wet pavement, the driver lost control and the vehicle departed the left side of the road. The vehicle traveled off the roadway for about 12 meters before the wheels tripped in the soft, wet earth. The vehicle tripped and overturned one-quarter turn onto the left side where it contacted the bushes and came to final rest. The vehicle was facing north at final rest. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
