The crash occurred in the intersection of two one-way, one-lane roadways. The roadways were straight, level, bituminous, and dry with a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). It was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions.
V1, a 2006 Dodge Ram quad cab pickup with one occupant was traveling northbound in lane one. V2, a 2006 Nissan Sentra four door, with one occupant was traveling westbound in lane one. At the intersection, the front of V1 contacted the left side of V2. V1 and V2 came to a final rest in the northeast corner of the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Dodge Ram, was driven by a 31 year-old male who reported no injuries. He advised that he was traveling from his friend's house and was going home. He stated that he was going straight through the intersection when the other vehicle came out from the right side and could not avoid the crash.
V2, the Nissan Sentra, was driven by a 60 year-old female who reported no injuries. She advised that she was coming from a baby shower and was returning her son's car. She stated that she stopped at the intersection and was attempting to cross through. When she saw the truck she steered right to try to avoid it. The driver wore contact lenses that corrected a myopic condition and she was also wearing non-prescription sunglasses.
