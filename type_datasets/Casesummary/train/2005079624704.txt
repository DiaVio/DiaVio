This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred on a straight stretch of a dry, level, bituminous roadway, with business driveways on both sides of the road. The roadway is designated a 56 kmph (35 mph) zone. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse atmospheric conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1972 Chevrolet Suburban, was traveling northbound in the third lane (left-turn only) of the five-lane, two-way, undivided roadway, intending to turn left into a private business drive. Vehicle two (V2), a 1964 Porsche 356 C two-door coupe, was traveling southbound in the second lane of opposing traffic. As V1 made its turn, the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
