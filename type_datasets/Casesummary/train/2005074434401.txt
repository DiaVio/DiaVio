This single vehicle crash occurred at 1230 hours and involved a passenger car that ran off the right side of the road and struck a metal pole. The streets were dry, no detrimental environmental conditions. The speed limit for the roadway is 35 mph (56 kph) where the vehicle left the roadway.
Vehicle one is a 1999 Honda Civic sedan with one occupant. The vehicle was traveling east on a business drive, stopped at a stop sign before turning right onto a main thoroughfare. Earlier, the driver had placed a fast food beverage (large paper cup with a lid) into the center console. As driver turned right the cup tipped over and the driver attempted to retrieve it. In the process, the driver allowed the vehicle to run off the right side of the road where it struck the metal utility pole where it came to rest. The driver was transported and the vehicle was towed. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
