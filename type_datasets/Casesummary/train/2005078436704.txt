This single vehicle ran off the right side of the roadway crash occurred on an interstate two lane, dry asphalt roadway. The crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours. There were no adverse weather condition and the weather was clear. The speed limit on the roadway where the crash occurred is posted 104 kmph (65 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2001 Ford Windstar minivan driven by a 16 year-old female with one passenger. They were traveling westbound in the number one lane between 104-112 kmph (65-70 mph) when the driver departed the right side of the roadway. The vehicle then struck a Milepost sign approximately 3.2 meters (10 feet) to the north of the edge of the roadway. The vehicle continued off the roadway traveling in a northwesterly direction when it struck a barbed wire fence. The vehicle then began to travel in a northerly direction where it struck two dirt mounds eventually coming to rest facing in a northerly direction. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver stated that she was thinking about something prior to running off the roadway. She could not recall the events that led up to the vehicle traveling off the roadway. The vehicle was occupied by a right front passenger that was reading a book prior to the crash.
