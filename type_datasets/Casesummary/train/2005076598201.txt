This two-vehicle crash occurred on a five-lane non-divided asphalt constructed roadway. This roadway runs east to west with a posted speed of 56 kmph (35 mph). This roadway has a painted center-left turn lane and a driveway entrance on the south side. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Pontiac MPV was stopped in the driveway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Dodge Intrepid was eastbound in the first lane. V1 turned left from the driveway and the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V1 and V2 then side slapped, with the right side of V2 impacting to the left side of V1. Both vehicles came to rest in the first lane of travel. V1 was not towed due to damage. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a sixty-seven year old female was not interviewed. There is no known pre-crash information for this driver.
The driver of V2, a seventy-four year old male was coming from the drug store heading home. This driver had been driving for about ten minutes at the time of the crash. This driver is taking Vicodin, Coumadin and Lasix. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a weekly basis.
