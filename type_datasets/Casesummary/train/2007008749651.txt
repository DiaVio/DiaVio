This crash occurred off of the right side of a seven-lane highway that was divided by a grass median and guardrail. There were four southbound lanes and three northbound lanes. The roadway curved to the left, had a downhill grade of 3.6 percent, was bituminous, and had a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55 mph). It was afternoon on a weekend, traffic flow was moderate, and conditions were clear and dry.
V1 was a 2003 Chevrolet Venture minivan, driven by a 53 year-old female. V1 was traveling southbound when it departed the roadway on the right side and drove up an embankment. The vehicle began rotating counterclockwise and it traveled through the grass and down the slope. Near the bottom of the embankment, the vehicle impacted a fallen tree, and then tripped over its wheels and began to roll over. The vehicle rolled eight quarter turns as it proceeded up a hill and then continued back down, until it came to final rest on the shoulder of the road, facing north. The vehicle traveled approximately 38 meters during the rollover sequence. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
