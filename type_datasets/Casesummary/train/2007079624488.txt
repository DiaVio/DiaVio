This two vehicle daytime collision occurred midday. The collision occurred on a north/south roadway. The roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt. It consists of five lanes, including a center two-way left turn lane and two additional lanes in each direction. The roadway is designated a 56 kmph (35 mph) zone.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1989 Honda Accord, four-door sedan, driven by a fifty eight (58) year old male was traveling northbound in the number two lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2006 Jeep Commander, sport utility vehicle, driven by a thirty six (36) year old female with one passenger. V2 was traveling southbound in the number two lane. As V1 was traveling a non-contact vehicle traveling in same direction in lane one changed lanes to the left causing V1 to travel into the southbound lanes. The front plane of V1 contacted the front plane of V2. V1 came to rest facing northbound in the southbound number two lane. V2 came to rest facing southbound in the southbound number two lane. V1 received major damage and was towed. V2 received major damage and was towed. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
