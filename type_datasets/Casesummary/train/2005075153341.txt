This crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours at an urban intersection with the east/west traffic controlled by a stop sign. Each leg has two undivided lanes, west leg has a left hand curve, the south leg also has a left hand curve consisting of two undivided lanes. Road surface dry, level asphalt. Posted speed limit 40 kmph (25 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Honda CRV SUV driven by a 30 year-old female was traveling westbound in lane one negotiating a left curve approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Saturn SL2,sedan driven by a 40 year-old female with two passengers was traveling southbound in lane one, negotiating a left curve approaching the intersection. Vehicle one stopped for the stop sign prior to entering the intersection. Both vehicles entered the intersection and the front of vehicle one struck the left side of vehicle two. Vehicle one rotated counterclockwise and the right side of vehicle one struck the left side of vehicle two. Both vehicles came to rest in the roadway south of the impact point. Fluid spillage marked the path of vehicle one as it came to rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
