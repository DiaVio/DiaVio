This single-vehicle crash occurred on a straight and level trafficway with two lanes in each direction that are divided by a grass median. This roadway was a marked construction zone at the time of the crash. The speed limit for this roadway is 80 kmph (50 mph). It was dark at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Ford Focus, was southbound in lane one. The driver of V1 swerved left to avoid a raccoon in the roadway. V1 traveled off the left side of the roadway and began to rotate clockwise. V1 rolled over to the left six quarter-turns. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
The driver of V1, a seventeen-year-old female, was traveling with two male passengers, from a football game to their homes. This driver was not interviewed. According to one of the passengers, this driver had been driving for only four months and this was her first time on this road. This passenger also stated that the driver of V1 lost control after the initial avoidance maneuvers and then steered right and applied the brakes in an effort to regain control.
