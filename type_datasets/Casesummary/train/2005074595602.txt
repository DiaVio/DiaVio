This three vehicle crash occurred just past noon during daylight hours on a 3-lane, divided, and dry bituminous roadway with a negative 4% grade. The weather was clear, and the posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph).
Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 black Mitsubishi Eclipse 2 door driven by a 26 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling south and stopped for traffic on the same roadway as vehicle one (V1). V1 was a 2002 Pontiac Grand Am sedan driven by a 19 year-old female who was also traveling south trailing behind V2. Vehicle three (V3) was a 2005 Dodge Stratus sedan driven by a 31 year-old female. V2 and V3 stopped for a few seconds due to traffic up ahead that had stopped. V2 was struck in the rear by the front of V1. V2 then struck the rear of V3. All three vehicles came to final rest still facing south. V1 and V2 were towed from the scene, V3 left under own power. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
