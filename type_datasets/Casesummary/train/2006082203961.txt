This two-vehicle collision occurred at the intersection of two two-way city streets. The intersection has no traffic controls and both roads are straight, level and paved with worn concrete. The speed limit for both roads is 48 kph (30 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was clear with no cloud cover.
Vehicle one, a 1970 Ford F100 pickup was southbound in the first lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle two, a 1987 BMW 325i was eastbound in the first lane approaching the intersection. The front of vehicle two impacted the left of vehicle one. Neither vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a forty-seven year old male refused to be interviewed.
The driver of vehicle two, a sixty-six year old female refused to continue the interview after giving a brief description of the crash. This driver indicated that right before the intersection she was looking right and then forward as she entered the intersection. This driver indicated that she steered and braked with unknown lock-up to avoid the collision.
