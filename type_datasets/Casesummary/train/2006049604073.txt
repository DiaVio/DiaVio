The crash occurred at 1146 hours involving two passenger cars at an intersection without traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). The road is a 2-lane undivided (two-way traffic) asphalt roadway for both vehicles. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash. The east-west roadway had a stop sign. The south-north roadway did not have a traffic sign.
V 1, a 2003 Chevrolet Trailblazer driven by a 23 year-old female was traveling west in the first lane, crossing through the intersection of both roadways. V 2, a 2001 Chevrolet Impala, Driven by a 29 year-old male with three passengers was traveling north in the first lane. The front of V 2 impacted the left side of V 1. Both vehicles came to rest within the intersection. V 1 was towed from the scene, there were no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
