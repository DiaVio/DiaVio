The crash involving two vehicles occurred at 1353 hours at the intersection of a one-way, three lane north/south roadway posted speed limit 64 kmph (40 mph) and a two-way, four lane (seven overall), divided east/west roadway posted speed limit 56 kmph (35 mph) without a positive barrier Both roads were dry, traveled, level concrete. Traffic flow controlled by a traffic signal,traffic was light at the time of the accident. There were no adverse weather conditions.
V 1 a 1992 Ford Escort Coupe driven by a 44 year-old female was southbound in the first travel lane. V 2 a 1996 Mercury Grand Marquis Sedan driven by a 50 year-old female was westbound in the first travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and it's front struck the right side of V 2. V 1 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing west in the westbound lanes. V 2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing north in the westbound lanes. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, no reported injuries.
