This two vehicle crash occurred during early evening daylight hours at a four leg on-colors controlled intersection. The road surface was dry, level concrete. There were six lanes divided north/south; and, two lanes east/west. The posted speed limit was 64 kmph (40 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a white 4-door 1995 Honda Civic LX sedan was driven by a 45 year-old male with one passenger. Vehicle two (V2) was a grey 4-door 2003 GMC Envoy utility vehicle driven by a 24 year-old male with two passengers. V1 was heading north in the third lane approached the intersection with the green light. V2 was traveling south in the left turn lane, as V2 made left turn in was struck on the right side by the front of V1. V1 applied brakes to avoid collision, but broad sided V2. V1 came to final rest in the intersection facing northeast, and was towed from the scene due to damage. V2 drove to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
