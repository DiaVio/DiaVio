The crash occurred on a north/south four-lane median divided highway. The highway had two travel lanes and left turn lanes in each direction. The highway was intersected by a two-lane road with turn lanes on the east and west. The intersection is controlled by traffic signals and the speed limit is 72kph (45mph). Conditions were daylight partly cloudy and wet, heavy rain had ended a few hours before the crash, on a weekday afternoon.
Vehicle #1 was beige 1998 Mazda 626 traveling southbound on the highway. Vehicle #2, a blue 1995 Nissan Altima, was northbound in the left lane of the highway. Vehicle #1 entered the left turn lane with a solid green light. The Mazda (V1) came to a stop at the intersection before attempting to turn left and go east on the intersecting road. When Vehicle #1 tried to turn left its front struck the left side of Vehicle #2. After impact, Vehicle #1 came to rest facing east in the intersecting eastbound lane 18m east of impact. The Altima (V2) came to rest on the northbound shoulder 44m north of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle #1 was driven by an 18-year-old female. She was unbelted with deployed airbags and transported to an area hospital with minor injuries. She was uncooperative, but in a brief statement said she didn't see any approaching traffic so she attempted to turn left onto the intersecting street.
Vehicle #2 was operated by a 42-year-old male. He was belted with no airbags deployed and transported with minor injuries. He stated he saw Vehicle #1 stop at the intersection and then 'out of the blue' attempt to turn left, accelerating into the side of his car.
