This four vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours. The location was a two lane undivided (one-way traffic) left curved downhill ramp connecting two sections of an interstate highway. The weather was clear and the roads surface dry concrete. The speed limit is 72 kph (45 mph).
Vehicle one (V 1), a 2006 Mini Cooper, driven by a 23 year-old female was traveling east in the second lane of traffic. Vehicle two (V 2), a 1987 Chevrolet Monte Carlo coupe driven by a 19 year-old male was traveling east to the rear of V 1. Vehicle three (V 3), a 2000 Chevy Astro Van driven by a 59 year-old male was traveling east in the second lane and Vehicle four (V 4) a 1988 Chevrolet Sport van were traveling in lane two behind V 3. An unidentified vehicle driving in lane two changed into V 1's lane of travel. The driver applied the brakes until the vehicle came to a complete stop. V 2 attempted to change into lane two to avoid a rear end collision. The left front corner of V 2 impacted the rear of V 1. V 3's front impacted the rear of V 2. V 4's front impacted the rear of V 3. All vehicles came to rest within lane two. All four vehicles were towed from the scene. None of the occupants was injured. The driver of V 1 stated the vehicle coming from lane one made a very abrupt and unsafe maneuver, forcing her to apply the brakes until her vehicle came to a complete stop. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V3 and V4 were not involved in first harmful event.
