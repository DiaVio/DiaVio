This two-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours at an intersection controlled by on-colors traffic signal. The north and southbound roadway consists of 3 lanes of travel including a left-turn lane for both directions and a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). The westbound roadway consists of 6 lanes of travel including a center left turn lane and a right-turn only lane. The eastbound roadway consists of 5 lanes of travel with a center left-turn lane. The northbound roadway is on a slight downhill elevation. The southbound roadway is level and is a private parking lot entrance and exit.
Vehicle one (V1) a 2001 Ford Ranger pickup driven by a 40 year-old male was stopped facing south at the red light exiting the parking lot with the intent of turning east. A full size pickup was stopped at the red light in front of V1. After the traffic control light turned green, the non-contact pickup immediately turned left. Vehicle two (V2) a 2000 Hyundai Elantra sedan driven by a 30 year-old female with three passengers was traveling northbound passing through the intersection going straight when V1 turned in front of V2. The front bumper of V1 contacted the front l of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
