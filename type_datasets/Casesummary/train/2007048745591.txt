This single vehicle crash occurred mid afternoon on a rainy day. The crash occurred on a two-lane, wet, bituminous, uphill, wet, roadway that curves slightly to the left (in the southbound direction) with a radius of 563 meters (1845 feet) and a super elevation of 3%. The posted speed limit for this roadway was 72 kph (45 mph). It was raining steadily at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 GMC Sierra 1500 pickup, was traveling southbound in lane one negotiating the left hand curve. V1 departed the left side of the roadway and the vehicle initiated a clockwise yaw on the roadside. The vehicle rolled 4 quarter turns to the left as it came to rest facing west in the northbound lane. The vehicle was towed from the scene. The driver was transported to a trauma center. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1.
