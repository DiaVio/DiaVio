This two vehicle daytime collision occurred on a four lane, divided, dry, level, asphalt roadway. The roadway is designated 56 kmph (35 mph). The intersection is controlled by a on-color signal that was working properly at the time.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2000 Kia Sephia 4-door sedan, driven by a seventy five (75) year old male who was traveling west in the number four left turn lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 1996 Chevrolet Astro van, driven by a thirty five (35) year old male who was traveling east in the number two lane. As V1 attempted a left turn V1's right side front was contacted by the front of V2 after the first impact the vehicle's side slapped. V1 came to rest in the intersection facing southeast in the eastbound number one lane. V2 came to rest at the east curb south of the intersection facing southeast. V1 and V2 were towed with major damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
