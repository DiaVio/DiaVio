This single-vehicle crash occurred just after noon on a weekday on the right roadside of a two-lane undivided roadway. The bituminous east/west roadway was dry, straight and level with a posted speed limit of 72kmph (45 mph). Traffic flow was normal and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
V1, a 2000 Audi A6 station wagon, was eastbound when it departed the roadway to the right and struck a tree with its right front corner. The vehicle rolled forward slightly and came to final rest facing east near the point of impact. The driver was transported from the scene with minor injuries and his vehicle was towed. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1 is a 47 year-old male that has three prior DUI arrests according to the officer on the scene. The state law mandates an immediate 90-day jail sentence for three DUI convictions. The medical report states that this driver admitted to drinking a pint of vodka this morning claiming, “I've got a lot of things going on”. The driver could be located to obtain a complete interview.
