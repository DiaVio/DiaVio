This crash occurred late in the afternoon on a straight and level section of asphalt roadway. The roadway consisted of two northbound and two southbound travel lanes with a center two-way turning lane. The posted speed limit was 45 mph (72 kph).
Vehicle 1 was a 1999 Nissan Altima with one occupant. Vehicle 2 was a 2003 Hyundai Santa Fe with one occupant. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
Vehicle 1 was traveling south in lane 2 and Vehicle 2 was traveling south in lane 1 ahead of Vehicle 1. A witness traveling behind Vehicle 1 told police that both vehicles appeared to be traveling at a speed less than the posted speed limit.
Vehicle 2 initiated a left U-turn across the path of Vehicle 1. The front of Vehicle 1 contacted the left rear of Vehicle 2. Vehicle 1 came to rest in its original travel lane. Vehicle 2 came to rest heading north in the left northbound lane.
The driver of Vehicle 2 refused to be interviewed.
