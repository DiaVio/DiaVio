This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged intersection. This intersection is stoplight controlled with seven- lanes for the east to west travel and 3 lanes for the north to south entrance lanes. The primary travel lane is the east to west, which is straight, and level. This roadway is asphalt constructed with no obvious defects. The posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred in the late afternoon with clear weather and the setting sun glaring into the westbound traffic.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Chevrolet Cavalier was westbound in the third lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Chevrolet Cavalier was stopped westbound in the third lane. V2 had just started on a green light when the front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. Both vehicles came to rest on the right shoulder. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, an eighty-six year old male was heading home after visiting his wife in a nursing home. This driver had been driving for less than one-half hour at the time of the crash. This driver was taking Aspirin and Mavik as medications. This driver stated that he could not see too far ahead due to the sun glare. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
The driver of V2, a twenty-year old female was heading home after working. This driver had been driving for about twelve minutes at the time of the crash. This driver stated that she was stopped and proceeded through the intersection on the green light. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
