This single-vehicle crash occurred in the evening hours during darkness with no overhead lighting. The location was in the eastbound lanes of a rural state route highway. This roadway was a bituminous four-lane divided roadway, with two lanes in each direction. The weather was clear and dry, and visibility was clear. The posted speed limit was 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Acura Integra 2 door hatchback driven by a twenty five (25) year old male, was traveling eastbound in lane two. V1 approached a cardboard box in the road. According to a witness, the driver of V1 hit the brakes and steered to the right to avoid the debris in the roadway. V1's driver then over corrected and impacted the center median concrete barrier with the front left corner. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
