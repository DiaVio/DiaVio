This two-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours on a north/south, undivided two-lane roadway in front of a school. At the south end of this roadway is an intersection that is comprised of an east/west two-lane road that comes from the east and tees with the intersection. There is also a northwest/southeast two-lane road. The road surface was level, dry asphalt. The posted speed limit on the north/south road was 32 kmph (20 mph) in the school zone.
Vehicle one, (V 1) a 1991 Mercedes Benz 300E sedan driven by a 46 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling northbound in the first lane. An unoccupied full-size school bus was parked in front of the school on the right (east) side of the road. V 1 entered the parking lane and the front of the V 1 collided with the rear of the school bus. V 1 came to rest with the majority of its front end underneath the rear of the bus. V 1 was towed due to damage and both the driver and passenger were transported to a local medical facility with non-incapacitating injuries.
