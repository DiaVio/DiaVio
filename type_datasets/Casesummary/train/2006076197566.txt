This single-vehicle crash occurred off the left side of a one-way, two-lane roadway. This roadway has a slight curve to the left (radius of curvature 1736 meters) and a downhill grade (5.7%). The speed limit on this roadway is 105 kmph (65 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Dodge Stratus, was southbound in lane one at a reported speed of 129 kmph (80 mph). V1 traveled off the right side of the roadway and the driver steered left. V1 then returned to the roadway, crossed both lanes and traveled off the left side of the roadway, rotating counterclockwise. The front of V1 struck an embankment. V1 then rolled over to the right six quarter-turns. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 129 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a forty-one-year-old female, was traveling from a medical appointment to her home. She was well rested, drives this vehicle several times per week and is familiar with the roadway. This driver takes Advair, Albuterol, Singulair and Morphine for asthma and chronic back pain. She stated that her doctor had just told her that she would have to be scheduled for surgery for Carpal Tunnel syndrome. This driver further stated that she was thinking about how this condition and the surgery would affect her job. Medical records did not confirm this medical condition.
