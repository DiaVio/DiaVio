The crash occurred at the intersection of two two-lane roads. There was a road going north/south with two lanes and a road going east/west with two lanes. The north/south roadway was curved to the right heading in the south direction with an 8.3% downhill grade, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). The east/west roadway was a level, straight, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). It was afternoon on a weekday and the roads were dry and skies were clear. Traffic flow was very light.
V1, a 1987 Chevrolet Camaro two door with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane one. V2, a 1994 Oldsmobile Ciera with one occupant, was stopped at a stop sign facing west waiting to turn left and proceed south. V2 proceeded to turn left and was struck on the left by V1's front. V1 came to rest 3 meters south of impact, V2 came to rest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V1, the Chevy Camaro, was driven by a 32 year-old male who reported no injuries. He advised he was on his way home when the woman in V2 pulled out in front of him and he could not stop in time. The driver related he had gotten into an argument at work prior to the crash.
V2, the Olds Cirea, was driven by a 78 year-old female who reported non incapacitating injuries. She stated she believed the road was clear prior to making her turn.
