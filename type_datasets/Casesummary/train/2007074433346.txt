It was dark (early morning hours) with no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of this single-vehicle crash. The roadway is undivided, straight, has a five percent downhill grade and consists of one lane in each direction. The speed limit for this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet K1500 pickup, was northbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the left side of the roadway and the front struck a tree, then a cluster of trees and then the left side struck a tree. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-five-year-old male, was not interviewed due to his injuries. His wife provided minimal information. She indicated that this driver had gone to a party at a friend's house. Prior to the crash, she spoke with the driver and he stated that he was on his way home. This driver's spouse further stated that the driver was healthy, well rested prior to going to work the previous day and familiar with his vehicle. He reportedly travels this route monthly. This driver's medical record indicates a BAC of .181.
