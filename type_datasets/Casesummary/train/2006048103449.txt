This single vehicle crash occurred on a four-lane, median-divided, level, bituminous roadway that curves to the left. The posted speed limit for this roadway was 113 kmph (70 mph) and there were no traffic control devices present at the location of the crash. The radius of the curve was 348 meters. The crash occurred during mid-afternoon daylight hours. It was raining at the time of the crash and the roadway was wet.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Jeep Liberty, was westbound in lane one, attempting to change lanes to the left. V1 departed lane one to the left and then moved right to avoid a non-contact vehicle. V1 re-entered lane one and then departed the roadway to the right, traveling down an embankment into a ditch, rotating clockwise. The front of V1 struck the ditch and the vehicle rolled four quarter-turns to the left for approximately 32 meters (105 feet). V1 came to rest on its wheels facing southeast. Police Reported Travel Speed is 129 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, an eighteen-year-old female, was traveling from her home to school. She was well rested and very familiar with the route and her vehicle. She stated that she checked rearview mirror prior to attempting to change lanes but had not turned her head to check for traffic in the left lane. She failed to use her left side mirror to check for the presence of other vehicles in the adjacent lane.
