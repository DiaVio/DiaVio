The crash occurred in the afternoon hours of a weekend at the intersection of a one-way roadway and a two-way roadway. The intersection consisted of a one-way southbound roadway and a two-way east/west roadway. The southbound roadway was controlled by a stop sign. Both roadways were dry, straight, level and had a speed limit of 48kmph (30mph).
V1, a 1992 Lexus LS 400 four door with three occupants, was traveling eastbound. V2, a 2000 Ford Explorer four door with a single occupant was traveling southbound. At the intersection, the frontal plane of V2 impacted the left side plane of V1. V1 and V2 came to a final rest within a few meters from the location of the impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Lexus LS 400 was driven by a 26 year-old male who sustained minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. He advised that he was coming from watching a football game with friends and family and on his way home.
V2, the Ford Explorer was driven by a 58 year-old male who sustained minor injuries and was also transported to a medical facility. He stated that he stopped at the stop sign, looked both ways and then proceeded through the intersection. He also advised that he was coming from home and on his way to a Church service.
