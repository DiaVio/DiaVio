This two-vehicle crash occurred on a divided, access-controlled roadway. The eastbound lanes consist of five through lanes. The left lane is designated for high occupancy vehicles (HOV) only. This roadway is level and straight. The speed limit on this roadway is 97 kmph (60 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 Toyota Camry, was traveling eastbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), an unknown vehicle, was stopped eastbound in front of V1. The front of V1 struck the back of V2.
