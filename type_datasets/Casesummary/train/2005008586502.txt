This crash occurred at the intersection of a three-lane roadway and a seven-lane roadway.
The asphalt roadways were straight and level. The speed limit for the seven-lane roadway was 45mph (72kph). The speed limit for the three-lane roadway was 25mph (40kph). It was daylight and raining at the time. The intersection was controlled by a traffic control signal that was functioning properly. It is unknown which vehicle had a red light because both drivers stated they had a green light and no witnesses were identified. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
Vehicle 1 was a 2001 Chevrolet Monte Carlo that was heading east across the intersection in lane two of the seven-lane roadway. Vehicle 2 was a 2000 Chevrolet Cavalier that was heading south across the intersection in lane one of the three-lane roadway.
The front of Vehicle 2 contacted the left side of vehicle 1. Vehicle 1 was driven from the scene, the driver and vehicle could not be located.
