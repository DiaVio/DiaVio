This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged intersection. The north/south roadway is undivided, straight and consists of two lanes in each direction. Both approaches have an uphill grade. The speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Volkswagen Beetle, was southbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1992 Chevrolet Corsica, was northbound in lane two. V1 stopped at the intersection and then entered the intersection turning left. The front of V1 struck the front of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, an eighteen-year-old female, was traveling from the mall to a friend's house. She was well rested, in good health and familiar with her vehicle. This was her first time driving on this road. This driver stated that she was turning her head to the right looking for an address, before and during her turn. She stated that she did not see V2 prior to the crash.
The driver of V2, a thirty-seven-year-old female, was on her way to a store from her home. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. She travels this route weekly. This driver stated that she applied the brakes and steered to the right when she realized that V1 was turning in front of her.
