This crash occurred on an urban 2-way, 4-lane, undivided, dry, asphalt, straight, level roadway with parallel parking allowed on both sides of the street. Double solid yellow lines mark the centerline of the roadway while dashed white lines mark the lane lines. The road runs north/south with a 4-way intersection located approximately 15 meters south of the intersection. Approaching the intersection from the east, the road is straight with an upgrade. Traffic on the cross street is controlled by stop signs. The speed limit is 30 mph (48 kmph). There were no environmental, roadway or traffic restrictions at the time of the crash that occurred about noon.
Vehicle two (V2), a 1985 light blue Buick Park Ave. 4 door sedan was traveling west on the cross street, stopped at the stop sign before accelerating and turning left to proceed south. The vehicle was proceeding south in lane two when the impact occurred. Vehicle one (V1), a white 1991 Buick LeSabre 4-door sedan was parked facing south when the driver of V1 left the parking lane and attempted a u-turn. The vehicle was in lane two when the front right of V2 struck the left front of V1. V1 rotated clockwise as it traveled southwest and came to rest adjacent to the west curb line. The driver of V2 drove about a block before he stopped his vehicle. The driver of V1 was transported to a local hospital. V1 was towed from the scene. The driver of V2 was not injured. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
