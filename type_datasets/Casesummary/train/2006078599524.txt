This is a two vehicle crash that occurred during mid-afternoon daylight at the entrance to a private driveway from a two way, bituminous roadway with three traffic lanes for southbound traffic, a center designated turn lane, and two northbound traffic lanes. There were no traffic controls present, no sight line restrictions, and no roadway deficiencies noted. There was congested traffic in both north and south directions. The roadways were straight and level. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph) for both north and south directions. The weather was clear with no adverse conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) a 1999 Chevrolet 1500 series Extra-Cab pickup driven by a 60 year-old female was traveling south in the number one, right turn lane at an estimated speed of 56 kmph 35 mph. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Honda CRV sport utility 4 door,driven by a 36 year-old female with two passengers was stopped in the center, left turn lane waiting for traffic to clear and make a left turn into the private driveway. Several vehicles were in the number two and number three southbound lanes. The driver of V-2 waited for 15-20 seconds until all southbound traffic in the number two and three lanes stopped and motioned the driver of V-2 to cross in front of them. The driver of V-2 accelerated and turned left. She did not see oncoming V-1 in the number one lane because it was obscured by other traffic. V-1 applied the brakes with no lockup and struck the right side passenger door of V-2. The driver of V-2 did not see V-1 until they collided. V-1 came to final rest facing generally southwest in the driveway entrance. V-2 came to final rest facing west against the south driveway curb. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
