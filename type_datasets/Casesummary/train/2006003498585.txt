The crash occurred in the intersection of a one-way roadway and a two-way roadway with one lane in each direction. The roadway was straight and level and it was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions. The speed limit was 48kmph (30mph).
Vehicle # 1 was a 2002 Ford Explorer that was traveling northbound on the one-way roadway and stopped at the stop sign. Vehicle # 2 was a 2002 Subaru Forester that was traveling eastbound on the two-way roadway. As Vehicle # 1 proceeded through the intersection and was impacted by the front of Vehicle # 2. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The Ford Explorer (Vehicle # 1) was driven by a 42 year-old female who did not sustain any injuries. She stated that as she was inching up at the stop sign because a parked Van was obstructing her view, when she was impacted by Vehicle # 2. The Ford was towed due to damage.
The Subaru Forester (Vehicle # 2) was driven by a 35 year-old male who sustained minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. The driver stated that Vehicle # 1 ran the stop sign. The driver was issued a citation for an unknown reason. The Subaru was towed due to damage.
