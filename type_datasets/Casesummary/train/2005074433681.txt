This three-vehicle crash occurred on a five lane concrete level, dry roadway,no adverse weather conditions. Time of the crash was mid-afternoon with a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2003 Honda CR-V SUV driven by a 51 year-old female who was traveling east in the first lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 2005 Dodge Magnum SUV driven by a 56 year-old female with one passenger. V2 was stopped in the first eastbound lane. Vehicle three (V3), a 1999 Chevrolet Lumina sedan driven by a 44 year-old female, was stopped in the first eastbound lane. As V1 was traveling east V2 and V3 had stopped for traffic. V1's front impacted with the rear of V2. This first impact pushed V2's front into the rear of V3. All three vehicle came to final rest in the same traffic lane. V1 was towed from the scene, V2 and V3 left the scene under there own power. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
