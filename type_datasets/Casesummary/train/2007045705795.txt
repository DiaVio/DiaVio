The crash occurred on a two-lane, two-way road that ran east/west in a residential area. The road was straight with a slight negative (if eastbound) grade measuring just over 2%. The road had a painted double yellow stripe with no traffic controls. Other residential side roads intersected the road. Conditions at the time of the crash were daylight and dry in the afternoon on a weekend. The speed limit was 48kph/30mph.
V1 was a 2001 Toyota Camry traveling east on the road when its front struck the back of V2, a 1999 Oldsmobile Intrigue that was stopped in eastbound lane 1 waiting to turn left onto a side road. V1 came to rest a few meters east of impact with the same heading angle it began with. V2 was pushed several meters east and then was driven onto a side road for safety. V1 was towed from the scene, and V2 was driven away. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a sixty-four-year-old-belted-female who was uninjured in the crash. Her airbags did not deploy. She was on her way home from a trip to the store. She said she wasn't sure if V2 had its turn signal on.
V2 was driven by a sixteen-year-old-belted-male who was uninjured in the crash. He was on his way home from work at the time of the crash. He stated that as he was getting ready to make his turn he saw V1 coming at him pretty quick then got hit.
