This single vehicle daytime rollover collision occurred on the south/west corner of a two-lane arterial roadway intersecting with a two-way city street. In the area of the crash, off the roadway on the right side of the city street there is a rocky strip and a retaining wall. The road is paved with asphalt and has a 7% uphill grade approaching the westbound arterial the city street. The rollover initiation was on an embankment with a 76% uphill grade at point of initiation of the rollover. The speed limit for the road is 32 kmph (20 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was clear.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 Chevrolet Impala sedan driven by a thirty nine (39) year old male, was heading west in lane one and was following a non-contact vehicle. The non-contact vehicle had pulled over to the right and V1 began to pass it on its left. Just as V1 was passing on the left, the non-contact vehicle began a U-turn. V1 steered left, crossed over the intersection, departed the roadway and hit a retaining wall. V1 proceeded up the steep embankment causing it to rollover. V1 came to final rest on its top and was towed due to disabling damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
