This two-car collision occurred on a two-way three-lane roadway in the early afternoon hours. The straight and level roadway consists of two lanes running southeast, one lane running northwest and a parking lane on the northwest side of the roadway. There is a double-yellow stripe that separates the two directions of travel. The posted speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). It was daylight with no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Jeep Grand Cherokee with one occupant, was traveling southeast in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Toyota Camry also with one occupant, was traveling northwest in lane one. The driver of vehicle one was attempting a left hand turn into the drive-thru entrance of a pharmacy when the front of that vehicle contacted the front of V2. After impact both vehicles came to rest near to area of impact. V1 was not towed; vehicle two incurred moderate front-end damage that made it inoperable. Both drivers received no injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
