The crash occurred at 1544 between a passenger car and a cargo van at the intersection of a two-way, three lane (five overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier and and a two-way, four lane, undivided roadway . Both roads were dry, traveled, level asphalt. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions.
V 1 a 2002 Honda Accord driven by a 49 year-old male was northbound in the second travel lane. V 2 a 1998 Chevrolet Express van driven by a 50 year-old male was westbound in the first travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V 2. V 1 rotated clockwise and was struck on the right side by the right side of V 2. V 1 came to rest facing east, off the roadway on the northwest corner. V 2 continued westbound and was struck on the right side by the right side of V 1. V 2 came to rest facing west in the westbound lanes west of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, driver of V 1 was transported to a local medical facility.
