This crash involving two passenger cars occurred shortly after noon during daylight hours on a two-way, three lane, undivided roadway, at intersection with a parking lot entryway. The road was dry, traveled, level asphalt. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph).There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2001 Plymouth Neon driven by a 24 year-old female was westbound in the second turn lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1996 Chevrolet Lumina driven by a 35 year-old male was eastbound in the first travel lane. V1 attempted a left turn into the business parking lot without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise and was struck on the right side by the left side of V2. V1 came to rest facing east, off the roadway to the south. V2 rotated clockwise and was struck on the left side by the right side of V1. V2 came to rest facing south, off the roadway to the south. Both vehicle were towed from the scene due to damage sustained in the collision. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
