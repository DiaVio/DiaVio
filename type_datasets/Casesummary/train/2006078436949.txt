This two-vehicle rear end crash occurred during early morning hours in the eastbound lane of a two-lane, two-way roadway in a residential area. The lanes are not separated. The posted speed limit for the residential roadway is posted 40 kph (25 mph). the roads were dry, level asphalt and the sky was clear. The area was dark and not lighted at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one( V-1), a 2005 Chevrolet Venture mini van driven by a 35 year-old female with one passenger was traveling eastbound at approximately 32 kph (20 mph). Vehicle two(V-2), a 19995 Geo Metro coupe, was legally parked on the right side of the roadway facing east. V-1 drifted to the right side of the roadway and struck the rear of V-2. V-1 continued to push V-2 for approximately 35 meters before V-2 came to a stop. V-1 came to a controlled stop behind V-2 and parked in the middle of the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
The pre-event movement for V1 coded going straight, category other motor vehicle in lane, other vehicle stopped. Critical reason for critical event driver related factor "Internal distraction".
