This two vehicle collision occurred shortly before noon during daylight hours on a Saturday. The collision occurred at a four-way intersection with two undivided northbound lanes and two southbound lanes. There is also one eastbound and one westbound lane. The roadway was dry, level and made of asphalt. There are no turn lanes present at the tri-light controlled intersection. The signal was working properly at the time of the collision. The north and southbound lanes are designated 56 kmph (35 mph) zones.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1990 Ford Aerostar mini-van, traveling south in the number two lane with the intention of turn left at the intersection. Vehicle two (V2) is a 1999 Ford F-150, traveling north in the number one lane with the intention of going straight. A non-contact vehicle described by the witness as a Ford F-150 was stopped in the number two northbound lane waiting to turn left. As V1 made a left turn to head east, the front of V2 struck the front of V1. V1 rotated 180 degrees counter clockwise and came to rest facing north, north of the impact point in lane one. V2 came to rest near the impact facing northeast. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
