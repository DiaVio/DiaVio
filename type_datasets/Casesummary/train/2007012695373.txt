The crash occurred off the edge of a five-lane, two-way roadway. It had three northbound lanes and two southbound lanes. This was a bituminous roadway with a level grade and a posted speed of 72 kmph (45 mph). It was afternoon on a weekday and the road was wet and that it was raining. Traffic was flowing.
V1, a 2004 Buick Lesabre four-door with two occupants was traveling north in lane three. V1 was approaching an intersection intending to turn left. The driver of V1 stated that he attempted to turn left onto an adjacent roadway but the wheel locked up and he could not steer the vehicle. He attempted to apply the brakes but could not stop. He slid through the intersection and contacted its front to a guardrail off the edge of the road, where it came to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1 was a 15-year old male. He reported no injuries and refused treatment. He stated that he and his friend had left his house and was out driving around. He stated that he had been traveling between 18-32 kmph (11-20 mph) before he initiated the turn.
