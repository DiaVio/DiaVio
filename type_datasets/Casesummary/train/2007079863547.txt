This two vehicle daytime collision occurred mid-morning on a four lane, undivided, dry, level, roadway made of asphalt. The roadway runs north and south and is designated 56 kmph (35 mph). The road narrows as you head south going from four lanes to three lanes and then to two lanes at the area of impact.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1998 Ford Windstar minivan, driven by a twenty seven year old female was traveling south in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2000 Toyota Avalon 4-door sedan driven by a seventy one year old female. V2 was traveling south in the number one lane behind V1. V1 attempted a u-turn at the point where the roadway goes from three lanes to two. V2 attempted to avoid V1 by passing on the left over the double yellow lines. V1's left plane was impacted by V2's right plane. V1 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southeast in the northbound lane. V2 headed southeast and came to rest facing southeast also in the northbound lane. V1 was driven from the scene. V2 was towed with moderate damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
