This two-vehicle crash occurred in the intersection of a five-lane roadway and a seven-lane roadway. The north south road is curved to the east. The east west road is curved to the north. The northbound lanes have five lanes, four through lanes and one left turn lane. The southbound lanes have three lanes, two through lanes and one left turn lane. The intersection is controlled by a stop light with pedestrian controls. The speed limit on the roadway is 56 kph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 2000 Kia Sportage was northbound turning left at the intersection. Vehicle two, a 1997 Honda Civic was southbound in the first lane. As vehicle one turned left, its front impacted the left of vehicle two. Both vehicles came to rest in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a thirty-three year old female was not interviewed, but according to the PAR and medical records this driver apparently experienced a seizure during the crash. Medical records indicate that this driver has experienced seizures in the past. There is no know sleep or work information on this driver.
The driver of vehicle two, a thirty-year-old male had been driving for about four hours at the time of the crash. This driver had come to shop. There is no known sleep information for this driver.
