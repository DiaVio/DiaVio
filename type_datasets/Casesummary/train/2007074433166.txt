This single-vehicle crash occurred on a freeway exit ramp. This roadway has a downhill grade, jersey barriers on both sides and consists of two through lanes that are curved to the right and then to the left. The speed limit for this roadway is 80 kmph (50 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Ford Focus, was westbound in lane one. V1 traveled to the left and the front struck the left side barrier. V1 then traveled to the right and the front and the left side struck the right side barrier. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a sixteen-year-old male, was on his way home from school. He was well rested, healthy and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route daily. This driver stated that he was in a hurry because he had argued with his mother who had caught him skipping school. He further stated that he was stressed because "my mom was going to take my stuff."
