This crash occurred in an intersection in the northbound lanes of a four-lane highway with no barrier. There were two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes. This was a straight bituminous roadway with a posted speed of 72 kmph (45 mph). It was evening on a weekday and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 2003 Mitsubishi Eclipse driven by a 16-year-old female. V2 was a 2004 Ford F-350 truck driven by a 62-year-old male. V1 was traveling southbound in lane number two. The driver made a left hand turn in the intersection in front of V2. V2's front collided into the right front side of V1. V1 was spun into the southbound lanes and the right side of V1 side slapped the left side of V2. V2 was forced off of the roadway to the right and up an embankment, knocking down a sign in the process. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
