This two-vehicle collision occurred during evening hours on a weekday night; street lamps illuminated the scene. The crash occurred at the four-leg intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-west roadway, and the intersection is controlled by a tri-light traffic signal. Both roadways are straight, level, five-lane, two-way, bituminous roads. The speed limit is 64 kmph (40 mph) in all directions. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Toyota Camry 4-door sedan, was traveling northbound in the first lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Nissan Altima 4-door sedan, was traveling eastbound in the second lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. In the intersection, the front of V1 impacted the right side of V2. Both vehicles rotated clockwise and moved northeast to final rest in the eastbound lanes. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
