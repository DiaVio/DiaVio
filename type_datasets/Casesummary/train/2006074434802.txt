This single vehicle collision occurred on an interstate exit ramp. This road has two lanes with multiple curves. This road is level, one-way and of concrete construction. This road has a speed limit of 97 kmph (60 mph) with a posted advisory speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). No adverse weather present at the time of collision.
Vehicle one, a 2003 Saturn sedan was eastbound in the first lane. Vehicle one departed the road to the right. Vehicle one traveled off road and the front impacted an embankment. Vehicle one went air borne and the front and then the right impacted a large utility pole. Vehicle one landed and came to rest on its wheels. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of vehicle one, a thirty-three year old female was driving to an electronics store to have her automatic start looked at. This driver had been driving for about one half hour at the time of the crash. This driver does not remember the specifics of the crash. This driver was taking Ibuprofen and Methylprenisolone as medications. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a monthly basis.
