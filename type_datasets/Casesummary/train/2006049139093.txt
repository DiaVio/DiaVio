The crash occurred at the intersection of a two-way, two lane, undivided roadway and a two-way, four lane, undivided roadway Both roads were dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit east/west is 48 kmph (30 mph). and 56 kmph (35 mph) for north/south. Traffic was light at the time of the crash. There were no adverse weather conditions. The crash time was 1105 in the morning on a sunny, clear day.
V 1 a 1996 Buick Park Avenue driven by a 77 year-old female was eastbound in the first travel lane. V 2 a 2005 Lexus RX330 driven by a 59 year-old female was northbound in the second travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V 2. V 1 rotated counterclockwise and was struck on the right side by the left side of V 2. V 1 departed the roadway and was struck on the left side by a traffic sign. V 1 came to rest facing east, off the roadway on the northeast corner. V 2 rotated clockwise and was struck on the left side by the right side of V 1. V 2 came to rest in the intersection facing southwest. Both vehicle's were towed from the scene, no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
