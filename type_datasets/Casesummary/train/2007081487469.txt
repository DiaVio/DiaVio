This three-vehicle crash occurred shortly after noon during daylight hours. The location was a busy intersection. The roadway was a bituminous seven-lane divided roadway. The impact location is just south of the intersection in lane four. The weather was cloudy and raining. Visibility was fairly clear. Traffic flow was controlled by overhead on-color traffic signals. The posted speed limit was 72 kmph (45 mph ).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1998 Hyndai Accent coupe driven by a seventy three (73) year old male. V1 was traveling northbound in lane four, approaching stopped traffic. Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 Toyota highlander SUV driven by a sixty one (61) year old male, was stopped facing northbound ahead of V1 in lane four. Vehicle three (V3), a 2005 Toyota 4-Runner SUV driven by a forty three (43) year old male, was stopped facing northbound ahead of V2 in lane four. V1's driver became dizzy and the suffered some sort of medical blackout. The front of V1 impacted the rear of V2, pushing V2 forward into the rear of V3. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 and V3 were driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle three V3 was not involved in the first harmful event. A critical reason was not assigned to this vehicle.
