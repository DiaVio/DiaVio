The crash occurred in lane two of the southbound lane of a 4-lane undivided roadway. There were two southbound lanes and two northbound lanes. This was a level, right hand curved, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 64KMPH (40MPH). It was afternoon on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic was light and moving freely.
V1, a 1998 Honda Accord two door with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane two. V2, a 2001 Chevrolet Cavalier two door with two occupants, was stopped in lane two waiting to turn left in front of V1. V2, waiting for a break in traffic in lane two, was struck from behind by V1. V1 then pulled up along side V2 in lane one, looked over at the occupants of V2 and drove off. V2 came to rest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1 and 64 kmph for V2.
V1, the Honda Accord, was driven by a 24-year old male who reported no injuries. No interview could be obtained for V1. The driver related to police he was reaching for his cell phone, when he looked back to the road there was a car stopped in front of him.
V2, the Chevy Cavalier, was driven by a 19-year old female who originally reported no injuries but was taken to the hospital later in the day by private vehicle. She related she and her sister were returning home from dinner when the accident happened. She had nothing more to add.
