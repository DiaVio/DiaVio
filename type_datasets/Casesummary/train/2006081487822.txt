This rear-end type collision occurred during mid-afternoon daylight hours on a Friday. The collision occurred on a residential two-way, two-lane undivided roadway, in lane one when vehicle one left the roadway to the right and struck a parked car. The pre-crash roadway is level, and straight with bituminous paving. The weather was clear and the roadway was dry. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 40.22 Kmph (25mph).
Vehicle 1 (V1), a 1999 Toyota Rav 4, was traveling southbound in lane one. Vehicle one drifted off the roadway into the parking lane on the right and the front of V1 impacted the rear of parked vehicle 2. Vehicle one rotated counterclockwise and came to rest partially on the right sidewalk facing southeast. Vehicle 2 1986 Mercury Cougar was parked, and unoccupied lurched forward and came to rest facing southeast. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
