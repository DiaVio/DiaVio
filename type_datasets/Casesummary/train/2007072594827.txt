This two-vehicle crash occurred on a straight roadway in an interchange area. The north/south roadway consists of three lanes in each direction with center double solid yellow lines. North of the crash area, in each direction, the right (curb) lane is divided (by a concrete barrier) from the two overpass lanes. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Chevrolet Cavalier, was southbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a Volvo tractor towing one trailer, was entering the roadway, turning left from a driveway on the west side of the north/south roadway. The front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1 and No PAR obtained (created) for V2.
The male driver of V1 was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
The driver of V2, a thirty-year-old male, was leaving a gas station and was on his way to pick up a load. He was healthy, well rested and travels this route weekly. He has been driving a truck for six months. This driver stated that he looked left before he entered the roadway and never saw V1 prior to the crash.
