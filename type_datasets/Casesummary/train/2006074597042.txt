This single vehicle crash occurred on a five-lane divided roadway. There are two lanes in either direction and a left turn lane for northbound traffic. This road is straight and has a three percent slope for southbound traffic. The speed limit on the road is 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 1992 Buick Park Avenue was westbound and turned left at an intersection. Vehicle one was in the second southbound lane and the driver decided to change lanes to the first. Vehicle one then departed the roadway and impacted a large utility pole. After impact with the pole, Vehicle one rotated and rolled over one quarter turn on to its left side. Vehicle one came to rest in the first southbound lane on its left side. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
The driver of vehicle one, a forty-six year old male was driving from dropping a friend off to work at the time of the crash. This driver had been driving for about twelve minutes at the time of the crash & was in a hurry because he was late for a business appointment. This driver was tested for alcohol and tested positive at .254. This driver was taking Propranolol and Wellbutrin sr. This driver has been diagnosed with bipolar disease, microbiological avian complex and intermediate explosive behavior. This driver is near sighted and was not wearing lenses. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
