The crash occurred in the northbound lanes of an intersection. There were two northbound lanes and two southbound lanes. This was a straight, level, bituminous, roadway with a posted speed of 56 kmph (35 mph). It was morning on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was cloudy. Traffic was flowing.
V1, a 1998 Buick Park Avenue four-door with two occupants was traveling southbound in lane two. V1 was intending to turn left at the light. V2, a 1999 Jeep Cherokee with three occupants was traveling northbound in lane one. V2 was intending to go straight through the intersection. The driver of V1 stated that she had stopped at the red light behind a van. When the light turned green the van ahead of her turned left and then she turned left. She stated that she did not see V2 until she was in to her turn and did not have time to attempt any avoidance actions. The driver of V2 stated that she also did not see V1 until she was halfway through the intersection. The driver of V2 applied the brakes but could not avoid V1. The front of V1 contacted the front of V2. V1 spun counterclockwise and came to final rest facing north. V2 came to final rest in the northbound lanes of the intersection facing northeast. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, was driven by a 21-year old female. She reported injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. She stated that she had just left home and was on her way to drop off her son at his father's house. She stated that she had been traveling 2-16 kmph (1-10 mph) prior to being struck. She stated that she did not have time to react.
V2, was driven by a 38-year old female. She reported injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. She stated that she had just come from one school and was on her way to another school to drop of her daughter. She stated that she had been traveling between 50-64 kmph (31-40 mph) when she made the evasive action.
