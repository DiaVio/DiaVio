This single-vehicle crash occurred in the morning of a weekend on a 4-lane undivided roadway. The bituminous roadway runs north and south and is curved to the right near the location of the crash. The conditions were clear, dry, and daylight.
V1, a 1994 Toyota Camry LE with one occupant, was northbound in lane two when it crossed the double-yellow line and departed the northwest roadside. V1 struck a tree (26 cm in diameter) with its front and was towed due to damage. The driver was transported from the scene and hospitalized with serious injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1.
The 51-year-old male driver was not interviewed for this case but the police reported he was impaired with unknown medication or alcohol. A crash history report indicates he involved in eleven other crashes the past five years and was driving with a revoked license when he was involved in this crash.
