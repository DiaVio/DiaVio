The crash occurred during the evening rush hour in daylight on a two-way, six lane, divided roadway with a positive barrier. The road was dry, traveled asphalt with a positive grade in the direction of travel. The crash occurred just after a hill crest. The posted speed limit was 97 kmph (60 mph). Traffic was heavy at the time of the collision. There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Honda Civic driven by an 18 year-old male with two passengers. V1's driver is in good health, was well rested and not in a hurry. V1 was traveling southbound in the sixth travel lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Jeep Cherokee driven by a 59 year-old male with one passenger, was stopped southbound in the sixth lane. Vehicle three (V3), a 2000 Toyota Corolla driven by a 28 year-old female, was stopped in the southbound sixth lane. V3's driver was well rested and in good health. Vehicle four (V4), a 1994 Mercury Sable driven by a 35 year-old female, was stopped southbound in the sixth travel lane. The driver of V1 stated that prior to the collision he had looked to his right in an attempt to find his cell phone. When he looked back to the roadway, traffic had stopped. The front of V1 struck the rear of V2. V was forced forward and it's front struck the rear of V3. V3 was forced forward and it's front struck the rear of V4. All of the vehicles came to rest just forward of their POI. V1 was towed from the scene., all other vehicles were driven from the scene. No one was transported for injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
