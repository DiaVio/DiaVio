This single-vehicle crash occurred on a median-divided, straight trafficway. The eastbound lanes have an uphill grade and consist of two through lanes. There are paved shoulders on both sides of this roadway with a 25% downhill grass embankment on the right side. The speed limit for this roadway is 113 kmph (70 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 GMC Envoy, was eastbound in lane two. V1 traveled off the left side of the roadway. V1 then reentered the roadway, crossed both lanes and traveled off the right side of the roadway, rotating counterclockwise. V1 rolled over to the right four quarter-turns down the embankment and the right side struck a tree. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a sixty-three-year-old male, was on his way home from work. He was healthy, well rested and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route daily. This driver stated that he started to change lanes to the right and saw a vehicle in lane one. He steered left and traveled off the road. He then "jerked it back to my right and started fishtailing."
