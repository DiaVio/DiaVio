This two vehicle daytime collision occurred early evening. The collision occurred at an intersection comprised of a one-way eastbound roadway and a one-way northbound roadway controlled by a traffic light. The eastbound roadway has 2 lanes and the northbound roadway has four lanes. Both roadways were bituminous, level. dry, free of congestion, poor weather conditions, and/or road deficiencies at the time of the collision. Both roadways are designated as 48 kmph (30 mph) zones.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Honda Civic hatchback driven by a 23 year-old male, was traveling eastbound. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Ford Crown Victoria taxi driven by a 40 year-old male, was traveling northbound. As the vehicles entered the intersection the front of V2 impacted the right side of V1. V1 rotated clockwise and continued after impact in a northeast direction. V1 came to rest in the northeast corner of the intersection facing south. V2 rotated clockwise continuing after impact in an easterly direction. V2 came to rest in the eastern quadrant of the intersection facing southeast. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
