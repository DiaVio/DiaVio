This crash occurred in the westbound lane of an undivided straight two-lane asphalt roadway with a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55 mph). The crash occurred during the evening hours while dark out. There were no adverse conditions at the time of the crash.
V1, a 1989 Mazda MX-6 2-door coupe, was traveling westbound in the first lane. V2, a 1965 John Deere tractor, was also traveling westbound in the first lane, in front of V1. The front right of V1 struck the left rear of V2. V2 was forced off the road to the right and into the ditch where it came to rest on its right side. V1 came to final rest a short distance from point of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a 16-year-old male, stated he was on his way to a friend's house when he saw what he believed to be the lights of another vehicle approaching from the opposite lane. He stated he didn't realize the vehicle was a tractor that was in the same lane until just prior to impact.
