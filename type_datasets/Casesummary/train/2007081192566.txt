This single vehicle crash occurred during the evening hours. The location was an inter-city elevated freeway within an interchange area. The roadway was a four lane concrete divided interstate with paved shoulders concrete barriers on both sides. There was a slight curve to the left with a negative grade. Traffic was light, it was dark, clear and the road surface was dry. The posted speed limits is 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Infiniti FX35 SUV driven by a twenty five (25) year old male was traveling eastbound in the center (number three) lane at a high rate of speed. The driver stated he was going 137 kmph (85 mph). As V1 entered the curve it departed the travel lane to the left, then the driver steered right and over corrected. V1 returned to the original travel lane and traveled across all three lanes and the driver lost control of the vehicle and it begin to rotate clockwise as it traveled. V1 struck the concrete barrier on the right shoulder with the left rear corner. On impact the vehicle was redirected parallel to the barrier. V1 traveled backwards a short distance and stopped facing west on the south shoulder. The police report indicated a BAC test result of 0.11%. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
