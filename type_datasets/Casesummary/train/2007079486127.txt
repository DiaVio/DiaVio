This single vehicle crash occurred on a clear afternoon on a four lane divided, dry, concrete highway. The right side is bordered with an asphalt shoulder with curb and an embankment for an adjoining on-ramp. A wood/metal guardrail borders the left side. The roadway is designated 105 kmph (65 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1965 Ford Mustang 2-door coupe, driven by a eighteen (18) year old female was traveling north in the number one lane. V1's accelerator became stuck and the vehicle accelerated to approximately 129 kmph (80 mph). As the driver applied the brakes the vehicle started to fish tail, causing the driver to loose control. V1 started to rotate clockwise. The driver then steered to the right and departed the roadway. V1 traveled up the dirt embankment and then rolled over to the right. As it came to final rest V1 caught on fire in the engine compartment. V1 came to rest on its wheels facing southwest on the roadside. V1 was towed with rollover & engine compartment fire damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 129 kmph for V1.
