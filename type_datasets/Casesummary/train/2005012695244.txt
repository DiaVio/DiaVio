V1, a 1995 Pontiac Grand Am was traveling westbound on a 3-lane one-way dry concrete highway with a negative grade in daylight conditions. V1 was making many lane changes before losing control of the vehicle departing the roadway and contacting its front to a guardrail on the North side of the roadway.
The driver of V1 was an 18-year old male who had left the scene of the crash. The passenger had stated that the driver had been traveling 145-177kmph (90-110mph). It is unknown if he had any health related problems or had taken any medication prior to the crash, as he was not available for an interview. Police Reported Travel Speed is 145 kmph for V1.
