This crash occurred in a four-way intersection. Both the north/south and east/west roadway were undivided with one lane in each direction. Both roadways were brick and straight with a posted speed limit of 24 kmph (15 mph). The east/west roadway was level, while the north/south roadway had an uphill grade of 2.4 percent in the northbound direction. The intersection was controlled by stop signs in all directions. It was early afternoon on a weekday and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 2000 Lexus RX 300 utility vehicle driven by a 52 year-old female. V2 was a 2005 Honda Civic 3-door hatchback driven by a 30 year-old male. V1 was traveling eastbound in lane one approaching the intersection when it passed through without stopping for the stop sign. V2 was traveling northbound in lane one and had just entered the intersection after stopping for its stop sign. The front left of V2 struck the right front of V1. After impact, V1 traveled in a northeast direction where its front right wheel struck a curb. It came to rest against the curb. V2 came to final rest facing in the same direction on the right side of V1. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
