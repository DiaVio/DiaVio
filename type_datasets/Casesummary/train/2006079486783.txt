This two vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours. The collision occurred at a four-leg intersection. The southbound and northbound roadway is seven lanes, dry, level and has a left turn pocket. The eastbound and westbound roadway is five lanes, dry, level and has a left turn pocket. The intersection is controlled by a tri-light traffic signal that was working properly at the time. All four legs are comprised of asphalt. Both roadways are designated 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 Lexus LX 470 4-door SUV driven by a 37 year-old female, was traveling east in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Honda Civic 2-door coupe driven by a 70 year-old male, was traveling south in the number two lane. As V1 entered the intersection, its left plane was impacted by V2's front plane. V2 continued southeast rotating clockwise where it came to rest in the south crosswalk area in the number three lane facing west. V1 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southeast in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
