This single vehicle crash occurred on a six-lane divided expressway. This road is divided with a concrete barrier with three-lanes in either direction. The crash occurred within a construction work zone. The speed limit on the roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 2006 Chrysler 300 was southbound in the second lane. As vehicle one was southbound in its original lane of travel, an unknown object shattered the driver window. Vehicle one then departed the right lane line and impacted a concrete barrier. Vehicle one then rolled over two quarter turns coming to rest on its roof. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of vehicle one, a fifty-seven year old female was not interviewed. The only known pre-crash information is that the driver window shattered while in the work zone.
