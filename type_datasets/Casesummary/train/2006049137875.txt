This three-vehicle crash occurred on a jersey barrier-divided roadway. The westbound lanes curve to the right, have a downhill grade and consist of four through lanes. The speed limit on this roadway is 97 kmph (60 mph). Traffic was heavy at the time of the crash due to morning rush hour. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Chevrolet Prizm, was westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Nissan Maxima, was stopped westbound in lane one. Vehicle three (V3), a 1998 Honda Accord, was stopped westbound in lane one in front of V2. The front of V1 struck the back of V2. V2 moved forward and the front struck the back of V3. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was a twenty-six-year-old male. The driver of V2 was a forty-year-old female. The driver of V3 was a thirty-three-year-old female. All three drivers refused to be interviewed; no further information is available for these drivers.
