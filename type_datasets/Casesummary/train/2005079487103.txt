This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred on a straight stretch of a four-lane, two-way, undivided, dry, level, bituminous roadway. The speed limit in the area of the crash was 56 kmph (35 mph). The first lanes in both directions are bordered by parking lanes. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse atmospheric conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Honda Accord four-door sedan, was traveling eastbound in the second lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Mercedes-Benz SL600 two-door coupe, was parked along the south curb, ahead of V1. V2 entered traffic and executed a U-turn. As V2 entered the second lane, the front of V1 impacted the left side of V2. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the first lane. V2 deflected left and came to rest in the second westbound lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
