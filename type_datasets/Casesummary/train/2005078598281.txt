This two vehicle crash occurred mid-morning during daylight hours in a intersection with no traffic controls for north/south traffic. The roadway is divided with three northbound traffic lanes and four southbound traffic lanes at an intersection with access to a major shopping mall on the west. The roadway is straight, level, bituminous and a posted speed limit of 64 kmph (40 mph)
. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
Vehicle one (V1) a 1992 Mercury Sable 4 door sedan. driven by a 83 year-old male was traveling northbound in a designated left turn lane attempting to make a left turn into a shopping mall. Vehicle two (V2) a 2000 Ford Ranger XLT 4x2 pickup with a covered cargo area driven by a 77 year-old female with one passenger was traveling southbound and crossing through the intersection. V1 turned left in front of V2. The driver of V2 attempted to avoid a crash by steering right and braking with lock-up. The front of V1 struck the left front corner of V2. V1 spun counterclockwise and came to final rest facing generally northeast in the intersection. V2 came to a final rest facing generally southwest. V1 and V2 were towed due to damages.
