V1, a 1988 GMC Sierra pickup, was traveling southbound on a four-lane undivided wet asphalt roadway in daylight conditions. V1 was approaching a 4-way intersection intending to make a left-hand turn. V2 was a 1999 Ford Econoline van that was traveling northbound on the same roadway. V2 was approaching the intersection intending to go straight. V1 thought that the green light gave him the right of way and proceeded to make a left-hand turn just seconds before V2 entered the intersection. The front of V2 impacted the right front side of V1 before they both came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 72-year old male who reported that he had been traveling between 2-16kmph (1-10mph) prior to the crash. He had no health-related problems and had taken no medications prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling home. He does not wear corrective lenses. He was uninjured in the crash and refused treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 35-year old male who reported that he had been traveling between 50-64kmph (31-40mph) prior to the crash. He had no health-related problems and had taken no medications prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling to another job. He does not wear corrective lenses. He was uninjured in the crash and refused treatment.
