This two-vehicle crash occurred shortly past noon on a dry, four-leg, on-colors controlled intersection with pedestrian signals. Westbound travel lanes consists of a right turn lane, two through lanes and one left turn lane. Southbound travel lanes consists of an implied right turn lane and a through/left turn lane. The speed limit for east and westbound traffic is 72 kmph (45 mph). The speed limit for north and southbound traffic is 48 kmph (30 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions at the time.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Buick Regal sedan, driven by a 66 year-old female, was traveling westbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Chrysler Sebring coupe, driven by a 23 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling southbound in lane two making a left turn. Both vehicles entered the intersection at the same time. V1's front impacted the left side of V2. V1 rotated counter clockwise and came to rest on its wheels in the middle of the intersection facing south. V2 rotated counter clockwise and came to rest on its wheels facing southeast in the middle of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
