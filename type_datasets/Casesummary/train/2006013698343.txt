This crash occurred in the intersection of a two lane, two-way, non-divided rural roadway with a posted speed limit of 56Kmh (45Mph). The crash occurred during daylight hours, and there were no adverse conditions present at the time of the crash.
V1, a 2003 Oldsmobile Bravada was stopped in the eastbound right curb lane preparing to turn left. V2, a 1998 Buick Regal was traveling westbound in the right curb lane approaching the intersection. V1 turned in front of V2, and was contacted in the left front by the front of V1. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 a 66 year-old female stated that she made a complete stop at the intersection, and then proceeded to turn left. She then stated that she did not remember seeing V2 at all.
The driver of V2 a 51 year-old male driver stated that he saw that V1 was stopped. The driver then stated that by the time he realized that there was going to be a crash he did not have time to react.
