This two-vehicle collision occurred just past noon. The weather was clear at the time of the collision. The collision occurred on a five lane, undivided, dry, level asphalt roadway with a two-way left turn lane. The roadway is designated 56 kmph (35 mph). A private driveway exists on the west curb. The driveway is one lane, dry, level and made of asphalt.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1998 Toyota Camry 4-door sedan, driven by a forty five (45) year old female. V1 was making a left turn from a private driveway. Vehicle two (V2) is a 1987 Dodge Ram Charger sport utility vehicle, driven by a nineteen (19) year old male was traveling south in the number two lane. Vehicle three (V3) is a 2003 Toyota Camry 4-door sedan that was legally parked on the west curb. Vehicle four (V4) is a 1996 Dodge Stratus that was also legally parked on the west curb just south of V3. As V1 made its left turn its left plane was impacted by V2's front plane. V2 continued southeast where it came to rest in the northbound lanes, facing southeast. V1 continued southwest impacting V3's left plane with its hood, which had become dislodged and was hanging off the right side. V1 then continued southwest impacting V4's left side with its front plane. V1 came to rest facing southwest at impact with V4. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
