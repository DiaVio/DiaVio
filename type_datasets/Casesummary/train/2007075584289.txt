This single-vehicle crash occurred on a straight, level, undivided roadway. This roadway consists of one lane in each direction with paved shoulders on both sides. The speed limit on this roadway is 89 kmph (89 mph). It was dark at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Escape, was northbound in lane one. The front of V1 struck an elk that was in the roadway. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-six-year-old male, was on his way from the pharmacy where he works to a nursing home in another city. He was healthy and familiar with his vehicle. This driver works two jobs but was well rested at the time of the crash. He travels this route several times each week. He stated that he applied the brakes when he saw the elk, but was unable to avoid the crash.
