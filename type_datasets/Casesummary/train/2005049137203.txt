This two-vehicle crash occurred at mid-afternoon on a four lane freeway in the northbound lanes, road surface is concrete,dry and free of defects. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time. The posted speed limit is 96 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Dodge Intrepid sedan driven by a 25 year-old male was traveling northbound in the second lane. Vehicle two ( V2), a 1997 Lincoln Towncar sedan driven by a 61 year-old female with one passenger was traveling northbound in the third lane. As V1 attempted to change lanes to the left into the third lane it's front impacted the rear of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in the roadway at the point of impact. V1 was towed from the scene due to damage sustained in the collision. The driver of V1 was well rested and familiar with the area. He stated that he did not notice that traffic in the adjacent lane had stopped. The driver applied his brakes but was unable to stop prior to colliding with V2. The driver was cited for failure to maintain financial responsibility and no driver's license. The driver of V2 was well rested and unfamiliar with the area. She stated that traffic had stopped, but could not remember if she had come to a complete stop prior to impact. She stated that she did not see V1 prior to impact and thus took no evasive action. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
