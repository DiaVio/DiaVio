V1, a 2000 Chevrolet Prism 4-door, was southbound in lane 2 of a 4-lane undivided bituminous roadway (2 lanes each direction). V2, a 1988 Ford Ranger pickup, was stopped on the same roadway in the same lane waiting to turn left, facing southbound. At the time of the early evening crash, it was daylight and there were no adverse weather conditions. The roadway was dry and straight. The crash occurred in the vicinity of a sag in the roadway. The posted speed limit was 72 kmph (45 mph). The front of V1 contacted the back of V2. A passenger in V2 was injured and transported. V1 was towed due to damage.
