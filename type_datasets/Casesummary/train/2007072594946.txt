This single-vehicle crash occurred on a jersey barrier-divided trafficway. The northbound roadway is level, curves to the left and consists of four through lanes with narrow paved shoulders on both sides. The speed limit for this roadway is 64 kmph (40 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours and it was raining.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Ford Contour, was northbound in lane four. V1 began to rotate counterclockwise and traveled to the left. The front of V1 struck the jersey barrier. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
The driver of V1, a female, was on her way to work from her home. She was healthy and well rested. This driver attempted to avoid the crash by steering right and applying the brakes. This driver provided no further information.
