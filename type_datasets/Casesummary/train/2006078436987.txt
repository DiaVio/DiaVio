This two vehicle crash occurred early evening during daylight hours in the westbound lane of a two-lane roadway that is divided by painted centerline stripe. There is one westbound and one eastbound lane. This was a straight, level, bituminous, roadway with a posted speed limit of 64 KPH (40 MPH). the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic flow was moderate at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one(V-1), a 1991 Honda Accord 4dr Wagon driven by a 22 year-old female was traveling westbound at about 70 kph (44 MPH). Vehicle two (V-2), an unknown year Ford Focus, was passing V-1 in the eastbound lane just prior to the impact. V-2 entered the westbound lane and stopped for other vehicles in front of it that were also stopped. These vehicles were apparently stopped to make a left turn into an apartment complex. V-1 skidded for approximately 29 meters (95 feet) before it struck the rear of V-2. After the impact, V-2 left then scene traveling westbound on the roadway. V-1 came to rest on the north shoulder of the westbound lane. The driver of V 1 advised that she was traveling westbound at about 70 kph (40 MPH) and decelerating when V-2 passed her on the left. V-2 then entered the westbound lane and stopped suddenly. She locked up her brakes and skidded before striking the rear of V-2. V-2 left the scene after the crash. The only description that the driver could give of the other vehicle involved was it was possibly a silver Ford Focus. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
