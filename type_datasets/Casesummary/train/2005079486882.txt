This single-vehicle crash occurred on a five-lane undivided, dry, level, roadway comprised of asphalt. The roadway has a speed limit of 56 kph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours.
Vehicle one, a 2003 Ford E350 cab chassis van with a box on the back was southbound in the first lane. Vehicle one departed the roadway and the right front wheel impacted the curb. Vehicle one then climbed up on the curb and impacted a pole with the front plane. Vehicle one continued right and the front impacted a block wall and the pole landed on the roof. Vehicle one came to rest off the roadway. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of vehicle one, a twenty-seven year old male was heading from one delivery to a second delivery. This driver gave a partial interview and revealed that he was reaching down on the passenger side floorboard to get his clipboard when he veered to the right. He said he did not realize he had moved the wheel to the right. This driver drives this vehicle daily but it is unknown how often he drives over this roadway.
