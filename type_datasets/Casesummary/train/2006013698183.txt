This crash occurred in the northbound lane of a five lane; two-way, non-divided roadway. There were two northbound and two southbound lanes with a center left turn lane for both directions. This was a straight, level, asphalt roadway with a posted speed limit of 56Kmh (35Mph). The crash occurred during daylight hours and there was no adverse conditions present at the time of the crash.
V1 a 2002 Chevy Cavalier was facing south in the center turn lane stopped awaiting to make a left hand turn into a private parking lot. V2 a 1988 Buick LeSabre was traveling northbound in the right curb lane. V1 proceeded to make her left turn and V1' right front came in contact with V2's front in the right curb lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, a 2002 Chevy Cavalier was driven by a 78yr old female who stated she was turning into the parking lot and looked to see if there were any cars coming and did not see any. She started to make her turn and turned right in front of the oncoming car and was hit.
V2, a 1988 Buick LeSabre was driven by a 17yr old female who stated she was going to the store and a car turned right in front of her, she did not have time to react.
