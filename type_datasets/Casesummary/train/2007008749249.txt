This crash occurred at a four-way intersection. Each roadway was two-lanes and undivided. The north/south roadway curved to the left, had a slight downhill grade, and had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). The east/west roadway was straight, had a downhill grade, and had a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). Both roadways were bituminous. It was early evening on a weekday and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 2002 Ford Expedition, driven by an 18 year-old female. V2 was a 2006 Hummer H3 driven by a 37 year-old female. V1 was traveling westbound and stopped at the stop sign to the intersection. The vehicle pulled into the intersection and impacted V2, which was traveling southbound. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
