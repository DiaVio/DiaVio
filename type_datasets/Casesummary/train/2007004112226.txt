This crash occurred just off the roadway of a north/south divided 5-lane roadway (three lanes southbound and 2 lanes northbound) just before a 3-way controlled intersection. The southbound approach had 2 lanes continuing south and one lane for right turn-only. This roadway was divided by a concrete median and had a posted speed limit of 80 kmph (50 mph). The roadway was level, straight, and bituminous roadway. The crash occurred in the middle of the afternoon on a weekday. The road was dry and the skies were clear. The roadway was slightly congested with moderate stop-and-go traffic activity.
V1, a 1998 Kia Sportage Sport Utility Vehicle with one occupant, was traveling south in lane one (a right turn-only lane) intending on making a right at the intersection. Just prior to the intersection, V1 drove off the roadway on the right side and the right front corner of the vehicle struck a utility pole. The vehicle came to final rest close to its point of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
