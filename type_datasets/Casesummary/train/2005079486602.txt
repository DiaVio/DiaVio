This two-vehicle crash occurred during daytime hours on a weekday. The crash occurred at the four-leg intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-west roadway. The intersection was controlled by a tri-light traffic signal. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse atmospheric conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet Cobalt two-door, was traveling southbound in the third lane of a seven-lane, two-way, undivided, dry, level, bituminous roadway, approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1986 Mercedes-Benz 560SEL four-door sedan, was stopped at the intersection ahead of V1 in the same lane. The front of V1 impacted the back of V2. Both vehicles moved ahead a short distance and came to rest in their original travel lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
