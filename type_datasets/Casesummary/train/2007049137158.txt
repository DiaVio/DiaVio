This two-vehicle crash occurred on a north/south, two lane, undivided, straight, level roadway. The roadway intersects an east/west, two lane, undivided, straight, level roadway. The speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred at night with light traffic. The residential roadway is illuminated at night by overhead lights. There were no adverse weather conditions at the time of the crash. Traffic at the intersection is controlled by stop signs.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Nissan Sentra with one occupant, was northbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Chevrolet Silverado with three occupants, was stopped at a stop sign northbound in lane one ahead of V1. V1 failed to stop behind V2 and the front of V1 struck the back of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-one year-old male, refused to be interviewed at the scene. The police report indicates the driver was legally intoxicated from an administered breathalyzer test.
The driver of V2, a twenty-eight year-old male, traveling with a thirty-three year-old male and a twenty-eight year-old male, was not interviewed.
