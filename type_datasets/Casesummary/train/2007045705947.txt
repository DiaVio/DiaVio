The crash occurred on a secondary arterial road that ran east/west in a rural area. The road had 2 lanes (1 each direction) that were divided by a double yellow line. The road was straight and level with a speed limit of 80kph (50mph). The north edge of the road was bordered by shrubs at a drop off with no shoulder and a few trees also. The crash occurred in the early evening hours on a weekday with no adverse weather conditions.
