This crash occurred in the southbound lane of a five-lane roadway with a center turn lane. There were two southbound lanes, two northbound lanes and the center lane was a left turn lane for both directions. The roadway was bituminous, level and had a right curve in the southbound direction. The posted speed was 72 kmph (45 mph). It was mid-afternoon on a weekday and the road was dry with no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle 1, a 2005 Chevrolet Blazer, was traveling north in lane two of the roadway. Vehicle 2, a 2001 Jeep Grand Cherokee, was traveling south in the left turn lane waiting to make a left turn into the parking lot. Vehicle 3, a 2005 Dodge Magnum, was traveling north in lane two of the roadway. As V2 made her left turn V1 swerved to the left to try to avoid hitting V2, however V1's front contacted V2's right back. V1 then continued heading left into V3's lane and V1's front contacted V3's front. V1 and V3 came to final rest in lane two of the northbound lane. V2 came to final rest in lane one of the southbound lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was a 21-year old male. The driver stated that he saw V2 turning from the center lane into the parking lot. He claimed that she turned to slow into the parking lot so he couldn't avoid hitting her. V1 swerved over the center lane and into lane two in the oncoming traffic, thus striking V3.
The driver of V2 was a 68-year old female. The driver of V2 stated that she saw V1 driving at a fast rate of speed but never thought he would have hit her even though she knows he saw her trying to turn into the lot.
The driver of V3 was a 51-year old male. The driver of V3 stated that he was on his way to the store to get a drink. He was traveling in lane two when V1 swerved to avoid hitting V2. V1 struck V2 in the right rear corner then struck V3 in the front in lane two of the northbound lane.
