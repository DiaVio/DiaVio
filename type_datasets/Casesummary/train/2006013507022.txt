This crash occurred in an intersection of an undivided two-lane roadway and an undivided four-lane roadway with a center turn lane. The two-lane roadway (eastbound) ended at the intersection and was controlled by a stop sign. The four-lane roadway (north/south) was uncontrolled. Both roadways were slightly curved, but both were level. North of the intersection the roadway had a downhill slope of 2 percent. The posted speed limit on both roadways was 56 kmph (35 mph).
V1 was a 1991 Buick Skylark traveling east, approaching the stop sign. The driver of V1, a 34-year old female, stated that she was intending to turn left at the intersection and had stopped for a couple of seconds before proceeding into the intersection. She stated that when she stopped she looked both ways and was sure the road was clear. When she entered the intersection her vehicle was struck on the left rear side by the front of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V2 was a 1997 Chevrolet Blazer driven by a 27-year old male. The driver of V2 was headed south, intending to continue straight through the intersection. As he entered the intersection, V1 pulled into his path. He locked up his brakes but was unable to avoid striking V1. The driver of V2 stated that there was a vehicle in the right turning lane, and that he was unable to see V1. The driver also stated that he was traveling about 15 mph above the posted speed limit.
