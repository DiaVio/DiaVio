This crash occurred off of the roadway on the right, on a two-lane, undivided highway. There was one northbound lane and one southbound lane. The roadway had an uphill grade, curved to the right, was bituminous and had a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). It was afternoon on a weekday and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 1991 Dodge Dakota, driven by an 18 year-old male. V1 was traveling southbound in lane one, driving straight while the road curved to the right when the vehicle continued off of the road to the left. Upon leaving the roadway, V1 impacted a gas meter and a utility pole where it came to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1.
