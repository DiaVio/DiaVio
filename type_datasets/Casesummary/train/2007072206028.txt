This single-vehicle crash occurred on a divided, straight, level trafficway. The eastbound lanes consist of three through lanes with paved shoulders on both sides. The median is grass with a jersey barrier next to the westbound lanes. The speed limit on this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Honda Prelude, was eastbound in lane two. A non-contact vehicle was eastbound in lane one. The driver of V1 attempted to change lanes to the right and abruptly steered left when he realized that the non-contact vehicle was in lane one. V1 rotated counterclockwise and traveled into the median. The front of V1 struck the jersey barrier. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-eight-year-old male, was on his way back into the city from his friend's house. He was healthy, well rested and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route weekly. This driver stated that he was thinking about his financial problems at the time of the crash. He also stated that he was speeding.
