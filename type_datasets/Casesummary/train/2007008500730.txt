This crash occurred in the westbound lane of a two-lane roadway. There was one westbound lane and one eastbound lane. The roadway curved slightly to the left, was level, bituminous, and had a posted speed limit of 56kmph (35 mph). It was early morning on a weekday and the roadway was covered with black ice.
V1 was a 2006 Pontiac Grand Prix, driven by a 40 year-old female who reported minor injuries. V1 was traveling westbound beginning to negotiate a left curve, when it lost traction on black ice. The vehicle went off of the roadway to the right and struck a utility pole where it came to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
