The crash occurred in the intersection of two roadways. The east/west roadway was undivided with one lane in each direction. The southbound roadway was one-way with one lane of travel. The east/west roadway was a straight, level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). It was the evening hours of a weekday, and it dark out but the roadway was illuminated by streetlamps. There were no adverse conditions.
V1, a 2007 Nissan Sentra four door sedan with one occupant, was traveling westbound in lane one and attempting to make a left turn at the intersection. V2, a 1997 Mercury Grand Marquis four door sedan with two occupants, was traveling westbound in lane one behind V1. At the intersection, V2 attempted to make a left turn in front of V1. V1 came to a final rest in the southeast corner of the intersection. V2 came to a final rest on the southwest curb. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Nissan Sentra, was driven by a 24 year-old male who reported minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. He stated to the police that he was traveling westbound and while he was making a left turn V2 also attempted to make a left turn.
V2, the Mercury Grand Marquis, was driven by a 32 year-old male who reported minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. A male occupant in V2 reported no injuries.
