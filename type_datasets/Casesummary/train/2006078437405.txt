This is a single vehicle ran off the right side of the roadway type crash that occurred on a five lane level, dry, asphalt roadway. The roadway where the crash occurred consists of two westbound and two eastbound lanes separated by a continuous left turn lane. The crash occurred at approximately 1500 hours; there were no roadway deficiencies and no sight line restrictions noted. There were no adverse weather conditions and the weather was clear. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 88 kmph (55 MPH).
V-1, a 1982 Chevrolet Suburban, driven by a 26 year-old male was traveling eastbound in the number one lane when the vehicle departed the right side of the roadway while passing through an intersection and struck a metal light pole off the southeast corner of the intersection. After impact, the vehicle came to rest near the southeast corner of the intersection facing in a southeasterly direction. driver stated that he last remembers traveling eastbound through the intersection approximately 1 mile west of where the crash occurred. The driver stated that he does not remember getting into a crash. He also stated that he has a history of seizures and this is the first time he knows of having a seizure while he was driving. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1.
