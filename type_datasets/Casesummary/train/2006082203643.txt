This five-vehicle crash occurred near a traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is level, straight and consists of two lanes in each direction with a center left-turn lane at intersections. The east/west roadway is level, straight and consists of two lanes in each direction. The speed limit on these roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours and it was raining.
Vehicle one (V1) was a hit-and-run vehicle. No information is available for this vehicle. Vehicle two (V2), a 2006 Chevrolet Malibu, was eastbound in lane one and was turning right (south) at the intersection. Vehicle three (V3), a 2004 Ford Escape, was northbound in lane two, approaching the intersection. Vehicle four (V4), a 2005 Chevrolet Equinox, was stopped southbound in lane three, south of the intersection. Vehicle five (V5), a 2002 Hyundai Sonata, was northbound in lane two behind V3. After V2 turned right at the intersection it traveled across the left lane line into the number two travel lane and the back of V2 was struck by V1. The driver of V2 then lost control, and the front of V2 struck the left side of V3. the left side of V4 and the front of V5. V2, V4 and V5 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
No information is available for the driver of V1 since he fled the scene in his vehicle.
The driver of V2, a twenty-four-year-old female, was traveling from her home to work. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle. This was her first time on this roadway. This driver stated that she did not see V1 prior to making her right turn and then noticed V1 behind her in her rear view mirror just prior to impact.
The driver of V3, a thirty-year-old male, refused to be interviewed. No data is available on this driver.
