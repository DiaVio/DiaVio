This two-vehicle crash occurred at the intersection of two two-lane roadways. Both roadways are straight and level with no defects. The east west road is controlled by a stop sign. Both roadways have a speed limit of 40 kph (25 mph). This crash occurred during nighttime hours with additional street lighting. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one, a 1998 Dodge Stratus was westbound in lane one. Vehicle two, a 1998 Subaru was northbound in lane one. Vehicle one crossed through the intersection and was impacted on its left side by vehicle two with its front plane. Vehicle one went off to the northwest and impacted a curb with its front right wheel. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, thirty-one year old female was heading from the store back home and had been driving on this portion of her trip for about twelve minutes at the time of the crash. This driver stated that she did not see the stop sign and took no avoidance actions to avoid the crash. It is unknown how often this driver drives this vehicle or over this roadway.
The driver of vehicle two, a thirty-six year old female was heading to a friends house from the supermarket. This driver had been driving for about two minutes at the time of the crash. This driver stated that she had slowed slightly for a dip in the road at the intersection. This driver was eating at the time of the crash; she had a sandwich in one hand and a drink in her lap. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
