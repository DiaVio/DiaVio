This collision occurred at the intersection of a two lane, two way, undivided concrete roadway and a three lane, two way, undivided asphalt roadway. Both roads were dry, traveled and level. The posted speed limit was 48 kmph (30 mph). Traffic was light at the time of the collision. There were no adverse weather conditions. The crash occurred just before noon on a sunny, clear day.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1987 GMC Sierra southbound in the first lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1998 Chevrolet Malibu negotiating a left curve northwest bound in the first lane. V1 slowed for the stop sign and then proceeded through without the right of way and it's front struck the front of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing east. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing east. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
