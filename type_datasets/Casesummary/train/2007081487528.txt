This three-vehicle collision occurred around dusk in the early evening hours The collision occurred on an interstate highway. The roadway is a ten-lane divided highway with five travel lanes in each direction. The pre-crash roadway is straight and a slight downhill grade with concrete paving. The weather was clear and the roadway was dry. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 97 kmph (60 mph). Access to the roadway is controlled.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 BMW 330i sedan driven by a twenty three year old female was headed north in lane four. Vehicle two (V2) a 2000 Audi A6 Quattro sedan driven by a thirty six year old male was ahead of V1 in lane four. Vehicle three (V3) a 2005 Honda Civic coupe driven by a twenty one year old female was traveling northbound in lane three. There was a non-contact vehicle ahead of V2 that slowed for a weaving non-contact van. When the vehicle ahead of V2 slowed rapidly, the driver of V2 avoided hitting the rear of that vehicle by steering left into lane five. The driver of V2 immediately steered back into lane four, as he was slowing rapidly. The front right of V1 hit the left rear of V2, causing V2 to rotate clockwise, and the front right corner of V2 impacted the left side of V3. V3 lost control, and rotated counterclockwise across lane four and lane five and came to rest facing southwest on the left shoulder. V2 and V3 were both towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
