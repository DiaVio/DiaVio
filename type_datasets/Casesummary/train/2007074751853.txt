This two vehicle crash occurred in the early morning of a snowy, foggy day in darkness. The roadway was a 3-lane, one-way, wet asphalt roadway with a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph) and a grade of 5%. At the time of the crash a thick fog had set in, along with light snow.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Chevrolet Cavalier 2 door coupe driven by a thirty eight (38) year old female, was traveling east in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Ford Explorer SUV driven by a thirty three (33) year old male, was traveling east in lane two. As both vehicles approached the intersection, V2 turned left from the second lane into the path of V1. The front of V1 contacted the left side of V2. After impact V1 came to final rest in the intersection, V2 came to a controlled stop on the east side of the northbound lanes. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
