This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekend afternoon. The crash occurred at the four-leg intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-west roadway. The intersection is controlled by a tri-light traffic signal, and the speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph) in all directions. Both roadways are five-lane, two-way, straight, level, bituminous roads. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the road was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Honda Civic 4-door sedan, was traveling northbound in the third (left turn only) lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of turning left into westbound traffic. Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 Nissan Altima 4-door sedan, was traveling southbound in the first lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. As V1 turned, the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V2 stopped at impact, and V1 continued forward, scraping its right side along V2's front. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
