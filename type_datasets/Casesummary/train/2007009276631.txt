The crash occurred in the intersection of a multi-lane east/west highway and an undivided north/south roadway. There were 4 eastbound lanes and 4 westbound lanes divided by a grass median. This was a level, bituminous roadway with a slight curve to the left in the westbound direction. It had a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55 mph). It was the late evening on a weekday. The roads were dry and the sky was clear. Traffic flow was moderate.
V1, a 1988 Nissan Pathfinder utility vehicle with one occupant, was traveling westbound in lane 4 of the highway, with the intention of turning left at the intersection. V2, a 1996 Chevrolet Tahoe utility vehicle with one occupant, was traveling eastbound in lane 3 intending to continue straight. V1 entered the intersection on a red signal while V2 was entering the intersection on a green light. The front of V2 struck the right side of V1. After impact, V1 rotated approximately 170 degrees clockwise and struck an embankment located on the southeast corner of the intersection. V1 came to rest on the embankment facing northeast. After impact, V2 spun clockwise about 45 degrees and came to final rest in the middle of the intersection facing south. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
