***Case Note: Driver of V2 refused interview on scene and during follow-up. No V2 interview. V2 PAF completed without V2 interview information.
The crash occurred in the center of a four-way intersection. The east/westbound roadway consisted of one eastbound and one westbound lane. The north/southbound roadway consisted of one northbound and one southbound lane. Both roadways were straight, level bituminous with a posted speed of 40 kmph (25 mph). It was early afternoon on a weekday. The roadways were dry and the sky was clear. There was no other traffic at the time. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, a 2004 Lexus GX470 SUV with one occupant, was traveling eastbound in lane one. V2, a 1998 Ford Explorer with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane one. V1 entered the intersection after failing to stop for a posted Stop sign and was struck on the driver's side by V2. V2 pushed V1 laterally until it struck the curb at the southeast corner of the intersect, at which time V1 rolled-over 2 quarter turns before coming to rest.
V1, the 2004 Lexus GX470 SUV, was driven by a 17-year-old male who reported no injuries, but was transported to the emergency room. He stated that he had just dropped his daughter off at the park and was on his way to a friend's house. The driver stated that this had been his first time on this road and failed to observer the posted Stop sign before entering the intersection.
V2, the 1998 Ford Explorer, was driven by a 33-year-old male who reported no injuries. V2 was southbound in lane one when it entered the intersection and struck V1, pushing it laterally until it struck a curb and rolled-over.
