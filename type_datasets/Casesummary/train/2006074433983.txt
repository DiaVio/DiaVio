This intersection related rear-end collision between three vehicles occurred during daylight hours with no adverse weather conditions. Traffic signal device located approximately 136-meters (446-feet) ahead of collision site controlled traffic flow. Roadway is two-way, divided, with two lanes of traffic in each direction. The road is straight, level, bituminous, with a posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph).
Vehicle three (V3), a 2005 Subaru Forester SUV occupied by a driver only, was traveling east in lane two with the intention of turning left at the intersection. Traffic was stopped for the traffic signal so driver had stopped behind non-contact vehicles. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Mazda MX6 2-door coupe with a driver only was traveling east (stopped) in lane two with the intention of going straight. V2 was stopped behind V3. Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Mercury Topaz 2-door coupe occupied by a driver only, was traveling east in lane two with the intention of going straight. The front of V1 impacted the back of V2 that caused V2 to move forward and strike the rear of V3. All three vehicles came to rest in lane two. V1 and V2 were towed from the scene: V3 was driven. None of the occupants were transported. The driver of V1 received a non-incapacitating injury but was not transported. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
