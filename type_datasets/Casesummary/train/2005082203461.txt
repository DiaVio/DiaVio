This two vehicle daytime collision occurred early afternoon daylight hours. The collision occurred in a controlled intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-west roadway. The pre-crash roadway for north/south travel is level worn concrete/blacktop paving. The east/west roadway is divided by parking under the bridge, a positive barrier divides the north/south roadway. There is a post speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). Signs were posted showing no left turn. It was raining and the roadways were wet.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Volkswagen Jetta driven by a 32 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling northbound on the roadway in lane 2. The driver of V1 was making an illegal left turn onto the westbound roadway. After the left turn V1 proceeded straight across an intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1990 Subaru Legacy driven by a 35 year-old female, was traveling southbound in lane 2. Both vehicles approached the intersection at roughly the same time, and the front of V2 collided with the right side of V1. V1 rotated clockwise coming to rest facing north. V2 came to rest in travel lane facing north. Both drivers of V1 and V2 stated that they didn't see each other until the crash was imminent. Both vehicles had sight line restrictions from the construction of the over head bridge. V1 also was making an illegal left had turn the signs were posted clearly. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
