This crash occurred during the morning hours of a weekday, on a four lane undivided roadway. The east/west roadway consisted of two lanes in each direction with a posted speed limit of 56kmph (35 mph). The bituminous roadway was level and featured an S-type curve. The traffic flow was normal, but the roadway was wet at the time of the crash.
V1, a 2004 Toyota Sienna, was eastbound in lane two negotiating the curve. V2, an unknown light truck, was also eastbound in lane two, just ahead of V1. As V1 continued negotiating the curve, its front struck the rear of V2. V1 came to final rest near the point of impact while V2 fled the scene.
