The accident occurred at 1220 in a two-way intersection and undivided roadways. Both roads were dry, traveled, level concrete. East, west traffic controlled by stop sign. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (56 kph). There was no other traffic on the roadway with the exception of V 1, V 2 There were no adverse weather conditions.
V 1 a 2005 Honda Accord driven by a 35 year-old female, stopped at a stop sign westbound in the first lane. V 2 a 2002 Lexus GS300 driven by a 54 year-old male approaching the intersection southbound in the first lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and it's front struck the left side of V 2. V 1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing southwest. V 2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing southwest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
