This crash occurred on a dark unlit section of a two-lane, curved, rural road on a weekend night. The roadway had a posted speed limit of 56kmph (35mph) and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions that evening. As V1, a 2002 Chevrolet Venture negotiated the curve traveling northbound the vehicle began drifting over the centerline into V2, a 2006 Honda Pilot, lane of travel. The front left of V1struck the left side of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage.
V1 was driven by a 69 year-old male who could not be interviewed on scene. The investigating officer stated that the driver had failed a field sobriety test and was being transported for a blood draw. Subsequent attempts to contact the driver were unsuccessful. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V2 was driven by a 36 year-old male in good health and wearing his prescription lenses that corrected a hyperopic (far-sighted) condition. He stated he saw V2 cross the centerline and braked, but could not avoid hitting the other vehicle. He was uninjured in the crash.
