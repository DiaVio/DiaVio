This crash occurred in the afternoon of a weekend at an intersection of a southbound one-way roadway and a two-way east-west roadway. The bituminous roadway was straight and level, and it was dusk and snowing causing the roads to be wet. The posted speed limit was 48kmph (30mph).
V1, a 1997 Jeep Wrangler, was southbound in lane three preparing to make a left turn at the intersection. As V1 commenced its turn the driver lost control and the front of V1 impacted a light pole that was located off the roadway at the southeast corner. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1 was driven by a 20 year-old female who sustained non-incapacitating injuries and was treated on-scene. The driver stated that her brakes failed causing her to hit the light pole. The driver also stated that the brakes were always a problem with her vehicle. V1 was towed due to damage.
