This two-vehicle collision occurred at a five-way intersection. The crash occurred on the four-lane through road. This road is straight and level with no defects. The intersection is controlled by a traffic signal. The road has a speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). No adverse weather conditions present at the time of collision. The roadway was dark, but lit.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Chevrolet Cavalier was traveling south in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Dodge Stratus was traveling north in the third lane with the intention of turning left. V2 turned left at the intersection and impacted with V1 front to front. Both vehicles came to rest in the area of impact. Both vehicles were towed due damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a twenty-year old female was on her way to a dinner party at the time of the crash. This driver had been driving for about ten minutes at the time of the crash. This driver stated that she had just passed through a previous intersection and approached this intersection and the light was red. The driver stopped for about one or two seconds before the light changed. Then she proceeded through the intersection. This driver watched a non-contact vehicle turn left in front of her but did not see the vehicle she hit. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis. This vehicle has low tread depths on all tires.
The driver of V2, an eighty-five year old male was returning home from a restaurant. This driver had been driving for about ten minutes at the time of the crash. This driver indicated that he watched and followed the white car ahead of him through the intersection. This driver was taking Lipitor and Lopressor as medications. This driver indicated that he was talking to his wife who was in the car with him at the time of the crash. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
