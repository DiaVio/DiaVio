This single vehicle crash occurred on a two-lane (four overall), east-west roadway divided by a concrete median intersection controlled by tri-color traffic signal. The roadway was level, concrete with a right curve and a posted speed of 56 kmph (35 mph). The crash occurred early evening during dark hours and the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic flow was moderate.
Vehicle one (V 1), a 2002 Volkswagen Passat sedan driven by a 26 year-old male with one passenger was traveling eastbound in lane one. V 1 had just completed a right turn when the vehicle departed the roadway on the right side and the front of the vehicle struck a fence post. V 1 came to rest at the point of impact. The driver stated that he was on his way home from a nearby bar. He advised that he was traveling at between 16-32 kmph (10-20 mph) when he departed the roadway. He made no evasive maneuvers. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
