This three-vehicle crash occurred a t 13:28 hours on a clear day. The roadway runs east to west and is four-lane with a -2/122cm slope. This road has a 64 KPH (40 MPH) speed limit. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 1995 Toyota Camry, was westbound in lane two. Vehicle two, a 2005 Honda Accord, was westbound in lane one. Vehicle three, a 1990 Ford Tempo, was legally parked in a parking area north of the roadway. Vehicle one-changed lanes to the right and the front of vehicle one impacted the left of vehicle two. Vehicle two departed the roadway and impacted vehicle three with its front plane. Vehicles one and two were towed due to damage. Vehicle three was not towed. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a twenty-two year old female, was on the way to a dentist appointment. This driver stated that she looked in her side mirror and then changed lanes. When she looked in the mirror she did not see vehicle two. This driver stated that she typically also turns her head when she changes lanes but did not do so this time. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road daily.
The driver of vehicle two, a thirty-one year old female, was not interviewed.
