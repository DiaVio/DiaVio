This two-vehicle crash occurred at 1245 hours on a clear day in heavy traffic. This crash occurred on a seven-lane divided asphalt roadway. This roadway is divided with a concrete barrier and has four lanes in the southbound direction. This roadway is straight and level with no defects. The speed limit on the roadway is 75 kph (50 mph). At the location of the crash, the roadway is straight and level. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 2003 Honda Accord was southbound in lane three. Vehicle two, a 2004 Chevrolet Impala was southbound in lane four stopped in traffic. Vehicle one was changed lanes from three to four. The front of vehicle three impacted the rear of vehicle two. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a nineteen-year-old female had been on this trip for about fifteen minutes. The driver indicated that as she made the lane change maneuver that she looked behind her with the side mirror to make sure that traffic was clear. This driver did not see the stopped traffic ahead in lane four. This driver drives in this vehicle daily and over this roadway daily.
The driver of vehicle two, a thirty-four year old male had been driving on this portion of his trip for about fifteen minutes. This driver was stopped for intersection traffic ahead. There were three occupants in the vehicle, two in child seats in the second row. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road daily.
