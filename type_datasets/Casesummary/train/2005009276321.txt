This crash occurred in the evening hours of weekday on a divided 3-lane highway. The dry and level roadway featured a slight curve to the left and had a posted speed of 89kmph (55mph). The atmospheric conditions were clear and the roadway was dark but illuminated by streetlights.
V1, a 2000 Ford Mustang, was traveling north in lane three. As V1 continued negotiating the curve a non-contact vehicle began encroaching into V1 lane from its right. The other vehicle cut him off and forced him off the road into the guardrail into, the vehicle spun around and hit it a second time and finally came to a rest in lane two. The non-contact vehicle that had cut him off didn't stop. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
V1 was driven by a 25 year-old male who upon arriving on the scene was removing personal belongings from the vehicle. The driver refused to be interviewed and would not take part in this study.
