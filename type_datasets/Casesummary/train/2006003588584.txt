The crash occurred in the eastbound lanes of a two-way roadway with two lanes going eastbound and three lanes going westbound. The roadway was straight and level and it was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions. The roadway was wet from previous rain and the speed limit was 48kmph (30mph).
Vehicle # 1 was a 2003 Dodge Neon that was traveling on the left lane and stopped to make a left into a parking lot. Vehicle # 2 was a 2001 Chevrolet Astrovan that stopped behind Vehicle #1. Vehicle # 3 was a 1999 International Bus that was traveling on the left lane behind Vehicle # 2. The front of Vehicle # 3 impacted the back of Vehicle # 2, and caused the front of Vehicle # 2 to impact the back of Vehicle # 1. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.
The Dodge Neon (Vehicle # 1) was driven by a 28 year-old female who did not sustain any injuries. The Dodge was driven from the scene.
The Chevrolet Astrovan (Vehicle # 2) was driven by a 44 year-old male who did not sustain any injuries. An 18 year-old male passenger also did not sustain any injuries. The Chevrolet was towed due to damage.
The 1999 International Bus (Vehicle # 3) was driven by a 48 year-old male who did not sustain any injuries. A 68 year-old female occupant sustained minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. The driver was issued a citation for failure to produce identification of insurance. The International Bus was driven away from the scene.
