This crash occurred at an intersection between on a four lane undivided straight roadway and the exit to a parking lot. The posted speed limit on the bituminous, level roadway is 48kmph (30mph). The roadway was wet from prior showers and the weather was cloudy. This crash occurred on a weekday evening.
V1, a 1996 Chevrolet S10 pickup, was traveling north in lane two after just exiting a parking lot on the east side of the roadway. The vehicle had entered lane two directly from the lot to avoid stopped uninvolved vehicles in lane one. V2, a 1991 Chevrolet Corsica, was leaving an adjacent parking lot on the east side of the roadway intending to make a left turn. V2 was waived out of the parking lot by an uninvolved motorist who was intending to enter the same lot. V1 impacted with the front left of V2 with its front. Both vehicles came to rest at the impact point. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a belted 41 year-old male who refused treatment. This driver refused to answer interview questions regarding medication and health. It should be noted, however, this driver stated he was on temporary disability and walked with a cane on his right side, leaning heavily on the cane and limping. The driver stated he had just come from a video store, which was located to the right rear of the impact point.
V2 was driven by a belted 17 year-old male who refused treatment. This driver stated he was waiting for traffic to pass before leaving a parking lot, adjacent to the video store, when an uninvolved motorist waved him out. He stated he looked both ways, saw no moving traffic and proceeded to make a left turn. It should be noted here this driver is inexperienced having had his license less than one year.
