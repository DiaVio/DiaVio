A daytime collision involving one in-transport vehicle that impacted three parked not-in-transport vehicles occurred at mid-afternoon on a Thursday. The urban roadway consists of two undivided lanes in each direction. The collision occurred on the northbound lanes that has a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1988 Ford Thunderbird with a driver and passenger, was traveling northbound in lane one. V1 departed the right side of the travel lane and sideswiped vehicle two's (V2) left plane, a 1992 Buick Lesabre, with its front plane and then sideswiped the left side of vehicle three (V3), a 1999 Land Rover Range Rover with its right plane. V1's front plane then impacted the rear of vehicle four (V4) a 1984 Mercury Grand Marquis, pushing V4 in to a parked vehicle, a 1992 Honda Civic, which then impacted another parked vehicle, a 1990 Plymouth Voyager. V1 was the only in-transport vehicle involved in the collision; the remaining vehicles were legally parked not-in-transport vehicles. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
