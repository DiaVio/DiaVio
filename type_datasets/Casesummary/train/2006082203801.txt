This two-vehicle collision occurred in the uncontrolled intersection of two roads, both with two-way traffic. The pre-crash roadway consists of asphalt paving and is level for both vehicles. Parking is allowed on the both sides of the roadway. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 Volvo V-50 wagon was northbound in the first lane. Vehicle two (V2), 1998 Ford Escort coupe was westbound in the first lane. The front of V2 impacted the right of V1. Both vehicles rotated and side slapped with the left of V2 impacting the right of V1. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-six year old male was on the way home from the store. It is unknown how long this driver had been driving on this trip. This driver indicated that he didn't see anyone approaching the intersection. He always slows at this intersection, due to the sight restriction caused by a raised yard and shrubbery to the right. The driver of V1 was also fatigued due to he and his spouse just having a new baby. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
The driver of V2, a thirty-one year old male, did not speak any English and refused to be interviewed due to the language barrier.
