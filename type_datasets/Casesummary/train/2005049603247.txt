The crash happened mid-afternoon. Involved two vehicles a passenger car and a pick-up truck. The location is a three lane divided (without positive barrier, six lanes overall) straight roadway. The weather was clear and the road is blacktop and dry. Speed limit 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) a 2002 Chrysler PT Cruiser driven by a 33 year-old female with two passengers was traveling south in the third lane of traffic. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Ford F-150 Pick up truck driven by a 68 year-old male was traveling in the same lane, ahead of V1. Two unidentified light motor vehicles were traveling in front of V2 at an intersection and the southernmost vehicle slowed down and to made a u-turn. The vehicle in front of V2 slowed down as well. V2 slowed down without coming to a stop. V1 applied its brakes the front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. Both vehicles came to rest within their original lane of traffic. V1 was towed from the scene. The driver of V2 was taken to a local hospital. V2 was driven from the scene by a relative of the driver. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
