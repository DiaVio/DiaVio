This single-vehicle crash occurred on a jersey barrier-divided, straight trafficway. The southbound lanes are level and consist of four through lanes with shoulders on both sides. The speed limit for this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Mitsubishi Eclipse, was southbound in lane two. V1 experienced a fire that started in the engine compartment. V1 then traveled off the right side of the road and the front struck the jersey barrier. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
The driver of V1, a male, was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
