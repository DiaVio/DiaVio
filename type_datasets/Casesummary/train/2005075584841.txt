This two-vehicle crash occurred just past noon on a straight, asphalt, three-lane, undivided, roadway approaching a private driveway. The road runs east and west at the location of the crash and has one lane in each direction with and left turn lane in the middle. The speed limit on the road is 89 kph (35 mph) and there were no adverse weather conditions at the time.
Vehicle one, a 1996 Ford Taurus sedan, driven by a 20 year-old female was traveling southbound and coming out of a two-lane private driveway to turn left. Vehicle two, a 1993 Honda Accord sedan, driven by a 37 year-old female was traveling westbound in lane one going straight. Vehicle one proceeded with her turn then stopped when she observed vehicle two thinking that vehicle two would go around. The front of vehicle two impacted the left side of vehicle one. Vehicle one rotated clockwise and came to rest on its wheels facing southwest in the westbound number one lane. Vehicle two rotated slightly counter clockwise and came to rest on its wheels facing southwest in lane one. Vehicle one was towed from the scene, vehicle two was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.
