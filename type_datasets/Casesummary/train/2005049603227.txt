The crash happened during early morning daylight hours. Involved were three vehicles - one car and two pick-up trucks. The location was a five lane divided (with positive barrier, ten lanes overall) The weather was clear and the road in dry level concrete. The traffic was stopped due to another vehicle collision located about 500 meters (1,640 feet) west of this crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Honda Accord, was traveling west in the first lane of traffic. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Chevrolet Silverado Pick up truck was stopped in lane 1. Vehicle three (V3), a 2005 Ford F-150 pick-up truck, was stopped 2.1-meters (7-feet) in front of V2. The front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. V2 rotated a little counter clockwise and moved forward. The front of V2 then impacted the rear of V3. V3 was slightly pushed forward and rotated counter clockwise. V1 rotated slightly counter clockwise after the first impact and came to rest partially in lane one and partially on the north shoulder of the roadway. V1 & V2 were towed from the scene. The driver of V1 was taken to a local hospital. V3 drove from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
There was no interview obtained with the 31 year old male driver of V1. There were no environmental or vehicle factors for this driver, so it was reasoned that the driver had an unknown driver factor. Based on the vehicle damage pattern to the front , it was reasoned that the driver steered to the right just prior to impact.
