This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged intersection. The southbound approach is straight, has a two-and-a-half percent uphill grade, is controlled by a stop sign at the intersection and consists of one lane in each direction. The speed limit on this roadway is 32 kmph (20 mph). The east/west roadway is straight, level and consists of two lanes in each direction. The speed limit on this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Buick Rendezvous, was southbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1997 Saturn SW1, was eastbound in lane one. V1 stopped at the stop sign and then proceeded through the intersection. The front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V2 rotated counterclockwise and rolled over to the right two quarter-turns. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a twenty-six-year-old female, was traveling from her home to a friend's house. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. She travels this route weekly. This driver stated that she was thinking about a personal problem at the time of the crash. She further stated that she thought she looked both ways and did not see V2 before she entered the intersection.
The driver of V2, a forty-eight-year-old male, was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
