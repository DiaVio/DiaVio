This four-vehicle crash occurred on a straight, level, curbed median-divided roadway, approaching a traffic signal-controlled intersection. The eastbound lanes consist of three through lanes and a left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph).
This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 Cadillac CTS, was eastbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 Nissan Altima, was stopped eastbound in lane one. Vehicle three (V3), a 2004 Kia Sedona, was stopped in front of V2 and vehicle four (V4), a 2003 GMC Envoy, was stopped in front of V3. The front of V1 struck the back of V2. V2 moved forward and the front struck the back of V3. V3 then moved forward and the front struck the back of V4. V1 was towed due to damage.
The driver of V1, a fifty-nine-year-old male, was traveling from work to a medical appointment. He was well rested, healthy and drives his vehicle often. He travels this route weekly. This driver was not interviewed; no further information is available for this driver.
The driver of V2, a thirty-year-old female, was traveling with two young male passengers. This driver was not interviewed; no further information is available for this driver.
The driver of V3, a fifty-one-year-old female, was traveling with a fourteen-year-old male passenger, from work to her home. She was healthy, well rested and comfortable with her vehicle and passenger. This driver travels on this roadway daily.
