This single-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions. The precrash roadway is two-lane, undivided and straight and has a slight downgrade. This roadway curves left (radius of curvature is 161 meters) just prior to the crash area. The speed limit on this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Chevrolet S-10 Blazer, was southbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the right side of the roadway and the front struck a concrete-base mailbox and a culvert. V1 rotated clockwise and rolled over onto its left side. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1 left the scene and was not located.
