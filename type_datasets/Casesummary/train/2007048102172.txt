This three-vehicle crash occurred on a divided trafficway on the approach to a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The northbound lanes in the crash area are straight, level and consist of two through lanes, a left turn lane and a right turn lane. In the precrash area, this roadway is curved to the right (the radius of curvature is 2250 meters). The speed limit for this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions. Traffic was congested at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Ford Expedition, was northbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2006 Jeep Liberty, was stopped northbound in lane three and vehicle three (V3), a 2002 Toyota Camry, was stopped in front of V2. The front of V1 struck the back of V2 and the front of V2 struck the back of V3. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, an eighteen-year-old female, was on her way from college to her male passenger's home. She was healthy, well rested and comfortable with her vehicle and passenger. She travels this route daily. This driver was talking to and looking at her passenger prior to the crash. Just before the crash, her cell phone rang and she looked down at it. This driver stated that she never saw V2 prior to the crash.
The driver of V2, a twenty-two-year-old female, was on her way home from her mother's house. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle. She travels this route weekly. This driver stated that she did not see V1 prior to the crash.
The driver of V3, a fifty-seven-year-old male, was on his way from work to a restaurant. He was healthy, well rested and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route daily. This driver stated that he did not see V2 prior to the crash.
