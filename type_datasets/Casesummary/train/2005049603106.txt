This crash occurred during early afternoon daylight hours involving two passenger cars at a curved two-lane, two-way traffic undivided asphalt roadway. The speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). The road surface was wet and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Honda RAV 4 driven by a 18 year-old female was traveling south in the first lane, approaching right curvature. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Chevrolet S-10 Pick up driven by a 20 year-old male was traveling north in the first lane. While negotiating the curve, V1 drove the lane line to the left. The front of V1 impacted the left side of V2. V2 rotated counter clockwise departed the roadway. V2 rolled over onto its right side. V1 slightly rotated counter clockwise after the impact, and came to rest in the northbound lane, facing south. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. The driver of V1 suffered a light injury but refused treatment from the ambulance. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
