This two-vehicle collision occurred at the T-intersection of a north-south main roadway and an east-west side street. The intersection is controlled by a stop sign for eastbound traffic. The north-south roadway is a seven-lane, two-way, bituminous street with a two-way left turn lane, and is designated a 56 kmph (35 mph) zone in the area of the crash. The east-west roadway is a two-lane, two-way, bituminous street, and is designated a 40 kmph (25 mph) zone. Both roadways were dry, straight and level. The roadways were free of defects, and there were no adverse weather conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Lincoln Town Car 4-door sedan, was stopped facing eastbound at the intersection with the intention of turning right into southbound traffic on the main roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Cadillac Seville 4-door sedan, was traveling southbound in the second lane, approaching the intersection on its right with the intention of continuing straight. A non-contact vehicle was in the first lane of southbound traffic, turning right onto the side street. V1 executed a wide right turn into the second southbound lane, where the front of V2 impacted the left side of V1. Both vehicles deflected south to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
