This two-vehicle crash occurred during early evening daylight hours on a four-way intersection with east/west traffic controlled by a stop sign. No adverse weather conditions present at the time of collision. Northbound traffic was intermittently heavy as a traffic signal device just south of where collision occurred controlled traffic. Bituminous roadway was level and dry. Posted speed limit 56 kph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) a 2001 GMC Sonoma pick-up driven by a 61 year-old male with one passenger was traveling west in the second lane approaching the intersection with the intention of turning left. Vehicle two (V2) a 2002 Ford Focus sedan driven by a 41 year-old female with one passenger was traveling north in the first lane. V1 driver stated traffic was heavy and had been waiting 15-20 minutes before finally having an opportunity to make left turn. V1 driver stated that he proceeded to make turn after another vehicle traveling north in lane one began making a right turn at intersection. As V1 the front collided with the right side of V2 Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
