V1, a 2005 Chevrolet Trailblazer was traveling northbound on a 4-lane, 2-way, dry asphalt roadway in daylight conditions. V1 was approaching the intersection intending to go straight. V2 a 2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee was also traveling northbound on the same roadway also intending to go straight. The font of V1 contacted the back of V2 before they both came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 69-year old male who had reported that he had been traveling between 18-32kmph (11-20 mph) prior to the crash. He stated that he was looking to the left at a vehicle, when he looked up the car ahead of him was stopping. He didn't have time to stop and ran into the back of V2. He was wearing his prescribed lenses that corrected a myopic (near-sighted) condition. He did not sustain any injuries and refused treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 54-year old male who reported that he had been traveling between 2-16kmph (1-10mph) prior to the crash. The driver stated that he was slowing down for the light, he was not completely stopped when he was hit from behind. He sustained minor injuries but refused treatment.
