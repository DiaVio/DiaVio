This crash occurred on an undivided two-lane, two-way, snow covered city street with a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). The crash occurred during the early afternoon hours.
V1, a 1984 Ford Tempo 2-door sedan, was traveling westbound. V2, a 1998 Olds Cutlass 4-door sedan, was stopped in the westbound lane ahead of V1. The front of V1 contacted the right rear bumper of V2. V1 was towed from the scene with moderate damage, and the driver was uninjured. V2 was not towed from the scene but the passenger was transported to the hospital. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a 68-year-old male, stated that he did not realize that V2 was stopped until it was too late. The driver also stated that he did lock up his brakes and tried to steer to the right to avoid the crash but the road was too snow covered.
The driver of V2 was a 33-year-old female. She could not be reached for an interview, so the passenger, her daughter was interviewed. She stated that they had stopped to talk to the mailman when they were hit in the rear by the other vehicle.
