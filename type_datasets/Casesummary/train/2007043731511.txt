Case 2007-43-181
This single-vehicle crash occurred in the late evening on a 2-lane divided roadway, which acts as the main thoroughfare for a subdivision. The east/west road has a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph) and is curved to the right at the location of the crash. There were no adverse weather conditions and there was no traffic. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1.
V1, a 1998 Infiniti I-30 with one occupant, was westbound fleeing the police when it departed the left side of the road and crashed into three small trees. V1 rotated counter-clockwise after the impact and ejected its driver. V1 came to final rest facing south and its driver was hospitalized.
