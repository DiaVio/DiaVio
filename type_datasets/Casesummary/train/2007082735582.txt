This single vehicle collision occurred heading south on a two lane transition ramp in an interchange of an interstate highway. In the area of the crash the roadway curves to the left and has a downgrade. The asphalt-paved roadway was wet with a speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Chevy Malibu sedan driven by a forty five (45) year old female, was heading south in lane two. As V1 entered the left hand curve, control was lost and the left side of the vehicle impacted the jersey barrier. The vehicle then continues in a counter clockwise spin and the front side impacts the left side jersey barrier. The vehicle then continues its rotation before coming to rest facing south. The vehicle was towed due to damages, Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
