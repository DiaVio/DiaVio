The crash occurred off the end of a T-intersection that was controlled by a stop sign. The posted speed limit was 35 MPH with a -3% downhill grade.
V1, a 2000 Buick LeSabre sedan, was traveling westbound in the 1st travel lane approaching a T-intersection controlled by a stop sign. The driver of V1 reportrted that he lost consciousness and V1 departed the end of the T-intersection. The front of V1 subsequently struck a small sign, a telephone pole guy wire anchor, and several small trees. V1 came to final rest facing northwest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1 was driven by a 57 year-old male who was not injured. He stated that he remembers driving down the road but lost consciousness and woke up when his vehicle was up against the guy wire. The driver of V1 reported that he was traveling between 31 and 40 MPH prior to impact. V1 was towed due to damage.
