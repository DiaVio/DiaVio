This single vehicle crash occurred early evening during daylight hours. The crash occurred on a six lane physically divided, dry, level, highway comprised of concrete. The roadway curves to the left. A concrete divider borders the left side. An asphalt shoulder borders the right side. The roadway is designated 105 kmph (65 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2003 Mitsubishi Galant ES, 4-door sedan. driven by a twenty five (25) year old female with three passengers. V1 was traveling west in the number six (HOV) lane. The driver panicked and lost control when her husband yelled at her. V1 then departed the roadway to the left. V1 impacted the center divider with its front left side. V1 continued west rotating counterclockwise. The rear plane impacted the center divider. V1 came to rest facing northwest in the HOV lane. The vehicle was towed with moderate damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 129 kmph for V1.
The driver was unable to be contacted for an interview. Case coding was based on information in the police report.
