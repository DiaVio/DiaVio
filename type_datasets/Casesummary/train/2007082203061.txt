This two-vehicle nighttime collision occurred in the intersection of a four lane arterial road, and an on ramp for a uncontrolled freeway. The roads are paved with asphalt. The roadway for westbound traffic has a downhill grade of 6%, for eastbound traffic the roadway has an uphill grade of 6%. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was cloudy and raining with wet roadways.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Nissan pickup truck driven by a thirty six (36) year old female, was traveling west in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1993 Ford Festiva hatchback driven by a thirty three (33) year old female with one passenger, was traveling east in lane one. V1 began to make the left turn to access the on ramp and crossed over the eastbound lanes. The front right corner of V1 impacted with the front of V2. After impact V1 rolled backwards and came to rest on the north/east curb. After impact V2 rotated approximately 30 degrees counter clockwise and came to rest. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was unable to be interviewed after numerous attempts to locate.
