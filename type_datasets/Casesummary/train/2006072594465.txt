This two-vehicle crash occurred at approximately noon on a four-lane southbound expressway. The northbound expressway is divided with a positive median barrier and is not a factor in this crash. The southbound expressway is level, straight and has no visible deficiencies. The speed limit for this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph) and is not controlled by any traffic control devices. The weather conditions were clear at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Honda Civic with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane three and attempted to change lanes to the right. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Ford Crown Victoria with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane two of the expressway adjacent to vehicle one. The driver of V1 was inattentive while changing lanes and the front right corner of V1 struck the left side of V2. After this initial contact the vehicles side slapped with the right rear side of V1 and the left rear side of V2 impacting. V1 had minor damage and was not towed. V2 had minor damage as well but was towed and the occupant was transported to a hospital with a non-incapacitating injury. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
