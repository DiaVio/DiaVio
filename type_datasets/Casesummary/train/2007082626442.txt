This two-vehicle daytime collision occurred in the intersection of a two-lane two-way city street and a two-way two-lane arterial road. Both roadways are paved with concrete. The city street is controlled by a stop sign for the north/south lanes, The roadway for both vehicles is straight and level. The speed limit for both vehicles is 40 kmph (25 mph). At the time of the crash was raining.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1990 Nissan pick-up Truck driven by a fifty one (51) year old male, was traveling south in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Audi AG4 sedan driven by a thirty five (35) year old male with one passenger, was traveling west in lane one. It is unclear if V1 stopped for the stop sign. V1 entered the intersection initiating a left turn and impacted the front right corner of V2. V1 came to rest on the north/west corner and V2 came to rest facing west in the oncoming lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was unable to be contacted for an interview after numerous attempts. The driver of V2 was unable to be contacted for an interview after numerous attempts.
