This single vehicle crash occurred during the morning on a two-lane, wet, uphill, bituminous roadway that curved to the right and runs east to west. The radius of the curve was 225-meters and the roadway has a super elevation of 2%. This roadway has a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). It was cloudy at the time of the crash and had stopped raining several minutes before the crash occurred.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1990 Buick Century sedan driven by a 22 year-old male was traveling eastbound in lane one. negotiating the curve, V 1 departed the right side of the roadway, the re-entered the roadway, departed lane one, the departed the left side of the roadway and the front of V 1 struck several small trees. Post-impact vehicle one rotated clockwise and the rear of vehicle one struck an embankment. V 1 came to rest off the roadway facing east. V 1 was towed from the scene the driver was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1.
