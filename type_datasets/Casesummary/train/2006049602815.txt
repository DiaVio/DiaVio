This single-vehicle crash occurred on a curbed median-divided, straight roadway. The eastbound lanes have a 3.3% downhill grade and consist of three through lanes and a left turn lane at intersections. The speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Ford Crown Victoria, was eastbound in lane three. V1 traveled onto the median and the front struck a utility pole. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-four-year-old female, was not interviewed. A witness, who was directly behind V1 during the precrash and crash and also stopped to help the driver of V1, was interviewed. This witness stated that the driver of V1 seemed very tired at the scene. This witness also stated that she heard a phone conversation that the driver of V1 had after the crash regarding an argument that she had with her boyfriend just prior to this crash. No further information is available about the driver of V1.
