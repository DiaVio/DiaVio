This two-vehicle crash occurred during early morning hours on a straight, bituminous, eight-lane, divided roadway at a four-leg intersection. The roadway runs north and south at this location. Northbound traffic, south of the intersection, consists of three through lanes and two left turn lanes. Southbound traffic, north of the intersection, consists of three through lanes and two left turn lanes. There are on-color traffic signals on all four legs of the intersection with pedestrian signals. The speed limit is 64 kmph (40 mph) and there was a light drizzle coming down and the roadway was wet.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet Cavalier sedan driven by a 31 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling south in lane five, the inside left turn lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Volvo XC70 station wagon driven by a 50 year-old female, was traveling north in lane two, Both vehicles entered the intersection, V1 intending to turn left and V2 intending to go straight. The right side of V1 contacted the front of V2. V1 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the intersection facing southwest. V2 came to a controlled final rest north of the intersection in northbound lane one. V1 was towed from the scene, Police Reported Travel Speed is 40 kmph for V1 and 40 kmph for V2.
