A two-vehicle collision occurred late afternoon around dusk on a Monday. The collision occurred on a two-lane, two-way section of an undivided rural highway. The pre-crash roadway is level and straight, with bituminous paving. Access to the roadway is controlled. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 89 kmph (55mph).
Vehicle 1 (V1), a 1996 Dodge Ram Pickup, was westbound in lane one. Vehicle 2 (V2) a 1994 Ford Taurus was traveling east in lane one. As V1 approached congestion the driver braked hard and steered to the left. The front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V1 was towed due to damage, and V2 was also towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
