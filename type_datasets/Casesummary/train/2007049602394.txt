It was dark with streetlights on at the time of this three-vehicle crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions. The trafficway is level, divided by a grass median and consists of three lanes in each direction. The speed limit for this roadway is 97 kmph (60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1990 Geo Prizm, was northbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1991 Ford Crown Victoria, was parked northbound on the right (paved) shoulder. Vehicle three (V3), a 2003 Mercury Sable, was northbound in lane three. V1 traveled off the right side of the roadway and the front struck the back of V2. V2 entered the roadway, rotating counterclockwise, and the front struck the front of V3. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a forty-nine-year-old male, was not interviewed. His medical report indicates a BAC result of 0.159. No further information is available for this driver.
V2, occupied by a nineteen-year-old male and a nineteen-year-old female, was disabled on the shoulder.
The driver of V3, a seventy-five-year-old male, was on his way from his home in another state to his son's home. He was well rested, healthy and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route monthly. This driver stated that he saw the crash between V1 and V2 and steered to the left in an attempt to avoid the crash.
