The crash occurred in the westbound lane of a two-lane undivided roadway. There was one eastbound and one westbound lane. Curbside parking was permitted on the right side of both travel lanes. There were no signalized traffic control devices at this mid-block location. The bituminous covered roadway was dry, level, and straight, and there were no adverse weather conditions. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph). The crash occurred on a weekday during the late-night dark hours. There were noticeable defects and the traffic flow was normal for this roadway.
Vehicle one, a 2002 Dodge Ram large pickup truck, was traveling westbound in lane one. V2 a 2004 Chevy Avalanche was legally parked on the west side of the road in the parking lane. V3 a 1997 Saturn SL1 was parked in front of V2 in the parking lane. V4 a 2000 Ford Mustang was parked in front of V3 in the parking lane. V5 a 2007 Lexus GX470 was parked in front of V4 in the parking lane. V1 departed the roadway on the right side and contacted it front with the sides of V2, V3, V4 and V5. The damages to vehicle one was disabling was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, was a 38 year old female, she was being arrested for DUI. Several attempts were made to contact the driver, which were not successful. The police recorded the crash as a DUI.
