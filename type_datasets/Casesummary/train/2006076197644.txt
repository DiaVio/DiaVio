This is a single vehicle crash that occurred in a residential area on a 2-lane asphalt roadway. The eastbound lane is straight and level and forks just after the area of impact. The speed limit for the area is 40 kmph (25 mph). Both directions of travel are bordered by private homes. The crash occurred during late morning on a Saturday. There were no adverse weather or road conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a black 1997 Plymouth Breeze 4-door sedan, was eastbound when it departed the right road edge where the front right corner struck a brick pillar located at the entrance of a private driveway. V1 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the area of impact facing southeast. The driver of V1 fled the scene of the crash and was never located or identified by police. The other occupant was extricated from the vehicle and transported due to injury. Both frontal airbags deployed in the crash. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 40 kmph for V1.
