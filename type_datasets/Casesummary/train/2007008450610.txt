The crash occurred in the intersection of a five lane undivided roadway. There were two southbound lanes and two northbound lanes plus a turning lane. This was a straight, level bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit 64KMPH (40MPH). It was early evening on a weekend and the road was dry and skies were clear. Traffic flow was congested, with stop and go activity.
V1, a 2002 Chevrolet Venture minivan, with one occupant, was facing north and turning left at the intersection. V2, a 2001 Chevrolet Blazer sport utility vehicle with two occupants, was traveling south in lane two. Unit one entered the intersection and was struck on the right by unit two's front. Both units came to rest in the southbound lane one. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V1, the Chevy Venture, was driven by a 20 year old female who reported no injuries. No interview for the driver of V1 could be obtained.
V2, the Chevy Blazer, was driven by a 19 year old male who reported no injuries. The driver was running errands when the crash occurred.
