This two-vehicle crash occurred on a cloudy morning. The crash occurred on a two-lane, dry, level, and bituminous roadway that runs from north to south. The posted speed limit is 89 kmph (55 mph). and the northbound lane has a left hand curve with chevron warning signs.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Nissan Maxima driven by a 54 year-old male, was traveling north negotiating a left hand curve. Vehicle two (V2), a 1999 International F 2574 straight truck towing a trailer, was driven by a 55 year-old male who was southbound . The driver of V1 fell asleep and V1 drifted in to the northbound lane. The front of V1 struck the left side of V2's trailer. V1 then departed the west road edge and struck a ditch. V1 came to final rest in the ditch facing north. The driver was not injured and the vehicle was towed due to disabling damage. V2 pulled off the west road edge. The driver was not injured and he drove the vehicle from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1 and 89 kmph for V2.
