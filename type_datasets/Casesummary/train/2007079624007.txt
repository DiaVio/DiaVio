This two vehicle collision occurred on a clear midday. The collision occurred on a north/south roadway that is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and comprised of two lanes. The roadway is designated a 40 kmph (25 mph) zone.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1998 Nissan Frontier pickup driven by a fifty five (55) year old male. V1 was illegally parked along the west curb blocking a driveway. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Chevrolet S-10 pickup driven by a seventy four (74) year old male, was traveling southbound in the number one lane. As V2 was traveling, V1 merged onto the roadway from its parked position. The front plane of V2 contacted the left plane of V1. V1 came to rest facing southwest blocking the number one lane. V2 came to rest facing southbound blocking the number one lane. V2 received minor damage and was not towed from the scene due to damage. V1 was towed from scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
