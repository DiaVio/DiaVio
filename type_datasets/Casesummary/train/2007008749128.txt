This crash occurred in the westbound lanes of a four-lane undivided roadway. There were two westbound lanes and two eastbound lanes. The roadway curved to the left, had an uphill slope, was bituminous and had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). The roadway was dry and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 1989 Ford F350 with a dump truck bed, being driven by a 54 year-old male. V1 was traveling westbound and had just finished negotiating the left hand curve when it suddenly left the roadway to the right side. The vehicle impacted a telephone pole, shearing it in half, and coming to final rest in a large hole in the ground with the pole resting on top of it. Police Reported Travel Speed is 80 kmph for V1.
