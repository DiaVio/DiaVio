The crash occurred on an urban, secondary arterial road along a straight portion that was also a bridge. The roadway was two-way, two lane and runs east / west with the westbound side bordered by parallel parking spaces. The roadway here was also level. The posted speed limit was 48KPH / 30MPH. Conditions were daylight and dry in the late morning hours on a weekday.
Vehicle 1, a 2001 Chrysler PT Cruiser, was westbound crossing the bridge trailing behind Vehicle 2, a 2003 Chevy Impala. As Vehicle 2 was crossing the bridge headed westbound a non-contact vehicle pulled out of one of the parallel parking spaces into the westbound travel lanes causing Vehicle 2 to stop abruptly. Vehicle 1 then collided with the rear end of Vehicle 2, striking Vehicle 2 with its front after skidding approximately 8 meters longitudinally. Vehicle 2 came to rest approximately 20 meters west of impact partially straddling the double yellow line still facing west. Vehicle 1 came to rest just a few meters west of impact, also still facing west. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle 1 was driven by a 47-year old belted female whose airbags did not deploy. She was transported to a local hospital for treatment of non-incapacitating injuries.
Vehicle 2 was driven by a 38-year old female who was belted and whose airbags did not deploy in the crash. She was transported to a local trauma center for treatment of non-incapacitating injuries. The EDR download indicated that five seconds pre-crash, the vehicle speed was 64 KPH / 40 MPH. At four seconds pre-crash her brake switch circuit was on. At one second pre-crash the brake switch is off again and the vehicle is moving 4 MPH. Longitudinal Delta V was recorded as zero.
