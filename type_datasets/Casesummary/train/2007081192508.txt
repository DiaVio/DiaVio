This two-vehicle crash occurred during the late afternoon daylight hours. The location was a heavily traveled arterial in an area of multiple industrial business and multiple driveways. Traffic lights and turn lanes for all traffic control the roadway. The roadway was a six lane, two way, undivided street with three southbound lanes, two northbound lanes and a center left turn only lane. The surface was level dry bituminous with curbs and sidewalks on both roadsides. The posted speed limit is 80 kmph (50 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Saturn Ion sedan driven by a sixty seven (67) year old female, was northbound stopped in the center turn lane waiting for traffic to clear to make a left turn across the southbound lanes into a driveway. The driver stated traffic was stopped and backed up from the traffic light in the southbound lanes. A large truck stopped in the southbound third lane and the driver signaled for V1 to make the left turn. V1 pulled forward and begin turning left. As V1 entered the second lane Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Toyota Celica 2 door hatchback driven by a forty seven (47) year old female was traveling southbound in the second lane next to the large truck. The front of V1 impacted the front of V2, on impact both vehicles veered in a southwest direction. Both vehicles jumped the curb and came to rest on a grass median between the sidewalk and the curb. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was not interviewed, so little pre-crash information is known.
