This crash two vehicle crash occurred at noon during daylight hours at an intersection with two lanes north and south undivided roadway. The other roadway is a seven lane divided roadway with concrete median. The speed limit for both roadways is 56 kmph (35 mph). Road surface wet, level concrete. Traffic flow controlled by tri-color traffic signal.
Vehicle one (V 1) a 1993 Dodge Spirit 4 door sedan driven by a 19 year-old female with one passenger was traveling west in the second lane. Vehicle two (V 2) a 1997 Chevrolet Lumina 4 door sedan driven by a 52 year-old female with one passenger was traveling north in the right lane. As V 1 entered the intersection the front struck the right side of V 2. After the impact V 1 came to final rest facing west in the north curb lane, V 2 came to final rest after rotating clockwise facing south. V 1 had never been on this roadway before and the driver and passenger were looking both left and right for an address. . Neither noticed the traffic signals. Only after V 2 was in front of V 1 did V 1 brake. V 2 was towed from the scene. The driver of V 2 was transported to the hospital with minor injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
