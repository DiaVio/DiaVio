This two-vehicle crash occurred on three-lane city street. This road runs north to south and has a painted center two-way left turn lane. This road is straight and level with a paved bike-path/shoulder on both sides of the road. This crash occurred in the morning daylight hours with a light rain falling and a wet asphalt road. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2002 Kia 4 door sedan driven by a thirty two (32) year old male with one passenger. V1 was northbound when the driver of V1 stated that he had taken his eyes off the road to look down for a CD case. When his passenger alerted him of the impending impact, it was too late. V1 impacted the rear plane of a 2003 Dodge pickup that was legally parked partially in the bike-lane/shoulder and partially on the adjacent sidewalk off of the travel lanes. After the impact V1 pushed the Dodge truck onto a sidewalk where both vehicle came to rest. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
