The crash occurred in the morning during dark hours at the intersection of a two-way, eight lane, undivided roadway and a one-way, three lane roadway. Both roads are dry, polished, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph). Traffic signal controlled traffic flow; it was dark, but lighted,
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1998 Dodge Stratus driven by a 48 year-old male who was northbound in the fourth travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1999 Chevrolet Blazer driven by a 23 year-old male with one passenger who was southbound in the first travel lane. V1 attempted a left turn without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing south in the southbound lanes. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southwest off the roadway on the southwest corner. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
