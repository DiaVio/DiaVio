This two-vehicle crash occurred on a divided roadway near an intersection. The southbound lanes are left-curved, level and consist of two through lanes and a right turn lane. The speed limit for this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Dodge Durango, was southbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1997 Acura 3.2TL, was southbound in lane three. As they neared the intersection, the driver of V1 attempted to turn left at the intersection. The front of V1 struck the right side of V2. The vehicles rotated and the left side of V1 struck the right side of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
The driver of V1, a sixty-three-year-old female, and the driver of V2, a thirty-seven-year-old female, were not interviewed.
