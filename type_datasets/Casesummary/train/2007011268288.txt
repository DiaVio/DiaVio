The crash occurred on a fully controlled highway with three lanes in each direction divided by a concrete median barrier. The level, bituminous roadway has a posted speed limit of 113 kph (70 mph). Conditions at the time of the weekday afternoon crash were daylight, clear and dry.
V1, a 1998 Ford Contour SE 4 door sedan driven by a 27 year-old female was westbound on the highway in a right hand curve in lane two traveling 113 kph (70 mph). The driver stated that she was 1.5 car lengths behind another car when it suddenly braked hard and slowed. The driver of v1 braked (full ABS application) and swerved left to avoid the vehicle in front of her. V1 crossed lane three in a counterclockwise yaw and left the road on the left to the paved shoulder. The front of v1 struck the concrete median wall. V1 came to rest facing southeast on the shoulder and was towed from the scene due to damage. The fully restrained driver and 23 year-old female front right passenger were treated on the scene for injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
