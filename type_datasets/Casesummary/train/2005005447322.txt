This crash occurred on a two-way, seven-lane roadway (seven lanes only at the intersection). The roadway is physically divided, and has traffic lights for each lane at this intersection. The conditions were as follows: late morning, sunlight, clear skies, the roadway was dry and there were no atmospheric conditions. The gradient for vehicle one (V1) and vehicle two (V2) was level, and just came off of a positive slope, and a curve to the right. The posted speed limit for V1 was 40 kmph (25 mph).
V1 was a 2000 BMW 328i, traveling northbound, and he had a green light at the intersection, but heard an ambulance approaching from the cross street from the right and stopped while in the middle of the intersection. V2 was a 1995 Nissan Altima, traveling northbound and was coming up to V1 that was stopped because of the Ambulance. V2 struck V1 in the left rear. V1 was pushed across the intersection, then into the curb lane for it's final rest. V2 went across the intersection, then into the curb lane for it's final rest. . Police Reported Travel Speed is 0 kmph for V1 and 0 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1 was a 42 year old male.that was taken to a local Hospital via ambulance to be checked out. He stated that he heard the ambulance and stopped to let the ambulance pass. While he was stopped a car struck him from behind.
The Driver of V2 was a 22 year old male that did not need treatment, because he did not suffer any injuries. The driver of V2 did not give the researcher an interview, after numerous attempts.
