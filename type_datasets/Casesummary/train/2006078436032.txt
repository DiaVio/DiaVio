This single vehicle rollover occurred during early morning daylight hours on a two-lane, two-way roadway that is separated by a depressed earthen median. There are two southbound and two northbound lanes in the area of the crash. This was a straight level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 104 KHP (65 MPH). the road was dry and the sky was clear.
Vehicle one(V-1), a 2004 Mitsubishi Galant sedan driven by a 21 year-old male was traveling southbound in the number 2 lane. The left side wheels struck the rumble and the tires departed the left side of the roadway. The vehicle then traveled back onto the roadway and began to rotate clockwise. It traveled across both southbound lanes while rotating clockwise in a southwesterly direction until it departed the right side of the roadway. The vehicle slid sideways to its left for approximately 20 meters until the left rear area struck a tree stump. The vehicle continued to travel for an additional 15 meters before it tripped on its left wheels and rolled 4 quarters turns before it came to rest on its wheels facing in a northeasterly direction. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
Additional factor, the driver woke up about 22 hours before the crash time.
