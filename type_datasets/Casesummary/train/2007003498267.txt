The crash occurred on an undivided east/west two-lane roadway with one lane of travel in each direction. This was a straight, level, bituminous roadway with a speed limit of 48kmph (30mph). It was the evening hours of a weekend and the road was dry and sky was clear. It was dark out, but the roadway was illuminated by streetlamps.
V1, a 1996 Honda Civic four door sedan with four occupants, was traveling eastbound in lane one. V2, a 2002 Ford Explorer sport utility vehicle with one occupant, was traveling eastbound in lane one in front of V1. The front of V1 impacted the back of V2. V1 came to a final rest on the crosswalk. V2 came to a final rest in front of V1. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Honda Civic, was driven by a 26-year-old female who reported no injuries. A 14 year-old female occupant reported non-incapacitating injuries and was treated on-scene. The 5 year-old and 6 year-old female occupants both reported no injuries. The driver advised the police that she was traveling eastbound when V2 slammed on its brakes, causing her to rear-end V2.
V2, the Ford Explorer, was driven by a 35 year-old male who reported pain. The driver advised the police that he was traveling eastbound when the driver of V1 slammed into the back of his vehicle. The driver was issued two citations for being unlicensed and driving without proof of insurance.
