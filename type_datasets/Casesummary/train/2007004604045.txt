The crash occurred in the eastbound lane of a two-lane east/westbound roadway. The roadway was bituminous and level; with a posted speed limits of 80 kmph (50 mph). It was afternoon on a weekday. The roads were dry and the sky was clear. Traffic flow was moderate.
V1, a 2002 Toyota Tacoma pickup truck with two occupants, was traveling west when it attempted a pass an unknown vehicle. V2, a 1990 Chevrolet Astro Van with two occupants, was traveling westbound ahead of the unknown vehicle when it slowed and attempted to turn left. As V1 was passing the unknown vehicle it struck V2 on the right side as V2 turned left across the eastbound lane. V2 then continued forward until striking a tree on the right side of the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, driven by a 22-year-old male and occupied by a 21-year-old male both reported no injuries, stated that he was traveling west when he attempted to pass an unknown vehicle in his lane, as he did so V2, traveling in front of the unknown vehicle, turned left in front of him, causing V1 to strike V2 on the right side. Driver states he was not aware that V2 was going to turn when he started his passing maneuver. V1 attempted to avoid V2 by "locking-up" his brakes.
V2 was driven by a 54-year-old female, and occupied by a 48-year-old female both were transported. Driver stated that she was traveling west when she slowed to turn left. As she attempted her turn she was struck by V1 on the left side and then continued traveling forward, striking a tree. V2 made no avoidance maneuver.
