This two vehicle crash occurred during early morning hours at the intersection of two, two-way, seven lane, divided roadways without a positive barrier. both roads were dry, traveled concrete. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph). There is an on-color traffic signal controlling traffic flow. There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) a 1993 Mercury Grand Marquis sedan driven by a 19 year-old male with one passenger was traveling eastbound attempting to make a left turn. Vehicle two (V2) a 2005 Scion XA sedan driven by a 21 year-old male with one passenger was approaching the intersection westbound in the third lane. V1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 and V2 then side slapped, V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing southwest. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing east. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
