V1, a 2002 Pontiac Grand Am was traveling southbound on a three-lane, one way, dry, concrete highway in daylight conditions. V1, was traveling in lane two, when a witness noticed him drifting over the lane line into lane one. The witness thought he was just making a lane change, but then noticed that he continued to drift off the road and into trees along the right side of the highway. The witness stated that he saw no brake lights.
The trees eventually stopped the vehicle, and it came to final rest in the trees. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1 was a 31-year old male who had reported that he had blacked out prior to the crash and did not know what had happened. He did not remember his speed. The witness stated that he was traveling 126kmph (78mph) and V1 was keeping up with him prior to the crash. The driver of V1 had no prior health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. Driver reported that he felt rested. Driver reported that he had been driving around for four hours before he started heading for work. He does not wear prescribed lenses. He sustained minor injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility.
