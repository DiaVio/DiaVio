The crash happened just past noon. The location was a four lane divided (without positive barrier, seven lanes overall) straight roadway. Road surface asphalt the weather was clear and the road dry. Posted speed limit 56 kmph (35 mph). Four vehicles involved in crash, two passenger cars a SUV and a pick-up truck. The traffic was stopped and backed up for a traffic light.
Vehicle one (V1) a 1985 Honda Accord driven by a 32 year-old male was traveling west in the third lane of traffic. Vehicle three (V3) a 2001 Toyota Land Cruiser driven by a 71 year-old male and Vehicle four (V4) a 1995 Ford Ranger driven by a 75 year-old male were slowing down in lane four. Vehicle two (V2) a 2004 Volkswagen Passat driven by a 24 year-old male with one passenger was decelerating in lane three. The traffic light west of the crash site was green, but the line of cars did not yet move in front of these vehicles. V1 was traveling in lane three approaching the slow moving traffic ahead. The driver fell to sleep at the wheel. The last thing he remembered was seeing the green light at the intersection ahead of him. V 1 continued to drive west. The front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. V2 moved forward. V1 left lane three and moved into lane four where it impacted the rear of V3. V3 was pushed forward and impacted the rear of V4. V1 was towed from the scene, the other three vehicles were driven, no one was transported for injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
