This single-vehicle crash occurred on a curbed median-divided trafficway. The eastbound lanes are level, curved to the left (the radius of curvature is 57 meters) and consist of two through lanes. The speed limit for this roadway is 40 kmph (25 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Honda CR-V, was eastbound in lane one. The tie rod on the right front wheel broke and V1 began to rotate clockwise. The right front wheel then struck the right curb and V1 rolled over to the left two quarter-turns. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-eight-year-old female, was on her way from work to a doctor appointment. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle. This was the first time she had driven this route and was reading a map at the time of the crash.
