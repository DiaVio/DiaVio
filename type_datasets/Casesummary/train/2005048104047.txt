This single vehicle crash occurred on a downhill, bituminous, two-lane, roadway that supports two-way traffic. This roadway curves sharply to the left. The speed limit for this roadway was 89 kmph (55 mph). There were no traffic control devices present and traffic was light at the time of the crash. The crash occurred on a clear day.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Ford Mustang was negotiating the curve traveling southbound in lane one at the posted speed limit. V1 departed the right side of the roadway and went through a ditch. V1 then rolled upon its longitudinal axis towards its left. V1 rolled 2 quarter turns and the front of V1 struck a tree. V1 came to rest on its roof, off of the roadway facing west. V1 was towed due to disabling damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1 was a twenty-five year old female who was leaving a friends house heading home. This driver had been driving for about fifteen minutes at the time of the crash. This driver indicated that she was depressed and taking Effexor XR for her depression. This driver also indicated that she was thinking about her depression. This driver drives this road daily and has been driving this vehicle daily for the last month.
