This crash occurred off the roadway to the right side of a two-lane, two-way, non-divided, curved to the left city street with a posted speed limit of 56kmph (35Mph). The crash occurred during daylight hours, and it had just started to rain at the time of the crash.
V1 a 1997 Pontiac Bonneville was traveling southbound approaching a curve in the roadway. V1 departed the roadway striking the turn sign and also striking a power pole. V1 was towed from the scene with moderate damage, and the lone occupant of V1 was treated on the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1 a 30 year-old female driver stated that she was familiar with the roadway. She then stated that when she applied her brakes to try and slow down they locked up and would not slow the car down. She stated that the tires were very low on tread depth and that her ABS had not worked in about a year.
