This single-vehicle crash occurred in the gore area of an exit ramp on a jersey barrier-divided, straight trafficway. The northbound lanes are straight and consist of four through lanes. The exit ramp has an uphill grade and consists of two lanes. The speed limit for this roadway is 97 kmph (60 mph). It was dark with streetlights on with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Ford F150 pickup, was traveling north in lane two. The driver attempted to exit the roadway on the ramp after passing the gore point. The front of V1 struck the impact attenuator in the gore area. V1 rotated counter clockwise and came to rest within the northbound lanes of the exit ramp, facing southwest. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1 left the scene. No further information is available for this driver.
