This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-leg, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is undivided, level and straight and consists of two lanes in each direction and a left turn lane. The east/west roadway is divided, level and straight. The westbound lanes consist of three through lanes and a left turn lane. The speed limit on these roadways is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Toyota Camry, was westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Ford Mustang, was stopped northbound in lane one and then entered the intersection when his signal turned green. In the intersection, the front of V1 struck the right side of V2. The vehicles rotated and the left side of V1 struck the right side of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 80 kmph for V1 and 80 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a seventeen-year-old female, was healthy and well rested. She drives on this roadway daily, but has been driving for only a year. She stated that she did not see V2 prior to the crash.
The driver of V2, a nineteen-year-old female, was healthy and well rested. This driver travels this route weekly. This driver stated that he did not see V1 prior to the crash.
