This two-vehicle crash occurred on an eastbound, four-lane expressway bordered by shoulders and concrete walls. Just past the crash area, an entrance ramp joins the expressway on the south side. This ramp, as well as the four-lane westbound section did not play a role in the crash. There was heavy congestion on the roadway at the time of the crash. The speed limit on the expressway was 72 kph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 2000 Freightliner FL-70 straight truck was eastbound in the third lane. Vehicle two, a 2003 Nissan Altima was eastbound in the third lane ahead of vehicle one. The front of vehicle one impacted the rear of vehicle two. Vehicle one was not towed due to damage. Vehicle two was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a thirty-eight year old male was driving from his office to make a delivery. This driver had been driving for about thirty-five minutes at the time of the crash. This driver stated that he was about two car lengths behind vehicle two when vehicle two stopped. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a weekly basis.
