This single vehicle rollover type crash occurred from the westbound number two lane of a divided four-lane section of rural Interstate roadway. The crash occurred during early morning daylight hours. The roadway was bituminous, straight and level with no traffic control devices present. The posted speed limit is 105 kmph (65 mph). There were no roadway deficiencies nor adverse environmental conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2005 Chevrolet Malibu LS 4-door sedan, driven by a 28 year-old male with one passenger, a 23 year old female. V1 was traveling westbound in lane two. The driver was operating with the cruise control set at 113 kmph (70 mph) The driver fell asleep and the vehicle departed the left paved shoulder into the depressed sand and gravel median. V1 rolled over coming to final rest on it's wheels in the eastbound number two lane facing southeast. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
