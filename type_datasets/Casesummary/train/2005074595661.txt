This two vehicle collision occurred just past noon during daylight hours at a four leg intersection with five divided lanes north/south. Road surface dry. level concrete. Traffic was controlled east/west by stop signs, posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1986 Honda Accord sedan driven by a 16 year-old female, was traveling north in the first lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Buick Century 4 door sedan driven by a 58 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling south in the third lane. V2 was stopped at the intersection waiting to turn left. After a few minutes of waiting a northbound vehicle waved the driver of V2 on to continue to make the left turn. The driver of V 2 accelerated forward and turned left in front of V1. The front of V1 contacted the right side of V2. After impact V2 rotated clockwise and came to final rest facing southwest in the middle of eastbound traffic. V1 continued forward in a northeasterly direction coming to final rest at the corner. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
