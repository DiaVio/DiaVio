This two-vehicle crash occurred in the early afternoon during daylight hours in the southbound lanes of a divided interstate highway. The roadway is level, concrete and right-curved and consists of four southbound lanes. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 97 kmph (60 mph). There were no adverse atmospheric conditions and the roadway was dry. The roadway was quite congested and the traffic was stop-and-go.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Ford Focus, driven by a 24 year-old female with one passenger was southbound in lane four. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Toyota Rav 4, driven by a 31 year-old female was southbound in front of vehicle one. V2 slowed for traffic and the front of V1 struck the back of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in the original travel lane. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
