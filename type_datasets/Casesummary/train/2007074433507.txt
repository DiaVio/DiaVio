This two-vehicle crash occurred in a five-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is undivided and level. Both approaches are curved to the right. South of the intersection, this roadway consists of one lane in each direction and a left turn lane. North of the intersection, this roadway consists of one lane in the southbound direction, two lanes in the northbound direction and a left turn lane. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Honda Civic, was southbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Volkswagen Beetle, was northbound in lane two. In the intersection, V2 turned left and the front struck the front of V1. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a forty-seven-year-old male, and his forty-five-year-old wife were on their way from their home to a park. This driver stated that he applied the brakes (skidding longitudinally) in an attempt to avoid the crash. He provided no other information regarding the crash and refused to provide any personal details regarding sleep, medical information or experience.
The driver of V2, a twenty-two-year-old male, was on his way to a friend's house from his home. He is well rested, familiar with his vehicle and travels this route daily. This driver stated that he was listening to his ipod that was plugged into his vehicle sound system and never saw V1 prior to the crash. He refused to provide any of his medical information.
