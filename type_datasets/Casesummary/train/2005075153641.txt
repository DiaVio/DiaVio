This two-vehicle crash occurred just before noon on an eight-lane, asphalt, straight, divided roadway near a on-colors controlled intersection with pedestrian crossings. The roadway runs east/west at this location and there are five lanes for westbound traffic. There is a right turn lane, three through lanes and a left turn lane. The roadway was wet at the time as a light rain (slushy snow) was coming down. The speed limit on the road is 64 kmph (40 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Chevrolet Trailblazer SUV driven by a 51 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling west in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 RTD Gillig Bus driven by a 50 year-old female with two passengers, was stopped in lane two in front of V1. The front of V1 contacted the back of V2. V1 deflected around the left side of the bus and came to rest facing west mostly in lane three of westbound traffic with the rear tires on the pedestrian crosswalk. V2 came to rest at impact. V1 was towed due to moderate frontal damage. V2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
