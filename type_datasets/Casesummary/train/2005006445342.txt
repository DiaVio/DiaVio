The four-vehicle crash occurred several feet after a four-way intersection. The intersection was controlled by traffic signals. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35mph). The asphalt roadway was dry, level, and there no adverse weather conditions. The crash occurred on a weekday around noon and the traffic flow was busy.
Vehicle one, a 1998 Toyota Rav4, was traveling north, in lane one. After crossing the intersection V1 attempted to change lanes to the parking lane along side the east curb edge, the parking lane had several vehicles parked unattended. The crash sequence is as follows; Vehicle one's front contacted the rear of vehicle two, a 2001 Mercedes Benz. Vehicle two's front contacted the rear-end of vehicle three, a 1989 Mercury Tracer. Then vehicle three's front contacted the rear of vehicle four, a 1989 Dodge Aries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
The driver of vehicle one was transported directly to the hospital with cuts to his forehead. The researcher couldn't get an interview with the driver of vehicle one, a 39-year old male. His vehicle was towed due to damages. Vehicle two was also towed due to damages.
