This crash occurred in the southbound lane of a two-lane, undivided roadway. There was one northbound lane and one southbound lane. The roadway curved to the right from the northbound direction, it was level, bituminous, and had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). It was afternoon on a weekday and conditions were clear.
V1 was a 2002 Lexus LX 470, driven by a 17 year-old male. V2 was a 2005 Chevrolet W3500, driven by a 26 year-old male. V1 had just left an ice cream stand on the west side of the roadway, crossed both lanes of the roadway and turned around in the parking lot of a gas station on the east side. V1 proceeded back onto the roadway, in order to go back to the ice cream stand, when it was impacted on the left side by V2, which was traveling southbound. V1 had never come to a complete stop before reentering the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
