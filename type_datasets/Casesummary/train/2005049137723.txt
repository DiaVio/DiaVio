This single vehicle crash occurred in the morning during daylight hours on a two-way, three lane (six overall) divided roadway. It was a dry, traveled concrete roadway. The collision involved a single vehicle which rolled over. The posted speed limit was 72 kmph (45 mph) . It is unknown if there was any additional traffic on the roadway. There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2004 Honda Accord traveling westbound in the third lane. V1 struck the center median to the left of the travel lane and lost control. V1 rotated began skidding clockwise across the travel lanes to the right. V1 impacted the north curb with the left side of the vehicle causing it to rollover. V1 continued northwest and came to rest off the roadway facing northeast. The westbound travel direction had a +2.5% grade at the point of initial control loss, +4.1% at the point of rollover initiation and +5.7% between rollover initiation and final rest. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
