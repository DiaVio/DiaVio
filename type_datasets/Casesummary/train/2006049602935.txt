This two vehicle crash occurred shortly before noon in daylight hours at an intersection without traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph) for the eastbound lanes. The road is a 4-lane divided concrete roadway from west to east. It is a two lane (two-way traffic) parking lot exit from north to south. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Honda Accord coupe driven by a 30 year-old female was traveling south in the first lane of traffic passing the intersection of both roadways with the intention to enter the southbound roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Nissan Sentra sedan, driven by a 33 year-old female with two passengers was traveling east in the first lane. At the intersection of both roadways, the front of V1 impacted the left side of V2. V1 rotated counter clockwise. V2 rotated counter clockwise after the impact, and moved southeast. Both vehicles came to rest at the southeast corner the intersection. V1 was, facing southeast; V2 was facing northeast. V2 was pushed off the roadway. V1 was towed from the scene the driver was transported to a local medical facility. V2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
