This single-vehicle collision occurred on an ice-covered, concrete, two-way, undivided, three-lane, straight, and uphill roadway. The posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph). It was dawn at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 GMC Jimmy, was traveling west in lane one. The driver of V1 noticed a vehicle traveling eastbound begin to lose control and move into the center two-way left-turn lane. The driver of V1 applied the brakes and steered right to avoid this vehicle. V1 began to rotate clockwise and departed the right side of the roadway. V1 tripped on the accumulated snow on the shoulder and rolled to the left three quarter-turns. V1 came to rest on its right side facing east and was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-seven-year-old female, was driving from her home to work. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle and route.
