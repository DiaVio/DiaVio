This two-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours .The location was an inter-city commercial area on a roadway bordered by multiple driveways. The roadway was a bituminous five lane, undivided, two way with a center left turn only lane. The weather was clear, the road was dry. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Toyota Paseo two door sedan driven by a fifty (50) year old female, was stopped southbound in a driveway waiting for traffic to clear, so she could turn right to head westbound. The driver stated a vehicle westbound in lane one stopped to allow her to enter the roadway. As V1 pulled forward, it crossed over into the inside lane and was struck in the left side front by Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Chevrolet Trial Blazer LS SUV. V2 was driven by a thirty two (32) year old female, after impact both vehicle's came to final rest facing west in the turn lane. Both vehicle's were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Neither driver involved in this crash was interviewed.
