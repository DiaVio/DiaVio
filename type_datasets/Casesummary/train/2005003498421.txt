The crash occurred in the eastbound lanes of a two-way roadway with one lane in each direction. The roadway was straight and level and had a speed limit of 48kmph (30mph). It was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions.
Vehicle # 1 was a 1994 Acura Vigor that was traveling eastbound. Vehicle # 2 was a Lincoln Town Car that attempted to make a left turn into a gas station. The front of Vehicle # 1 contacted the front of Vehicle # 2. The front of Vehicle # 1 then impacted the right side of Vehicle # 2 before coming to a final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The Acura Vigor (Vehicle # 1), was driven by a 47-year old male who did not sustain any injuries. The driver stated that he was going straight when Vehicle # 2 attempted to turn in front of him. The Acura Vigor was towed due to damage.
The 1993 Lincoln Town Car (Vehicle # 2), was driven by a 50-year old female who did not sustain any injuries. The 49-year old male passenger in the front-right seat position did not sustain any injuries but was transported to a local medical facility.
