This two vehicle crash occurred along a 4-lane, one-way, interstate highway road with no traffic controls. The road is concrete, straight, and level with a speed limit of 97 kmph (60 mph). The road was dark, but well lit with overhead streetlight's. The road was dry, and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Dodge Dakota pick-up driven by a twenty nine (29) year old male, was traveling south in lane four. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Hyundai Elantra sedan driven by a sixty seven (67) year old female, was traveling south in lane two. A non-contact eighteen wheeler in the number three lane started to change lanes to the right, the driver of V2 swerved to the right losing control. The driver of V2 overcompensated steering back to the left and contacting the right side rear of V1 with the front of V2, after contact V2 spun counterclockwise into the jersey barrier with the rear continuing to rotate and coming to final rest between lanes 3 and 4. V1 came to a controlled stop at the exit. V2 was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
