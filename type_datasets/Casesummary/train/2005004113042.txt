The crash occurred in the eastbound lane of a six-lane intersection with a positive median controlled by a traffic control signal. Conditions were daylight and dry in the afternoon on a weekend. The posted speed limit was 80 KMPH (50 MPH).
V1 was a 1998 Subaru Outback wagon traveling eastbound in the 1st travel lane approaching an intersection controlled by a traffic control signal intending on going straight. An unknown vehicle encroached into V1's travel lane from the right side. As a result, V1 swerved left to try and avoid the unknown vehicle. V1 lost control of her vehicle, struck a curb and struck a light post. V1 came to final rest facing northeast. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1 was driven by a 45-year-old female who was not injured. She stated that she was approaching the intersection when she saw an unknown vehicle from her right encroach into her travel lane. V1 swerved left to avoid the unknown vehicle and crossed over two-travel lanes then struck a curb. The front of V1 then struck a breakaway light post. V1 continued on, entered the oncoming traffic lanes, and came to final rest facing northeast. V1 was traveling between 51 and 60 MPH at the time of impact. V1 was towed due to damage.
