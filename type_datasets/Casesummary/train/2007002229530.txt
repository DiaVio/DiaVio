This crash occurred on a straight two lane undivided roadway with a posted speed limit of 64KMPH (40MPH). It was cloudy and the roadway was dry. This crash occurred on a weekday afternoon. Traffic flow was normal for that time of day.
Vehicle #1, a 1996 Honda Accord was traveling south on the two lane roadway when it impacted with Vehicle #2, a 2002 Mitsubishi Gallant, in the rear right, with its front left. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Both vehicles were towed from the scene due to damage. Both vehicles came to final rest on the southbound lane facing southwest.
Vehicle #1, the Honda Accord, was driven by a belted 16-year-old female who was not injured. This driver stated she had been looking at the bagel store to see if it was still open, as she was hungry after working her after school job. She stated she did not notice the vehicle in front of her had stopped until too late and she hit it. She stated she used her brakes and pulled to the right but was too close to stop.
Vehicle #2, the Mitsubishi Gallant, was driven by a belted 28-year-old female who was transported to an area hospital, treated and released. This driver refused to be interview stating merely that 'it wasn't her fault'.
Police did not consider alcohol or illegal drugs to be a factor in this crash and ordered no tests for either driver.
