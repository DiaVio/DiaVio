This two-vehicle collision occurred during morning daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred at the three-leg intersection of a north-south main roadway, and an east-west side street, which intersected the east edge of the main road. The speed limit on the main roadway was 56 kmph (35 mph); the speed limit on the side street was 40 kmph (25 mph). At the time of the crash, it was cloudy with light fog, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Honda Accord four-door sedan, was traveling westbound in the first lane of a two-lane, two-way, undivided, dry, level, bituminous roadway, approaching the intersection with the intention of turning left. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Honda Civic four-door sedan, was traveling northbound in the second lane of a five-lane, two-way, undivided, dry, level, bituminous roadway, approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. As V1 made its turn, the front of V2 impacted the left side of V1. Both vehicles deflected northwest and came to rest in the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
