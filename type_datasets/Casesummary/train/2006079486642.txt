This two-vehicle collision occurred on a weekday evening, after dark. Streetlamps illuminated the scene at the time. The collision occurred at a four-leg intersection. The north/south roadway has five lanes including a left turn pocket. The roadway is dry, level and made of asphalt. The roadway is designated 56 kmph (35 mph). The intersection is controlled by a tri-light signal, which was working properly at the time. There were no adverse weather conditions and the roadway was free of defects in the area of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2007 Honda Civic 4-door sedan, was traveling southbound in the third lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of turning left. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Geo Prizm 4-door sedan, was traveling northbound in the number two lane, approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. As V1 made its turn, the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. Both vehicles deflected north to final rest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
