This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged intersection. Both roadways are undivided, level and straight and consist of one lane in each direction with parking on both sides. The eastbound and westbound approach roadways are controlled by stop signs. The speed limit on this roadway is 40 kmph (25 mph). The speed limit on the north/south roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). There is no traffic control for this roadway.
This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Geo Prizm, was southbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Mazda Protégé, was eastbound in lane one. In the intersection, the front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V2 rotated counterclockwise and the left front wheel struck the curb in the southeastern corner of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage.
The driver of V1, a thirty-seven-year-old female, was traveling from her home to a bank. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle. This driver travels this route daily.
The driver of V2, a forty-five-year-old female, had left her home and stated that she was "just driving around." She was in good health, well rested and had driven this vehicle infrequently in the past three months. She rarely drives on this roadway. This driver stated that she was focused on a vehicle across the street traveling in the opposite direction.
