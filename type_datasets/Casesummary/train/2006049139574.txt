This crash involving one vehicle in the early morning in daylight at the intersection of a two-way, four lane (six overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier and a two-way, five lane overall, divided roadway without a positive barrier. Both roads were dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph). Traffic was very light at this time.
Vehicle one (V 1) was a 2005 Chrysler 300 Sedan driven by a 37 year-old male. It was eastbound in the first travel lane. The driver of V 1 was in a right turn only lane when he fell asleep. V 1 departed the roadway onto a traffic island, struck a signal light and crossed the north-south lanes of the intersection. V 1 came to rest facing east in the southbound lanes south of the intersection. V 1 was towed from the scene no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
