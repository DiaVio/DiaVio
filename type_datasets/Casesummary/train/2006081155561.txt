A two-vehicle crash occurred a few hours before mid-night during dark hours. The collision occurred in the northwest area of a busy intersection. Traffic controlled by tri-color traffic signal. The north-south section of the roadway is a 7 lane, two-way roadway divided by a center raised curb median. The east-west section of the intersection is a three lane, two-way roadway with bituminous paving straight and level. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Buick Regal sedan driven by a male with one passenger, was traveling west in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1979 Ford Pickup driven by a 24 year-old male, was turning from east to head north. V1 had a green light and went straight through the intersection when V2 turned left in front of V1. The front of V1 impacted the right front of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
