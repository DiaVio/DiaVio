This three vehicle daytime collision occurred in the very early after noon hours. The collision occurred heading northbound on a four lane undivided roadway. The pre-crash roadway for all vehicles is straight with an uphill grade of 5.7% on paved concrete in the lane of the crash. The roadway has a speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). The crash occurred after an intersection with traffic signals and pedestrian crossings, but this intersection was not a factor in the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Ford E-350 cargo van, driven by a 46 year-old male was traveling northbound on the roadway in lane 2. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Ford Thunderbird coupe, driven by a 23 year-old male was traveling northbound and stopped in lane 2. Vehicle three (V3), a 2004 Subaru Forester compact utility vehicle, driven by a 39 year-old male was traveling northbound and stopped in lane 2. V3 stopped for an emergency vehicle that was turning left off the same road. V2 stopped behind V3 and they both remained stopped for approximately 5 seconds while the emergency vehicle turned. V1 did not stop and its front struck the rear of V2, pushing V2 into V3. None of the drivers claimed injuries, and only V2 was towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
