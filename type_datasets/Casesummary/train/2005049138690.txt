The accident occurred at 1945 hours at the intersection of a two-way, three lane (six overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier and a one way, two lane roadway. Both roads were unlighted, dry, traveled, level concrete. Involved were one passenger car and a pick-up truck. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions.
V 1 a 1998 Honda Civic driven by a 28 year-old male was turning left from the third northbound travel lane to the westbound roadway. V 2 a 2004 Ford F-150 pick-up truck driven by a 53 year-old male with one passenger was approaching the intersection southbound in the third travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right front by the front of V 2. V 1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing west. V 2's path of travel was redirected to the to the southwest and came to rest partially off the right side roadway facing south. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
