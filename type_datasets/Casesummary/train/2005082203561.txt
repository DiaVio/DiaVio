This two vehicle daytime collision occurred shortly before noon. The collision occurred in a controlled intersection with a stop sign for the north-south roadway and no stop sign for the east-west roadway. The east-west roadway is straight with a downhill grade of -16% grade, paved with concrete and bituminous in the lane of the crash. There is a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), an unknown make and model, was traveling north in lane one, Vehicle two (V 2), a 1997 Chevrolet Geo Metro coupe, driven by a 41 year-old male was traveling westbound in lane 1. Both vehicles approached the intersection at roughly the same time, and the front of V2 collided with the right side of V1. V1 left the scene. V2 was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
