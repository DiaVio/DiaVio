This crash occurred just before midnight involving two passenger cars at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 64 kmph (40 mph). The road is a 4-lane divided (no positive barrier) asphalt roadway for both vehicles. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash. The intersection was illuminated at night.
V 1, a 2003 Chrysler PT cruiser driven by a 18 year-old female was traveling south in the third lane. V 2 a 1983 Oldsmobile Cutlass Cierra driven by a 41 year-old male was traveling north attempting a left turn from the fourth lane of traffic at the intersection the front of V 2 impacted with the left side of V 1. V 2 rotated counter clockwise and moved further south. It came to rest within the intersection facing southeast. V 1 rotated counter clockwise after the impact, and came to rest within the intersection, facing west. V 1 was towed from the scene. The driver was transported to a local medical facility treated and released. V 2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
