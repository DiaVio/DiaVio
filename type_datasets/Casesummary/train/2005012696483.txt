V1, a 1995 GMC Van was traveling northbound on a three lane, one way, wet, concrete roadway in dark-lighted conditions. . V2 was a 1996 GMC Van who was traveling in lane two on the same roadway. V1 was in lane three when he noticed the cars ahead of him were slowing down. V1 changed lanes to the right, but then noticed that the cars in that lane were also slowing down. He applied the brakes but could not avoid contacting its front to the back of V2. V2 then spun around counterclockwise and contacted its left side to V1's front. V2 then contacted its left side to a light pole before coming to final rest on a snow bank up against the barrier wall.
The driver of V1 was a 42-year old male who had reported that he had been traveling between 82-97kmph (51-60mph) prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling home. He was wearing his prescribed lenses that corrected a myopic (near-sighted) condition. He was uninjured in the crash and refused treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 33-year old male who had reported that he had been traveling between 98-112kmph (61-70mph) prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and taking members of the church home. He was wearing his prescribed lenses that corrected a hyperopic (far-sighted) condition. He was uninjured and refused treatment.
