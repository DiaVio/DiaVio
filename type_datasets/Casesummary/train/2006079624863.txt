This three vehicle crash occurred late afternoon during daylight hours. The collision occurred on a four-leg intersection comprised by a north/south roadway and an east/west roadway. The north/south roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of five lanes (including a left-turn lane). The east/west roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of two lanes. Both roadways are designated 56 kmph (35 mph) zones. The intersection is controlled by a tri-light traffic signal that was working properly at the time of the collision.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Lincoln Navigator sport utility vehicle driven by a 40 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling northbound in the number two lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Honda Pilot sport utility vehicle driven by a 26 year-old female with three passengers, was traveling southbound in the number three (left-turn) lane. Vehicle three (V3), a 1993 Toyota Corolla sedan driven by a 19 year-old male with two passengers, was traveling southbound in the number three (left-turn) lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
As V2 made its left turn, the front plane of V1 contacted its right plane. V1 then traveled towards V3 The front plane of V1 contacted the front plane of V3. V2 after impact rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southwest blocking the westbound number one lane. V1 came to rest facing northbound against V3.
The driver of V1 explained to the police, that he entered the intersection on a green light. He also stated that the driver of V2 suddenly turned in front of him. The driver of V2 explained to the police, that she made her left-turn on a yellow light.
