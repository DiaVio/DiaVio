This two-vehicle collision occurred in a controlled intersection with a pedestrian signal. This is a roadway with one-way traffic for northbound and eastbound traffic. The pre-crash roadway consists of bituminous paving with a level roadway for both vehicles. Parking is allowed on the roadway on both sides of the north/east lanes. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 48 kph (30mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 1996 Honda Passport was northbound in the second lane. Vehicle two, a 2001 Saturn SC was eastbound in the first lane. The front of vehicle one impacted the right of vehicle two. Both vehicles came to rest in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a twenty-three year old female was not interviewed. The PAR indicates that this driver stated that right before the crash she looked at a pedestrian. It is unknown which side of the street this pedestrian was on.
The driver of vehicle two, a thirty-three year old female was not interviewed.
