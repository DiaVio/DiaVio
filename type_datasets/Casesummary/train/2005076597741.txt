This two-vehicle vehicle rear-end type crash occurred at a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west lanes have a center left turn lane and right turn lanes. This roadway is straight with a slight down grade in the eastbound direction. It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash with no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1979 Dodge D-50 pickup, was eastbound in lane two, approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Chevrolet Suburban, was stopped in lane two. The front of V1 struck the back of V2. V1 came to rest in the west entrance heading in a northerly direction.V2 came to rest to the east of this intersection facing east. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1 and 72 kmph for V2.
