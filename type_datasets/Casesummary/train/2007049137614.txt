This three-vehicle crash occurred on a four-lane, undivided, east/west roadway at the intersection with a four-lane, undivided, north/south roadway. Both roadways were straight, dry and of concrete construction. The westbound and northbound travel lanes have a negative grade. Overhead traffic control lights control traffic at the intersection. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). Traffic was light at the time of the crash. It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Hyundai Accent, was westbound in the second travel lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Honda Civic, was eastbound in the first travel lane. Vehicle three (V3), a 2006 Ford Fusion, was stopped in the first travel lane facing north. V1 attempted a left turn and the front struck the front of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the intersection facing southeast. V2 rotated clockwise and the front of V2 struck the left side of V3. V2 came to rest facing southeast in the intersection. V3 stayed at rest at the point of impact. Both V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2, a twenty-two year old male, was heading from home to work. This driver had been driving for about five minutes, drives the route daily and is familiar with the vehicle. The driver detected V1 early but assumed the vehicle would yield the right of way at the intersection.
The driver of V3, a thirty-five year old female, was heading from home to the babysitter's. This driver had been driving for about two minutes, drives the route daily and is familiar with the vehicle.
