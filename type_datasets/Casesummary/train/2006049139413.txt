The crash involving two vehicles during daylight hours shortly after noon at the intersection of a two-way, four lane, undivided roadway and a two-way, four lane, undivided roadway. Both roads were dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). Traffic was light at the time of the collision. There were no adverse weather conditions. Traffic flow was controlled by stop signs.
Vehicle one (V1) a 2002 Cadillac Escalade was driven by a 25 year-old male was eastbound in the first travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1994 Toyota Camry driven by a 28 year-old male was southbound in the first travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the left side by the front of V 2. V 1 rotated clockwise, departed the roadway and it's front struck a pole on the southeast corner. V 1 came to rest facing southeast at the second point of impact. V 2 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing southeast in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, there were no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
