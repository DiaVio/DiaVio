This single vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours in a rural area on a 2-lane, dry, asphalt roadway. The westbound travel lane curved left with a speed limit of 72 kph (45 mph). with no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 GMC 4-door Jimmy SUV driven by a 24 year-old female was traveling westbound when it departed the right road edge while driver was retreiving a fallen soda cup. V1 steered left and returned to the roadway. It then departed the original travel lane, braked and steered right and departed the right road edge again in a clockwise rotation where it tripped, flipped and rolled 5 quarter turns. V1 came to rest on its left side on the westbound roadside facing east. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The pre-event movement coded negotiating a curve, category this vehicle traveling off the edge of the road on the right side. Critical reason for the critical event "Overcompensation".
Associative factor, inattention- reaching to the floor for fallen soda cup.
