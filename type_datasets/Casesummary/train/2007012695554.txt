The crash occurred off the left side of a two lane undivided roadway. This was a bituminous roadway with a level grade and a posted speed of 89 kmph (55 mph). This roadway curved left with a radius of 1406m and a superelevation of 3 percent. It was late morning on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was cloudy. Traffic was flowing.
V1, a 2002 GMC Envoy compact utility vehicle with one occupant, was traveling southbound in lane one. The driver of V1 experienced a diabetic related blackout. Witnesses reported that V1 departed the roadway on the right side and contacted its right to a mailbox a few miles back. V1 then reentered the roadway and regained stability. V1 traveled approximately another mile before almost contacting another vehicle. The other vehicle pulled off the roadway into a ditch to avoid V1. V1 then departed the roadway on the left side and traveled approximately 120meters before contacting its undercarriage to an embankment where it came to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1 was a 64-year old female. She reported injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. She stated that she had just left her eye doctor's and was on her way to another doctor, when she realized that she had made a wrong turn. She could not remember what happened after that due to a blackout caused by low blood sugar.
