This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-way intersection. The south leg of the intersection has one southbound lane and three northbound lanes including a left turn lane. The north leg of the intersection has two lanes in each direction; the two northbound lanes include a left turn lane. The intersection roadway runs northeast by southwest in both directions. The northeast leg is comprised of one northwest lane and three lanes going in a southwest direction including a left turn lane. The southwest leg has two lanes running northeast; one of them is a left turn lane, and one lane heading southwest. The speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one, a 1995 Geo Prism was traveling northbound in the first lane. Vehicle two, a 1985 Buick La Sabre was traveling southwest in the first lane. Vehicle one proceeded through the intersection and the front of vehicle one impacted the left of vehicle two. Vehicle two deflected to the northwest and its front impacted a pole. Vehicle two came to rest against the pole. Vehicle one came to rest in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of vehicle one, a sixty-year-old male was not interviewed. This driver is not licensed.
The driver of vehicle two, a nineteen-year-old male was driving home after work. It is unknown how long this driver had been driving. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
