This crash occurred on a divided two-lane southbound interstate. The concrete roadway curved to the right with a 400m radius of curvature. There was a downhill grade of 3 percent and a super elevation of 5percent. The speed limit was 89 kmph (55 mph). The crash occurred on a weekday in the late afternoon during daylight hours.
Vehicle 1 was a Mini Cooper of an unknown model year. Vehicle 2 was a 2003 Nissan Murano utility vehicle. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1 and No PAR obtained (created) for V2.
Vehicle 1 was traveling south in the right lane and Vehicle 2 was traveling south in the left lane. As Vehicle 1 changed lanes to the left, the left of Vehicle 1 contacted the right of Vehicle 2. Both vehicles lost control and departed the right side of the travel lanes. Vehicle 1 came to rest on the right roadside facing west with its front end under a guardrail. Vehicle 2 came to rest on the right shoulder facing north.
