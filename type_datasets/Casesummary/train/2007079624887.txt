This two vehicle early morning daytime collision occurred at a four-leg intersection comprised by an east/west roadway and a north/south roadway. The east/west roadway is undivided, dry, level, and asphalt. The east leg consists of four lanes. The west leg consists of three lanes. The north/south roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of five lanes. Both roadways are designated 56 kmph (35 mph) zones. The intersection is controlled by on-color signals that were functioning properly.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Honda Element sport utility vehicle driven by a twenty two (22) year old male, was traveling northbound in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1991 Honda Accord station wagon driven by a forty eight (48) year old female, was traveling eastbound in the number one lane. As V1 entered the intersection, the front plane of V2 contacted its left plane. V1 came to rest facing the north curb, blocking the westbound number one lane. V2 came to rest facing northeast, blocking the northbound number one lane. V1 received moderate damage and was towed. V2 received major damage and was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
