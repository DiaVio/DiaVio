This single vehicle crash occurred during the early afternoon during daylight hours The location was a major arterial in an industrial area with numerous driveways. The roadway was a six lane, undivided, two-way and level with a dry bituminous surface. There are three lanes for southbound traffic, a center turn only lane and two lanes for northbound traffic. The weather was clear and dry and there were no sight restrictions. The posted speed limit is 80 kmph (50 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Toyota Camry sedan driven by a forty (40) year old female, was traveling southbound in lane two. The driver stated a mini-van in lane three swerved into her lane. She tried to steer right, but saw another vehicle approaching in lane one. She stated she swerved back left and applied the brakes. The driver lost control and V1 rotated in a counterclockwise yaw across all lanes. V1 struck the curb with both right side tires, jumped the curb and struck a utility pole. On impact V1 rotated clockwise off the pole and came to rest in a grass field facing west. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
