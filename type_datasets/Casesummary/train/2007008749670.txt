This crash occurred in the east/west lanes at a four-way intersection controlled by a traffic signal with no pedestrian signals. The roadway had two lanes of travel, was undivided, bituminous, and straight. The roadway had a downhill grade in the westbound direction and had a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). It was evening on a weekday, the roadway was dark but lighted, it was raining at the time, and traffic flow was moderate.
V1 was a 2006 Subaru Impreza 4-door sedan driven by a 48 year-old male. V2 was a 2006 Kia Optima 4-door sedan driven by a 34 year-old male. V2 had one female passenger in the right front seat. V1 was traveling eastbound in lane one; as it approached the intersection it turned left in order to proceed north. V2 was traveling westbound in lane one. When V1 turned left at the intersection it impacted the front of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
