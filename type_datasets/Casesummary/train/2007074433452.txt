This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged intersection. Both roadways are undivided and consist of one lane in each direction. The northbound approach is straight, has a downhill grade and is controlled by a stop sign. The speed limit on this roadway is 80 kmph (50 mph). The westbound approach is curved to the left and has a downhill grade. The speed limit on this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Dodge Ram, was stopped northbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Honda Civic, was westbound in lane one. V1 entered the intersection and the right side was struck by the front of V2. The vehicles rotated and the right side of V1 struck the left side of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-one-year-old male, was on his way from a friend's house to another friend's house. He was healthy, well rested and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route monthly. This driver stated that he looked left and right but did not see V2 prior to the crash.
The driver of V2, a thirty-five-year-old female, was on her way from a store to a friend's house. She was well rested, in good health and comfortable with her vehicle. She travels this route daily. This driver stated that she saw V1 stopped at the intersection. She applied the brakes and steered to the right when she realized that V1 was entering the intersection.
