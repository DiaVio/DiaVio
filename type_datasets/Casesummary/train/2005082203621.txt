This two vehicle daytime collision occurred during mid-afternoon daylight hours. The collision occurred in an uncontrolled intersection, this is a residential roadway with traffic in both directions. The pre-crash roadway consists of fresh concrete paving with a -9% downhill grade for east/west and a +5% uphill grade for north/south roadway. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Toyota 4-Runner, driven by a 36 year-old male was traveling eastbound on the roadway in lane 1. Vehicle two (V2), 1995 Toyota Camry, driven by a 49 year-old male was traveling northbound in lane 1. Both vehicles approached the intersection at roughly the same time, the front of V2 impacted the right side of V1 causing V1's rear to rotate clockwise, as V1 was rotating the left rear tire tripped the vehicle over causing it to roll several times. V1 came to rest on the right side facing southwest. V2 came to rest facing northeast. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 stated he didn't see anyone approaching the intersection, so he proceeded through it looking at his brother that was parked a block away. V2 also stated he did not see anyone coming, and that the trees and parked cars make it hard to see anything on that corner.
