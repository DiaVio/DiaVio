This crash occurred early evening involving two passenger cars at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 54 kmph (35 mph). The road is a 4-lane divided (no positive barrier) concrete roadway for northbound travel and a 5-lane roadway for southbound travel . The road surface is blacktop, which was dry. There were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1985 Buick Le Sabre Station wagon was traveling north in the second lane, crossing through the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Dodge Ram Pick up, was traveling south in the fifth lane turning left. V2 entered the intersection from this left turn lane. The front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V1 rotated clockwise and came to rest within the intersection. V2 rotated counter clockwise after the impact, and came to rest within the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was a 28 year-old male in good health. He was not in a hurry and his right front passenger both went to local hospitals by private transportation after the scene was cleared by police. V1 was towed from the scene. The driver of V2 was in good health and not in a hurry. V2 was driven away from the scene.
The driver of V2 was a 47 year old male who stated he did not see V1 at first because another vehicle was blocking his view of V1. He assumed that after the light changed V1 would yield him the right of way and he commenced with the turn. He was on his way home after grocery shopping.
