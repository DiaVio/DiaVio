The crash occurred mid-afternoon at the intersection of a two-way, two lane, undivided roadway and a two-way, two lane, undivided roadway. Both roads were dry, traveled, level asphalt. The posted speed limit for east/west is 48 kmph (30 mph).north/south 56 kmph (35 mph). Traffic was light at the time of the collision. There were no adverse weather conditions. Traffic was controlled by stop signs for the east/west traffic.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1995 Toyota Corolla driven by a 53 year-old female with one passenger. It was westbound in the first travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1999 Lincoln Town Car driven by a 23 year-old female with one passenger. It was northbound in the first travel lane. V1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was impacted on the left side by the front of V2. V1 then rotated clockwise and struck a bicyclist crossing the road heading south along the west curb. V1 came to rest facing south in the southbound lane. V2 came to rest facing north in the northbound lane. V1 was towed from the scene. The cyclist was treated by EMS and released. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
