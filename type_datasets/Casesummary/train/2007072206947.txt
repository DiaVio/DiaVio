This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged intersection. Both roadways were straight, level, undivided and consist of one through lane with parking on both sides. One of the roadways is one-way in the eastbound direction and the other is one-way in the southbound direction. The eastbound roadway is controlled by a stop sign at the intersection. The speed limit for these roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Ford Contour, was westbound on the eastbound roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Ford Contour, was southbound. In the intersection, the front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V2 rotated and the back struck the stop sign post. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a twenty-eight-year-old female, was on her way home from a friend's house. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. This was her first time driving this route. This driver stated that she did not realize that she was on a one-way roadway and did not see V2 prior to the crash.
The driver of V2, an eighteen-year-old female, was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
