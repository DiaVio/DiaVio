A crash involving two vehicles occurred during early afternoon hours at the intersection of: a two-way, four lane (seven overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier: and, a two-way, four lane (seven overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier. The road for both drivers was dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 64 kmph (40 mph) for both driver. Traffic was light at the time of the accident. There were no adverse weather conditions. Traffic flow controlled by a traffic signal.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2005 Kia Rio Sedan driven by a 22 year-old male that was northbound in the fourth travel lane waiting to turn left at the intersection. Vehicle two (V2) was a 2001 Cadillac Sedan DeVille driven by a 60 year-old male that was southbound in the first travel lane. V1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing southwest in the westbound lanes. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southwest in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
