This two-vehicle crash occurred shortly after noon on a Thursday. The location was an urban area with commercial business. The roadway was a bituminous five lane state route with two lanes in each direction and a center left turn only lane. There are curbs and sidewalks on both sides of the road. The weather was clear and dry. It was daylight with no visibility problems. The posted speed limit 72 kmph (45 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2003 Chevrolet Blazer LS 4 door SUV, was exiting a fast food parking lot intending to cross both northbound lanes, to turn left and go southbound. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Yamaha YZF motorcycle, was traveling northbound in the inside lane approaching V1's location. As V1 pulled across the northbound lanes, it struck the left side of V2 with it's front. On impact the driver of V2 was thrown from the vehicle. V1 and V2 were both towed due to damage. Both drivers were transported to a medical facility for treatment. No other information is available. There were no interviews and no police report was obtained. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
