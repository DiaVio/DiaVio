This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west trafficway is straight, level and divided by a curbed median. The eastbound approach lanes consist of three through lanes and a left turn lane. The westbound approach lanes consist of three through lanes, a left turn lane and a right turn lane. The speed limit for this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). Traffic was congested and it was dark with streetlights on with no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Chrysler Town and Country, was eastbound in lane four. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Buick LeSabre, was westbound in lane three. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1 was an eighty-four-year-old male and the driver of V2 was a nineteen-year-old male traveling with a twenty-seven-year-old female passenger. No one was interviewed; no further information is available for these drivers.
