This two vehicle nighttime collision occurred in a parking lane of a four-lane roadway. The road has four lanes of regular two-way travel paved with concrete and is straight and level in the area of the crash. The speed limit for the road is 56 kmph (35 mph).The sky was clear and there were no adverse conditions.
Vehicle one, (V1), a 2005 Acura RSX coupe driven by a twenty eight (28) year old male, was traveling south in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2007 Volkswagen Passat sedan, was legally parked in the southbound parking lane. V1 traveling in lane one departed the lane to the right and impacted the rear of V2 in the parking lane. V2 was pushed forward and impacted a second parked pick-up truck before coming to final rest. V1 and V2 came to rest approximately at the point of impact. V1 was towed do to damage. V2 was left on-scene and not towed. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
