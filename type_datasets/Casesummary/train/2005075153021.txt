This single vehicle crash occurred early evening dark hours on a two lane undivided curve left asphalt, snow and ice present roadway. With a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph) and an advisory limit of 32 kmph (20 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) a 1994 Jeep Cherokee Laredo driven by a 17 year-old male with one passenger was traveling northeast in lane one, the driver had just finished negotiating a left curve when he lost control and traveled off the right side of the roadway. It's front contacted a wooden fence rotated counterclockwise and reentered lane one. It than began to rotate clockwise departed the roadway to the right and contacted the same fence, coming to final rest facing south. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1.
