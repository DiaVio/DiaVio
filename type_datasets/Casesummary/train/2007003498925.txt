The crash occurred on a one-way roadway with one lane of travel. There was parking on both sides of this roadway. The roadway was straight and level, bituminous with a speed limit of 48kmph (30mph). It was dark but lighted, and clear with no adverse conditions. It was the evening hours of a weekday.
V1, a 1999 Mercedes-Benz C280 four door with three occupants, was traveling northbound in lane one. V2, a parked and unoccupied 2002 Chevrolet Tahoe sport utility vehicle, was parked in the eastern parking lane. V3, a parked and unoccupied Daewoo Leganza four door, was parked in the western parking lane. The front of V1 impacted the back of V2, and then crossed over into the western parking lane and the front of V1 contacted the back of V3. Then the right side of V2 impacted a wood utility pole approximately 10 meters north of the point of impact. V1 came to a final rest contacting the back of V3. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Mercedes-Benz, was driven by a 14 year-old male who reported incapacitating injuries and was transported to a medical facility. A 13 year-old male occupant reported no injuries, an 11 year-old male occupant reported minor injuries and were transported to a medical facility. V1 was reported stolen and the driver was unlicensed. He attempted to flee the scene after the crash but was unable to go far due to his injuries.
