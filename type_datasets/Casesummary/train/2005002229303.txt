This crash occurred on a two lane undivided roadway, slightly curving to the right prior to the crash site, with no grade and a posted speed limit of 89 KMPH (55MPH). the weather was clear and the roadway was dry. This crash occurred on a weekday afternoon.
Vehicle #2, a 1990 Ford Mustang, was traveling south on the two lane undivided roadway when it proceeded to pass a box truck (as per the driver of vehicle #1). It impacted with Vehicle #1 a 2000 Pontiac Grand Am, head on in the northbound lane. Vehicle #2 came to rest on the east shoulder facing south. Vehicle #1 came to rest on the east shoulder facing north. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1 and No PAR obtained (created) for V2.
The Ford Mustang (Vehicle #2) was driven by a 20 year old male who was flown to an area trauma center with head neck and back injuries. No interview was obtained as researcher was unable to locate or contact this driver.
The driver of the Pontiac Grand Am (Vehicle #1) was a 49 year old belted male who was flown to an area trauma center. This driver stated that Vehicle #1 came from behind a box truck into his lane. It should be noted here that the driver of this vehicle stated he has a heart condition for which he takes prescription medications. Both vehicles were towed from the scene.
