This single vehicle crash happened on a clear weekend morning on the southbound lanes of a divided highway. The bituminous, dry, straight, and level highway is divided by a concrete barrier and has a posted speed limit of 97kmph (60 mph). There were no adverse atmospheric factors in the daylight conditions.
V1, a 2004 Cadillac Escalade, was southbound in an unknown lane traveling at 129kmph (80 mph) when it drifted off the left side of the road and struck the concrete barrier. The vehicle then bounced back into the roadway and rolled onto its roof (2 quarter turns). V1 slid on its roof for over 100 meters across all the travel lanes and departed the right side of the road where it struck a steel guardrail. V1 slid and another 20 meters and came to final rest on its roof in southbound lane one. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1.
V1 was driven by a 39-year-old-male that was arrested for driving while influenced. The driver refused a sobriety test and was arrested and taken to the county jail. His right front passenger suffered non-incapacitating injuries and was transported from the scene. The driver, passenger, and a witness listed on the crash report could not be reached for an interview.
