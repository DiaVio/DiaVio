The crash occurred on the westbound lanes of an elevated one-way roadway with three-lanes. On the left side of the roadway there is concrete wall and on the right side there is a guardrail. The roadway had a posted speed limit of 80kmph (50 mph). The road was wet and it was daylight with a slight drizzle. The roadway had no grade and it was straight and level. The pre-crash of this roadway was curved left.
Vehicle # 1 was a 1999 Nissan Quest which was traveling on the right lane when the right/front tire and wheel contacted the raised curb on the right side of the travel lane, and when the tire blew, the front contacted the guardrail. The vehicle came to final rest on the right lane after impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
