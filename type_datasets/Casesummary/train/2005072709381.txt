This two-vehicle crash occurred just past noon on an East/West expressway. The expressway has four straight and level travel lanes in each direction separated by a median wall; at no point did west bound lanes play a part in the crash. The speed limit is 92 kmph (55 mph), road surface concrete and there were no roadway or weather restrictions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 Freightliner tractor with a Great Dane trailer, traveling eastbound in the first lane and occupied only by its driver. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1995 Acura Integra with one occupant was traveling eastbound behind V1. There was an other vehicle, a 1995 Buick Skylark with one occupant, traveling eastbound in the first lane in front of the V1. V1 hit the other vehicle in a previous crash that occurred seconds before this qualifying crash. After V1 stopped due to this first crash, V2 struck the back of V1's trailer. All vehicles were traveling in congested traffic conditions. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
