This single-vehicle crash occurred on a clear morning in daylight. The crash occurred on a rural two lane roadway with no center line that curves to the left for northbound travel.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Toyota Solara coupe driven by a 32 year-old female, was traveling northbound negotiating a left hand curve. V1's right wheels departed the right road edge and the driver steered left and re-entered the roadway. V1 then departed the left side of the travel lane and the driver steered right and over corrected causing the vehicle to go into a clockwise yaw. V1 departed the right road edge again and struck two trees with the front plane coming to final rest off of the right road edge facing east. V1 was towed from the scene; the driver refused medical treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
