The crash happened at 1134 hours. The location was a five lane divided (no positive barrier) straight roadway surface dry concrete at a lighted intersection. Involving three vehicles with a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). Traffic signal controlled traffic flow,the weather was clear and the road was dry.
V 1, a 1996 Mercedes C 220, driven by a 36 year-old female was traveling west in the third lane of traffic, approaching the intersection. V 2, a 2001 Chevrolet Blazer driven by a 35 year-old male was traveling west in the third lane. V 3, a 1999 Toyota Corolla driven by a 47 year-old male was slowing down to stop for traffic signal. V 1 was approaching the intersection when light was turning red. V 2 and V 3, slowed down. The driver of V 1 applied her brakes V 1's front impacted the rear end of V 2. V 2 was pushed forward into the rear of V 3. All vehicles came to rest within lane 3. V 1 was towed from the scene. The driver was treated at the scene. V 2 and V 3 were driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
