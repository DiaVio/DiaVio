This single-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours on a physically divided, wet, right-curve roadway. This roadway runs east and west at the location of the crash and has two through travel lanes for westbound travel. The crash occurred on a bridge overpass that crossed north and southbound traffic. The speed limit on this roadway on the bridge is 89 kmph (55 mph) and the weather conditions at the time were cloudy with light rain.
Vehicle one, a 1982 Honda Civic, was westbound in lane one, traveling at the posted speed limit. The vehicle traveled off the right side of the roadway and the front struck a guardrail, the right rear wheel struck the curb and then the back right side struck the guardrail. The vehicle re-entered the highway, crossed both lanes and then traveled off the left side of the roadway. The front struck the bridge rail and vehicle one came to rest facing southwest in lane two. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.
The driver of vehicle one, a forty-four-year-old female, was driving from her home to her friend's home. She stated that it is a two-mile trip that she has made many times. The actual distance from her home to her stated destination is approximately twenty miles. At the time of the crash, the driver admitting to having consumed four glasses of wine prior to leaving on this trip. Her blood alcohol test result was .373; this test was done 1.12 hours after the time of the crash. Additionally, the driver was not wearing her prescription lenses at the time of the crash.
