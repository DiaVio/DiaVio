Case 2007-151
The crash occurred on an five-lane east/westbound roadway with two lanes in each direction and a left turn only lane in the middle. The asphalt roadway was straight, dry and level with a posted speed limit of 64kmph/40mph. There was an overhanging traffic control signal without pedestrian control about 25m east of the impact point. The crash happened at mid-afternoon on a weekday while traffic flow was moderate under clear sunny skies. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, a 1988 Mercury Grand Marquis four-door with five occupants was traveling eastbound in lane two. V2, a 2000 Honda Accord four-door with one occupant was stopped in lane two ahead of V1. V1 braked in an unsuccessful attempt to avoid impact. V1's front end struck the rear of V2 pushing it forward about two meters. Both vehicles came to rest facing east in lane two. V1 came to rest at the impact point. Only V1 was towed from the scene.
V1, the Mercury Grand Marquis, was driven by a 29-year old male. Neither he nor any of his passengers were injured. The driver reported that he was traveling approximately 56kmph/35mph in route from home to his mother's home. He said he was not distracted or talking to anyone. He added that about two seconds before impact he saw V2 stopped ahead of him for no apparent reason. He said he tried to brake but skidded and ran into the rear of V2.
V2, the Honda Accord, was driven by a 72-year old female who was not injured. She reported that she had stopped behind other vehicles stopped ahead of hers for the traffic signal at the next intersection. She said she was not aware that she was going to be struck and she took no evasive action.
