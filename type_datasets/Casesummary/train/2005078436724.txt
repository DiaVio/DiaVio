This two-vehicle rear end type crash occurred in the number one lane of three-lane, north/south, dry bituminous asphalt roadway. The roadway has one southbound and one northbound lane separated by a continuous left turn lane. The crash occurred just before noon in daylight hours. The speed limit on the roadway where the crash occurred is posted 56 kmph (35 mph). There were no environmental factors that contributed to this crash. There were no sight line restrictions or obstructions noted at the scene.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Ford F-150 pickup (V1), was traveling southbound in the number one lane at approximately 56 kmph (35 mph) when the V1 driver, a 45 year old male, looked to his left down a cross street at another truck similar to the one he was driving at the time. The front of V1 struck the rear of V2. V1 was then driven from final rest. There were no injuries reported by the V1 driver at the scene. V1 was driven from the crash scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Chevrolet Aveo 4-door sedan (V2), was traveling southbound in the number one southbound lane when the driver apparently slowed down for a vehicle in front of her that was slowing to make a right turn into a neighborhood in the area. The rear of V2 was struck by the front of V1. The vehicle then came to rest in an unmarked area of the number one southbound lane. The driver of V2, a 29 year-old female, was transported from the scene by EMS where she complained of pain to her neck and back pain. V2 was towed from the scene due to damage.
