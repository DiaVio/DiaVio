This crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a 10 lane highway. There were five northbound lanes, and five southbound lanes that were divided by a positive barrier. This is a dry, straight, level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 105 kmph (65 mph). The exit ramp was closed at the point of impact.
V1, a 2003 Land Rover Range Rover was traveling north in lane one. V2, a 2006 Mitsubishi Lancer was stopped in northbound lane one. The right front of V1 made contact to the left rear of V2. V2 began to rotate clockwise and contacted a guardrail before coming to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, a 2003 Land Rover Range Rover, was driven by a 42 year-old female who was unable to be located for interview.
V2, a 2006 Mitsubishi Lancer, was driven by a 42 year-old female who refused to be interviewed.
