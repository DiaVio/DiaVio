This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred on a straight, level stretch of a five-lane, positively divided, concrete expressway. The speed limit in the area of the crash is 105 kmph (65 mph). A wide, paved shoulder borders the right-most travel lane. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Nissan Quest minivan, was traveling southbound in the first lane, approaching slower traffic ahead. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Mercedes-Benz C280 4-door sedan, was ahead of V1 in the same lane, decelerating. A marked state police cruiser was behind V1. The front of V1 impacted the back of V2. Both vehicles moved ahead and came to rest in their original lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is 80 kmph for V1 and 80 kmph for V2.
