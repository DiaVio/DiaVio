This two vehicle crash occurred mid-morning during daylight hours. The roadway was a concrete, five lane curve left divided interstate with paved shoulders on both sides and no rumble strip. The weather was clear and dry with no visibility problems. The posted speed limit is 97 kmph (60-mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Chevrolet Prizm four door sedan, driven by a 69 year-old female was traveling northbound in lane four. Vehicle one applied the brakes and swerved left to avoid a large rock in its travel lane. Vehicle one left it's travel lane to the left and struck the right side front of Vehicle two (V2), a 1992 Mazda MPV mini van, driven by a 67 year-old female with one passenger who was traveling northbound in lane five (HOV lane). On impact vehicle one was redirected back north and drove the vehicle to a controlled stop on the left shoulder. Vehicle two continued northbound with a disabled right front wheel. The vehicle departed lane five onto the left shoulder, returned back into lane five, then departed to the left shoulder again and came to rest on the shoulder approximately ninety meters north of the point of impact. Vehicle one was disabled and towed due to damage. Vehicle two had a restricted right front wheel. The wheel was replaced at the scene and the vehicle was driven away from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
