The north/south roadway is straight and divided by a curbed median. The southbound lanes have an uphill grade and consist of two through lanes and a left turn lane. The northbound lanes have a downhill grade and consist of two through lanes and a left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet Malibu, was southbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1999 Mercury Sable, was northbound in lane three, intending to turn left. The vehicles entered the intersection and the front of V1 struck the front of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a twenty-five-year-old female, was traveling from her home to work. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. This driver travels this route daily. She stated that she was late for work but was not in a hurry.
The driver of V2, a nineteen-year-old female, was traveling from her workplace to deliver a package to a customer. She was well rested, healthy and comfortable with her vehicle. This driver rarely drives this route. She stated that she was stopped at the light and when the green arrow illuminated, she assumed that V1 would stop, so she proceeded with her turn.
