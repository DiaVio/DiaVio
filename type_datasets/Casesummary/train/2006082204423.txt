This single vehicle nighttime collision occurred during the early evening hours. The collision occurred heading south on a one-way raised highway with three lanes of travel, where the highway returns to ground level. The pre-crash roadway is concrete paving with an unknown downhill grade and a gradual S-curve that curves right then left. Access to the roadway is fully controlled, with no junctions in the immediate area of the crash. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 80 kmph (50 mph).
Vehicle one (V 1), a 1998 Ford E-150 van, driven by a 55 year-old male was traveling south in lane three of three. V1 struck the left curb and scraped to a stop after the road leveled out. There were no other cars involved in the collision, and the driver was taken into custody for DUI. V1 was towed due to disabling damage. According to the police report, V1 was straddling lanes on the road before the crash and weaving between lanes. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
