The crash occurred late in the afternoon during daylight on a one-way, three lane roadway with a right curve. Involving two vehicles one passenger car and one mini van. The road was dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 105 kmph (65 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions. The roadway is lighted.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 Saturn LS2 driven by a 36 year-old female, was southbound in the second travel lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1999 Mercury Villager mini-van driven by a 42 year-old male, was southbound in the second travel lane. V2 began a counterclockwise longitudinal skid and was struck on the left side by the front of V 1. The cause of the loss of control is unknown. After impact V2 continued rotating counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing north. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in the roadway facing east. V1 was towed from the scene, there were no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
