This two-vehicle crash occurred during morning daylight hours on a clear day. The crash occurred on a five-lane state highway with a speed limit of 72 kmph 45 mph) that is crossed by a two-lane roadway that is controlled with a stop sign and also has a speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). The roadways were dry, level and bituminous. The two-lane run east to west and the five-lane north to south.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1994 Honda Civic, was westbound with one occupant attempting to cross the five-lane highway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Toyota Rav-4 was southbound with one occupant in lane one attempting to pass through the intersection. The front of V1 impacted with the left side of V2. V1 rotated slightly counterclockwise and then stopped at impact facing southwest. V1 was driven from the scene. V2 veered right and the front right of the vehicle struck a utility pole located in the southwest corner of the intersection where it came to rest. V2 was towed from the scene. Both drivers refused treatment by EMS. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
