This single-vehicle crash occurred near a three-legged intersection. Both roadways are undivided, straight, level and consist of one leg in each direction. The speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). The north/south roadway is on the south side of the east/west roadway and the northbound travel lane is controlled by a stop sign. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Ford Windstar, was northbound in lane one. V1 traveled across the east/west roadway and off the north side. The front of V1 struck a dirt/grass embankment. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a thirty-five-year-old female, was on her way home from a store. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle. She rarely travels this route. This driver stated that she knew the intersection was ahead but it "snuck up" on her.
