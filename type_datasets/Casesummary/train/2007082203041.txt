This single vehicle nighttime collision occurred in the intersection of a five lane arterial road with a left hand turn lane and a four lane arterial road with a left hand turn lane. The road is paved with asphalt and is straight and level. Traffic flow controlled by on-color traffic signal. The posted speed limit is 64 kmph (40 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was cloudy with slight rain.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2007 Mitsubishi Galant sedan driven by a thirty seven (37) year old female with one passenger. V1 was traveling south in lane one. As V1 approached the intersection, the driver locked up the brakes for an unknown reason and began to head towards the south/west corner of the intersection. V1 departed the roadway and impacted a utility pole with its front. V1 come to final rest facing south. V1 was towed due to damage and both driver and passenger were transported due to injury. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver was unable to be interviewed after numerous attempts.
Witness #1 stated that he was parked on the north/west corner in the gas station. He had watched her speed through the intersection, heard her lock up her brakes let them off and then reapplied them just before she impacted the pole. He also stated that there were no other vehicles in the intersection and she had a green light.
