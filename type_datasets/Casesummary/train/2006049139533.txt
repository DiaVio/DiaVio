The crash occurred at shortly after the noon hour on a two-way, two lane, undivided roadway The road was dry, traveled, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph). There were three involved vehicles that were part of a larger funeral procession. There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V 1) a 1998 Chevrolet Tahoe SUV driven by a 27 year-old female with three passengers was southbound in the first travel lane. Vehicle two (V 2) a 2001 Lincoln Navigator driven by a 50 year-old male with one passenger was southbound in the first travel lane ahead of V 1. Vehicle three (V 3) a 2005 Chevrolet Malibu driven by a 22 year-old female with two passengers was southbound in the first travel lane ahead of V 2. V 2 and 3 came to a stop in the roadway ahead of V 1. The front of V 1 struck the back of V 2. V 2 was moved forward and the front struck the back of V 3. All three vehicles came to rest in the original travel lane, facing south. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
