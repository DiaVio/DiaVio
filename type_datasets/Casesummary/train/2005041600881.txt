The crash occurred off the right side of a three-lane urban roadway at an intersection with a five-lane urban roadway. There were three lanes running westbound, with lane three being a left turn-only lane. The speed limit was posted as 56 kmph (35 mph) and traffic flow was normal. It was mid-afternoon on a weekday and the roads were dry roads and the sky was clear.
Vehicle #1 was a 2005 Honda Civic driven by a 26-year old male. He was traveling westbound in lane one going approximately 35 mph through the intersection when he started to have an epileptic seizure. He blacked out and his vehicle veered off the roadway to the right, drove onto a paved sidewalk and then into a building window. Luckily, he was not critically injured but he was transported to a hospital and his car was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 40 kmph for V1.
