V1, a 1997 Honda CRV, was westbound, accelerating from a traffic signal, in lane one of a 2 lane, 2 way, straight, bituminous roadway. V2, a 2003 Ford F-150 pickup truck, was stopped, westbound, in lane 1 of the same roadway west of the intersection that v1 was accelerating from. At the time of the crash it was daylight and a heavy thundershower had just ended leaving the roadway damp. V1, front, impacted v2, back. Neither driver was injured. V1 was towed due to damage.
