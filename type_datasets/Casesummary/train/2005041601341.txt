This crash occurred off the roadway to the right of the southbound lanes of high-volume, high-speed divided highway. The conditions were clear and dry on a weekend evening. The traffic flow was normal and the roadway was straight and level with a posted speed limit of 105 kmph (65 mph).
V1, a 2002 Daewoo Nubira station wagon, was southbound in lane one when it suffered an unknown mechanical problem causing the driver to lose control and depart the roadway to the right where he struck a ditch before coming to final rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
V1 was driven by a 70-year-old male in good health who was wearing his prescription lenses that correct a hyperopic (far-sighted) condition. He stated he was traveling about 72 kmph (45 mph) in lane one when he claimed his vehicle started to wobble and shake out of control causing him to leave the roadway. He was not injured but his vehicle was towed due to damage.
