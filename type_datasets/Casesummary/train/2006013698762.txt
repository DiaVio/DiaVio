The crash occurred on a three lane positively divided asphalt highway with a posted speed limit of 113 kmph (70 mph). The conditions at the time of the crash were as follows, snow, blowing snow, with very poor road conditions.
V1 a 1999 Ford Explorer was traveling south in the right curb lane at about 64 kmph (40 mph), when the driver stated that the four-wheel driver system malfunctioned. The driver then stated that the back end of the vehicle fishtailed out from under him and he lost control of the vehicle. V1 went off the roadway to the right side where it rolled to the right three-quarter turns coming to final rest on the driver's side. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
