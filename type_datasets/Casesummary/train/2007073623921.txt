This two-vehicle crash occurred at a driveway on a curbed median-divided, straight, level, east/west roadway. The driveway is on the north side of this roadway. This roadway consists of two lanes in each direction. The speed limit on this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Dodge Ram pickup, was stopped southbound in the driveway. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Chevrolet Malibu, was westbound in lane one. V1 entered the roadway, turning right and the front struck the right side of V2. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a fifty-two-year-old female, was traveling with three adult male passengers. She was healthy, well rested and comfortable with her vehicle and passengers. She rarely travels this route. This driver was on her way from taking her son-in-law's car to a body shop and then taking him to work. She was not in a hurry, but had had a hectic day and was thinking about her autistic son and her son-in-law's problems. This driver stated that she looked left but did not see V2.
The driver of V2, a twenty-two-year-old female, was not interviewed.
