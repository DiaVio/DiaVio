This two-vehicle crash occurred at night on a north/south, two lane, undivided, straight, level, bituminous, roadway with a posted speed limit of 40 kph (25 mph). Access to the roadway is not controlled. Parallel parking is allowed on both sides of the roadway in this residential area. At the time of the crash it was raining, the road was wet, and it was dark, but the road is illuminated with streetlights. Except for the rain, there were no adverse weather conditions, sight restrictions or road defects.
Vehicle #1 (V1), a light blue 1992 Chevrolet K2500 Pickup, was traveling north in lane one. . Vehicle #2 (V2) a maroon 1990 Toyota Corolla 4 door was stopped in the parking lane to pick up the passenger. The driver, a 22 year-old male, accelerated in the parking lane and attempted to enter the northbound lane. As V2 entered the travel lane the front of V1 struck the left passenger side of V2. Both vehicles moved together as they crossed the centerline of the roadway and came to rest in the southbound lanes, facing northwest. V1 was towed due to damage, and V2 was left at the scene. The V1 driver, a 44 year-old male, was arrested on-scene for driving under the influence Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
