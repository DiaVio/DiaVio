This two-vehicle crash occurred on an undivided, straight trafficway. This north/south road consists of one lane in each direction with parking lanes on both sides. The southbound lane has a 3.3 percent uphill grade. The speed limit on this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). It was dawn at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1982 Oldsmobile Delta 88, was southbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Ford F350 pickup, was parked on the west (right) side of the southbound lane. V1 traveled to the right and the front struck the back of V2. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a forty-four-year-old male, had just left his girlfriend's house and was on his way to pick up his son to take him to school. He was healthy and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route monthly. At the time of the crash, this driver had been awake for more than twenty-four hours except for a one-hour nap. This driver stated that he fell asleep and awoke after the crash.
