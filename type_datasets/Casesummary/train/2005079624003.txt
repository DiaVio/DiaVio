This two vehicle crash occurred early evening hours on a dark but lighted, seven-lane, roadway which was wet due to earlier rain: however, it was not raining at the time of the collision. This roadway runs east and west and has a curbed median with a left and right turn lane. The posted speed limit on this road is 56 kmph (25 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Acura Legend sedan driven by a 71 year-old male, was entering the roadway heading south from a driveway on the north curb. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Toyota Corolla sedan driven by a 26 year-old female, was traveling west in the number two lane. As V1 attempted its left turn, its left side was impacted by the front of V2. V1 rotated clockwise to face southwest at final rest. V2 came to final rest in a southwest direction next to vehicle one. Both vehicles came to rest in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
