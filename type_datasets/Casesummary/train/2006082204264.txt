This two vehicle daytime collision occurred at 0827 hours at an intersection. The collision occurred in a controlled intersection with a traffic control light with pedestrian signal. The roadway surface concrete, wet, straight and level. There is a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph) for both vehicles.
Vehicle 1 (V 1), a 1987 Dodge Pickup, driven by a 21 year-old female was traveling eastbound in lane 3 wanting to take a left hand turn at the intersection. Vehicle 2 (V 2), a 2002 Volkswagen Jetta sedan,driven by a 24 year-old female was traveling eastbound in lane 1. V 1 was in a two-way turn lane, after the driver realized there was no left hand turn and that the freeway entrance was on the right she began to change lanes. V 1 cut across lane 2 entering lane 1. The right side of V 1 impacted the front left corner of V 2 causing V 2 to deflect and head southeast. After passing through the intersection, the front right of V 2 impacted a light pole and came to rest. V 1 was driven from scene; V 2 was towed do to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
