This two-vehicle collision occurred in the early evening hours when it was dark but lighted. The collision occurred in a busy intersection with traffic flow controlled by overhead on-colors traffic signals. The east-west leg of the intersection has 6 lanes, and the posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph). The pre-crash roadway is straight and level, with bituminous paving. It was clear and dry.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1994 Mazda 626 sedan driven by a twenty nine (29) year old female, was traveling eastbound in lane three. V1 initiated a left turn to the northbound freeway on-ramp. Vehicle two (V2) was a 2000 Dodge Durango SUV driven by a thirty three (33) year old female with one passenger. V2 was westbound in lane two, when V1 turned in front of them without yielding. The front of V2 impacted the front right corner of V1. After the first impact V1 was redirected in a northerly direction and impacted a traffic barrier and came to final rest facing north. V2, after the lst impact, continued west and came to a controlled stop. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1's pre-crash factors are unknown, as she did not want to participate in our survey.
