This crash involving three vehicles occurred late in the morning during daylight hours at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). The road is divided with no positive barrier. In the northbound direction it is a 4-lane uphill roadway that curves to the right as it approaches the intersecting roadway. For southbound travel it is a 3-lane downhill roadway that curves to the left. The east leg of the "T" intersection is comprised of three lanes and curves left upon the west bound approach to the intersection . All road surfaces were concrete and dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1995 Chevrolet Tahoe driven by a 25 year-old male. He was traveling north in the number four left turn lane to turn left at the intersection. Vehicle two (V2) was a 1997 Chevrolet Venture minivan driven by a 32 year-old female with four passengers. They were traveling south in lane two of the southbound lanes, attempting to pass straight through the intersection. Vehicle three (V3) was a 1991 Acura Legend sedan driven by a 42 year-old male. V3 was stopped at the stop light in the west bound lane of the east leg of the intersection, waiting for the light to change to make a left turn into the southbound lanes. As V1 turned left in the intersection, the front of V2 impacted with the right front of V1. Both vehicles started to rotate and the left side of V2 impacted the right side of V1 in a side slap. V2 moved east and until the it's front impacted with the front of V3. Both vehicles came to rest at this point of impact. V1 continued to move eastward, crossing over a traffic island and a westbound left turn lane. V1 came to rest partially on the north curb of the left turn lane and partially on the roadway. All three vehicles were towed from scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
