This two vehicle daytime mid-morning collision occurred on a dry, but cloudy morning. The collision occurred on a seven lane, dry, level, asphalt roadway with a two-way left turn lane. The roadway runs east and west. The roadway is designated as a 56 kmph (35 mph) speed limit. There is a private driveway on the north curb that has three lanes consisting of left turn and right turn only lanes it also has one entrance lane. There is a stop sign for southbound traffic. On the north curb there are several trees, a power pole, and a light pole.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2006 Suzuki Aerio SX 4-door hatchback, driven by a fifty six (56) year old female with one passenger. V1 was traveling south in the number two lane of a private driveway. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2007 Toyota Yaris 4-door sedan, driven by a twenty nine (29) year old female with one passenger. V2 was traveling west in the number two lane going straight. V1 attempted a left turn out the private driveway. As V1 made the left turn, its left plane was impacted by V2's front plane. V1 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing northwest in the number two westbound lane. V2 continued west where it came to rest in the number one lane facing northwest. Both vehicles were towed with moderate damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The researcher was unable to reach either driver for interviews, so most precrash factors are unknown.
