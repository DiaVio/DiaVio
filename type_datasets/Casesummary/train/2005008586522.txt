This crash occurred on a four-lane asphalt roadway at the entrance of a highway on-ramp. The road was straight and level and the speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle 1 was a 1996 Dodge Caravan with one occupant. Vehicle 2 was a 1990 Chevrolet Lumina with one occupant. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
Vehicle 1 was traveling east in lane 2 approaching the intersection and vehicle 2 was turning left (south) from the westbound lane 2. Vehicle 2 turned left across the path of vehicle 1 and the front of vehicle 1 contacted the right of vehicle 2. Both vehicles came to rest at the southeast corner of the intersection, where the left of vehicle 2 contacted the curb and guardrail.
The driver of vehicle 1 was a 39 year-old female who stated that she was traveling east to go north onto the ineterstate. There was a vehicle coming the opposite direction and it turned right in front of her. The driver of vehicle 2 claimed that I had my turn signal on, but I did not.
