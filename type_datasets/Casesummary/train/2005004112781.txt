The crash occurred off the right side of a two-lane roadway with a posted speed limit of 50 MPH. Conditions were daylight with clear skies in the afternoon on a weekday.
V1 was a 1985 Oldsmobile Cutlass sedan traveling southbound in the first travel lane. The brakes failed and the vehicle drifted off the right side of the roadway and the front of V1 struck a tree. V1 came to final rest facing southwest against the tree. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1 was driven by an 82-year-old female who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital. The driver of V1 stated that she was traveling southbound in the right lane when she went to hit the brakes and they did not work. The driver of V1 stated that the brakes felt spongy and did not work when she pressed them firmly. The vehicle then swerved right, departed the roadway and struck a tree. V1 was traveling between 41 and 50 MPH at the time of impact. V1 was towed due to damage.
