V1, a 1998 Chrysler Cirrus was traveling northbound on a three-lane, one way, concrete roadway in dark conditions. V2, a 2004 freightliner was also traveling northbound in lane one on the same roadway. V1 was traveling in the second lane when the driver stated that she lost control and impacted its right to the left of V2. V1 then contacted its front to a concrete barrier wall before they both came to final rest in the roadway.
V1 was driven by a 36-year old female in good health who was rested and traveling home. She reported that she had been traveling 98-112kmph (61-70mph) prior to the crash. She and her passengers sustained no injuries and did not require treatment. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V2 was driven by a 40-year old male in good health who was rested and traveling home. He reported that he had been traveling 98-112kmph (61-70mph) prior to the crash. He was properly licensed and felt comfortable driving the semi. He was not injured and did not require medical treatment. V2 was driven from the scene.
