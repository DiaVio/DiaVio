The crash occurred in the southbound on-ramp on a interstate highway; there was one lane with two emergency paved shoulders and a concrete barrier on both the right and left sides. The posted speed was 105 KPH (65 MPH). Conditions were evening on a weekday with dry roads and clear skies.
Vehicle # 1 was a 1990 Honda Accord that was traveling on the entrance ramp going 89 KPH (55 MPH) when she stated that her car started to wobble and shake. She lost control causing her to make impact with the left concrete barrier, causing her car to rotate right. She came to rest facing northbound facing the on coming traffic. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The Honda was driven by a 19-year-old female who was transported to the local hospital with injuries. Her car was towed due to damages. An associated factor could be the driver's age and inexperience.
