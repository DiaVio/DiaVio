This three-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday. The crash occurred on a straight stretch of a three-lane, divided (six lanes overall, divided by a raised median), dry, level, bituminous roadway. This east-west roadway is designated a 56 kmph (35 mph) zone, and the first lane of each direction is bordered by a parking lane. At the time of the collision, there were no adverse atmospheric conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Geo Metro two-door hatchback, was traveling westbound in the first lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1988 Kenworth semi tractor-trailer, was legally parked along the north curb, ahead of V1. Vehicle three (V3), a 1996 Toyota Corolla four-door sedan, was traveling westbound, behind V1 in the same lane. V1 drifted into the parking lane, where the front of V1 impacted the back of V2's trailer. V1 rotated clockwise and re-entered the first lane, where the front of V3 impacted the back of V1. V1 and V3 came to rest in the first westbound lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
