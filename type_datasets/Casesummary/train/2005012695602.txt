V1, A 1999 Pontiac Sunfire, was pulling out of a commercial driveway onto a three lane, two way, dry, asphalt roadway in daylight conditions. V1 was making a left hand turn onto the roadway. V2 a 1989 Chevrolet Cavalier was traveling northbound on the same roadway. The driver of V1 stated that she could not see V2 approaching because of a SUV in the way. The driver of V2 stated that she saw V1, but did not think that she would pull out. The driver of V1 entered the roadway from the drive and the front of V2 contacted the left side of V1 before they both came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 21-year old female who had reported that she had been traveling between 2-16kmph (1-10mph) prior to the crash. She had no health related problems, and had taken no medication prior to the crash. She was rested and traveling home. She does not wear prescribed lenses. She did not sustain any injuries from the crash. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 17-year old female who had reported that she had been traveling between 82-97kmph (51-60mph) prior to the crash. She had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. She was rested and traveling home. She does not wear corrective lenses. She sustained minor injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility.
