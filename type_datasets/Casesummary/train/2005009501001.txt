This crash took place in the southbound lanes of a four lane divided roadway with a grass median. There were two southbound lanes and two turn lanes, the left turn lane beginning 85 meters back from a traffic signal-controlled intersection. Conditions were daylight and dry with overcast skies in the early afternoon on a weekday. The roadway had a posted speed limit of 80 kpmh (50 mph).
V2, a 1993 Oldsmobile Cutlass Ciera, was stopped for the traffic control signal in lane two behind a late model SUV. V1, a 1997 Dodge Ram Van was traveling south in lane 2 alongside another vehicle in the adjacent lane 1, both approaching the intersection. V1 was distracted by the driver of the vehicle in lane 1 and was not able to stop before making contact to the rear of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V1 was driven by a 53 year-old male who was not injured. The driver refused to be interviewed. The policeman at the scene stated that the driver of V1 was distracted by the driver of the vehicle in lane one and was tracking her via the right front window. V1's driver did not return his attention to V2 or the traffic ahead in time to stop.
V2 was driven by a 20 year-old male who was not injured. He had stated that he had come to a complete stop behind the SUV, and was watching the cross traffic pass through the intersection for 3-4 seconds before being struck. V2 was towed due to damage.
