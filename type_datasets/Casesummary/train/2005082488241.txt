This two vehicle daytime collision occurred during early morning hours. The collision occurred in a 4-way intersection with a 3-color control signal and pedestrian signals. The pre-crash roadway for both vehicles is straight and level paved with bituminous. There is a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph) for both vehicles.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1986 Toyota pickup, driven by a 23 year-old male who was heading east in lane 2 on his morning commute from home to work. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Volkswagen Jetta sedan, driven by a 41 year-old male who was traveling north in lane 2 who was also on his morning commute from home to work. Both vehicles entered the intersection at approximately the same time, and the front of V2 collided with the right side of V1. V1 then spun to the northeast corner of the intersection, facing southwest. V2 deflected to the northwest corner of the intersection, still facing north. Both airbags in V2 deployed, and the driver of V2 was transported to the hospital. V2 was towed due to damage, and V1 was left parked at the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
