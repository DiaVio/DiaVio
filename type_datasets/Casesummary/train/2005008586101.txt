This crash occurred at the intersection of a four-lane asphalt roadway and a parking lot entrance. It was mid-afternoon on a weekend. The posted speed limit was 56 kph (35mph.)
Vehicle 1 was a 2003 Buick Regal driven by an 80 year-old female. This vehicle was traveling east in the left turn lane. This lane was lane three of three eastbound lanes. Vehicle 2 was a 1997 Saturn SL2 driven by a 48 year-old male. This vehicle was traveling west in lane one. Vehicle 1 turned left at the intersection and the front of Vehicle 1 contacted the front of Vehicle 2. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
