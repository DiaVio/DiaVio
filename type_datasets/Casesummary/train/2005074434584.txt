This two-vehicle collision occurred in a four-way traffic signal controlled intersection. Although no adverse weather conditions were present at the time of collision, roadway was wet due to melting snow. The speed limit for both roadways is posted at 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1989 Ford F-150 pickup with one occupant, was traveling east in lane two of a three-lane, one-way, level, straight, and bituminous roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Oldsmobile Bravada SUV with three occupants, was traveling south in lane one of a two-lane, two-way, undivided, level, straight, and bituminous roadway. As both vehicles entered the intersection, the front of V1 impacted the right side of V2. V2 veered into oncoming lane as it crossed the intersection and drove onto a southeast sidewalk. V1 then contacted a small tree with left font bumper before coming to final rest. V1 veered into the southeast corner of intersection before coming to final rest. V1 was towed from the scene and V2 was driven. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
