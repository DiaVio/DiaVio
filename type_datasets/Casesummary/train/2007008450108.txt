The crash occurred in the northbound lanes of a four lane undivided roadway. There were two northbound and two southbound lanes. This was a left curve, level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 64 kmph (40 mph). It was early afternoon on a holiday weekday the road surface was wet from rain and the skies were cloudy. Traffic flow was light and moving freely.
V1, a 1998 Infiniti QX4 sport utility vehicle with one occupant, was traveling northbound in lane two behind V2. V2, a 1996 Mercury Sable with two occupants, was stopped for a traffic signal in lane two behind V3. V3, a 2007 Dodge Grand Caravan with one occupant was also stopped for the traffic light in lane two. V1 approached the stop light and did not stop, impacting V2 in the rear with the front of V1. V2 then impacted V3 with its front to V3's rear. The vehicles came to rest on the roadway in lane two. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V1, the Infiniti QX4, was driven by a 21 year old male who reported no injuries. The driver of V1 could not be reached for an interview, however the drivers of V2 and V3 stated he was driving without valid insurance.
V2, the 1996 Mercury Sable, was driven by a 72 year old male who did report injuries to himself or his passenger. The driver related he was taking his wife to the hospital for a check up from open heart surgery a month ago.
V3, the 2007 Dodge Grand Caravan, was driven by a 43 year old male who reported no injuries. The driver of V3 didn't see the vehicle coming and could do nothing further.
