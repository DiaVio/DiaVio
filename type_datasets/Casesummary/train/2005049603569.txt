This crash occurred on a Saturday evening involving two passenger cars at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadway is 48 kmph (35 mph). The road was a 3-lane divided (no positive barrier) curved concrete roadway for westbound approach to the intersection. It was a straight four-lane roadway for eastbound approach to the intersection. The eastbound fourth lane was a left turn lane with a left turn arrow at an intersection. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Chevrolet Equinox, was traveling east attempting a left turn from the fourth lane of traffic at an intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Honda Accord, was traveling west in the third lane. At the intersection, the front of V2 impacted with the right side of V1. V1 rotated counter clockwise and came to rest facing north. V2 rotated clockwise after the impact, and came to rest within the intersection facing northwest. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. No injuries were reported. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
