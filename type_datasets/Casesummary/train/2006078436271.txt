This two-vehicle collision occurred during pre-dawn hours on a weekend. The crash occurred at the four-leg intersection of a north-south roadway and an east-west roadway. The intersection is controlled by stop signs for northbound and southbound traffic, and the speed limit is 89 kmph (55 mph) in all directions. The south leg of the intersection is a three-lane, two-way, curved, level, bituminous roadway. The east-west roadway is a six-lane, two-way, curved, level, bituminous roadway. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the road was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Volkswagen Jetta 4-door sedan, was stopped facing northbound at the intersection in the second lane, and was waiting to turn left into westbound traffic. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Nissan Altima, was traveling eastbound and approaching the intersection with the intention of passing straight through. As V1 made its turn, the front of V2 impacted the left side of V1. The vehicles rotated towards each other and the right side of V2 contacted the left side of V1 in a side-slap configuration. The vehicles separated and came to rest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
