This crash occurred just after noon on a weekday in the southbound lanes of a divided (guardrail, grass median) highway. The north/south highway consisted of two lanes in each direction and featured a speed limit of 89kmph (55 mph). The bituminous roadway was dry, straight and level. Traffic flow was normal and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
V1, a 1992 Honda Prelude, was southbound in lane two. V2, an unknown vehicle was also southbound behind V1. As both vehicles continued south, V2 struck V1 in the right rear, the impact caused V1 to rotated counter-clockwise and strike the center guardrail with its front. V1 came to final rest against the guardrail near the point of impact. V2 left the scene of the crash without stopping. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a 22-year-old female who was uninjured in the crash. After numerous attempts she unable to be contacted therefore an interview was not obtained.
