This three-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. Both roadways are straight and level and consist of two lanes in each direction and a left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Dodge Caravan, was northbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2006 Ford Focus, was southbound in lane one. Vehicle three (V3), a 1995 Ford Contour, was stopped eastbound in lane three (left turn lane). V1 turned left in the intersection and the right side was struck by the front of V2. V1 rotated clockwise and the left side struck the front of V3. V1 and V2 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 29 kmph for V1 and 29 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a seventy-two-year-old male, was traveling with a fifty-seven-year-old female, from a gas station to his home. He was healthy, well rested and comfortable with his vehicle and passenger. He travels this route weekly. This driver stated that he saw V2 but thought that he had enough time to make his turn.
The driver of V2, a twenty-one-year-old female, was on her way to work from her home. She was well rested, in good health and familiar with her vehicle. She drives on this roadway daily. This driver stated that she saw V1 and applied the brakes in an effort to avoid a crash.
The driver of V2, a thirty-eight-year-old female, was traveling with a fifty-nine-year-old female, from a store to a homeless mission. This driver was healthy, well rested and comfortable with her vehicle and passenger. She travels this route daily.
