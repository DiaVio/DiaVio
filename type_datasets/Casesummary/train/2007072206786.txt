This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. Both roadways are straight, level and undivided. The northbound approach consists of two through lanes. The westbound approach consists of two through lanes and a left turn lane. The speed limit for these roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1988 Honda Civic, was northbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 1999 Mitsubishi Montero, was westbound in lane one. In the intersection, the front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V2 rotated counterclockwise, traveling to the northwest corner of the intersection. V2 rolled over to the right, the back struck a building and V2 continued to roll and rotate. The top of V2 struck a utility pole. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was a twenty-six-year-old male and the driver of V2 was a thirty-one-year-old female. Neither driver was interviewed. No further information is available for these drivers.
