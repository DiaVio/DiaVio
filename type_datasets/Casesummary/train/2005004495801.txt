Case Note: According to Police on scene; the driver of V1 has had similar crashes in the past and due to those crashes had voluntarily surrendered her license until recently.
The crash occurred off the right side of a two-lane roadway with a -2% downhill grade and a posted speed limit of 45 MPH. Conditions were daylight and clear in the afternoon on a weekday. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1 was a 2003 Buick LeSabre sedan traveling southbound in the first travel lane. The driver stated that the wheel slipped from her hand and the vehicle drifted off the roadway and the front of V1 struck a tree. V1 came to final rest facing southbound.
Associated factors that may have contributed to the crash were the driver's poor health, the numerous amounts of medications the driver was on, and conversing with the passenger.
