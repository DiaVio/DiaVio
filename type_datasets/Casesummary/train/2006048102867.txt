This rear-end type crash occurred during the early afternoon on a clear day. The crash occurred in the eastbound lanes of a two-lane, straight, level, and dry bituminous roadway. The posted speed limit is 89 kmph (55 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a white 2006 International Straight truck with only the 28 year-old male driver, was traveling eastbound behind vehicle two. Vehicle two (V2), a black 1999 Mercury Cougar two door hatchback with a 23 year old male driver and one passenger, was traveling eastbound ahead of V1 slowed and stopped to pull to the left side of the road, into a produce stand. V1 driver advised that he did not realize that V2 was stopped. The front of V1 struck the rear of V2. V1 after impact continued forward and the driver pulled to the left side of the roadway to final rest facing east. V1 was not towed and the driver was not injured. V2 was knocked forward and the driver steered to the left off of the roadway to final rest facing east. Both occupants sustained incapacitating injuries and were transported for treatment. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1 and 89 kmph for V2.
