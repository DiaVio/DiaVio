This single vehicle daytime collision occurred early evening on a north/south roadway. The roadway is undivided, dry, asphalt, consists of five lanes, curves left for southbound traffic, and has a -5.7% downgrade slope. The roadway is designated a 72 kmph (45 mph) zone.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1993 Honda Civic four-door sedan, driven by a seventeen (17) year old male with one passenger. They were traveling southbound in the number one lane. As V1 negotiated the curve, it began to rotate counter-clockwise. V1 traveled across all lanes towards the east curb. The left plane of V1 contacted the east curb. The left plane of V1 then contacted a light pole located east of the east curb. V1 came to rest facing northbound against the pole and was towed from the scene due to major damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
