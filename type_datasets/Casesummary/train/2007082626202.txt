This two-vehicle daylight collision occurred in the intersection of a three lane arterial roadway and a two-lane city street in the early evening hours. The northbound road is paved with asphalt and is straight with a downhill grade of - 6% . There was an uphill grade of 5% for southbound travel, which was also straight. The speed limit for the roadway is 40 kmph (25 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was cloudy with no adverse conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Toyota Corolla sedan, driven by a thirty two (32) year old male, was traveling north in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Toyota Camry sedan driven by a twenty six (26) year old male, was traveling south in lane three. V1 was waiting to make a left turn, entered the intersection and began its left hand turn. V2 then entered the intersection and the front left corner impacted the front right corner of V1. Both vehicles came to rest in the north/east corner of the intersection. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was unable to be located and interviewed after numerous attempts. The driver of V2 refused to be interviewed half way through.
