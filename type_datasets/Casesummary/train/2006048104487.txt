This two-vehicle crash occurred in the morning on a six-lane, uphill, bituminous, dry, median divided roadway that has a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (55 mph). This roadway runs east to west and supports two-way traffic. This roadway intersects with a two-lane, dry, level, bituminous roadway that runs north to south. The intersection is controlled by a stop sign. There were no atmospheric conditions present at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Cadillac Sedan Deville driven by a 76 year-old male, was traveling northbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1992 Honda Accord sedan driven by a 29 year-old male, was traveling eastbound in lane three. V1 was attempting to make a left turn to travel westbound. As V1 entered the intersection the front of V2 struck the left side of V1. V2 came to rest in lane three of the eastbound roadway and V1 was driven off of the roadway. Both vehicles were towed from the scene; both drivers were transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
