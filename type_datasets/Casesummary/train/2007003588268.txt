The crash occurred in the intersection of two roadways. The east/west roadway was one-way with one lane of travel. The north/south roadway was two-way with two lanes in each direction. This was a straight, level, bituminous roadway with a speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). It was the afternoon hours of a weekend, and it was daylight and clear with no adverse condition.
V1, a 2002 Nissan Altima four door, with three occupants was traveling westbound in lane one. V2, a 1999 Acura RL four door, with one occupant was traveling northbound in lane two. V3, a 2004 Ford Explorer sport utility vehicle, with one occupant was traveling southbound in lane two and stopped for the traffic control device. At the intersection, the front of V1 impacted the right side of V2. Then the right side of V2 contacted the front of V3. V1 came to a final rest in the intersection. V2 and V3 came to a final rest right after the second event. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Nissan Altima, was driven by a 61 year-old male who reported no injuries. The 55 year-old female, and a 25 year-old female all reported no injuries. The driver stated to the Police that he was traveling westbound and had the green light and he was struck by V2.
V2, the 1999 Acura RL, was driven by a 23 year-old male who reported no injuries. He stated that he had the green light, and was struck by V1 and caused him to strike V3.
V3, the Ford Explorer, was driven by a 37 year-old male who reported no injuries. He stated to the Police that he was stopped at the intersection when the driver of V2 ran the red light.
