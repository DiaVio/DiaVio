This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday morning. The crash occurred at the intersection of a straight, level, five-lane, two-way, east-west, bituminous roadway and a private business driveway that intersects the south curb of the main roadway. The speed limit on the main roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph), and there is a posted speed limit on the private driveway of 8 kmph (5 mph). At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects. The view of crossing traffic is not blocked by trees and lighting fixtures along the south curb.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 Toyota Corolla 4-door sedan, was traveling northbound in the private drive and was approaching the main roadway with the intention of turning left into westbound traffic. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 GMC half-ton Suburban SUV, was traveling eastbound in the first lane of the main roadway, approaching the private drive on its right with the intention of continuing straight. As V1 executed its turn, the front of V2 impacted the left side of V1. The vehicles rotated towards each other and the left side of V1 contacted the right side of V2 in a side-slap configuration. Both vehicles deflected east to final rest in the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
