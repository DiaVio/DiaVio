This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west trafficway is straight and divided by curbed medians. Both approaches are level and consist of two through lanes and a left turn lane. The speed limit for this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Chevrolet Lumina, was westbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Nissan Sentra, was eastbound in lane one. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a twenty-eight-year-old female, was on her way home from a gas station. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. She travels this route daily. This driver stated that she never saw V2 prior to the crash and "just wasn't paying attention to the traffic."
The driver of V2, a twenty-seven-year-old male, was on his way home from the gym. He was well rested, healthy and familiar with his vehicle. He travels this route daily. This driver stated that he did not see V1 prior to the crash.
