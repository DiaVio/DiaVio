The crash occurred in the right westbound lane of a U.S. Highway that ran east/west along the portion where the crash occurred. There were five lanes where the crash occurred. Two lanes ran east, two ran west and there was a left turn only lane for westbound traffic to head south on an intersecting roadway. The crash occurred at a four-way intersection although the crossing roadway was not involved in this crash. The intersection was controlled by traffic lights, on colors with no pedestrian signal. The right (north) shoulder of the U.S. Highway was paved. The roadway was straight and level in the crash area. The speed limit was 80kph/50mph. Conditions at the time of the crash were daylight and dry at late morning on a weekday.
V1 was a 1997 Nissan Quest minivan traveling west in the right lane of the highway approaching the intersection when it struck V2, a 2003 Saturn LW200 station wagon that was stopped in the right westbound lane of the highway at a red light waiting for a green light. V1 came to rest at the point of impact. V2 was driven to the west side of the intersection and pulled to the right (north) shoulder to await police assistance. V1 was towed from the scene. V2 was driven away. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a belted thirty-seven year old female who was not injured in the crash. She was on her way home from a co-workers house at the time of the crash.
V2 was driven by a forty-six-year-old belted female who was uninjured in the crash. Her two occupants were also uninjured (though not listed in PAR). She was on her way to see her niece's new home at the time of the crash.
