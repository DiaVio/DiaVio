The crash occurred on a two-way, two-lane, bituminous road with a posted speed limit of 80 kmh (50 mph). Conditions at the time of the weekday, morning crash were daylight, cloudy and dry.
V1, a 1999 Mitsubishi Galant ES 4 door, driven by a 37 year-old female on her way to work, was southbound on the road negotiating a left hand curve, on a > 2% downhill slope. The driver of V1 stated that she looked down at the floor for a second and the vehicle went off of the roadway to the gravel shoulder on the right. The driver stated that she over corrected to the left and hit the gas pedal instead of the brake. V1 rotated counterclockwise, crossed the centerline, and left the roadway on the left side. V1 impacted a >2% positive slope embankment and slid sideways. V1 came to rest on the embankment facing southeast. V1 was towed from the scene due to damage. The fully restrained driver was treated at the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
