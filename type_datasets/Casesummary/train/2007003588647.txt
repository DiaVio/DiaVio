This crash occurred in the signalized intersection of two roadways. The north/south roadway was two-way with one lane of travel in each direction. The east/west roadway was two-way with three lanes of travel with a divided service lane in each direction. The roadway was straight and level, and it was clear with no adverse conditions. It was daylight during the morning hours of a weekday. The speed limit was 48 kmph (30 mph), and traffic conditions were normal.
V1, a 2001 Volkswagen Passat four door with one occupant was southbound in lane one. V2, an unknown van, with an unknown number of occupants was traveling eastbound in lane one of the service lane. At the intersection, the front of V2 impacted the right side of V1. Both vehicles came to a final rest in the intersection and V2 fled the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a 28 year-old female, was uninjured in the crash. She advised that she was traveling to a job interview from home when she was struck by V2. The driver advised that she was traveling southbound through the intersection with a green light and V2 ran the red light, she attempted to avoid the crash by braking but was unsuccessful.
The driver V2 was an unknown person who fled the scene after impacting V1. No further information could be obtained regarding this driver therefore an interview was not possible.
