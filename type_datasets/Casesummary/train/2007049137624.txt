This single-vehicle crash occurred in the northbound lanes of a five lane, positively divided, roadway with a hillcrest and a right curve with an unknown radius. The speed limit was 97 kMph (60 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with moderate traffic. The weather was overcast and the roadway was wet at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Chrysler PT Cruiser with one occupant, was northbound in lane five. The driver of V1 lost directional control of the vehicle and began to rotate clockwise. V1 passed through all lanes and the right shoulder where the front of V1 struck the guardrail. V1 continued to rotate and the left and back of V1 also struck the guardrail. V1 moved back into the traffic way and came to rest in the third lane facing south. V1was towed due to damage. The driver was transported. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-eight year-old female, was transported from the scene and was unable to be located afterward to be interviewed.
