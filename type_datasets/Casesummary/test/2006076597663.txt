This two -vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged light controlled intersection. This intersection runs east to west as the primary roadway and north to south in a business and housing area road. The posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph) for the east to west roadway and no posted speed for the north to south entrance roads. This crash occurred during darkness with overhead lighting and the intersection lights working correctly. The roadway was dry and traffic conditions were light.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Ford Explorer was southbound in the second lane leaving the shopping center. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Chevrolet S-10 was northbound in the second lane. V1 turned left at the intersection and the front of V1 impacted with the front of V2. Both vehicles came to rest near the point of impact. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a nineteen-year-old male, was leaving this shopping center and heading to a party in a remote part of the town. This driver had just started driving. The driver of V1 stated that he attempted to apply the brakes, but stated he could not apply them due to an electronic control box for the security system had fallen down onto the floorboard and blocked the movement of the brake pedal. Upon inspection of this device it was noted that the control box was too thin to be able to block the movement of the pedal. The driver and occupants were heading out to celebrate the end of the school year. This driver had been up for almost fourteen hours at the time of the crash. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis. This driver was having discussions with the passengers as he entered the intersection.
The driver of V2, a twenty-nine year old male, was heading from home to the shopping center at this intersection. This driver had been driving for about ten minutes at the time of the crash. This driver was conversing with the passenger about a family matter at the time of the crash. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis.
