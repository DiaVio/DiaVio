This two-vehicle collision occurred just past noon in the southbound lanes of a four lane undivided roadway with a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). The asphalt roadway was dry, flat and straight. There were no adverse weather conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Mercedes-Benz ML320, driven by a 62 year-old male was traveling southbound in lane two. Vehicle one (V1), a 1994 Ford Escort Lx, driven by a 33 year-old female was traveling behind V2 in the same lane. V2 came to a stop, waiting for traffic to clear so it could make a left turn. The front of V1 braked and under rode the back of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in lane two just a short distance south of the point of impact. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 was not towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
