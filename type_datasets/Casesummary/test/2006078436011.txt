This single vehicle ran off the left side of the roadway crash occurred several hours before mid-night on a two lane, two-way roadway. There is one westbound lane and one eastbound lane that is separated by a painted centerline stripe. The roadway has a slight curve to the left for westbound traffic. This was a bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 88 KHP (55 MPH). It was an evening crash with dark conditions and no lighting and the sky was clear.
Vehicle one(V-1), a 2006 Mazda Miata convertible driven by a 41 year-old female was traveling westbound when the right rear tire failed as the vehicle was going around a slight curve to the left. The vehicle then began to rotate counterclockwise and travel in a southwesterly direction across both lanes of travel. The vehicle then changed rotation and began to rotate clockwise in the eastbound lane. The vehicle slid sideways off the left side of the roadway until it tripped on it's left wheels. The vehicle rolled to it's left for 2 quarter turns until it came to rest on its top facing west. The vehicle was towed due to damage. The driver of V-1 was transported to the hospital by EMS. Police Reported Travel Speed is 97 kmph for V1.
