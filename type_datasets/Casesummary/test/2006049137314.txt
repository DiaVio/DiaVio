The single-vehicle crash occurred near the intersection of a seven-lane divided roadway and a two-lane undivided roadway. For northbound traffic there is four travel lanes. Both roads were dry, traveled, level asphalt. The posted speed limit is 64 kmph (40 mph). For northbound traffic, the fourth travel lane is a left turn lane. There are some raised Bott dots on the road separating the travel lanes. Traffic was light at the time of the collision. This crash occurred during daylight hours with clear skies.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Oldsmobile Alero was northbound weaving in and out of the third lane and the designated left turn lane, lane number four. The driver of V1 lost control and crossed lanes three, two and one. V1 struck the east curb with its back right tire. V1 then veered back to the west and crossed through lanes one, two and three, then departed the roadway to the left and struck a tree in the median with its right plane. V1 then rotated counterclockwise approximately 360 degrees and moved back to the east and came to rest facing west in the second lane. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-five year old male fled the location without leaving any information. The driver was unidentified and was not able to be interviewed.
