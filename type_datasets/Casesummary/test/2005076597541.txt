This two-vehicle crash occurred on a five-lane curved asphalt roadway. This road has two travel lanes in either direction and a center left turn lane. For westbound traffic the roadway curves right. The posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during dark hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1973 Ford F150 pickup truck was backing down the center turn lane going west and entered the second westbound lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1997 Ford Aspire sedan, was westbound in the second lane. As V1 departed the center left turn lane the left side of V1 was impacted by the left front of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in the westbound lanes. V1 was not towed due to damage. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
The driver of V1, a forty-six year old female, was not interviewed. It is unknown why this driver was backing in the center left turn lane or why she departed her lane. The driver of V2, a thirty-two year old male was not interviewed.
