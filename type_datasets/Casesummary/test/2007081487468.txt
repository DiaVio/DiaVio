This two-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon during daylight hours. The location was an intersection in a residential neighborhood with stop signs controlling only the east / west traffic. The north south roadway is uncontrolled, and is a two-way, two-lane bituminous roadway with an uphill grade. The weather was overcast, and the roadway was wet from a previous rain, however visibility was clear. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Ford Econoline van driven by a sixty (60) year old male , was westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Volkswagen Jetta sedan driven by a forty eight (48) year old female with one passenger, was traveling southbound in lane one, heading up the hill. V1 stopped for the stop sign and then proceeds to enter the intersection. The front of V2 impacted with the right side front of V1. Both vehicles then side slapped. V2 came to rest facing northwest in the eastbound lane. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
