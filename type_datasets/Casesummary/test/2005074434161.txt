This two-vehicle collision occurred in a four-way intersection with north and south traffic controlled by a stop sign. Both roadways were three-lane (with a left turn lane at the intersection), undivided, straight and level. The speed limit on both roadways was 72 kmph (45 mph). No adverse weather conditions present at the time of this daytime crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Ford Aerostar, was traveling south in lane one intending to pass through the intersection. V2, a 2005 Volkswagen Touareg, was traveling in lane one of the eastbound roadway, also intending to travel straight through the intersection. V1 driver failed to notice the stop sign or V2 approaching from the right and went through intersection without stopping. V2 driver noticed V1 approaching intersection without slowing down and accelerated into intersection with the intention of avoiding collision. In the intersection, the front of V1 struck the back left side of V2. V1 rotated counterclockwise, crossed two additional lanes of traffic and rolled over four-quarter turns off the left side of roadway. During the rollover, the right side struck a small sawhorse type traffic sign. V1 came to rest on the roadside facing north. V2 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest in middle of eastbound traffic lanes facing southeast. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
