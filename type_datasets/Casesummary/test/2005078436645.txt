This is a two vehicle crash that occurred at 1720 hours on a five lane, north/south dry asphalt roadway. The roadway consists of two southbound and two northbound lanes that are separated by continuous left turn lane. There were no adverse weather conditions. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Chevrolet S-10 pickup, driven by a 51 year-old female The vehicle was traveling southbound in the number two lane at approximately 32 kmph (20 mph) indicated by the police report and the statements made by the driver at the scene. Vehicle two (2) a 1998 Dodge Stratus driven by a 61 year-old male with one passenger crossed from a driveway in the path of V1. The front of V1 struck the right side of V2. The driver of V1 came to a stop in lane 2 facing in a southerly direction. The driver of V1 stated that there was a line of vehicle stopped in the northbound lanes and the left turn lane prior to the crash. There were no injuries reported by the driver of V1. V1 was towed from the scene there were no reported injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is 32 kmph for V1 and 32 kmph for V2.
