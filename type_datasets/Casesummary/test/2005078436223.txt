This is a three vehicle rear type crash occurred during early morning hours on a straight two-lane bituminous asphalt roadway. There is one westbound lane and one eastbound lane that are separated by a painted raised median. There are also left turn lanes at the intersections to enter the residential housing in the area. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 56 kmph (35 mph). The weather at the time was clear and the roadway had no apparent defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 GMC pick-up driven by a 16 year-old male with one passenger was traveling westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2) a 1986 Honda Accord sedan driven by a 18 year-old male was traveling west in lane one. Vehicle three (V3) a 1979 Chevrolet Camaro coupe driven by a 18 year-old male with one passenger was traveling west in lane one. V2 stopped in traffic lane because of non-contact vehicle ahead which stopped for two skate boarders when V1 braked and tapped the rear bumper of V2 with V1's front and came to a stop. V3 was unable to stop and struck the rear of V1 with V3's front causing V1's front to strike the rear of V2 a second time. All three vehicle came to final rest in lane one facing west. V1 was towed from the scene, V2 & V3 were driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.
An associative factor of external distraction was coded for V1 & V2.
