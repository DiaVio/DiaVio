This two-vehicle crash occurred shortly past noon during daylight hours. The weather was overcast, visibility was clear and the roadway was dry asphalt. The location of the crash is a busy four way, level intersection with left turn lanes and overhead traffic signals with crosswalks. The east leg of the intersection is an off-ramp/on-ramp. The speed limit for both vehicles is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1987 Honda Civic two door driven by a 78 year-old male, was traveling southbound in the center left turn only lane. V1 entered the intersection and turned left to go eastbound. V1 was struck in the right side by the front of Vehicle two (V2), a 1991 Peugeot sedan driven by a 56 year-old female. On impact V2 pushed V1 toward the northeast corner of the intersection. V1 came to rest facing east in the north crosswalk with the front of the vehicle up on the median. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 came to rest facing northeast in the roadway at the northeast corner. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
