This two vehicle rear end crash occurred in daylight approaching the stoplight controlled intersection of an East/West seven lane two way raised median divided roadway with a crossing two lane, two way roadway. The seven-lane roadway (four lanes for eastbound traffic) is fresh concrete, straight, level, and dry at the time of the crash. The speed limit on the roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). The crash occurred in heavy rush hour traffic.
Vehicle two (V2), a 1989 Mitsubishi Galant passenger vehicle occupied by the driver only, was stopped in traffic in the second lane. It was the second vehicle in line waiting for the eastbound traffic signal to turn green. Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Honda CR-V sport utility vehicle occupied by the driver only, was also eastbound in traffic in the second lane approaching the intersection. The front of V1 impacted the rear of V2. Both vehicles came to final rest on the roadway in the second lane. V2 was towed from the scene due to damage. V1 was towed from the scene by a private towing firm. The driver of V2 was transported to a local trauma center for treatment. The driver of V1 was reported uninjured. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
