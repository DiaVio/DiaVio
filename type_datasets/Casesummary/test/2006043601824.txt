This single-vehicle crash occurred in the evening of a weeknight on a 2-lane undivided roadway near a location where the road curves to the left. The bituminous east/west roadway featured a posted speed limit of 89 kmph (45 mph). The conditions were clear, dry, and dark.
V1, a 2006 Pontiac Grand Prix with one occupant, was eastbound when it departed the right side of the roadway and struck a bridge with its left side. Following the bridge impact, V1 continued to drive into a creek and became partially submerged before coming to final rest. The driver had to extracted and V1 was towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a 36-year-old male, was hospitalized for over 30 days and has not been able to speak since the crash. The driver's wife refused to comment about her husband or the crash. No further information could be obtained.
