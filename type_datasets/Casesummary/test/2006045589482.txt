Case #2006-45-015
The crash occurred on a rural east/west two lane residential road. West of the impact area the road curved sharply to the north with a radius of curvature of 45 meters. The posted speed limit was 48kph (30mph). Conditions were dark, dry and cold on a weekend evening. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1.
Vehicle #1 was a 1989 Ford F-150 pickup traveling east on the two-lane road. After the vehicle exited the sharp curve left the driver lost control and began to rotate counterclockwise. The pickup departed the left (north) side of the road and struck a fence and utility pole with its right side. It came to rest off the north side of the road facing west and was towed due to damage. The driver fled the scene of the crash and was never located. The vehicle was not registered.
