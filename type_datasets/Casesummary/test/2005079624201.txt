This three-vehicle crash occurred on a dry, level, asphalt, undivided, two-way roadway at a little past noon. No adverse weather conditions existed at the time of the collision. The speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2002 Mazda MPV mini van driven by a 51 year-old female. Vehicle two (V2) a 1999 Toyota Camry sedan driven by a 64 year-old male. Vehicle three (V3) a 1991 Nissan short-bed 2-wheel drive pick-up truck driven by a 28 year-old male. V1 was traveling west in the number one lane, one car length behind V2. V2 was heading west, attempting a right turn into a private driveway from lane one. V3 was backing into the roadway heading south from the same driveway that V2 was attempting to turn into. The front of V1 impacted the left rear of V2, which caused V2 to move in a northwesterly direction. The front of V2 impacted the right rear of V3. V1 and V2 came to rest in the roadway. V3 was parked in the driveway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
