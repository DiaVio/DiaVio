This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west roadway is straight, level, undivided and consists of two lanes in each direction with a center left turn lane at the intersection and parking on both sides. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Dodge Caravan, was northbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2006 Chrysler PT Cruiser, was southbound in lane one. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. The vehicles rotated and the right side of V1 struck the left side of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
The driver of V1 was a twenty-two-year-old male and the driver of V2 was a twenty-three-year-old female traveling with a four-year-old male passenger. No further information is available for these drivers.
