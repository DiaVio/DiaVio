This crash occurred at an intersection of a four-lane undivided north/south roadway and an undivided two-lane roadway. The north/south lanes were straight, level asphalt. There were four lanes of travel with two lanes of travel in each direction. The posted speed for the north/south roadway was 56 kmph (35 mph). The east/west roadway had one lane in each direction and was posted at 40 kmph (25 mph). The east/west roadway was controlled by stop signs. There was no control for the north/south roadway. The crash occurred at mid-day on a weekday.
Vehicle #1, a 1998 Chevrolet Cavalier 4-door sedan, was traveling west towards the intersection. The driver was not attentive to the stop signs at the intersection and traveled through the stop sign and struck the right front side of Vehicle #2, a 2003 Chrysler Jeep Liberty utility vehicle that was traveling north. After contact, V1 rotated clockwise and was struck at its driver's door by the right rear bumper of V2. V1 continued to rotate and struck a curb at the northeast corner of the intersection. Vehicle 1 then contacted a utility pole with its left side and traveled to final rest facing southeast in lane 1 of the westbound roadway. Vehicle 2 skidded in a northwest direction, coming to final rest in lane 2 of the southbound roadway. The drivers and occupant of V2 were checked out on scene but refused transport to a local trauma unit. Both vehicles were towed from the scene due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
A 55-year-old female in fair health drove V1. She reported being under doctor's care and taking medication for hypertension. She stated she does not wear corrective lenses at this time. She stated that she had just left her daughter's home and was on her way to a friend's home to visit. She stated she does not remember what she was thinking about at the time and does not remember coming to the intersection and where the other vehicle came from. The driver of V1 was very distraught at the scene. She stated she did not know there was a crossing street coming up. She repeatedly stated that she was going just two doors down the street to her friend's home and that there was no street there before. After a few more questions she stated that yes there was a street there but she did not think there was a stop sign there.
A 34-year-old female drove V2. She reported being in good health and taking no medications at this time. She stated she wears corrective lenses for nearsightedness. She also reported being on maternity leave for the past 13 weeks. She stated that she volunteers to help people in need and that is what she was doing at the time of the crash. She stated she was helping a client take her dogs to their new home. She stated she saw the other vehicle at the last second and thought the driver was going to stop. Then as the other vehicle started to enter the intersection, she braked and steered to the left. She stated she was not injured but her occupant obtained minor injuries.
