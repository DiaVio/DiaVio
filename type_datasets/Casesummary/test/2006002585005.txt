Case 2006-02-018
The crash occurred on a level two lane undivided roadway with a posted speed limit of 35 MPH (56 KPH). It was raining, the roadway was wet and it was daylight at the time of this weekday afternoon crash. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
Vehicle 1, a 1994 Dodge Caravan, was traveling west on the two lane undivided roadway. Vehicle 1 crossed into the eastbound lane, off the left side of the roadway and struck a 33 cm diameter pole with its front. Vehicle 1 came to rest facing west. Vehicle 1 was towed due to damage.
The Dodge Caravan (Vehicle 1) was driven by a belted 49-year-old male who was treated at the scene for minor injuries, but refused transport. Information from a surrogate source indicated that the driver was on his way home from work, blacked out for unknown reasons and struck a pole on the left side of the roadway. No additional information was obtained.
