This two-vehicle crash occurred during early evening hours. The roadway surface is concrete,dry and free of defects. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at that time. This intersection with stop signs controlling the east/west roadway, has a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1985 Mercedes-Benz 300-D sedan driven by a 37 year-old female, was traveling westbound in the right lane of a two-lane roadway. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Chevrolet S-10 pick-up driven by a 18 year-old male, was traveling northbound in the first lane of a two-lane roadway. V1 was attempting to pass through the intersection after stopping at the stop sign. When V1 entered the intersection, it was struck on the left side by the front of V2. V1 rotated counter clock-wise and came to rest facing westward partially off the roadway northwest of the point of impact. V2 continued along its path of travel and came to rest facing northward on the roadway. V1 and V2 were towed from the scene due to damage sustained in the collision. The driver of V1 was transported to the hospital for treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
