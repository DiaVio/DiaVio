This four-vehicle daytime rear-end collision occurred on an arterial roadway consisting of two-lanes of travel in each direction and a left hand turn lane. The intersection is controlled by on-colors traffic signal with pedestrian crossing. The road is paved with asphalt and has an downhill grade of -6% in the westbound direction of travel. The speed limit for the road is 48 kmph (30 mph). At the time of the crash the sky was cloudy with no adverse conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 2003 Ford Mustang convertible with a driver and unknown number of passengers. V1 was traveling west in lane one. Vehicle two (V2) was a 2001 Saturn SL2 sedan driven by a thirty nine (39) year old male. V2 was traveling west and decelerating in lane one. Vehicle three (V3) was a 1996 Lexus LS4 sedan driven by a twenty three (23) year old female with one passenger; and, Vehicle four (V4) was a 2006 an IC School Bus driven by a thirty seven (37) year old female was also heading west in lane one. V4 was stopped at the stoplight; V3 and V2 were coming to a stop behind the bus. As V1 came over the crest of the hill it slammed on the brakes skidding into the rear of V2. The front of V2 then hit the rear of V3. V1 then hit that back of V3 pushing it into the back of V4. V2 came to rest at point of impact with V3. V1 and V3 came to rest behind the bus. V1 and V3 were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was an unknown male. The driver and passengers of V1 ran from scene.
