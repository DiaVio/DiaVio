This two vehicle daytime collision occurred during early evening hours. The collision occurred on a seven lane, undivided, dry, asphalt roadway with a two-way left turn lane. The roadway is designated 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1) is a 1998 Mazda 626 4-door sedan, driven by a twenty one (21) year old unlicensed male who was traveling east in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 1993 Mercedes Benz SL600 2-door convertible, driven by a thirty nine (39) year old male who was traveling east in the number one lane. As V2 slowed to make a right turn V1's front plane impacted V2's rear plane. V1 was towed with major damage. V2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
