This two-vehicle crash occurred at noon during daylight hours. The roadway was a straight, divided, seven-lane asphalt, wet with on-color traffic signals. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph). Westbound traffic consisted of a right turn lane, two through lanes and a left turn lane.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1986 Toyota 4-Runner pickup driven by a 16 year-old male with one passenger was westbound in lane number three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Ford F450, flatbed pick-up driven by a 29 year-old male was stopped westbound in lane number three. V2 stopped behind traffic stopped at a red light. V1 tried to stop and was unsuccessful. V1's front collided with the back of V2. Both V1 and V2 came to final rest in lane three, V1 was towed from the scene V2 left under own power. Police Reported Travel Speed is 56 kmph for V1 and 56 kmph for V2.
