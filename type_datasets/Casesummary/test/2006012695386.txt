The crash occurred in the intersection of two four-lane two-way roadways. There were two north and southbound lanes and two east and west bound lanes. They were both straight asphalt roadways with a negative slope of 1%. The posted speed limit was 89 kmph (55 mph). It was early afternoon and the road was dry and the sky was clear.
V1, a 1998 Saturn SL four door with one occupant was traveling northbound in lane two. V2, a 2003 Buick Lesabre four door with one occupant was traveling in lane two of the westbound lanes. Both vehicles had a stop sign (it was a four-way stop). V1 stopped at the stop sign and then proceeded to go. A witness stated that V2 did not stop for the sign. The front of V2 contacted the right of V1 before they both came to final rest in the roadway. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Saturn SL was driven by an 84-year old female who reported minor injuries. She advised that she was on her way home from the salon. She advised that she had stopped 3-4 seconds before proceeding. She stated that she was traveling 2-16 kmph (1-10 mph) at the time of impact. She stated that she did not see V2 coming at her so she did not attempt any avoidance actions.
V2, the 2003 Buick Lesabre was driven by a 77-year old female who reported no injuries. She advised that she was on her way to her brother's house. She advised that she did not really know what happened, but she thought she stopped at the stop sign. She advised that she thought she was traveling between 18-32 kmph (11-20 mph) prior to the crash.
