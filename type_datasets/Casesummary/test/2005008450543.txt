This crash occurred on a two lane bituminous asphalt roadway on a weekday. It was daylight with no adverse weather conditions. The roadway was straight and level with access to a shopping center to the left. Vehicle 2 was planning to turn into the shopping center. As the vehicle neared the entrance it had to stop for traffic and was struck from behind by vehicle1.
Vehicle 1 was a 2003 Ford F150 Super Crew light truck driven by a 72 year old man. There were no other occupants. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
Vehicle 2 was a 1987 Chevrolet Celebrity driven by a 62 year old male. There were no other occupants.
