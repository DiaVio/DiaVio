This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, stop sign-controlled intersection. The westbound approach is straight, level, one-way and consists of one lane with parking on both sides. The north/south roadway is straight, level, undivided and consists of one lane in each direction with parking on both sides. The speed limit for these roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Ford Econoline van, was westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 1990 Chevrolet Cavalier, was northbound in lane one. V1 turned left in the intersection and the left side was struck by the front of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The drivers of V1 (a male) and V2 (a forty-three-year-old male) were not interviewed. No further information is available for these drivers.
