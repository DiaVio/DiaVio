V1, a 1997 Pontiac Grand Am, was traveling eastbound on a 4-lane, 2-way, dry asphalt roadway in daylight conditions. V1 was traveling in lane one intending to turn right at a T-intersection. V2, a 2005 Dodge Grand Caravan, was stopped for a red light in lane one in front of V1 on the same roadway, also intending to turn right. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph). The front of V1 contacted the back of V2. Both vehicles came to final rest off the south road edge.
The driver of V1 was a 21-year old male who had reported that he had been traveling between 18-32 kmph (11-20mph) prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling to a restaurant. He does not wear corrective lenses. He was uninjured and refused treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 70-year old male who had reported that he had been stopped at the red light prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling to a restaurant. He was wearing corrective lenses for astigmatism. He was uninjured and refused treatment.
