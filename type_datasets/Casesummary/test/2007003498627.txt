The crash occurred in the intersection of two roadways. The eastbound roadway was one-way with one lane of travel. The north/south roadway was two-way with one lane in each direction. The roadway was straight and level, and it was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions. The speed limit was 48kmph (30mph), and it was the morning hours of a weekday.
V1, a 2004 Jeep Liberty sport utility vehicle, with one occupant was traveling eastbound in lane one. V2, a 2001 Toyota Sienna minivan, with one occupant was traveling northbound in lane one. At the intersection, the front of V1 contacted the left side of V2. The front of V2 then contacted a gate on the northeast corner of the intersection. V1 came to a final rest approximately five meters from the point of impact. V2 came to a final rest on top of the sidewalk of the northeast corner. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the Toyota Sienna, was driven by a 51 year-old female who reported minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. The driver stated that as she proceeded through the intersection after stopping, she was struck by V2.
V2, the Jeep Liberty, was driven by a 30 year-old male who reported no injuries. He advised that he was traveling home from a nearby convenience store. He advised that when he was traveling straight, V1 did not stop at the stop sign at the intersection, and he attempted to brake and steer right.
