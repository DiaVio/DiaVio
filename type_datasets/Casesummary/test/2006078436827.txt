This three-vehicle rear end crash that occurred mid-morning on a two-way, two-lane dry asphalt roadway. There is one northbound and one southbound lane. The lanes are not divided. The roadway where the crash occurred is used during the weekday for parking at a local high school. The speed limit on the roadway is 40 kph (25 mph). The roadway has a 2% uphill/downhill grade. The sky was clear.
