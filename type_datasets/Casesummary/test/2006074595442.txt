This two vehicle crash occurred shortly after noon in daylight hours in the northbound lane of a 4 lane divided roadway with a raised median. The roadway was concrete, dry, level and a posted speed limit of 64 kph (35 mph). weather was clear with no sight-line restrictions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Mitsubishi Galant 4 door sedan driven by a 37 year-old female with two passengers. was traveling north in lane one when it was struck in the rear by the front of Vehicle two (V2), a 1992 Acura Integra coupe driven by a 17 year-old male. V1 was stopped about 10 seconds due to heavy traffic at the intersection up ahead. The impact pushed V1 into the intersection, and the driver drove the vehicle up onto the curb to get it out of traffic. V2 was traveling north in lane one when the driver reached down on the console to get a ringing cell phone. At the time of the call the driver stated that V 1 was 3 car lengths in front. When the driver looked up he was right on top of V1. V2 braked but struck the rear of V1. V2 rolled down hill into a private driveway facing south. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
