This single-vehicle crash occurred on an entrance ramp to an access-controlled roadway. This roadway is level, curves to the right (radius of curvature is 138 meters) and consists of one lane with a right-side paved shoulder. There is a concrete wall (part of the bridge support) on the right side of the road. The speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Mazda 6, was northbound on the entrance ramp. V1 traveled off the left side of the roadway. The driver abruptly steered right and V1 returned to the roadway, rotating clockwise. V1 continue to rotate and traveled off the right side of the road. The front of V1 and then the back of V1 struck the concrete wall. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-four-year-old female, was traveling from a lounge to her home. She was familiar with her vehicle and travels this roadway weekly. This driver stated that she had been drinking beer and watching a football game at the lounge. She stated that she had been thinking about what she was going to order for dinner. This driver also stated that she was fatigued.
