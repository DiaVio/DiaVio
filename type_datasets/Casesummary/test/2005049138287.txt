The crash occurred during the early afternoon daylight hours, in the intersection of a two-way, four lane (seven overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier. Three vehicles involved a passenger car, pick-up truck and a van type truck. Both roads were dry, traveled concrete. The intersection has a traffic light and a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2002 Ford Ranger driven by a 69 year-old male who was in good health and well rested. V1 was traveling southbound in the second travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2004 BMW 530i driven by a 50 year-old female who was in good health and rested. V2 was traveling eastbound in the first travel lane. Vehicle three (V3) is a Isuzu straight truck driven by a 34 year-old male who was in good health and rested. V3 was stopped northbound in the fourth travel lane. V1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V2. V1 began to rotate clockwise and rolled over onto the left side. V1 then struck the front of V3. V1 came to rest on it's top in the intersection. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the intersection facing southeast. V3 came to rest back at the point of impact. All three vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
