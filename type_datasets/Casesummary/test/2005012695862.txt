V1, a 1996 Buick Riviera was traveling northbound on a three lane, one way, dry asphalt roadway in daylight conditions. A passing vehicle cut the driver of V1 off. V1, saw another vehicle merging into his lane. He attempted to change lanes to avoid hitting the other vehicle but overcorrected and lost control. V1 departed the roadway on the right side and then rolled eight quarter turns before coming to final rest off the roadway.
The driver of V1 was an 18-year old male who had reported that he had been traveling 113-129kmph (71-80mph) prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling to work. He suffered injuries and was transported to a local trauma facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
