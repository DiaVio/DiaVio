The crash occurred on an east/west, two way, two lane road that was undivided, had a curve left (radius = 58 m) followed by a straight portion and had no shoulders. The roadway traveled through a fairly wooded residential area at the crash location. The curve was super-elevated to 2.6% and had a negative grade of 4.3%. The crash area was level. The speed limit for the road was 56 kmph (35 mph) with an advisory limit for the curve of 25 mph. Conditions at the time of the crash were heavy rain with wet roads, overcast sky around mid-morning on a weekday.
V1 was a 2005 Toyota Matrix traveling west on the road when as it negotiated the curve control was lost and V1 apparently broke traction departing the right side of the road along the straight portion and striking a tree with its left side. V1 came to rest at impact. V1 was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
V1 was driven by an 18 year-old belted female who was listed on the police report as sustaining possible injuries. She told police that she lost control of her vehicle while negotiating the curve. She was unable to be contacted after the crash.
