This crash occurred early morning hours (EMS dispatch time, no crash time on police report) involving two passenger cars at an intersection The speed limit for the roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph). The road is a 4-lane undivided blacktop roadway from south to north, and a two lane (two way traffic) from west to east controlled by a stop sign. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1988 Oldsmobile Cutlass driven by a 28 year-old female who was not fatigued, was traveling with with three passengers. V1 was stopped at a stop sign for the west-east direction of travel waiting for north-south traffic to pass before crossing through the intersection of both roadways. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 Hyundai Excel driven by a 37 year-old female who not fatigued, was traveling with with two passengers. V2 was traveling north in the first lane. When the traffic cleared for V1 the driver attempted to pass through the intersection of both roadways. The front of V2 impacted with the right side of V1. V1 rotated counter clockwise and moved into the northbound lanes. V2 rotated clockwise after the impact, and moved north. It came to rest within the intersection both vehicles were facing northeast at final rest. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
