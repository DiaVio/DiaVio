This four-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon on a on-colors controlled three-way intersection. No adverse weather conditions present and traffic was heavy. Road surface dry, level concrete, posted speed limit 64 kmph (40 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Ford Windstar minivan, driven by a 70 year-old male was traveling northbound approaching intersection with the intention of turning left without stopping. V1 was struck on right rear side by Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Mercury Mystique sedan driven by a 33 year-old female that was traveling southbound in the first lane with the right front bumper. V2 had just departed from another traffic signal controlled intersection located over 60 meters (197 feet)north from the harmful event intersection. V2 driver responded to impending danger by applying brakes and steering to the left. Despite V2 evasive maneuver, V2 right front bumper impacted V1 and proceeded across lane 2 of southbound traffic, though center left turn lane, and came to rest in lane 2 of northbound traffic after striking V4 with left front bumper. After impact with V2, V1 left rear side struck Vehicle three (V3) a 2002 Buick LeSabre sedan driven by a 48 year-old male with one passenger. V3 was struck on the left front bumper, V1 continued traveling east, departed roadway, returned to roadway, and came to final rest 98 meters (322 feet) from first harmful event in lane one. Vehicle four (V4) a 1991 Ford Tempo sedan driven by a 47 year-old female was traveling northbound in the second lane when the front of V2 struck the left rear side. V4 rotated counterclockwise coming to final rest in the intersection. All four vehicles were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
