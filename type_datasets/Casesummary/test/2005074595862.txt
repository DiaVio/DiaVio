Vehicle one (V1) was a blue 2000 Subaru Impreza 4 door sedan occupied by the 21-year-old male driver and two passengers that were heading north on a 5 lane divided roadway. V1 was in the fourth lane turning left at the intersection. The roadway was level and concrete. V1 stopped at the intersection for about 3-4 seconds before the light turned from green to yellow. The driver of V1 saw vehicle two (V2) approaching from about 1/2 to 1 block away. V1 turned left at the intersection in front of V2. V1 was struck on the right front side by the front bumper of V2. Both V1 and V2 ended up facing southwest after the crash.
V2 was a maroon 2001 Dodge Stratus 4 door sedan, occupied by the 40-year-old male driver and one passenger. Vehicle 2 was traveling south. As vehicle 2 approached the intersection, just about 1/2 a block from the intersection, the driver noticed the light starting to turn yellow. The driver scanned the intersection for on-coming traffic and decided to continue going south through the intersection. When V2 entered the intersection it struck the right side of V1. Both vehicles were towed from the scene. The driver and passenger of V2 sustained minor injuries but were not transported to any hospital for treatment. The posted speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
