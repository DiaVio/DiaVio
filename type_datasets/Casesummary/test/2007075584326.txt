This five-vehicle crash occurred on a divided, five-lane roadway. On the eastbound side, there were two through lanes. The roadway was curved to the right, level with a posted speed of 56 kmph (35 mph). The crash scene continued into a light controlled, four-leg intersection. It was also curved to the right and level with a posted speed of 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with moderate traffic.
Vehicle one (V1), with one occupant, was eastbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), with three occupants, were eastbound in lane one in front of V1. Vehicle three (V3), with two occupants, was stopped westbound at a red light in lane two. Vehicle four (V4), a 1991 Toyota Camry with one occupant, was stopped westbound behind V3 in lane two. Vehicle five (V5), with one occupant, was stopped westbound behind V4 in lane two. Police Reported Travel Speed is 113 kmph for V1 and 113 kmph for V2.
At a very high rate of speed V1 departed the roadway up onto the right curb and came back down onto the roadway and continued eastbound. V1 then rear-ended V2, with its front to V2's back. V2 ran up the same curb impacting a bush with its front. V2 continued off the right side of the road and started to rotate clockwise, then snagged on a guy wire and rolled onto its left side, one-quarter turn and then impacted a wooden telephone pole with its top. The telephone pole snapped in two and V2 continued to roll three-quarter turns rotating counter clockwise. V2 came back into lane one and came to rest on its wheels facing north. After the first impact, V1 continued eastbound ran up a curbed center median and approached the intersection. V1 continued through the intersection in the number two lane of westbound traffic, where V3 was stopped for a red light. V1 impacted with its front to V3's front. V1 pushed V3 backwards. V3 impacted V4 with its back to V4's front. V4 then was pushed backwards and impacted with its back to V5's front. V1 came to rest on its wheels facing southwest in the middle of another intersection close to the impact. V3 came to rest on its wheels facing northwest in the middle of the same intersection as V1. V4 came to rest at the impact with V5. V5 came to rest at the impact with V4. V1, V2, V3 and V4 were towed from the scene. V5 was driven.
The driver of V1 a seventy-seven year-old male, was legally intoxicated according to the police report and it is unknown where or why he was driving. The investigating officer indicated that the driver was traveling at a speed of 113 kmph (70 mph), which is twice the legal speed limit. During an in-person attempt by the researcher, the son of the driver stated he heard his father blew a BAC of 0.23. The driver was transported to a trauma center with injuries. Medical records indicate the driver tested positive for alcohol consumption with a BAC result of 0.236 and was admitted for observation due to suicidal tendencies. He takes the medications: Coumadin (Warfarin), a blood thinner; Lasix, a loop diuretic; and an unspecified Potassium supplement.
The driver of V2, a fifty-one year old male, was from out of town and attending his mother's funeral. Two additional relatives also occupied V2. The driver elected not to talk about this because of all the things going on in his life at the moment. The driver was transported to a trauma center with injuries.
The driver of V3, a fifty year-old female, reported no injuries. The driver stated she is "way to busy" to talk and will call the researcher back when she gets some time. No further information about this driver is known. There was a two year old male passenger also in V3.
