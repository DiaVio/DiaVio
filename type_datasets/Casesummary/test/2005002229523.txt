This crash occurred on a 3 lane straight roadway with a positive barrier. The posted speed limit is (89KMPH) (55MPH). This crash occurred on a weekday afternoon.
Vehicle #1, a 1991 Dodge Caravan minivan, was traveling west on the on-ramp when it failed to yield right of way to on coming traffic, continued off the end of the on-ramp and rode the top of the guardrail before coming to rest facing west. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The Dodge Caravan (Vehicle #1) was driven by a belted 52 year old male who refused treatment stating he was not injured. The driver of the vehicle was watching on coming traffic while eating a cheeseburger. He stated that a driver of an uninvolved vehicle refused to let him into its lane. It should be noted here that the driver has an asthmatic condition and required his medication immediately after the crash. He also requested his cigarettes be taken out of the vehicle. He stated he was thinking about health concerns as he had taken medication, to counteract side effects of an antibiotic he was taking, on an empty stomach.
The police did not consider illegal drugs or alcohol to be involved and ordered no tests.
