This three-vehicle crash occurred a little past noon during daylight hours, on a seven lane divided concrete roadway with a +1% slope roadway was wet from earlier rain. Traffic flow controlled by on-colors traffic signal. Posted speed limit 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Mitsubishi Galant 4-door sedan, driven by a 16 year-old male with one passenger was traveling north in lane two. Vehicle two (V2) was a 2001 Dodge Ram 4X4 pickup driven by a 41 year-old male with one passenger, which was stopped in lane three. Vehicle three (V3) was a 2003 Jeep Liberty SUV driven by a 30 year-old female, which was also stopped in lane three. V1 while passing a large slow moving truck in lane 3. the passenger informed driver that he needed to merge left due to the lane being closed beyond the intersection. As driver change lanes to lane two the front of V1 struck the rear of V2 causing V2's front to strike the rear of V3. All three vehicles came to final rest facing north in lane three. V1 was towed from the scene, V2 and V3 were driven from scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Associative factors assigned to V1 driver are as follows: the driver was inexperience and had a physical impairment (cerebral palsy) along with wet roadway and upcoming closed lanes all contributed to collision. Driver has only one year of driving experience and has general motor skills impairment caused by physical impairment (cerebral palsy). The latter two factors led to driver to not being able to properly control vehicle with ordinary skill expected (performance error).
