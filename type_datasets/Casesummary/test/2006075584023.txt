A four-vehicle crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a six-lane interchange, divided by a curbed median. Southbound lanes consist of two through lanes and one right turn lane onto an entrance ramp. Northbound lanes consist of a right turn lane onto an entrance ramp, two through lanes and one left turn lane onto an entrance ramp. This was a straight, level, bituminous, roadway with a posted speed of 64 kmph (40 mph). It was rush hour on a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic flow was congested and was stopped for a red light south of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 GMC Yukon four-door with one occupant, was southbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Ford Focus four-door with three occupants, was southbound in lane three stopped in front of V1. Vehicle three (V3), a 1997 Toyota 4-Runner four door with two occupants, were southbound in lane three stopped in front of V2. Vehicle four (V4), a 2005 Saturn ION four-door with one occupant was southbound in lane three stopped for forward traffic, which was stopped at a red light that was at an intersection south of the crash. The driver of V1 applied her brakes after seeing the vehicle in front of her had stopped and impacted with its front to V2's back. V2 was pushed forward and impacted with its front to V3's back. V3 was pushed forward and impacted with its front to V4's back. V1 came to rest on its wheels facing southwest in lane two. V2 came to rest on its wheels facing southeast on the curbed median. V3 came to rest on its wheels facing southwest in lane three. V4 came to rest on its wheels facing south in lane three. V1 was towed from the scene due to moderate fontal damage. V2 was towed from the scene due to severe back damage. The drivers drove V3 and V4 from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.
V4 was not involved in the 1st harmful event and the Critical Reason was not coded to this vehicle.
