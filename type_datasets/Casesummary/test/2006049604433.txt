This crash occurred shortly before noon involving two passenger cars at an intersection with traffic lights. The speed limit for the roadways is 48 kmph (30 mph) for the southbound lanes and 54 kmph (35 mph) for the eastbound lanes. The road is a 5-lane divided asphalt roadway from west to east. It is a two lane (two-way traffic) asphalt roadway from north to south. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V 1), a 1993 Ford E-350 Van driven by a 29 year-old male was traveling south in the first lane of traffic approaching the intersection of both roadways. Vehicle two (V 2), a 1999 Isuzu Rodeo, driven by a 31 year-old female with two passengers was traveling east in the second lane. At the intersection of both roadways, the front of V 1 impacted the left side of V 2. V 1 rotated counter clockwise and came to rest within the intersection, facing southeast. V 2 rotated clockwise after the impact, and moved southeast. Its front impacted a traffic light pole with a concrete base, located in the southeast corner of the intersection. The driver of V 1 attempted to avoid a collision by steering to the left and applying his brakes. The driver of V 2 was transported to a local medical facility from the scene. V 2 was towed from the scene. V 1 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
