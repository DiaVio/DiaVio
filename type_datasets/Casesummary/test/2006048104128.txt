This two-vehicle crash occurred in the morning during daylight hours on a wet, two-lane, level, bituminous, straight roadway that runs north to south. The posted speed limit for this roadway was 40 kmph (25 mph). This roadway intersects with a wet, two-lane, level, straight, bituminous roadway that runs east to west. The speed limit for this roadway is 40 kmph (25 mph). This intersection is controlled by a stop sign. The eastbound and westbound lanes are the only lanes required to stop. It was raining at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Chevrolet Cavalier driven by a 55 year-old female, was traveling westbound . Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Jeep Cherokee driven by a 35 year-old female, was traveling southbound. As V1 entered the intersection, the front of V2 contacted the right side of V1. The driver of V1 was not injured and the driver of V2 was transported to a local medical facility with incapacitating injuries. V1 was towed due to damage and V2 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
