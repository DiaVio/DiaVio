This crash occurred on a level bituminous two lane undivided roadway with a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). The roadway curved to the left with a warning sign and a suggested speed of 40 kmph (25 mph). It was cloudy with a snow/slush-covered roadway at the time of this weekday morning crash. Traffic flow was normal for that time of day.
V1, a 1989 Toyota Corolla with one occupant, was northbound on the roadway negotiating the curve to the left. V1 lost control on the snow/slush-covered roadway, went off the right side of the roadway and struck a 50 cm diameter tree with its front. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing west blocking both north and southbound lanes. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a belted 18-year-old female was transported for treatment of minor injuries. No further information was obtained as the driver could not be contacted or located.
