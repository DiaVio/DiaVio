A two-vehicle nighttime collision occurred a few hours before midnight on a Friday. The collision occurred in the southwest corner of a busy four-leg intersection. The east/west roadway is a two-way, six-lane undivided, bituminous roadway. The pre-crash roadway is level and straight. The roadway was wet, as it was raining lightly. Access to the roadway is not controlled. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Volkswagen Golf 4 door hatchback, was traveling east in lane two, the right lane of the two that head straight through the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 1995 Saturn SL was traveling westbound initiating a left turn to head southbound on a green left-turn arrow of the tri-light. V2 was in lane #3, the right lane of the two left turn lanes. The front of V1 impacted the right rear of V2 in the intersection. V2 was rotated around clockwise and came to rest facing northeast in straddling lane 1 and 2 of the southbound lanes. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
