This single vehicle daytime collision occurred in the early evening hours. The collision occurred on the southbound lane of a dry concrete two-way roadway which was straight and level . The roadway consists of two undivided lanes in each direction. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), 1996 Honda Accord sedan, driven by a 40 year-old female was traveling southbound in lane one departed the roadway to the right. V1 impacted a utility pole hitting the right front rotating her counter clockwise. Impacting a set of stairs and landscaping rocks on private property coming to rest facing east. V1 was towed from the scene and the driver was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
