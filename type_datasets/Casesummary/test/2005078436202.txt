This two-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon on a three lane urban street at the entrance to a private driveway. This crash involves a vehicle exiting a driveway traveling north, attempting to make a left turn across northbound lane one, and a center designated turn lane, into southbound lane one. The roadway was straight and level. The crash occurred several hundred feet south of an intersection controlled by operating traffic signals and traffic was backed up. The roadway design was adequate; there were no roadway deficiencies, no environmental deficiencies, paved asphalt and speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph)
Vehicle one (V1) is a blue 1995 Oldsmobile Ciera 4-door sedan driven by a 61 year-old female with one passenger. This vehicle was facing west and attempting to exit a private driveway onto the southbound traffic way. V1 sat for some period of time waiting for backed up northbound traffic in lane one to clear. A non-contact driver motioned for the driver of V1 to turn left and cross into southbound traffic. The driver of V1 began to move forward, crossed the northbound lane and entered the center designated turn lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2004 Mercedes Benz, E-320 4 door sedan. driven by a 29 year-old female with two passengers V2 was northbound in a designated left turn lane approaching an intersection further north that was controlled by operating traffic signals. There were numerous vehicles stopped in northbound lane one. The front of V1 struck the right side of V2. The impact caused V1 to rotate clockwise and come to final rest straddling the center turn lane and northbound lane one facing generally west. V2 continued forward after impact and drove into a private parking area west of the roadway where it came to final rest facing generally northwest. V1 was not towed. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 8 kmph for V1 and 8 kmph for V2.
