This crash occurred just before noon involving two passenger cars at an intersection. The speed limit for the roadways are: 56 kmph (35 mph) for the northbound lanes and 48 kmph (30 mph) for the westbound lanes. The north/south road is a 4-lane median-divided asphalt roadway with two lanes in each direction and no traffic controls. The east/west road is a two-lane, two-way, asphalt roadway with stop signs posted for traffic controls. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V 1) was a 1995 Geo Prizm driven by a 17 year-old male with one passenger. They were listening to the radio and traveling west in the first lane. Vehicle two (V 2) was a 2000 Honda Accord driven by a 48 year-old male. He was traveling south in the first lane of traffic approaching the intersection of both roadways. At the intersection of both roadways, the front of V 1 impacted the left side of V 2. V 2 rotated counter clockwise departed the roadway to the left, and entered a grass median. It came to on the median, facing southwest. V 1 rotated counter clockwise after the impact, and came to rest within the intersection, facing northeast. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
