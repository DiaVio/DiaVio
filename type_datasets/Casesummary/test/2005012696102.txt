V1, a 2000 Buick Century was traveling eastbound on a two-lane, two way dry asphalt roadway in daylight conditions. V2 a 2005 Chevrolet Silverado was also traveling eastbound on the same roadway. V2 stopped to allow a non-contact vehicle in front of him to turn left onto an adjacent road. The front of V1 contacted the back of V2 before they both came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 17-year old male. The driver of V1 stated that V2 stopped suddenly and he could not avoid hitting him. He did not sustain any injuries and refused treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 36-year old male who was not available for an interview. He did not sustain any injuries and refused treatment.
