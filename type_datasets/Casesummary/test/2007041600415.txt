The crash occurred in the southbound lanes of a high occupancy, high volume, and high-speed interstate highway. There were five lanes running southbound with both right and left side paved emergency shoulders. There were concrete wall barriers separating the northbound traffic. The road widths were a standard 3.7 meters and the emergency-paved shoulders were 3.5 meters. The road was straight and level. The conditions were dry and clear on a weekday night. The posted speed was a 105 kmph (65 mph)
V1 was an unknown SUV that was traveling southbound in lane two when the driver decided to change lanes into lane three and hit the right front of V2, a 2003 Honda Accord, that was traveling in lane three. The hit and run vehicle, V1, fled the scene and no other information was obtained on either the driver or the vehicle involved. V2 started to rotate to the left leaving yaw marks on the roadway. V2 continued off the roadway to the left until he hit the left concrete wall causing right side front and rear damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
V2 was driven by a 27-year-old male who was on his way to his mom's from his job. The driver stated that he was traveling the speed limit 105 kmph (65 mph) in lane three when a white SUV changed lanes into his lane and clipped V2's right front, sending his car into a spin. He stated that he thought that he was in V1's blind spot and the driver didn't see him in the lane. The driver of V2 stated that he tried to steer to the left but his car started to spin counterclockwise till he departed the left side of the roadway and hit the left concrete wall with the right front and rear of his vehicle. His vehicle was towed due to damages.
