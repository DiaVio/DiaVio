This is a three vehicle crash that occurred in the early morning hours in darkness prior to sunrise. The roadway was a four-way intersection controlled by operating overhead traffic signals on colors with a functional pedestrian light. The intersection consists of five east/west traffic lanes and three north/south traffic lanes. The speed limit is posted 72 kmph (45 mph) for east/west traffic and 64 kmph (40 mph) for north/south traffic. There were no environmental factors that contributed to this crash. There were no sightline restrictions or obstructions noted at the scene. The roadways are bituminous, straight, level and dry.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Toyota Highlander four door SUV, driven by a 25 year-old male with four passengers was traveling eastbound in the number two lane approaching the intersection on a green light. The vehicle entered the intersection at approximately 64 kmph (40 mph) when the driver saw vehicle two (V2) a 2001 Kia Spectra sedan driven by a 48 year-old male traveling westbound towards him in the number three left turn lane. The driver of V1 then saw V2 attempt to make a left turn in front of him. The driver of V1 attempted to avoid the crash by steering right with hard ABS brake application. The maneuver was not successful and the front of V1 struck the right front wheel area of V2. The impact caused V1 to rotate clockwise and V2 to rotate counterclockwise. During the rotation a side -slap impact occurred to the left side of V1 and the right side of V2. V1 continued forward and right until it struck V3 a 1999 Dodge Durango driven by a 41 year-old male that was stopped in the number one lane of the northbound traffic lanes. V2 continued to rotate counterclockwise until it came to final rest in the number one lane of the eastbound roadway facing generally northeast. V1 came to final rest facing generally southbound at the front of vehicle three (V3) which was facing north. V3 came to final rest at impact location. V1 was towed from the scene and the right front passenger was transported to a local medical facility. V2 was towed from the scene and the driver transported to a local medical facility. V3 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
