This single-vehicle crash occurred on a four-lane, left-curved, level roadway. This roadway was wet at the time of the crash and consists of two lanes southbound, one lane northbound and a center left-turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during nighttime hours with street lights on, with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1989 Chrysler Lebaron, was southbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the right side of the roadway and the left side struck a metal pole. V1 rotated counterclockwise and rolled to the right. This vehicle rolled over six quarter-turns and came to rest on its top facing north. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-year-old male, was traveling with a twenty-four-year-old male and another unidentified passenger. This driver could not be reached for an interview.
