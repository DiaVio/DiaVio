This single-vehicle crash occurred on a four-lane, rural roadway with an extended median separating the north and southbound lanes. The southbound lanes are downhill and curve to the left at the crash location. The speed limit on this roadway is 105 kmph (65 mph). The crash occurred during daylight with no adverse weather or road conditions.
Vehicle one(V1), a 2006 Chevrolet Impala 4-door sedan, was southbound in lane one. A non-contact vehicle in lane two moved to the right. The driver of V1 applied the brakes and steered right. The vehicle traveled off the right side of the road rotating clockwise and the front struck the dirt embankment. The vehicle continued rotating clockwise and the back struck the embankment. The vehicle then rolled two quarter-turns and the top struck the embankment. The vehicle rotated on its top across both southbound lanes and came to rest off the left side of the roadway, facing north. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 105 kmph for V1.
The driver of V1, a twenty-six-year-old male, was traveling with a thirty-three-year-old female and a three-year-old male. This driver stated to police that the non-contact vehicle suddenly swerved into his lane and he steered right to avoid it. This driver was not located for an interview.
