A two vehicle daytime collision occurred on a weekday morning. The collision occurred on a five lane, dry, level, positively divided concrete expressway. The speed limit on the expressway is 104 kmph (65 mph), and the left-most lane is designated for high-occupancy vehicles only. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects. Traffic was congested from the morning work commute.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Ford Escort 4-door hatchback, was traveling southbound in the third lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Toyota Tacoma 2-door extra-cab pickup, was ahead of V1 in the same lane. V2 was approaching slow traffic ahead, and came to a complete stop. V1's front plane impacted V2's rear plane. Both vehicles came to rest facing south in the same lane. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
