This crash occurred in the northbound lanes of a two-lane highway. There is one northbound and one southbound lane. This was a slightly elevated street that curved to the left. It has a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). It was the early morning on a weekend and the roads were wet, the sky was cloudy, and there had been heavy rains very early in the morning.
V1, a 1999 Mazda Millenia with 4-doors was driven by a 27 year old female who was transported due to injuries. V1 was traveling northbound in lane one when the driver went off the road towards the right and struck a tree on the right front side, and spun about 90 degrees clockwise coming to a rest in lane one in the southbound lane. An attempt to talk to the driver at the hospital was denied. Several attempts to get in contact with the driver have turned up unsuccessful. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
