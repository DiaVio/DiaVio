This single-vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions. The north/south roadway is straight, level, undivided and consists of one lane in each direction and parking lanes on both sides. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 Ford Mustang, was southbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the left side of the road and the front struck a utility pole. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
The driver of V1 was a thirty-one-year-old male. His passenger, a twenty-two-year-old female, stated that she and the driver were talking and that she distracted him. No further information is available for this driver.
