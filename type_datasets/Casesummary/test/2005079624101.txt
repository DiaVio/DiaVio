This two vehicle crash occurred during early morning daylight hours at an intersection comprised of a six lanes a north/south and five lanes west/east. Road surface was level asphalt. Traffic flow was governed by tri-light signals. Both roadways were wet due to mist. They were free of congestion and sight restrictions at the time of the collision. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1993 Honda Accord sedan driven by an unknown age male, was traveling eastbound in the number three lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1993 Honda Civic coupe driven by a 28 year-old male with one passenger, was traveling westbound in the number one lane. V1 attempted a left turn at the intersection and the front plane of V1 contacted the front plane of V2 that was crossing through the intersection. After the impact V1 came to rest facing southwest blocking the westbound number two lane and eastbound left turn pocket. V2 came to rest facing northwest blocking the southbound number one lane. V1 and V2 were towed with major damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
