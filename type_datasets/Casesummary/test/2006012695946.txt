The crash occurred in the eastbound lane of a two-lane two-way roadway. This was a straight, level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed of 40 kmph (25 mph). It was evening on a weekday and the road was dark but lighted, dry and the sky was clear.
V1, a 1992 Buick Riviera was traveling eastbound. The driver of V1 stated that she was approaching a stop sign and attempted to hit her brakes. The brakes went to the floor so she put the vehicle in park. The steering then locked up and the front of her vehicle contacted the back of V2, 1990 Buick Estate Wagon that was parked on the south side of the road facing the same direction. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1 was a 40-year old female who had reported that she had been traveling between 50-64kmph (31-40mph) prior to the crash. She had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. She was rested and traveling to the nephew of the passenger's house. She does not wear prescribed lenses. She was uninjured in the crash and refused treatment.
