This single vehicle rollover occurred on a two-way, two lane roadway. There is one southbound and one northbound lane separated by a painted centerline stripe. There are shoulders on both sides of the roadway. There is a drop off to the west side of the roadway. The speed limit on the roadway is posted 105 kmph (65 mph). The roadway is straight, level and constructed of bituminous asphalt. The roadway was dry and the sky was clear at the time of this afternoon daylight crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 Honda Passport with two occupants, was traveling northbound. The vehicle departed the eastbound lane and then departed the roadway on the left side. The driver then steered the vehicle to the right and the vehicle returned to the roadway. The vehicle began to rotate clockwise and traveled across the roadway in a easterly direction until it departed the right side of the roadway. The vehicle traveled onto the dirt shoulder and up a slight uphill embankment where it tripped on it's left wheels. The vehicle rolled to its left and slid on it's top for approximately 7 meters (23 feet) before it came to rest on it's right side facing in a northerly direction. Both occupants were transported from the scene by EMS. The driver was injured. The right front passenger, a 21-year old female, was transported from the scene by EMS. The vehicle was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
A twenty three (23) year old male drove V1. He said that he fell asleep. He said that he had the cruise control set at 105 kmph (65 mph). He said that his wife was sleeping in the right front passenger seat with the seat back reclined. He said that he woke up and his vehicle was about to go off the edge of the cliff to the left. He said that he steered his vehicle to the right to get back onto the roadway and avoid going off the edge. He said that he over corrected and his vehicle began to spin clockwise. He said he steered to the left and his vehicle went off the right side of the roadway and rolled 3 times. He said that he remembers the car sliding on it's top before it came to rest on the right side facing the road. He said that he had been driving for about 2 hours before the crash. He said that he went to work the morning of the crash at about 0600 hours and worked until 1400 hours. He said that he went home after work. He said that he and his wife were on their way to his father in laws house for his birthday party. He said that got approximately 6 hours of sleep the night before the crash and had been working for the last 5 days before the crash. He said that he usually drives long distances and this is the first time that he has fallen asleep while driving. He said that he felt a little tired before he began this trip but felt ok to drive. He said he had the radio on low because his wife was sleeping in the seat next to him. The driver is in good health and takes no medication. The driver did say that he had a hard day at work. The driver is an electrician.
