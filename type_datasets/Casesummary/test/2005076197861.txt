This two-vehicle crash occurred during morning daylight hours on a 2-lane, 2-way, straight, level, non-divided highway located in a rural area. The speed limit for the road is 88 kmph (55 mph). The crash occurred during daylight hours, the weather was clear, and there were no adverse weather or road conditions present. Both vehicles were initially traveling in the same direction in the #1 eastbound lane.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Ford F-150 pickup driven by 60 year-old male, was traveling eastbound in the number one lane following Vehicle two (V2). V2, a 2000 Volkswagen Jetta 4 door, was driven by a 35 year-old female who was in the number one lane and began to slow and signal to prepare to turn left onto a private business driveway. V1 had already changed into the oncoming lane in a passing zone to pass V2 on the left. As V2 made its left turn, the front right tire and side of V1 contacted the left side of V2 near the front bumper. After impact, V1 continued forward and pulled off the left side of the roadway and came to rest facing east. After impact, V2's driver moved the vehicle into the private drive facing north. V1 was not towed. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1 and 89 kmph for V2.
