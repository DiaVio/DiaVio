This three-vehicle rear end type crash occurred shortly afternoon in daylight hours on two-way, five lane roadway. There are two westbound and two eastbound lanes separated by a continuous left turn lane. This was a straight level, bituminous roadway with a posted speed limit of 56 KPH (35 MPH). It was an early afternoon crash, the road was dry and the sky was clear. Traffic was heavy at the time of the crash.
Vehicle one (V-1), a 1994 Toyota 4 Runner driven by a 46 year-old male was traveling eastbound through the intersection in the #2 lane. Vehicle two (V-2), a 1996 Dodge Caravan driven by a 66 year-old female with one passenger was stopped in the #2 eastbound lane. Vehicle three (V-3) a 2000 Pontiac Grand Am sedan driven by a 17 year-old female was also stopped in the #2 eastbound lane. V-1 was traveling eastbound when it struck the rear of V-2. V-2 was stopped behind V-3 about 1-2 car lengths when it was pushed forward and struck the rear of V-3. V-3 stopped for another vehicle in front for about 1-2 seconds. Police Reported Travel Speed is 40 kmph for V1 and 40 kmph for V2.
The driver of V 1 stated that he was traveling eastbound in the #2 lane. He had just traveled through the intersection when V-2 stopped in front of him. He said that he was behind V-2 about 2 car lengths before it stopped. The driver said that he was on his way to work and was not paying attention to what was going on in front of him when V-2 stopped in front of him. He said that he hit his brakes but it was too late. He struck the rear of V-2. The driver of V 2 stated that she had just traveled through the intersection and the car in front of her stopped. She was stopped about 1-2 car lengths behind V 3 and then was pushed into the rear of V 3. V 2 was towed from the scene, V 1 and V 3 were driven from the scene.
V3 was not involved in first harmful event.
