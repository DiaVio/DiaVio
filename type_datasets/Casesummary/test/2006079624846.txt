This two-vehicle collision occurred during daylight hours on a weekday morning. The crash occurred on a straight, level stretch of an eight-lane, two-way bituminous north-south roadway, just prior to an intersection with an east-west roadway. The intersection was controlled by a tri-light traffic signal, and the speed limit was 56 kmph (35 mph) in all directions. At the time of the crash, there were no adverse weather conditions, and the roadway was free of defects.
Vehicle one (V1), a Toyota 4-Runner SUV of unknown year, was traveling southbound in the fourth lane, approaching the intersection. The fourth lane is designated for left turns only. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Honda Odyssey, was stopped at the intersection ahead of V1 in the same lane. V1 applied the brakes and skidded forwards, where the front of V1 impacted the back of V2. Both vehicles came to rest in their original lane. After the crash, V1 backed up, made a U-turn, and fled the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
