This crash occurred during the late afternoon hours. The weather was clear and it was daylight. The roadway was an urban, bituminous, four lane divided interstate with paved shoulders on both sides and a guardrail on the inside median divider. There is a 2% negative grade for the approach and area of impact. The posted speed limit 97 kmph ( 60 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Chevrolet Silverado LS extra cab pickup, driven by a 47 year-old male was traveling eastbound in lane two. The driver looked in his left side rearview mirror to prepare to make a lane change. When the driver looked back forward to the roadway vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Chevrolet 1500 Suburban driven by a 36 year-old female with two passengers, had move into the lane and was directly in front of V1. The driver was unable to stop and struck the rear of V2. V1 stopped on impact in it's original traffic lane. V2's driver pulled to the right shoulder and stopped. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 was driven from scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Chevrolet 1500 Suburban, was traveling eastbound in lane one just ahead of V1. V2 changed lanes into lane two and slowed for traffic ahead. V1 struck the rear of V2. On impact V2's driver pulled to the right shoulder and stopped. V2 was driven from the scene.
