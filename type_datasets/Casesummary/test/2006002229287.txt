This crash occurred on a straight two lane undivided roadway. This crash is driveway related. The crash occurred on a weekday afternoon. The weather was clear and the roadway was dry.
Vehicle #1, a 1991 Mazda B6S Pick-up, was entering the roadway from the driveway when it impacted with the right side of Vehicle #2, a 2001 Dodge Neon, with its front right. Vehicle #1 came to rest at the scene, then drove down the street before stopping. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
Vehicle #2 rolled down the street to a parking lot before coming to final rest.
Vehicle #1 was not towed. Vehicle #2 was towed by the father and grandfather of the driver, after the police left the scene, when they discovered the front right door would not close.
The 1991 Mazda pick-up (Vehicle #1) was driven by a belted 43 year old male who was not injured.
This driver has refused to answer messages left at his residence by phone and at his home, for an interview.
Driver Related Factor, Recognition Error, Inadequate Surveillance.
The 2001 Dodge Neon (Vehicle #2) was driven by a belted 18 year old female who stated she had seen Vehicle #1 stop at the end of the driveway and then continue without yielding. She stated she steered to the left and braked but could not avoid the crash.
Police did not consider illegal drugs or alcohol to be a factor and order no tests for either driver. The driver of Vehicle #1 (1991 Mazda pickup) was given a summons for Failure to Yield.
