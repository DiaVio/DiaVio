This three-vehicle crash occurred on a four-lane southbound roadway in the fourth lane. The roadway contained no traffic control devices, consists of bituminous asphalt, curved to the right and had an uphill grade at the area of the crash. Concrete barriers and a middle grassy area separate the southbound and northbound roadways. The grassy median is approximately 5 meters (16-feet) in width. The speed limit of the roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Chevrolet Malibu was southbound in the fourth lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Volkswagen Passat was stopped southbound in the fourth lane ahead of V1. Vehicle three (V3), a 1992 Oldsmobile Cutlass Supreme was southbound in the fourth lane behind V1. As V1 crested the small hill, traffic stopped. V1 steered left and braked. Vehicle one departed the road to the left and the left side of V1 sideswiped the center median. V1 then re-entered the number three travel lane and its front impacted the rear of vehicle two. The front of vehicle three then sideswiped with the rear of vehicle one. V1 and V2 came to rest near the point of impact. V3 moved around V1/ V2 and came to rest ahead of both vehicles in the fourth lane. V1 and V3 were towed due to damage. V2 was not towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
