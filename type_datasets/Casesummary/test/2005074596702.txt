A two vehicle rear-end collision occurred in the eastbound travel lanes of a 4-lane divided, bituminous roadway on a clear day just before noon. The speed limit was 64 kmph (40 mph) where the crash occurred and traffic was stop & go.
Vehicle one (V1) was a 1992 grey Honda Accord 4-door sedan with one restrained occupant. Vehicle two (2) was a 2005 tan Jeep Grand Cherokee 4-door 4x4 with three occupants inside; one of those was in a child restraint, the other two were unrestrained. Both vehicles were heading east in the second lane. V2 stopped because forward traffic was backed up behind the stoplight. V1 was behind V2 traveling east in the second lane at a slow steady pace when it impacted the rear of V2. V1 was towed from the scene, and V2 was left at scene. The driver of V2 was in her third trimester of pregnancy, and the driver along with the passengers was transported to the hospital. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
