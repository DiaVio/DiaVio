This two vehicle collision occurred at 0835 hours on a Monday. The collision occurred at a four-leg intersection comprised by an east/west roadway and a north/south roadway. The north/south roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of five lanes including a left-turn lane. The east/west roadway is undivided, dry, level, asphalt, and consists of seven lanes. Both roadways are designated 35 mph (56 kph) zones. The intersection is controlled by a tri-light signal that was functioning at the time of the collision. Traffic or weather conditions were not a factor.
Vehicle one (V1) is a 2000 Dodge Caravan minivan that was traveling north in the number two lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2000 Toyota Camry that was traveling west in the number three lane. As both vehicles entered the intersection, the front of V1 struck the left side of V2. V2 was deflected to the right as it traveled towards the northwest corner of the intersection, mounted the curb, and continued in a northerly direction where the front of the vehicle struck a pole and a chain link fence before coming to rest. V1 came to rest facing northwest near the northwest corner. Both vehicles were towed with major damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
