This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is straight and level and has two lanes in each direction with a center left turn lane at the intersection. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 GMC Safari minivan, was southbound in lane three (left turn lane). Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 Freightliner tractor-trailer, was northbound in lane one. V1 turned left in the intersection and the right side was struck by the front of V2. V1 rotated clockwise and the left rear wheel struck the curb in the northeast corner of the intersection. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1 and No PAR obtained (created) for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-five-year-old male, was on his way to work from his home. According to this driver's son, he was healthy, well rested and familiar with his vehicle and his route. This driver was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
The driver of V2 was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.
