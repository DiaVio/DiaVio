The crash occurred in the middle of a T-intersection with a median controlled by a stop sign. Conditions were daylight and clear in the afternoon on a weekday. The northbound lane did not have a posted speed limit. The eastbound lane had a posted speed limit of 45 MPH with a negative 2% downhill grade.
V1 was a 1995 Dodge Intrepid sedan traveling northbound in the first travel lane approaching a T-intersection controlled by a stop sign. V2, a 1999 Lexus ES300 sedan, was traveling eastbound in the first travel lane approaching the T-intersection intending on continuing straight. As V1 approached the stop sign, he was stopped 5-10 seconds prior to proceeding on turning left. The driver of V1 looked right then left and turned left. The front of V1 struck the right side of V2 in the middle of the intersection. V1 came to final rest facing northbound and V2 came to final rest facing eastbound. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1 was driven by a 91-year-old male who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital. He stated that he was stopped at the stop sign, looked right then left and did not see the other vehicle then proceeded on turning left. The driver of V1 did not take any avoidance actions and was traveling between1-10 MPH at the time of impact. V1 was towed due to damage.
V2 was driven by a 57-year-old female who was not injured. She stated that she was traveling eastbound in the first travel lane when she saw V1 stopped at the stop sign then V1 just pulled out in front of her. The driver of V2 did not take any avoidance actions and was traveling between 41-50 MPH at the time of impact. V2 was towed due to damage.
