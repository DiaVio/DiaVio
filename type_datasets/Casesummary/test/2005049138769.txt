The crash occurred late afternoon/early evening hours between two passenger cars at the intersection of a two-way, two lane (four overall), divided roadway without a positive barrier and a two-way, two lane, undivided roadway. Both roads were dry, traveled concrete. Southbound traffic had a +3.3% grade and Northbound traffic had a -2.5% grade. The posted speed limit was 56 kmph (40 mph). There were no adverse weather conditions. Traffic flow was controlled at the intersection by traffic signals.
Vehicle one (V1) a 2002 Honda Civic driven by a 30 year-old male was northbound in the second travel lane. Vehicle two (V2) a 1998 Nissan Maxima driven by a 30 year-old male was southbound in the second travel lane. V2 attempted to make a left turn across the path of V1. V2 had no protected turn arrow. The driver of V2 stated that his view of V1 was obstructed by a non-contact vehicle in the northbound turn lane. As V2 entered the intersection it was struck in the front by V1. V rotated counterclockwise and came to rest facing northeast in the intersection. V1 rotated slightly clockwise and came to rest facing north in the intersection. Both vehicles were towed from the scene Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
