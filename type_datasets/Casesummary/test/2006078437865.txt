This is a three-vehicle intersection crash that occurred in the early morning hours at a four-way intersection that is controlled by a traffic light on colors. There are two northbound and two southbound lanes that are separated by a left turn lane. There is one eastbound lane and one westbound lane separated by a left turn lane. The roadways are straight, level and constructed of bituminous asphalt. The speed limit for the westbound and the eastbound roadway is posted speed limit of 40 kmph (25 mph). The speed limit for the southbound and northbound lanes is posted 56 kmph (35 mph). The roadway was dry and the sky was clear.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 Chevrolet 4X2 pickup driven by a 19 year-old male was stopped northbound in the left turn lane waiting to turn left. Vehicle two (V2), a 1993 Ford Explorer 4X2 driven by a 45 year-old male was traveling southbound in the #1 lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle three (V3), a 1987 Honda Accord 2 door, driven by a 31 year-old male was stopped eastbound at the intersection waiting for the traffic light. The traffic light turned green for north/south traffic and V-1 entered the intersection. V-1 made a left turn and a Non-contact vehicle traveling in the #2 southbound lane had to stop to avoid striking V-1. V-2 was traveling southbound and locked the brakes up and skidded for about 7-meters (29-feet) before it struck the right rear corner of V-1. V-2 came to a stop in the intersection facing south. The impact caused V-1 to rotate clockwise. V-1 continued out of the intersection in a westerly direction. The left rear area of V-1 struck the left front corner of V-3. The vehicle then rotated counterclockwise and began to travel west and came to a stop in a parking lot to the west of the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is 24 kmph for V1 and 24 kmph for V2.
The driver of V 2 stated that he was traveling southbound in the #1 lane at about 40 kmph (25 mph). He said that there was another car in the lane next to him also about to enter the intersection. He said that when he entered the intersection, V-1 turned across his path. He said that he did not see V-1 until it was in front of him. He applied the brakes but still struck V-1. The driver of V3 stated that he was stopped eastbound on his way to work at the traffic light. He said that he was stopped for about 30 seconds when V1 made a left turn in front of V-2. He said that V-1 was stopped in the intersection waiting to turn left prior to making the left turn. V-1 spun around and struck his vehicle and left the scene and stopped in a parking lot to the west of the intersection. He said that as the V-1 was turning left, he saw the driver looking to the right a he was making the turn.
V3 was not involved in first harmful event.
