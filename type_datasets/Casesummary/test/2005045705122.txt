The crash occurred in the right northbound lane of a four-lane, north-south suburban highway with a grass median. There were two northbound lanes and a right side shoulder that was four meters wide with uncontrolled access points on the right. Conditions were daylight and dry with partly cloudy skies in mid afternoon on a weekday. The posted speed limit was 72kph (45mph) and the portion of road where the crash occurred was straight and level.
Vehicle #2 was a 1999 Freightliner Tractor pulling a 53 foot dry van trailer with a combined Gross Vehicle Weight Rating of 80,000lbs. The trailer was empty at the time of the crash. Vehicle #2 was in the right northbound lane slowing to almost completely stopped while attempting to turn right into a private lot. Vehicle #1 was a 2002 Chrysler Sebring traveling north in the right lane and was approaching Vehicle #2 at a rapid rate. Vehicle #1 then impacted the back end of Vehicle #2 after skidding longitudinally for almost 12 meters. Vehicle #1 went under the rear of Vehicle#2 bending Vehicle #2's underride guard to an almost horizontal position. Both vehicles came to rest within 2 meters of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
