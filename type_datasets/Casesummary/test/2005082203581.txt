This two vehicle daytime collision occurred at 1155 hours. The collision occurred heading southbound on a non-divided two-way roadway with on-street parking on both sides. The pre-crash roadway is downhill with a grade of 5% and straight, dry bituminous paving. The roadway has no posted speed limit, although the general speed limit for non-arterial streets is 40 Kmph (25 mph).
Vehicle 1 (V 1), a 1997 Nissan Altima sedan, driven by a 82 year-old male was traveling southbound on the roadway after turning out of a parking garage on the same street. Vehicle 2 (V 2), a 1999 Toyota 4Runner compact SUV, was parked against the east curb facing north. V 1 accelerated down the street, swerved left and the front of V 1 struck the front of V 2. The driver of V 1 was transported to a hospital for back injuries. V 1 was towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
