V1, A 2004 Buick LeSabre, was attempting to enter a five lane, two way, asphalt north/south roadway in daylight conditions. The roadway was dry, straight and level. The posted speed limit was 64 kmph (40 mph). V2, a 1988 Pontiac Bonneville, was traveling southbound on the roadway, intending to go straight. V1 began to make a left hand turn out of a driveway onto the roadway when the front of V2 contacted the left side of V1. Both vehicles came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 90-year old male who reported that he had been traveling between 2-16 kmph (1-10 mph) prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling home. He was wearing his prescribed lenses that corrected an unknown condition. He did not sustain any injuries and refused treatment. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 62-year old male who reported that he had been traveling between 67-80 kmph (41-50 mph) prior to the crash. He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash. He was rested and traveling home. He did not wear prescribed lenses. He was uninjured in the crash and refused treatment.
