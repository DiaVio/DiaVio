The crash occurred in signalized intersection of divided two-way roadway with three lanes and one left turning lane in both directions. The roadway was straight and level, and it was dark, but lighted and clear with no adverse conditions. The posted speed limit was 48kmph (30 mph).
Vehicle # 1 was a 1997 International M/H Truck that was traveling eastbound on the left turning lane. Vehicle # 2 was a 2005 Scion XB that was traveling eastbound on the left travel lane. At the intersection, Vehicle # 1 attempted to go straight when the back of Vehicle # 1 was impacted by the front of Vehicle # 2. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The International M/H Truck (Vehicle # 1) was driven by a 32 year-old male who did not sustain any injuries. The International M/H Truck was not towed due to damage.
The Scion XB (Vehicle # 2) was driven by a 26 year-old male who did not sustain any injuries. The passenger, a 21 year-old female also did not sustain any injuries. The Scion was towed due to damage.
