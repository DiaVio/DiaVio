The crash occurred in the center of a "T" intersection composed of a two-lane east/west roadway, and a two-lane north/southbound driveway. Both roadways were bituminous. The east/west roadway had a 3% grade and a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35mph). The driveway was level with no posted speed. The roads were dry and the traffic flow was moderate.
V1, a 2003 Nissan Altima with one occupant, was traveling east when it attempted to turn left into the driveway and was struck by V2 on the rightt side. V2, a 2003 Honda Accord with one occupant, was traveling west in lane one. As V2 passed through the intersection it struck V1 on the right side. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, driven by a 45-year-old female who reported a Complaint of Pain was transported, stated that she slowed down to turn into the driveway and thinking she had enough room attempted to turn left. V1 made no avoidance maneuver.
The Driver of V2, a 19-year-old female, reported a Complaint of Pain and was transported. Driver stated that she was traveling at approximately 56 kmph (35 mph) as she entered the intersection, when V2 turned across her path and she struck V1 on the right side. V2 attempted to avoid the collision by applying her brakes.
