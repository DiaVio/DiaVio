This two-vehicle crash occurred on a divided, straight, level roadway. There are three through lanes in the westbound direction. There is a driveway on the right (north) side of this roadway. The speed limit on this roadway is 64 kmph (40 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash and there were no adverse atmospheric conditions.
Vehicle one (V1), a 1992 Honda Civic, was stopped facing south in the driveway. Vehicle two (V2), a 2002 Dodge Intrepid, was westbound in lane one. V1 entered the roadway, turning right. The front of V2 struck the left side of V1. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V1, a thirty-three-year-old female, with a one-year-old female passenger, was traveling from the gas station to their home. She was well rested, healthy and familiar with her vehicle and passenger. She travels this route daily. This driver stated that she did not see V2 before the impact.
The driver of V2, a thirty-four-year-old male, was traveling from his friend's home to his home. He was in good health, well rested and comfortable with his vehicle. He drives on this roadway daily. This driver stated that he saw V1 but did not realize that V1 would enter the roadway until it was too late to make any avoidance maneuvers.
