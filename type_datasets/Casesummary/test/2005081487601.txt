This two-vehicle morning collision occurred during daylight hours on a two-way, straight, undivided five lane bituminous roadway consisting of 2 travel lanes in each direction and a 2-way center turn lane. . There is a slight uphill grade for V1, and the roadway is level for V2. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 48 kph (30 mph). At the time of the crash, the weather was clear and there were no sight restrictions or road defects.
Vehicle #1 (V1), a red 1998 Honda Accord 2 door was traveling north in lane 2. Vehicle #2 (V2), a dark green 2005 Honda Accord 4 door was stopped in the center turn lane preparing to turn left, when V1 swerved into the center turn lane, and the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The V1 driver, a 26-year-old male, was not injured, and stated to police that he had swatted with a newspaper at a bee that flew at his face. The V2 driver, a 59 year old female, stated to police that she noticed the newspaper and that her 1st impression was that the V1 driver was reading, but that after hearing the V1 driver's account of what happened, that account was believable. The V2 driver was transported, treated and released for soft tissue neck and back injuries.
Neither driver was interviewed, so driver related factors are unknown.
