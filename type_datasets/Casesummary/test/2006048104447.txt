This two-vehicle crash occurred shortly before noon on a dry, downhill, ten-lane, bituminous, dry roadway that runs east to west and has a posted speed limit of 80 kmph (50 mph). This roadway intersects with a eight-lane, level, straight, bituminous roadway that runs north to south and has a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). This intersection is controlled by a traffic control device (on colors) with no pedestrian signals. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions present at the time of the daytime crash.
Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Chevrolet Trailblazer driven by a 34 year-old female with one passenger, was traveling eastbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2004 Chrysler PT Cruiser driven by a 64 year-old female, was passing through the intersection traveling northbound in lane one. As V1 entered the intersection the front of V2 struck the right side of V1. Post-impact, V1 rotated clockwise and began to roll towards its left upon its longitudinal axis. V1 rolled three quarter turns coming to rest on its right side facing west. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing north. Both vehicles were towed form the scene, both drivers and the passenger of V1 were transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1 and 64 kmph for V2.
