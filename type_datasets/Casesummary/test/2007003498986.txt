The crash occurred on an east/west two lane undivided roadway with one lane in each direction. The roadway was straight, level, and bituminous roadway with a speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). It was the afternoon hours of a weekday and the road was dry and the sky was clear.
V1, a 2004 Honda Pilot sport utility vehicle with one occupant, was traveling westbound in lane one. V2, a 2006 Acura RL four door sedan with three occupants, was traveling westbound in lane one in front of V1. V3, a 2003 Honda Pilot sport utility vehicle with three occupants, was traveling westbound in lane one in front of V2. V2 and V3 were stopped for the traffic control device, when the front of V1 impacted the back of V2 twice, causing the front of V2 to impact the back of V3. All three vehicles came to a final rest shortly after impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
V1, the 2004 Honda Pilot, was driven by a male who reported no injuries. He advised the police that he was stopped for the traffic light when for some unknown reason his car went back and forth and struck V1 twice.
V2, the Acura RL, was driven by a 56-year-old female who reported no injuries. A 22 year-old female occupant reported minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility, and an 86-year-old female occupant reported no injuries. The driver advised that she was coming from her mother's house and going to the store, and while stopped for the traffic light, she was impacted twice from behind causing her to impact the vehicle in front of her.
V3, the 2003 Honda Pilot, was driven by a 27-year-old female who reported minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility. A 58 year-old female and a 7-month old female occupant both reported minor injuries and were both transported to a medical facility. She advised that she was stopped at the traffic light when she was struck from behind.
