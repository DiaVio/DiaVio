V1, A 1995 Chevrolet Beretta was traveling southbound on a five lane, two way, dry asphalt roadway in dark but lighted conditions. V1 was traveling in lane three, intending to turn left at the intersection. V2 a 2002 Chevrolet Cavalier was traveling northbound on the same roadway intending to go straight. As V1 began turning left the front of V2 contacted the right of V1. Both Vehicles came to final rest in the roadway.
The driver of V1 was a 16-year old female who was not available for an interview. She was uninjured but the two other occupants of her vehicle suffered non-incapacitating injuries and were transported to a local trauma facility. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
The driver of V2 was a 41-year old male who reported that he had been traveling 50-64kmph (31-40mph) prior to the crash. He was in good health, rested, and traveling to his girlfriend's house. He was uninjured and his vehicle was towed due to damage.
