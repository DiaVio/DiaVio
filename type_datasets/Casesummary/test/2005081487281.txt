A two vehicle daytime collision occurred at noon. The collision occurred in a busy intersection. Both the north/south direction of travel, and the east/west direction of travel have 6 lanes in each direction. The intersection is controlled by on-colors with pedestrian signals. The pre-crash roadway is level, and straight bituminous paving. The roadway has a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).
Vehicle one (V1), a 1986 Ford Bronco, driven by a 70 year-old female with one passenger was traveling eastbound on the roadway, crossing through the intersection in lane two. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Dodge Durango SUV, driven by a 55 year-old male was traveling northbound crossing through the intersection in lane two. The front of V2 impacted the right side of V1 in the intersection, causing V1 to roll to its left, coming to rest on its roof facing south west. V2 was moved to a nearby service station for final rest. V1 & V2 were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
