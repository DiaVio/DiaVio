This crash occurred at a in the southbound lane of a two-lane undivided roadway at a T-style intersection. Traveling southbound the roadway curved to the left and had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). Entering the roadway from the intersection was straight and level. Both roadways were bituminous.
V1 was a 1999 Ford Escort, driven by a 17 year-old male. V2 was a 2006 Jeep Grand Cherokee, driven by a 28 year-old female. V1 was stopped at a stop sign, facing east, and was preparing to enter the roadway and proceed northbound. V1 entered the intersection when V2, which was traveling southbound, struck V1 in the left rear portion of the vehicle. Both vehicles came to final rest on the roadway with no further impacts. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.
