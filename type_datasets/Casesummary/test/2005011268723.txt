V1, a 2003 Ford Taurus, was southbound in the left turn lane of a 5 lane, bituminous, 2 way, urban roadway intending to left at a controlled intersection with an identical roadway. V2, a 1996 Mercury Villager minivan, was eastbound in the left turn lane of a 5 lane, bituminous roadway at the same controlled intersection intending to turn north. The intersection was flat, with 90-degree angles, it was daylight and the roads were dry. Both vehicles had left turn arrows. As v2 was making a left turn to go northbound, V1 began its left turn as well striking V2's left side with it's front. V1 was cited for disobeying the signal. Both drivers were injured and transported. V2 was towed due to damage.
