This crash occurred at 1049 hours involving two passenger cars at an intersection with stop signs north/south. The speed limit for the roadways is 54 kmph (35 mph) for the eastbound lanes and 48 kmph (30 mph) for the northbound lanes. The road is a 4-lane divided concrete roadway from west to east. It is a two lane (two-way traffic) asphalt roadway from south to north. The road surface was dry and there were no detrimental atmospheric conditions at the time of the crash.

V 1, a 2000 Ford Crown Victoria Sedan driven by a 79 year-old male with one passenger was traveling north in the first lane of traffic approaching the intersection of both roadways. V 2, a 1993 Mazda 929 Sedan, driven by a 18 year-old male was traveling east in the third lane. V 1 was attempting to pass straight through the intersection and enter the entry road to a shopping mall. Just prior to impact V 1 driver apparently steered to the right to avoid a collision with V2. The front of V 2 impacted the left side of V 1. V 1 rotated clockwise. V 2 rotated counter clockwise after the impact, and V2's right rear corner impacted with the left rear of V 1. V 1 traveled east and entered the concrete median of the roadway. V 1 came to rest partially on the median, facing northeast. V 2 followed a u-turn path within the intersection before it came to rest within the west bound lanes, facing northwest. Both vehicles were towed from the scene, the drivers were transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
