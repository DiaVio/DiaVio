This two vehicle collision occurred shortly before noon in daylight hours. The collision occurred on a two-lane, one-way roadway approaching a controlled intersection. The grade for both vehicles is +7% uphill, paved with concrete and dry. The posted speed limit for both roads is 40 Kph (25 mph).

Vehicle 1 (V 1), a 2005 Chevrolet Astro cargo van, driven by a 35 year-old male was traveling westbound in lane two. Vehicle 2 (V 2), a 1998 Dodge Durango utility vehicle driven by a 69 year-old male was traveling westbound in lane one of the same roadway. V 2 attempted to turn left from lane two and crossed over the left lane line, the front of V 1 impacted the left side of V 2. After impact V 1 continued on a southwesterly direction and traveled off the southwest corner of the intersection. The front of V 1 impacted a building and traveled through the wall of the building and continued through the building with front again impacting the south wall. V 1 came to rest inside the building facing southeast up against the south wall. V 2 continued west and came to rest west of the intersection parked next to the left curb. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
