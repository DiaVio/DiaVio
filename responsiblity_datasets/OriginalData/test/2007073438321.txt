This single-vehicle crash occurred on a straight, level, undivided roadway. This roadway consists of one lane in each direction. The speed limit on this roadway is 32 kmph (20 mph). It was snowing at the time of the crash and the road was slush-covered. This crash occurred during daylight hours.
