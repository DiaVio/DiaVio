This crash occurred at dusk on a weekday evening along an unlighted two-lane asphalt roadway. There was an uphill grade of 9.8% and the road curved to the right with a radius of 72m. The speed limit was 25mph (40kph).

Vehicle 1 was a 1992 Pontiac Bonneville driven by a 46 year-old male. There was a 39 year-old male in the front passenger seat. The driver declined participation in this survey. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.

Vehicle 1 departed the right road edge and the front right of the vehicle contacted a wooden utility pole. The vehicle came to rest across its original travel lane.

The critical event was this vehicle traveling over the lane line on the right side of the travel lane. The critical reason for the critical precrash event was poor directional control. A contributing factor is that the driver had been drinking.
