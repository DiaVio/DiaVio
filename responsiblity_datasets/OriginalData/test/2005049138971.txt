The crash occurred just before noon at the intersection of a two-way, six lane undivided roadway and a one-way, three lane roadway. Both roads were dry, polished, level concrete. The posted speed limit is 48 kmph (30 mph) for V 1 and 64 kmph (40 mph) for V 2. There were no adverse weather conditions. There were two passenger cars involved in the crash.

V 1 a 1999 Honda Prelude driven by a 32 year-old male was westbound in the second travel lane. V 2 a 2000 Toyota Celica driven by a 24 year-old male was southbound in the first travel lane. V 1 entered the intersection without the right of way and was struck on the right side by the front of V 2. V 1 rotated counterclockwise and struck the west median with it's front left tire. V 1 continued west and pulled over into a nearby parking lot. V 2 rotated clockwise and came to rest facing southwest in the intersection. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The pre-event movement for V 1 coded going straight, category this vehicle traveling crossing over passing through intersection. Critical reason driver related "inattention" (disregard of stop light). Pre-event movement for V 2 coded stopped in traffic lane, category other vehicle encroachment from crossing street. Critical reason not coded to this vehicle.
