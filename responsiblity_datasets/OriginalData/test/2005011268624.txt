V1, a 2003 Pontiac Sunfire, was traveling westbound in lane 2 of 3 westbound lanes of an interstate roadway. The straight, bituminous, positive grade >2% roadway was dry and it was daylight. V2, a 1990 dodge Caravan, was westbound in lane 1 of the same roadway. V1 was overtaking v2 when it drifted to the right over the lane line. V1, right, contacted v2, left. V1 the rotated cw striking the median wall with it's back. V2 rotated ccw striking the median wall with it's back then it's front. Neither driver was injured. V2 was towed due to damage.

A complete driver interview and vehicle inspection was obtained for v1. The Critical Pre-Crash Event for v1 was passing or overtaking another vehicle. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

A complete driver interview and vehicle inspection was obtained for v2. The Critical Pre-Crash Event for v2 was going straight.
