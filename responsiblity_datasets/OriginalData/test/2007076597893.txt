This three vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged light controlled intersection. This intersection has curved lanes on the east to west roads and curved roads on the north to south entrances. This crash occurred early evening with no overhead street lighting. The east to west lanes are on uphill grade for the east direction. The north to south are on a hill crest of +2 percent for both directions. The posted speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph) for the east to west and 56 kmph (35 mph) for the north to south lanes. The road surface was dry asphalt.

Vehicle one (V1) was a 1999 Chevy S-10 pickup driven by a eighty one (81) year old male. V1 was eastbound in the #2 lane when he failed to stop for red light in the intersection and was impacted on the left side by Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Chevrolet two-door Cavalier coupe. V2 was driven by a thirty two (32) year old female with two passengers who were traveling south in lane #2. After impact V1 rotated counter-clockwise and crossed the intersection and impacted a control signal light pole in the center on the east/west travel lanes. V1 snapped off the light pole at the base then crossed into the westbound lanes where it impacted the front of Vehicle three (V3) a 1993 Chevrolet pickup driven by a sixty four (64) year old male . V1 came to rest in the westbound #1 lane heading in a northerly direction. The driver of V2 continued forward and pulled off the roadway to the right shoulder of the northbound travel lanes coming to a stop. V1 and V3 came to final rest at impact. Both V1 an V3 were towed from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 64 kmph for V1 and 64 kmph for V2.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded: this vehicle traveling; crossing over (passing through) intersection. The critical reason for the critical event was coded to V1 as a driver related factor: inadequate surveillance (e.g., failed to look, looked but did not see).

The critical pre-crash event for V2 was coded: other vehicle encroachment; from crossing street, across path. The critical reason was not coded to this vehicle.

V3 was not involved in first harmful event. The critical reason was not coded to this vehicle.
