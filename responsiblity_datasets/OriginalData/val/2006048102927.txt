This single-vehicle crash occurred just past noon during daylight hours on a clear day. The crash occurred off of the roadway. The roadway is two-lane, straight, level, and bituminous. The speed limit is 72 kmph (45 mph).

Vehicle one (V 1), a 1996 Mazda 626 sedan driven by a 52 year-old female, was traveling east. The driver blacked out and departed the left side of lane one then departed the left road edge. V 1 traveled approximately 121-meters (397-feet) through back yards then struck a sign. V 1 then traveled another 160-meters (525-feet) crossing the roadway to the right roadside before coming to final rest in a ditch headed east. The driver was transported to a local trauma center with possible injuries and the vehicle was towed due to disabling damage. The driver stated that she had a history of seizures. The driver also stated that she does not sleep well due to chronic back pain and always feels tired. The driver had been shopping and was headed home. She remembered leaving the store but had no recollection of driving to the crash scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 72 kmph for V1.
