This two-vehicle crash occurred on a dry, five-lane roadway with a light controlled intersection with pedestrian signals. The roadway runs northeast to west. Eastbound traffic has two through lanes and one left turn lane and westbound traffic has two through lanes and one left turn lane. The east bound lanes have a curve of 87 meters. The speed limit on the roadway is 72 kph (45 mph) and there were no adverse weather conditions at the time. This crash did occur during early morning hours with a rising run to the east.

Vehicle one, a 1997 Ford F150 was northeast bound on a right curve in lane three. Vehicle one, a 2001 Mitsubishi Eclipse was westbound in lane one. Vehicle one was stopped at a red light behind another vehicle when the light turned green. The non-contact vehicle was able to make the left turn. Vehicle one proceeded into the intersection and started its turn the same time vehicle one entered the intersection to go through the light. Vehicle one was impacted on the right side to vehicle two's front. Both vehicles came to rest near the point of impact. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.

The driver of vehicle one, a forty-one year old male was taking his son to school. This driver drives this road daily and in this vehicle daily. This driver stated that he saw this vehicle coming but thought that it was much farther back. This driver stated that the sun was creating a lot of glare in the direction he was looking. This driver is required to wear prescription lenses for unknown vision impairment but was not wearing glasses or sunglasses.

The driver of vehicle two, a seven-teen year old male was heading to a friends house from home. This driver had been driving for about thirteen minutes at the time of the crash. This driver drives this vehicle daily and over this road on a daily basis. This driver had only been driving for about ten months. This driver stated that he was driving down the road with his cruise control activated.

The critical pre-crash event for vehicle one was coded as “this vehicle traveling: turning left at intersection”. The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for the crash was attributed to vehicle one and was coded as “driver related factor: misjudgment of other's speed”. The critical pre-crash event for vehicle two was coded as “other vehicle encroachment: from opposite direction- over left lane line”. The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for vehicle two was coded as “not coded to this vehicle”.
