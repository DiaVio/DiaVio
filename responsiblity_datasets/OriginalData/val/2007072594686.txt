This two-vehicle crash occurred in a three-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is straight, level, undivided and consists of one lane in each direction with parking on both sides. The speed limit for this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.

Vehicle one (V1), a 1995 GMC Jimmy, was northbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V2), a 2005 BMW 645, was southbound in lane one. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. The vehicles rotated and the right side of V1 struck the left side of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.

The driver of V1 was a twenty-one-year-old male who was traveling with a twenty-one-year-old male passenger. This driver was not interviewed. No further information is available for this driver.

The driver of V2, a twenty-year-old male, was traveling with a twenty-one-year-old male passenger. This driver's father was interviewed and provided minimal information. This driver was familiar with his vehicle and travels this route occasionally. No further information is available for this driver.

The critical pre-crash event for V2 was coded as "other vehicle encroachment; from opposite direction - over left lane line." The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for V2 was "not coded to this vehicle."
