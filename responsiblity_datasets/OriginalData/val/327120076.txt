This case involves a left, offset-impact between the front of a passenger car and a large tree. The 67-year-old male driver is the case occupant. He was restrained by an integrated three-point belt and the deployed steering-wheel mounted air bag. The case vehicle a 2005 four-door Buick LeSabre was northbound on a two-lane, wet, level asphalt traffic-way. It was daylight and cloudy. The case vehicle mounted the east barrier curb , struck a large tree and rotated counterclockwise before coming to rest facing north. The 67-year-old male driver was restrained by an integrated three-point belt and the deployed steering-wheel mounted air bag. He sustained multiple moderate injuries and was transported to a level-one trauma center.
