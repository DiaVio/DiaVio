This single vehicle collision occurred on an interstate highway. The pre-crash roadway is a slight downhill grade on-ramp, on a straight section of bituminous roadway. Access to the roadway is fully controlled, with an on-ramp in the immediate area of the crash. The posted speed limit for the roadway is 96 kph (60 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with snow and the roadway was slick.

Vehicle one lost control and departed the on-ramp to the left across the gore and entered the main lanes. Vehicle one traveled across all three lanes and impacted the concrete median with its front plane. Vehicle one rotated and the left plane impacted the concrete median. Vehicle one was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.
