Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 2001 Plymouth Neon, four-door (case study vehicle). Vehicle 2 (V2) is a 1986 International tractor-trailer. Vehicle 3 (V3) is a 2004 Jeep Grand Cherokee, four-door. V1 was occupied by a male, age 21 as its driver (case study participant). The case study participant was wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal air bags didn't deploy as a result of impact.

The crash occurred during the hours of daylight. The weather at the time of the crash was clear and dry. The roadway surface was dry. The posted speed limit is 55 mph (88 kmph).

The crash occurred on an interstate roadway. The roadway consists of six lanes, four through lanes and two deceleration lanes. The roadway travels in a north-south direction in the area of the crash. The roadway is divided by a raised concrete barrier. The roadway is level in the area of the crash.

V1 was traveling northbound in the left deceleration lane. V2 was traveling northbound in the right through lane. V3 was traveling northbound in the right deceleration lane. V2 changed lanes strking V1 in the left side with its right front in a sideswiping impact. V1 was then struck in the rear by the front of V3. V1 then struck the concrete barrier on the right shoulder with its right side rear. V1 then re-engaged the concrete barrier with its right side front.

The case study participant was transported by air to a trauma center.
