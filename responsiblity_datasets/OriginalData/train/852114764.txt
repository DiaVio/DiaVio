This crash occurred during daylight hours with clear, dry conditions. Vehicle one (V1), a 1996 Saturn SL2 four door sedan (side impact compliant vehicle), was westbound on a two lane, two way roadway. The roadway has an upgrade and a curve to the left with a posted speed limit of 25 mph/40kmph. V1 completed the curve and then began to rotate clockwise on the roadway before departing the roadway to the right sliding lateral into a sign post and then a large tree both impacting into the driver side of V1. V1 then rotated clockwise back on to the roadway and then continued south into the ditch and impacted the front of V1 into a guard rail where it came to final rest. The driver of V1 is the case occupant who was a 17 year old male who was wearing a manual lap/shoulder belt. No airbags deployed in the vehicle. Other occupants in V1 were a 16 year old male in the front right passenger seat wearing the lap/shoulder belt, and a 16 year old female in the back right seat also wearing a lap/shoulder belt. The vehicle was disabled and towed.
