This single-vehicle crash occurred early in the morning with no adverse atmospheric conditions. The roadway is two-lane, undivided, concrete and straight in the pre-crash area. This roadway curves to the right just beyond the point where vehicle one left the roadway. The speed limit for this roadway is 40 kmph (25 mph).

Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Dodge Caravan, was traveling westbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the left side of the roadway and the left front wheel struck the curb. V1 continued in a southwest direction and the left exterior side mirror struck a small tree and the front struck a building. The vehicle came to rest next to the building facing southwest and was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.

The driver of V1, a fifty-eight-year-old male, was traveling from his home to work. He had been prescribed Lisinopril for high blood pressure.
