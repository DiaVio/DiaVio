This single vehicle crash occurred on a left-curve, undivided, level, dry, asphalt roadway. Prior to the crash location, this roadway consists of two lanes, one in each direction. At the crash location, this roadway widens to include a center turn lane. This roadway has paved sloped shoulders and cotton fields outside the trafficway. The posted speed limit on this roadway is 89 kmph (55 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse weather conditions.

Vehicle one (V1), a 2004 Dodge Intrepid, was westbound negotiating the left curve. This vehicle traveled off the right side of the roadway, the driver steered left and the vehicle returned to the roadway. The vehicle crossed the left lane line and the driver braked and steered right. The vehicle then rotated clockwise, traveled off the right side of the roadway and rolled over to the left two quarter-turns. V1 came to rest on its top facing east and was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 89 kmph for V1.

The driver of V1, a sixty-nine-year-old female, was driving from her friend's house to her own home. She was well rested and very familiar with her vehicle and route. She has medical conditions (for which she takes prescription medications) that did not affect her driving ability. She stated that she unbuckled her seat belt to straighten the twisted webbing and as she looked down to re-buckle the seat belt, she drove off the roadway.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded: this vehicle traveling, off the edge of the road on the right side. The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded as a driver related factor: internal distraction.
