This crash occurred at the intersection of a driveway and a four lane undivided roadway. Conditions were daylight and dry, and the bitumous roadway had a posted speed limit of 48kmph (30 mph).

V1, a 1984 Cadillac Seville, was traveling east in lane two. V2, a Dodge Caravan, was stopped in eastbound lane two waiting to initiate a left turn into the driveway. The front plane of V1 came into contact with the rear plane of V2. Police Reported Travel Speed is No PAR obtained (created) for V1 and No PAR obtained (created) for V2.

The Critical Precrash Event for V1 was coded Other Vehicle in Lane, (stopped). The Critical Reason for the Critical Precrash Event was coded unkown.

The Critical Precrash Event for V2 was coded Other Vehicle in Lane, (traveling at higher speed). The Critical Reason was not coded to V2.
