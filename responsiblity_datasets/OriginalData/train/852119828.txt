This crash occurred at an traffic light controlled intersection at night with streetlights on in the area. The roadway was wet with overcast conditions. Case vehicle one ( V1), a 2002 Kia Sportage compact utility vehicle was eastbound in the left turn lane of a three lane, two way street. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Chevrolet K2 large pickup truck was westbound on a three lane, two way street. At the intersection of the travel streets, case V1 was turning left and V2 was traveling straight through the interection when the front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V1 post impact rotated counterclockwise 90 degrees and came to final rest facing northwest in the northwest area of the intersection. V2 veered slightly clockwise post impact and came to final rest along side of V1 with a minor secondary contact occurring with the right side of V1 with the left side of V2. V1 was towed and V2 was driven from the scene. The case occupant in V1 is the driver and no other occupants were in the case vehicle. The case occupant was a 52 year old female who was found to not have been wearing the seat belt and the frontal steering column and knee bolster air bags deployed. The case occupant had serious injuries and was transported hospital and then transferred to the trauma center.
