This crash occurred at night with street lights at an traffic signal controlled intersection. The weather was clear with dry conditions. Vehicle one (V1), a 1999 Honda Civic, four door sedan, was traveling eastbound in lane two of a four lane, two way street with a speed limit posted at 35 mph/56kmph. V1 crossed over the intersection and then entered into the opposite lane two where vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Lexus RX300, utility vehicle, was stopped westbound at the intersection. The front left of V1 impacted the front left of V2. The case occupant is the driver of V1 who is a 53 year old female wearing a lap/shoulder belt with the frontal airbag deploying. No other occupants were travelling in V1. The case occupant was injured and tranported to the trauma center. V1 was disabled and towed and V2 was not reported as being towed from the scene.
