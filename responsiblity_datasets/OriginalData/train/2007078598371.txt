It was dusk with no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time of this single-vehicle crash. The roadway is undivided, level and consists of one lane in each direction. In the westbound direction, this roadway curves to the left. The speed limit for this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph).

Vehicle one (V1), a 1985 Chevrolet C20 pickup, was westbound in lane one. V1 traveled off the right side of the roadway. The driver steered to the left and V1 returned to the roadway and began to rotate counterclockwise. V1 continued rotating and traveled off the left side of the road. V1 rolled over to the right eight quarter-turns and the right side struck a concrete ditch. V1 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

The driver of V1, a seventy-two-year-old male, was ejected from the vehicle and was killed. No surrogate was available for an interview. No further information is available for this driver.

The critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded: this vehicle traveling; off the edge of the road on the right side. The critical reason for the critical pre-crash event for V1 was coded as: type of driver error unknown. Overcompensation was an associated factor in this crash. It is unknown why the vehicle initially departed the roadway to the right. There were no known vehicle or environmental related factors.
