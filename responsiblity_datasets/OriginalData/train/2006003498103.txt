This crash occurred in the westbound parking lane of a one-way roadway with one lane of travel. The roadway was straight and level, and it was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions. The speed limit was 48kmph (30 mph), and it was an early morning on a weekend and the traffic flow was normal.

V1, a 1985 BMW 318i with one occupant, was westbound. V2, a 1995 Nissan Altima was unoccupied and legally parked in the parking lane. V3, a 1998 Dodge B-Series Cargo Van was also legally parked in the parking lane in front of V2. V1 swerved into the northern parking lane and the front impacted the left side of V2, and then the front of V1 impacted the back of V3. V1 came to a final rest next to V3. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

V1, the BMW 318i was driven by an unknown person who fled the scene shortly after the crash.

The Critical Pre-Crash Event for V1 was coded this vehicle traveling, over the lane line on right side of travel lane. The Critical Reason for the Critical Event was coded as a driver related factor, an unknown type of driver error. There are no known associated factors that could be coded to V1.
