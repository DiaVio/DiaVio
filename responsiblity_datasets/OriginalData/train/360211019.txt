This moderate severity single vehicle frontal crash involved a 22 year-old female driver who was restrained with the lap/shoulder belt and the pretensioner actuated and the steering column mounted airbag deployed. This driver sustained serious injuries and was transported to a trauma center. This crash occurred on a freeway at night with street lights and clear, dry conditions. Vehicle one (V1), a 2008 Hyundai Accent two-door hatchback, was travelling southbound in lane 2 of four one-way lanes of a divided freeway bordered with paved shoulders and concrete traffic barriers. V1 departed the lane to the right and crossed over lane 1 and the shoulder and then the front of V1 impacted the concrete barrier. V1 then came to final rest mainly facing south on the paved shoulders. V1 was towed due to damage. The driver of V1 is the case occupant. She is 22 years old and was wearing the manual lap/shoulder belt and the pretensioner actuated and the front steering column mounted airbag deployed. She sustained injuries and was transported to a trauma center.
