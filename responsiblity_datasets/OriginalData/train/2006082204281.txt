This two vehicle collision occurred at 1240 hours . The collision occurred on a two way north and southbound roadway. The pre-crash roadway for V 1 is curved to the right; V 2 is straight then curved to the left and level. Both roadways were paved with concrete and were wet. Traffic controlled by stop sign. There is a posted speed limit of 48 Kmph (30 mph) for both vehicles.

Vehicle 1 (V 1), a 1989 Ford Bronco, driven by a 63 year-old male was traveling northbound on the roadway in lane 1 negotiating a curve. Vehicle 2 (V 2), a 2003 Hyundai Santa Fe, driven by a 49 year-old female was traveling southbound in lane 1. V 1 was negotiating the curve at a high rate of speed and was unable to stay in his travel lane. V 1 then cross over the centerline impacting the front of V 2. Both vehicles came to final rest in the travel lane. Both vehicles towed due to damage, both driver were transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.
