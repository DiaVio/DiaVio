This two-vehicle crash occurred late in the afternoon on an eight-lane, downhill, dry, median divided, bituminous roadway that runs east to west and has a posted speed limit of 72 kmph (45 mph). This roadway intersects with a four-lane, dry, uphill, bituminous roadway that runs north to south and has a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). This intersection is controlled by a traffic signal (on colors). Traffic was very heavy at the time of the crash.

Vehicle one (V 1), a 2005 Toyota Prius driven by a 42 year-old female was traveling eastbound in lane two. Vehicle two (V 2) , a 2004 Toyota Four-Runner driven by a 43 year-old female was traveling southbound in lane two. Both vehicles were attempting to pass through the intersection. As V 1 entered the intersection, the front contacted the right side of V 2. Post impact, V 1 rotated clockwise and came to rest in lane one facing east while V 2 rolled two quarter turns towards its left upon its longitudinal axis and came to rest on its roof facing west. Both vehicles were towed from the scene the driver of V 2 was transported to a local medical facility. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.
