This crash occurred off the left side of the roadway. The roadway being traveled was a two-lane, two-way, non-divided, asphalt city roadway with a posted speed limit of 72 Kmph (55 mph). The crash occurred during daylight hours and there were no adverse conditions present at the time of the crash.

V1 a 1998 Plymouth Breeze was traveling west bound in the right curb lane. V1 lost control and departed the roadway to the left side, striking a mailbox, then a power pole. V1 was towed from the scene with moderate damage, and the driver was transported to the hospital. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

The driver of V1 a 25 year-old female stated that she was driving down the road and something in her steering broke. She then stated that she tried to steer and stop but could not regain control of the vehicle.

The Critical Pre-Crash Event Coded for V1 is "this vehicle control loss due to disabling vehicle failure, broken tie rod. The Critical reason for the Critical Event Coded for V1 is 'Vehicle related, steering failed".
