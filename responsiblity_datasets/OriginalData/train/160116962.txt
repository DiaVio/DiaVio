Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 2003 Hyundai Accent, four-door (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a female, age 25 as its driver and a female, age 26 as the front right passenger (case study participant). V1's occupants were not wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbags did not deploy.

The crash occurred during the hours of darkness. The weather at the time of the crash was clear and dry. The roadway surface was dry. The posted speed limit is 45 mph (72 kmph).

The crash occurred on a two lane rural route. The roadway travels primarily in a north-south direction. A small gravel shoulder and a grass ditch border both sides of the roadway. The northbound direction has a negative elevation between the roadway surface and the ditch area that leads to a wooded area. The roadway is level in the area of the crash.

V1 was traveling northbound. V1 lost control for unknown reasons. V1 entered the southbound lane and corrected to the right. V1 ran off the right side of the road. V1 travelled into the ditch and struck a tree with the right side, V1 continued on impacting a tree head-on.

The case study participant were transported by air to a trauma center. The driver was treated and released at a local hospital emergency room.
