This single vehicle crash occurred during daylight hours with clear, dry conditions on a two way, two lane business/campus street. Case vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Chrysler PT Cruiser four door hatchback, was southbound on this street and departed the lane left, crossed the opposite lane of travel and then departed the street over the curb. V1 then continued on a grassy area before the front of V1 impacted a large propane tank where it came to final rest. V1 was damaged and towed. The driver of V1 is the CIREN subject who is a 44 years old male who was not belted and the frontal steering column air bag deployed. The subject sustained serious injuries and was transported to the trauma center. No other occupants were in V1.
