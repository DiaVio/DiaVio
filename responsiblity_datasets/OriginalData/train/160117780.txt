Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 2004 Toyota Tacoma, pickup truck (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a male, age 57 (case study participant) as its driver and the front right passenger was a female, age 84 . V1's occupants were wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbags deployed.

The crash occurred during the hours of daylight. The weather at the time of the crash was clear and dry. The roadway surface was dry. The posted speed limit is 35mph (56 kmph).

The crash occurred on a five lane roadway. The westbound direction has one left turn lane, one through lane and a right turn-through lane. The eastbound direction has two through lanes. The roadway is divided by a raised concrete median. The roadway is level in each direction. The westbound shoulder is bordered by a concrete sidewalk.

V1 was traveling westbound in the right turn-through lane. V1 ran off the right side of the roadway due to the driving being ill. V1 mounted the curb and the sidewalk continuing westbound. V1 struck a wooden utility pole head-on.

The case study participant and passenger were transported by air to a trauma center.
