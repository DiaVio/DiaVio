This crash occurred at intersection during daylight hours with clear, dry conditions. Vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Toyota Camry four door sedan, was travelling southbound in lane 2 of a five lane, two way street with a center left turn lane with a posted speed limit of 30 mph/48 kmph. V1 was passing through an uncontrolled intersection when vehicle two (V2), an unknown passenger vehicle, was travelling northbound and attempted to make a left turn in front of V1. The front of V1 impacted the front of V2. V2 was forced rearward and counterclockwise as V1continued south of the intersection and came to rest in lane one. Both vehicles were damaged and towed. The case occupant is the driver of V1, a 44 year old female, who was wearing the manual lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbag deployed. No other occupants were in the case vehicle. The case driver was transported locally to a hospital and then transferred to the trauma center.
