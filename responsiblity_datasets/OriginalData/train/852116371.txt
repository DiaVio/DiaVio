This crash occurred at an intersection with clear, dry conditions and speed limits posted at 35mph/56kmph for all directions. Vehicle one (V1), a 2000 Saturn SW2, four door station wagon, was eastbound in the left turn lane a five lane street with the center left turn lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1978 Chevrolet Camaro, two door coupe, was southbound in lane one of a five lane street with a center left turn lane. V1 entered the intersection to make a left turn and V2 locked up the brakes prior to entering the intersection and the front of V2 impacted the left side of V1 in the intersection. V1 was pushed eastward and rotated counterclockwise and came to final rest in the middle of the intersection. V2 continued southeasterly through the intersection and came to rest facing southeast in the south part of the intersection. Both vehicles were disabled and towed. The case occupant, an eight-year-old female in the second left seat position, was wearing the lap and shoulder belt along with sitting on a thick kitchen chair cushion as a booster. The impact to V1 was directly into passenger door nearest to this case occupant with direct damage of the V2's hood at the window and window frame which was near the height of the child's head. The case occupant was transported to the trauma center. The only other occupant in the vehicle was the driver who was 51 year old female who was wearing the lap/shoulder belt with the frontal air bag deployement and was treated for minor injuries.
