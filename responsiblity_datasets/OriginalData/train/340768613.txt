This case involves a frontal impact between the front of a passenger car and the rear of a transit bus. The impact involved the front of the passenger car under-riding the rear of the bus. The case vehicle a 2014, four-door, Kia Rio was traveling in the inside northbound lane of a divided, three-lane, dry, asphalt traffic-way behind vehicle two (V2), a transit bus. It was daylight and clear. V2 slowed as it approached a bus stop. As the case vehicle closed on V2, the front-end of the case vehicle struck the rear-end of V2. The front of the case vehicle under-rode the rear of V2. The case vehicle was pulled approximately 35 meters before V2 came to a controlled stop in the inside northbound lane, facing north with the case vehicle in tandem. The sole occupant of the case vehicle was the unrestrained 29-year-old male driver. He had the benefit of a deployed frontal-impact air bag. The driver sustained a serious injury and was transported to a level-one trauma center and enrolled as a case occupant.
