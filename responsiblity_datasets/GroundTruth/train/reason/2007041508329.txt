The driver of V1 admitted to not knowing which lane she was traveling in and not knowing the color of her light as she entered the intersection. A witness stated that the driver of V1 ran the red light and was also talking on a cell phone at the time of the crash. The Critical Pre-crash Event for V1 was "other vehicle encroachment from crossing street, turning into opposite direction," indicating that V1 failed to yield to oncoming traffic. Therefore, V1 is primarily responsible for the accident due to the driver's failure to recognize the red light, illegal maneuver of running the red light, and being distracted by a cell phone conversation.
