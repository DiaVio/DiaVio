Case Summary

The crash occurred on a two-lane, one-way, curved to the left, dry daylight roadway with a speed limit of 45Mph(72Kph). The weather at the time of the crash was good with clear skies. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

V1 a 2001 Mercury Cougar was being driven by a 56yr old male driver. V1 was traveling North in the right lane. The driver of V1 stated that this was his first time on the road, and he did not know the area very well. He then stated that he saw the traffic light ahead and hit the brakes very hard. The vehicle started to go into a counter-clockwise rotation. He steered to the left but this made it worse. The vehicle went off the roadway to the left, struck a light pole then spun 360 degrees around striking a sign and coming to final rest.

The critical pre-crash event coded for V1 is off the edge of the road to the left. The critical reason coded for V1 is a performance error( Panicking).
