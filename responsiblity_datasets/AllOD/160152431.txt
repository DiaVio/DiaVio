This frontal crash involves a 69-year-old restrained male wit no air bag deployment who sustained serious injury. The crash occurred during night time hours in dry and clear weather. V1 was driven by a 69-year-old case occupant who was restrained by the available three-point manual belt. No air bags deployed during the crash.
