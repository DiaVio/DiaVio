Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 2001 Toyota Corolla, four-door (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a female, age 19 as its driver and lone occupant. V1's occupant was wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbags did not deploy.

The crash occurred during the hours of darkness. The weather at the time of the crash was clear and dry. The roadway surface was dry. The posted speed limit is 50 mph (80 kmph).

The crash occurred at an intersection with a seven lane divided trafficway. The intersection is controlled by a traffic signal. The northbound lanes consist of two through lanes and a dedicated right turn lane. The southbound lanes consist of two through lanes and two dedicated left turn lanes. The trafficway is divided by a grass median. The trafficway is level in the area of the crash.

V1 was traveling northbound in the left turn lane. V1 attempted to turn left and lost control, running off the left side of the roadway. V1 entered onto the west shoulder of the southbound lanes profiling the left side of the vehicle. V1 slid through a grassy area and over a large ditch. V1 impacted the embankment of the ditch with its left side then rolled over onto its top.

The case study participant were transported by air to a trauma center.
