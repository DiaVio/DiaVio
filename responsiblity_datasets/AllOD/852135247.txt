This single vehicle crash occurred at night with street lights at an intersection with freeway access on a bridge overpass. The weather was overcast with dry conditions. Vehicle one (V1), a 2005 Scion Xa four door hatchback, was eastbound in lane one of a seven lane, two way street that has an intersection for freeway access that is just before a bridge overpass. V1 was making a right turn on to the freeway access ramp and departed the roadway to the left and the front of V1 impacted the barrier end of the bridge overpass structure. V1 was damaged and towed. The driver was initially treated and released at a local hospital, and then later transferred to the trauma center with serious injuries.
