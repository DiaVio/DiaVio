Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 2000 Dodge Stratus, four-door (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a female, age 36 as its driver and lone occupant. V1's occupant was wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbags deployed.

The crash occurred during the hours of daylight. The weather at the time of the crash was clear and dry. The roadway surface was dry. The posted speed limit is 35 mph (56 kmph).

The crash occurred at an intersection controlled by a traffic signal. The southbound lanes on the north intersection leg consist of two right turn lanes and one through lane. The westbound lanes on the west intersection leg consist of three through lanes. The westbound left through lane is bordered by a concrete wall. The the north leg of the roadway has a positive grade of 2%.

V1 was traveling southbound in the left right turn lane. V1 was attempting to make a right turn when it ran off the left side of the roadway and struck the concrete wall head-on.

The case study participant was transported by air to a trauma center.
