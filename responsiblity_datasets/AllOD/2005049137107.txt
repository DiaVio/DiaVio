This one-vehicle crash occurred during early evening hours, just past an intersection with a curve to the right. The roadway is asphalt with four east/west lanes. It was dark but lighted and the roadway was dry and free of defects. There were no adverse atmospheric conditions at the time. The posted speed limit is 56 kmph (35 mph).

Signal lights with pedestrian signals control the intersection. West of the intersection, the roadway is a 3-lane divided concrete roadway. After V1 passed through the intersection, the vehicle entered the curbed median and the front left impacted a light pole. V1 rotated counter clock-wise and came to final rest with a portion of its front end in the median west of the point of impact. V1 was towed from the scene due to damage sustained in the collision. A 38-year-old male, police at the scene unofficially identified as the driver of V1, had been drinking and would not admit to operating the vehicle.
