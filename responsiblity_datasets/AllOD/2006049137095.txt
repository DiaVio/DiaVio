This two-vehicle crash occurred in a traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west roadway is level, straight and divided by a curbed median. The speed limit on this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). The eastbound lanes consist of two through lanes, a left turn lane and a right turn lane. The westbound lanes consist of two through lanes and a left turn lane. This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.

Vehicle one (V1), a 2007 Lexus RX350, was stopped eastbound in lane four, waiting to turn left (north). Vehicle two (V2), a 2007 Toyota Camry, was westbound in lane two. V1 turned left and the front struck the front of V2. V2 was towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The driver of V1, a fifty-two-year-old male, was traveling from work to his home. He was healthy, well rested and familiar with his vehicle. This driver travels this route daily. He stated that he believed that V2 would stop because the light turned yellow.

The driver of V2, a twenty-seven-year-old female, was traveling from her home to the airport. She was well rested, healthy and comfortable with her vehicle. This driver travels on this roadway several times each month.
