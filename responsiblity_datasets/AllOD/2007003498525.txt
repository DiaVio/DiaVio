The crash occurred in the eastbound lane of a one-way roadway with one lane of travel. This was a straight, level, and bituminous roadway with a speed limit of 48kmph (30mph). It was dark and lighted, and clear with no adverse conditions. It was an evening on a weekday, and the traffic flow was normal.

V1, a Chrysler Town and Country, minivan with one occupant was traveling eastbound in lane one. V2, a parked and unoccupied Kia Rondo was parked on the northern parking lane. The front of V1 impacted the back of V2 and came to a final rest approximately five meters east of the point of impact. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

V1, the Chrysler Town and Country, was driven by a 44 year-old male who reported no injuries. He stated to the Police that while driving eastbound, he struck the rear of V2. The driver declined to participate in the survey.

The Critical Pre-crash event for V1 was "This vehicle traveling, over the lane line on left side of travel lane". The Critical Reason for the Critical event was coded as an "unknown reason". The possible Associated factors were also coded unknown because no interview was obtained.
