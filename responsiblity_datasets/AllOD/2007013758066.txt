This crash occurred off the right side of the roadway. The roadway that was being traveled is a two-way, four-lane, non-divided asphalt road with a posted speed limit of 48 Kmph (30 Mph). The crash occurred during daylight hours and there were no adverse conditions present.

V1 a 1998 Dodge Ram 1500 was traveling westbound in the right curb lane when it went off the roadway to the right side contacting a power pole. V1 was towed from the scene with moderate damage, and the driver of V1 was transported to the hospital with minor injuries. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1.

The driver of V1 a 49 year-old female stated that she was in the right lane traveling about 64 kmph (40 mph). She stated that the car next to her swerved into her lane and forced her off the roadway. She tried to avoid the pole but could not.

I talked with officers on the scene and they spoke with a witness that stated there were no other cars around her when she left the roadway.

The Critical Pre-Crash Event coded for V1 is "This vehicle traveling, off the edge of the roadway to the right side". The Critical Reason for the Critical Event coded for V1 is "Driver related, Overcompensation". The medicals reported her as having COPD, hepatitis C, vasculitis, colostomy and post-traumatic stress disorder. She was taking 21 medications which include a drug to stop her from drinking alcohol. The Police report reported her as positive for alcohol. Her alcohol test was negative. The medical report also stated that her husband is recently deceased.
