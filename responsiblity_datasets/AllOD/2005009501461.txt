This crash occured on a two lane, undivided roadway, in a rural area. Conditions were daylight and dry just after noon on a weekday. The roadway had a posted speed limit of 64kmph (40mph).

V1, a 2001 Honda Civic, was traveling north negotiating a set of curves. V1 departed the roadway to the right, re entered the roadway, entered the oncoming lane, departed the roadway to the left and contacted a tree before coming to rest. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1.

V1 was driven by a 20 year-old male and had a 20 year-old male occupant who were both transported via medivac with incapacitating injuries. Both driver and occupant refused to be interviewed. The vehicle was towed due to damages.

The Critical Pre Crash Event for this vehicle was coded off the edge of the roadway to the right side. The Critical Reason was coded Unknown.
