Vehicle one (V1), a 1998 Chevrolet S-10 pickup, was traveling north in lane two of a four lane, two-way roadway under dry, daylight conditions. V2, a 1997 Freightliner Medium-Heavy Dump Truck, was traveling north in the same roadway and lane in front of V1, stopped waiting to make a left turn. The front of V1 contacted the rear of V2. V1 was towed due to damage, V2 was not towed. The driver of V1 and case study occupant, a 19-year-old male, was not wearing the available 3-point lap and shoulder belt but had the frontal air bags deploy. The driver of V1 was transported to a level-one trauma center for treatment.
