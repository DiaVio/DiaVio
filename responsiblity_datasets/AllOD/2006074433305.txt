This two-vehicle crash occurred near a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west roadway is straight, level, undivided and consists of two lanes in each direction. The speed limit on this roadway is 48 kmph (30 mph). It was dark with streetlights on at the time of the crash and light rain was falling.

Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Chevrolet Cavalier, was stopped at the intersection, westbound in lane two, waiting to turn left (south). Vehicle two (V2) was westbound in lane two behind V1. The front of V2 struck the back of V1. V1 was towed due to damage. V2 left the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

The driver of V1, an eighteen-year-old female, was traveling with a nineteen-year-old female and a seventeen-year-old male. She had just dropped her child off at a friend's home and was on the way to another friend's home. This driver was well rested, in good health (takes Albuterol and Advair) and was comfortable with her vehicle and passengers. She travels this route daily.

The driver of V2 left the scene. No further information is available for this driver.
