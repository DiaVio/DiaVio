This two-vehicle crash occurred mid-afternoon on a two-lane, two-way roadway separated by a painted centerline stripe. There is one westbound and one eastbound lane. The posted speed limit for the roadway is posted 80 kph (50 mph). the roads were dry, straight asphalt, and the sky was clear.

Vehicle one(V-1), a 2000 Chevrolet Astro Van driven by a 25 year-old male was traveling westbound and pulled onto the right shoulder and stopped waiting to make a U-turn to travel eastbound. Vehicle two(V-2), a 1994 Ford F-250 pick-up driven by a 35 year-old male with one passenger was traveling westbound to the rear of V-1. V1 began to make a U-turn and was struck on the left side by the front of V-2. V-1 rotated clockwise and traveled off the south edge of the roadway and came to rest in a dirt field facing south. V-2 attempted to avoid V-1 by steering to the left and braking (no lock up). The impact forced V-2 off the south edge of the roadway coming to rest with the front on a berm facing in a southwesterly direction. V-1 was towed due to damage. V-2 was also towed due to damage. Police Reported Travel Speed is 16 kmph for V1 and 16 kmph for V2.
