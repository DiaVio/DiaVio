This three-vehicle crash occurred in the morning on a three-lane, level, bituminous, dry, roadway that curves to the left. The posted speed limit for this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). This roadway intersects with a three-lane, downhill, straight, bituminous roadway that had a posted speed limit of 56 kmph (35 mph). The intersection was controlled by a traffic light on colors with no pedestrian signals.

Vehicle one (V 1), a 1993 Chevrolet Corsica driven by a 27 year-old male was traveling westbound in lane one. Vehicle two (V 2), a 1996 Saturn SC sedan driven by a 57 year-old female was traveling southbound in lane two. Vehicle three (V 3), a 1998 Toyota Camry driven by a 50 year-old female was stopped facing eastbound in lane one. As V 1 passed through the intersection the front struck the left side of V 2. Post-impact V 1 deflected to the left and the front struck the front of V 3. V 2 came to rest facing west in lane one. V 1 came to rest facing south. And V 3 came to rest facing east in lane one. V 1 and V 3 were towed from the scene the driver of V 2 was transported to a local medical facility. V 3 was driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 48 kmph for V1 and 48 kmph for V2.

The critical reason driver related factor "illegal maneuver" (disregard of traffic control signal, witness driving behind V 2 stated they had the green light). The pre-event movement for V 2 coded going straight, category this vehicle traveling turning left at intersection. Critical reason not coded to this vehicle.
