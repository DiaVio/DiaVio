Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 1998 Chevrolet Malibu, four-door (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a female, age 16 as its driver and lone occupant. V1's occupant was wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbags deployed.

The crash occurred during the hours of daylight. The weather at the time of the crash was clear and dry. The roadway surface was dry. The posted speed limit is 35mph (56 kmph).

The crash occurred on a four lane roadway. The northbound direction lanes has a left hand curve. The northbound side has two through lanes and the southbound side has two through lanes. The roadway is divided by a raised grass median. The roadway is level in each direction. The northbound shoulder is bordered by a concrete sidewalk. A large brick fence borders a subdivision just off the edge of the northbound direction.

V1 was traveling northbound when it attempted to negotiate a left hand curve. V1 lost control and started to rotate in a clockwise direction. V1 continued to skid off the right side of the roadway and struck the brick fence with the left side.

The case study participant were transported by air to a trauma center.
