This crash occurred at night with street lights on the southbound lanes of a six lane divided freeway with dry conditions with a slight downgrade in the location. The posted speed limit was 60mph/97kmph. Vehicle one (V1), a 2006 Scion TC 2-door hatchback, was southbound in lane two of the three lanes and attempted to change lanes to the right behind an unknown vehicle in lane two. Another unknown vehicle was travelling at a slower speed in lane one and V1 then swerved left and began braking as it crossed over lanes two and three before impacting the left side concrete barrier with the front of V1. V1 rotated slightly counterclockwise and came to final rest in lane three facing east. The driver of V1 is the case occupant and the only occupant in V1. The case occupant is a 27 year old male who was wearing the lap and shoulder belt with the seat belt pretensioners firing. Two significant loading burns were located on the seat belt. The frontal airbag deployed as well as a knee bolster airbag. The driver was injured and transported to the trauma center. The vehicle was disabled and towed.
