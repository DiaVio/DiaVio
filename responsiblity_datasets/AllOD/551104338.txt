This crash occurred at night with streets lights and wet roadways with overcast. Case vehicle one (V1), a 1997 Mercury Sable four door, was northbound on a two lane, two way roadway with the speed limit posted at 35 mph/56 kmph. V1 approached an intersection with a stop sign for a seven lane, two way divided highway with a speed limit of 50 mph/80 kmph. Signs are posted that only one vehicle is permitted in the median crossing area. Case V1 crossed the eastbound lanes and then stopped in the median before continuing across the westbound lanes to continue straight northbound. Vehicle two (V2), a 2001 Ford E-Series van, was travelling westbound and the front of V2 impacted the right side of V1 at the intersection. V1 rotated counterclockwise post impact and the right side of V1 side slapped the left side of V2 as both vehicles then separated into the northwest corner of the intersection. The driver of V1 is the case occupant, an 80 year old female, who was wearing a lap/shoulder belt and the frontal airbag deployed. No other occupants were in V1. Case occupant was transported to the trauma center for her injuries.
