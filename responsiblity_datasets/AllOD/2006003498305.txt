The crash occurred in the westbound lane of a one-way, one lane roadway. The roadway was bituminous, straight and level. It was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions. The traffic conditions were normal, and the speed limit was 48kmph (30mph).

V1, a 2005 Toyota Corolla four door with one occupant, was traveling westbound in lane one. V2, a 2005 Honda Accord four door, was legally parked and unoccupied on the southern parking lane. V3, a 1998 Toyota Camry four door, was legally parked and unoccupied in front of V2. V4, a 1993 Toyota Camry four door, was legally parked and unoccupied in front of V3. V1 swerved into the parking lane and impacted V2 on the right side, then V3 on the right side and then V4 on the right side before coming to a final rest after impacting V4. Police Reported Travel Speed is Not reported for V1 and Not reported for V2.

V1, the Toyota Corolla was driven by a 23 year-old female who reported no injuries. The driver stated that she lost control of her vehicle causing her to sideswipe V2, V3 and V4. The driver's father declined the driver to participate in the study.

The Critical Pre-crash Event for V1 was "this vehicle traveling, off the edge of the road on the left side". The Critical Reason for the Critical Event was an “unknown reason”. The possible associated factors were coded unknown because no interview was obtained.
