Vehicle 1 (V1) is a 1996 Jeep Cherokee, four-door (case study vehicle). V1 was occupied by a female, age 30 as its driver and lone occupant. V1's occupant was not wearing the available lap and shoulder belt and the frontal airbag did not deploy.

The crash occurred during the hours of daylight. The weather at the time of the crash was cloudy and dry. The roadway surface was covered in ice. The posted speed limit is 45 mph (72 kmph).

The crash occurred on a two lane roadway. The northbound lane has a right curve and a ditch for a shoulder. The ditch is bordered by a wooden fence. The southbound lane has a left curve and a ditch for a shoulder. The roadway is divided by a double yellow line. The roadway is level in the area of the crash.

V1 was traveling southbound when it attempted to negotitate the curve. Due to the ice covering the roadwy, V1 lost control and ran off the left side of the roadway. V1 ran off into the ditch and started to roll onto its left side, during the roll V1 struck a wooden fence with the left side windshield header. V1 continued to roll, coming to rest on its roof.

The case study participant was transported by ground to a local emergency room then transfered by air to a trauma center.
