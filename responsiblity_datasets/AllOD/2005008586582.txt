This crash occurred on a straight section of a two-lane asphalt roadway. The speed limit was 40mph (64kph).

Vehicle 1 was a 2003 Hyundai Elantra with one occupant. Vehicle 2 was a 2004 Chevrolet S10 pickup with one occupant. Police Reported Travel Speed is Unknown for V1 and Unknown for V2.

Vehicle 1 was traveling west in the westbound lane. Vehicle 2 was traveling east in the eastbound lane. As vehicle 1 completed a right curve, the vehicle crossed into the eastbound lane. The front right of vehicle 1 contacted the front left of vehicle 2. Vehicle 1 came to rest in the westbound lane. Vehicle 2 departed the right road edge and the front left of the vehicle contacted a fire hydrant. Vehicle 2 rotated 90 degrees counterclockwise and rolled laterally to the right. Vehicle 2 came to rest on its roof in a yard off the right road edge.

The critical precrash event was vehicle 1 encroaching into the travel lane of vehicle 2.

The critical reason for the critical precrash event was the internal distraction of the driver of vehicle 1. Both drivers refused to be interviewed, but a witness stated that the driver of vehicle 1 told him he was looking down within the vehicle moments before the crash.
