This three vehicle crash occurred at 1450 hours on the eastbound two lane bituminous urban level roadway with a raised concrete divider separating the east from west travel lanes. The posted speed limit is 40 kmph (25 mph) with no adverse weather conditions.

V-1 a 2005 Nissan Maxima sedan driven by a 37 year-old female with four passengers was traveling east in the number one lane. V-2 a 2001 Chevrolet Tahoe SUV driven by a 47 year-old female with two passengers was traveling east about one car length behind V-1. V-3 a 1999 Chevrolet Suburban driven by a 35 year-old female with four passengers was traveling east behind V-2. The driver of V-3 looked in the rearview mirror to check on her children and when she looked back V-2 had stopped. V-3's front collided with the back of V-2 causing V-2 to go forward and hit the rear of V-1. All vehicle came to final rest in the travel lane. V-3 was towed from the scene, V-2, V-1 were driven from the scene. Police Reported Travel Speed is 0 kmph for V1 and 0 kmph for V2.
