This crash occurred at an intersection during daylight hours with clear, dry conditions. Case vehicle one (V1), a 2007 Acura TSX four door sedan, was traveling westbound in lane 2 of a five lane, two way street with a center left turn lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1994 GMC medium/heavy garbage truck was eastbound travelling opposite direction on the same street as V1 and was stopped in the left turn lane at an intersection waiting for oncoming traffic to clear to make a left turn. V1 entered the intersection and crossed into the eastbound left turn lane and the front left corner of V1 impacted the front left corner of V2. V2 remained in the same stopped position after the impact. V1 rotated counterclockwise and came to final rest in lane two facing south. Both vehicles had to be towed due to damage. The driver of V1 is the case occupant and no other occupants in V1. This driver of V1 was a 20 year old male who was wearing the lap and shoulder belt and the steering column mounted air bag deployed on impact. This driver sustained serious injuries and was transported to the trauma center.
